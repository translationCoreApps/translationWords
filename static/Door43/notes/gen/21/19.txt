===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God opened her eyes, and she saw** - God making Hagar aware of the well is spoken of as if he literally opened her eyes. AT: "God caused Hagar to see" or "God showed her" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the skin** - "the container made of skin" or "the bag"
  * **the lad** - "the boy" or "Ishmael"
  * **God was with the lad** - Here the phrase "was with" is an idiom that means God helped or blessed the boy. AT: "God guided the boy" or "God blessed the boy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **became an archer** - "became very skilled at using a bow and arrows"
  * **got a wife** - "found a wife"
