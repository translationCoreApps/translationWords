===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sixty-one thousand ... five thousand ... one hundred** - "61,000 ... 5,000 ... 100" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **gold darics** - A "daric" was a small gold coin used by the Persian Empire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **minas** - A mina is a unit of weight. One mina equals 550 grams. Minas are normally linked with measuring silver. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
  * **tunics** - garments worn next to the skin
