===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They will do these things because they have not known the Father nor me** - They will kill some believers because they do not know God the Father or Jesus. 
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **when their hour comes** - Here "hour" is a metonym that refers to the time when people will persecute Jesus' followers. AT: "when they cause you to suffer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the beginning** - This is a metonym that refers to the first days of Jesus' ministry. AT: "when you first started following me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
