===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the man of integrity** - This does not refer to a specific person. It is a general statement. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **mark the upright** - "note carefully the good people" 
  * **the future for the wicked man is cut off** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will bring their family line to an end" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the future** - This refers to his descendants. AT: "his descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the wicked man** - This does not refer to a specific person. It is a general statement. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **is cut off** - The destruction of the wicked is spoken of as if they were a branch of plant that was cut off and thrown away. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:037:008|37:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
