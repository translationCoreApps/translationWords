===== translationNotes: =====



  * **David took these words to heart** - Here "took ... to heart" is a metonymy to think seriously about what was said. AT: "David thought seriously about what the servants said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in their hands** - Here "hands" are a metonym for the person. AT: "in their presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
