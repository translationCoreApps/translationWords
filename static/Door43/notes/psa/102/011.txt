===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My days are like a shadow that fades** - David compares his time left to live on earth to a fading shadow. AT: "My time to remain alive is short like an evening shadow that will soon be gone" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **I have withered like grass** - As David's body becomes weak and he is nearing the end of his life, he compares himself to withering grass. AT: "my body has become weak like withered grass" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **withered** - dry and wrinkled
  * **your fame is for all generations** - "you will be recognized for all generations to come"
  * **fame** - being known by many people
