===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a house of idols** - This refers to a house used specifically for worshiping idols. This can be stated clearly. AT: "a house for worshiping idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **everyone did what was right in his own eyes** - Here each person's "eyes" is a metonym for each person's judgment or discretion. AT: "each person did what he decided was right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
