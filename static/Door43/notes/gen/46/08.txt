===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the names** - This refers to the names of the people that the author is about to list.
  * **of the children of Israel** - "of Israel's family members"
  * **Hanoch and Pallu and Hezron and Carmi ... Jemuel, Jamin, Ohad, Jachin, Zohar, and Shaul ... Gershon, Kohath, and Merari** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
