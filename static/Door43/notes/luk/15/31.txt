===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The father said to him** - The word "him" refers to the older son.
  * **this brother of yours** - The father was reminding the older son that the one who just came home was his brother. 
  * **this brother of yours  was dead, and is now alive** - This metaphor speaks of the brother being gone as if he was dead. AT: "it was as if this brother of yours was dead and became alive again" or "had died, but he is now alive." See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:15:22|15:24]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he was lost, and has now been found** - This metaphor speaks of the son being gone as if he was lost. AT: "it is as if this brother of yours was lost and now I found him" or "this brother of yours was lost and has returned home." See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:15:22|15:24]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
