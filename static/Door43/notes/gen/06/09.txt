===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This begins the story of Noah, which continues into chapter 9.

  * **These were the events concerning Noah** - "This is the account of Noah"
  * **walked with God** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:05:21|5:21]].
  * **Noah became the father of three sons** - "Noah had three sons" or "Noah's wife had three sons"
  * **Shem, Ham, and Japheth** - Translators may add the following footnote: "The sons are not listed in the order in which they were born."
