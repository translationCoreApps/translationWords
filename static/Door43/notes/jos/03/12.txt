===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Joshua continues to tell Israel of the miracle that Yahweh will do.
  * **General Information:** - Just as Israel's fathers experience crossing the Red Sea, these people will experience crossing the Jordan river on dry land.

  * **the soles of the feet** - This refers to the bottom of their feet.
  * **upstream** - This word refers to the direction the Jordan River water is flowing toward Israel.
  * **stand in one heap** - The water will stay in one spot or place. It will not flow around the priests.
