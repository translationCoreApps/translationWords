===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This verse has a command followed by the reason for the command. If your language requires the reason to precede the command: "Because of what Yahweh has done for you, you righteous people, be glad and give thanks when you remember his holiness."

  * **Be glad in Yahweh** - "Be glad because of what Yahweh has done for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **when you remember his holiness** - Possible meanings are 1) "when you remember how holy he is" or 2) "to his holy name," a metonym for "to him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
