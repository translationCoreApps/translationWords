===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Gog.
  * **General Information:** - These verses list the various nations who will join the Gog's army. 

  * **set hooks in your jaw** - Here "hooks in your jaw" represents God's control over Gog. People put hooks in the mouths of animals so that they could lead the animals wherever they wanted. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in full armor ... holding swords ... with shields and helmets** - These phrases describe an army that is ready for battle.
  * **Gomer** - a nation that lived north of the Black Sea
  * **Beth Togarmah** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:27:14|27:14]].
