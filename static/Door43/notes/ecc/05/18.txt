===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Look** - "pay attention" or "listen up"
  * **what I have seen to be good and suitable** - Here the words "good" and "suitable" mean basically the same thing. The second intensifies the meaning of the first. AT: "what I have seen to be the best thing to do." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **during the days of this life that God has given us** - AT: "as long as God allows us to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **man's assignment** - Possible meanings are 1) "man's reward" or 2) "man's lot in life."
