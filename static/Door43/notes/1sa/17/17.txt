===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ephah** - An ephah is a unit of measurement equal to about 22 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **the captain of their thousand** - "the captain of their unit of one thousand soldiers." The army was divided up into groups of one thousand men, and each of these groups had a captain leading them.
  * **See how your brothers are doing** - "Check and find out how your brothers are doing"
