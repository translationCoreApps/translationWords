===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he became the father of Enosh** - Here "father" means his actual father, not his grandfather. AT: "he had his son Enosh."
  * **Enosh** - This is the name of a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **and became the father of more sons and daughters** - "and had more sons and daughters"
  * **Seth lived 912 years** - "Seth lived a total of 912 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **and then he died** - This phrase is repeated throughout the chapter. Use the ordinary word for "died."
