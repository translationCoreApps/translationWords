===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A song of ascents** - Possible meanings are 1) "song people sang while going up to Jerusalem for a celebration" or 2) "song people sang while going up the steps into the temple" or 3) "song whose words are like steps." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]].
  * **lift up my eyes** - This expression means "look at" or "focus my attention on." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **From where will my help come?** - The writer uses this question to focus attention on the source of his help. AT: "I will tell you from where my help comes." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **My help comes from Yahweh** - This is the answer to the previous question.
