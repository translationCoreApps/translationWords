===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The speaker changes from Jeremiah to Yahweh.

  * **the priests are polluted** - AT: "the priests are sinful"
  * **in my house** - Here Yahweh's temple is compared to a house, a place where Yahweh is said to live among his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **this is Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:07|1:8]]. AT: "this is what Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **like a slippery place in the darkness** - AT: "unstable, or dangerous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **in the year of their punishment** - AT: "when their time for punishment comes"
