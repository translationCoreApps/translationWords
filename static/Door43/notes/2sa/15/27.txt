===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zadok ... Ahimaaz ... Jonathan ... Abiathar** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Are you not a seer?** - This rhetorical question is used to rebuke Zadok and can be translated as a statement. AT: "You will be able to find out what is happening." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **See** - This is just used to continue the quotation.
  * **until word comes from you to inform me** - "until a messenger comes from you to tell me what is happening in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
