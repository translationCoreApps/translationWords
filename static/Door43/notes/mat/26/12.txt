===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ointment** - This is oil that has a pleasing smell. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:26:06|26:7]].
  * **Truly I say to you** - "I tell you the truth." This adds emphasis to what Jesus says next.
  * **wherever this gospel is preached** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "wherever people preach this gospel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **what this woman has done will also be spoken of in memory of her** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they will remember what this woman has done and will tell others about her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
