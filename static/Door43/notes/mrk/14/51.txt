===== translationNotes: =====


  * **linen** - cloth made from the fibers of a flax plant
  * **that was wrapped around him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that he had wrapped around himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **When the men seized him** - "When the men seized that man"
  * **he left the linen garment** - As the man was trying to run away, the others would have grabbed at his clothing, trying to stop him.
