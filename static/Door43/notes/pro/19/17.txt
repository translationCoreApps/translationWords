===== translationNotes: =====


  * **while there is hope** - AT: "while he is young" or "while he can still be taught"
  * **do not set your desire on putting him to death** - AT: "do not punish him so that he might die" or "you should always want your child to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **set your desire on** - AT: "be determined to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
