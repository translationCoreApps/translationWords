===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you made your judgment heard** - "you pronounced judgment" or "you announced how you were going to punish wicked people"
  * **the earth was** - "the people of the earth were" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **execute judgment** - punish bad people and reward good people
