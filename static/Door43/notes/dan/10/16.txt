===== translationNotes: =====

  * **One who looked like a human being** - This refers to the one who had just spoken to Daniel. AT: "This one who looked like a human"
  * **agony** - severe emotional suffering
  * **I am your servant. How can I talk with my master?** - The question emphasizes that Daniel cannot speak to the angel because Daniel is not his equal. These sentences can be combined. AT: "I am not able to answer you because I am only your servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **there is no breath left in me** - This idiom refers to breathing. AT: "I cannot breathe" or "it's very hard to breathe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]).
