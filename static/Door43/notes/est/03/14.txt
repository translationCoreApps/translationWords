===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A copy of the letter was made law in every province** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The officials in every province made a copy of the letter become the law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **province** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **In every province it was made known to all the people** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They told all the people in every province" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **this day** - "that day"
  * **The decree was also distributed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They also distributed the decree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the fortress** - This refers to a castle, stronghold or fortified city. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:2]].
  * **Susa** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **was in turmoil** - "was in a state of great confusion"
