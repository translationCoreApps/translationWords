===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I am not good ... why will Pharaoh listen to me?** - Moses asks this question hoping to change God's mind. This rhetorical question can be translated as as statement. AT: "I am not good ... Pharaoh will certainly not listen to me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
