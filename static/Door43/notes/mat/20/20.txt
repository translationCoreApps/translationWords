===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - In response to the question that the mother of two of the disciples asks, Jesus teaches his disciples about authority and serving others in the kingdom of heaven.

  * **the sons of Zebedee** - This refers James and John.
  * **at your right hand ... at your left hand** - These refer to having positions of power, authority, and honor. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in your kingdom** - Here "kingdom" refers to Jesus ruling as king. AT: "when you are king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
