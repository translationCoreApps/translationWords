===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul begins to close his letter and sends greetings from other churches, as well as from Prisca, Aquila, and Paul himself.

  * **household of Stephanas** - Stephanas was one of the first believers in the church at Corinth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Achaia** - This is the name of a province in Greece. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
