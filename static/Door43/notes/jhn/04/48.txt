===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Unless you see signs and wonders, you will not believe** - "Unless ... not believe" is a double negative. In some languages it is more natural to translate this statement in a positive form. AT: "Only if you see a miracle will you believe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]]) 
  * **believed the word** - Here "word" is a metonym that refers to the message that Jesus spoke. AT: "believed the message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
