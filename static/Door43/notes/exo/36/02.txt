===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bezalel** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:01|31:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Oholiab** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:06|31:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in whose mind Yahweh had given skill** - Here "mind" refers to the person who was made skillful by Yahweh. AT: "to whom Yahweh had given skill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **whose heart stirred within him** - Here "heart" refers to the person. The heart that responded to God is spoken of as if it were water stirred up by a storm.  AT: "who responded to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
