===== translationNotes: =====


  * **rise up and** - Possible meanings are 1) literally "stand up and" or 2) as a metonym, "actively." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **call her blessed** - saying that good things have happened to her because she has done good things. This can be translated as a direct quote. AT: "congratulate her" or "say, 'Yay, Mom!'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **surpassed** - "have done better than"
