===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have trodden grapes** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh. This imagery refers to Yahweh destroying his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For I looked forward to the day of vengeance** - Here "day" refers to a period of time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **vengeance** - Yahweh will punish in an appropriate way to bring about justice. His kind of vengeance differs from man's vengence.
  * **the year for my redemption** - Here "year" refers to a specific time appointed by Yahweh for restoring Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
