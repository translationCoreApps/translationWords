===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking to Job.

  * **I have heard the sound of your words saying** - AT: "I have heard you say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I am clean and without transgression; I am innocent, and there is no sin in me** - All of these statements mean the same thing. AT: "I am completely innocent of any wrongdoing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
