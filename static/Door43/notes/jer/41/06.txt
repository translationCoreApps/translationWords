===== translationNotes: =====


  * **to meet them** - "to meet the 80 men"
  * **Then it happened** - This phrase is used here to mark where the action starts. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **It came about that** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **Ishmael son of Nethaniah slaughtered them and threw them into a pit, he and the men who were with him** - It is stated it in 41:8 that Ishmael and his men did not kill all 80 men. You can state here that they killed most of the 80 men. AT: "Ishmael son of Nethaniah, and the men who were with him, killed most of the 80 men and threw them into a pit"
