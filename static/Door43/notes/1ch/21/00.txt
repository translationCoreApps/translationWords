====== 1 Chronicles 21 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Trust ==
To trust in one's own power instead of God's protection is a sin. David had been trusting God to protect him; but now he counted the men of military age so he could know the strength of his army. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
