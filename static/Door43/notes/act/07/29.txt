===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Stephen's audience already knew that Moses had married a Midianite woman when he fled Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])

  * **after hearing this** - The implied information is that Moses understood that the Israelites knew that he had killed an Egyptian the day before ([[:en:bible:notes:act:07:26|7:28]]). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When forty years were past** - "After 40 years passed." This was the amount of time Moses had been in Midian. AT: "Forty years after Moses fled from Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **an angel appeared** - Stephen's audience knew that God spoke through the angel. The UDB makes this explicit.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
