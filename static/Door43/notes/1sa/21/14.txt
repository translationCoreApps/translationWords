===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why have you brought him to me?** - Possible meanings are 1) Achish demands that his servants explain why they have brought David to him or 2) Achish is scolding them with a rhetorical question. AT: "You should have known not to bring him to me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Do I lack madmen, so that you have brought this fellow to behave like one in my presence?** - This rhetorical question is a rebuke. AT: "There are enough madmen here who waste my time. You should not have brought this fellow to behave like one in my presence." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Will this fellow really come into my house?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "Do not allow this fellow to enter my house." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
