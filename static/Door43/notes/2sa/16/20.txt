===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ahithophel** - See how you translated this man's name in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:15:11|15:12]].
  * **all Israel** - This metonym means all the people in the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **become a stench to your father** - This metaphor means to become offensive. AT: "become offensive to your father" or "become an enemy to your father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the hands of all who are with you will be strong** - Here "hands" is a synecdoche representing people. The "hands of all who are with you" means all those with you or all those loyal to you.  AT: "that news will strengthen the loyalty of all who follow you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
