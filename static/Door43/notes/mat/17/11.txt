===== translationNotes: =====

  * **restore all things** - "put things in order" or "get the people ready to receive the Messiah"
  * **But I tell you** - This adds emphasis to what Jesus says next.
  * **they ... their** - All occurrences of these words may mean either 1) the Jewish leaders or 2) all the Jewish people.
  * **the Son of Man will also suffer at their hands** - Here "hands" refers to power. AT: "they will make the Son of Man suffer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Son of Man** - Jesus is referring to himself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
