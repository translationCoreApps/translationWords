===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This psalm continues to tell about how God cares for us as a shepherd cares for his sheep. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **Even though I walk through a valley of darkest shadow** - The writer describes a person experiencing terrible trouble as if he were a sheep walking through a dark and dangerous valley. There a sheep could get lost or attacked by a wild animal. AT: "Though my life is like walking through a dark and dangerous valley" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will not fear harm** - The abstract noun "harm" can be stated as a verb. AT: "I will not be afraid of something harming me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **you are with me** - Here "you" refers to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **your rod and your staff comfort me** - The rod and staff represent protection because shepherds use them to protect their sheep from danger. AT: "I am not afraid because you protect me like a shepherd who protects his sheep with his rod and staff" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
