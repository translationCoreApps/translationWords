===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The story of Paul and Barnabas in Iconium continues.

  * **It came about in Iconium that** - Possible meanings here are 1) "It happened in Iconium that" or 2) "In Iconium as usual"
  * **spoke in such a way** - "spoke so powerfully." It may be helpful to state that they spoke the message about Jesus. AT: "spoke the message about Jesus so powerfully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the Jews who were disobedient** - This refers to a portion of the Jews who did not believe the message about Jesus.
  * **stirred up the minds of the Gentiles** - Causing the Gentiles to become angry is spoken of as if calm waters were disturbed. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the minds** - Here the word "minds" refers to the people. AT: "the Gentiles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the brothers** - Here "brothers" refers to Paul and Barnabas and the new believers.
