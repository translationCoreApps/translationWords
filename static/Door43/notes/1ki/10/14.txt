===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in one year** - "each year" (UDB). This refers to every year of Solomon's reign, and not to just one time.
  * **666 talents of gold** - A talent is a unit of weight equal to about 34 kilograms. AT: "almost 23,000 kilograms of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
