===== translationNotes: =====

  * **unclean** - A person who God considers spiritually unacceptable or defiled is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **because of a dead body** - This refers to someone touching a dead body. AT: "because you have touched a dead body" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **keep the Passover** - Here the word "keep" means to observe. AT: "observe the Passover" or "celebrate the Passover" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
