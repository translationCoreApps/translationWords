===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bezalel made ... for the south side** - For 36:20-23 see how you translated many of these words in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:15|26:15-18]].
  * **ten cubits ... one and a half cubits** - "10 cubits ... 1.5 cubits" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **two wooden pegs for joining** - A wooden peg is a small piece of wood sticking out beyond the end of the board so it can be secured.
