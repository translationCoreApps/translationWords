===== translationNotes: =====

  * **"It was because ... this law," Jesus said to them** - Some languages do not allow you to state in the middle of a quote who the speaker is. If so, you may identify Jesus as the speaker at the beginning. AT: "Jesus said to them, "It was because ... this law."" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_quotations]])
  * **your hard hearts that he wrote you** - Moses wrote the law for their ancestors, which they now were also supposed to follow. AT: "your ancestors' hard hearts that he wrote them this law" 
  * **your hard hearts** - This means that they were very stubborn. AT: "your stubbornness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **God made them** - "God made people"
