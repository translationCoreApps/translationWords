===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So it came about** - "So it happened." This phrase is used to introduce the next event in the storyline. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]]) 
  * **a whirlwind** - a strong wind that spins around and around
  * **As Yahweh lives, and as you live** - "As surely as Yahweh lives and as you live." Here Elisha compares the certainty that Yahweh and Elijah are alive to the certainty of what he is saying. This is a way of making a solemn promise. AT: "I solemnly promise you that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
