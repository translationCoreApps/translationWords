===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Anyone who acquits wicked people or condemns those who do right** - AT: "The one who acquits wicked people and the one who condemns those who do right"
  * **acquits** - justifies, declares someone not guilty
  * **Why should a fool pay money to learn about wisdom, when he has no ability to learn it?** - AT: "Money to buy wisdom is of no use to a fool since he has no sense." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
