===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Through all their suffering** - "Through all our suffering." Here "their" refers to the people of Israel. Isaiah included himself as a member of the people.  
  * **he suffered too** - Here "he" refers to Yahweh.
  * **the angel from his presence** - This is a representative who is sent from God's presence.
  * **he lifted them up and carried them** - This refers to when God protected and saved the people of Israel from the Egyptians many years earlier. The full meaning of this statement can be made clear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
