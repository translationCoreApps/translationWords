===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

  * **ark of the covenant decrees** - The ark is the chest that contains the commandments. This can be stated clearly in the translation. AT: "the chest containing the commandments." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:31|26:33]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **atonement lid** - This is the lid that sits on top of the ark where the atonement offering was made. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:15|25:17]].
  * **where I will meet with you** - Here "you" refers to Moses. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
