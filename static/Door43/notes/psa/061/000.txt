====== Psalm 061 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 61 is a psalm of praise. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Vow ==
The author has vowed to praise God every day of his life because God has blessed him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:vow]] and [[:en:obe:kt:bless]])
