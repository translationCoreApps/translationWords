===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Women are raped in Zion ... virgins in the cities of Judah** - AT: "All the women, young and old, are raped in Jerusalem and in the cities of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Princes are hung up by their own hands** - Possible meanings are 1) "With their own hands, they hung princes" or 2) they tied each prince's hands together with one end of a rope and tied the other end so the prince's feet could not touch the ground.
