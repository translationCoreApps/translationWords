===== translationNotes: =====

  * **disabled, or maimed** - These words refer to defects caused by accidents.
  * **warts, sores, or scabs** - These refer to types of skin diseases.
  * **will not be accepted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will not accept" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **deformed or small** - These words refer to defects the animal has from birth.
