  * **his sons gave him the blood** - It is implied that the sons caught the blood in a bowl as the blood drained from the animal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **inner parts** - **the inner parts** - This is the stomach and intestines. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:01:07|1:9]].
