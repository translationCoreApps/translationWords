===== translationNotes: =====

  * **by their armed groups** - "by their military divisions." This means that each tribe had their own men, who were armed, to protect them. See how you translated "armed groups" in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:01|1:3]]. 
  * **as commanded by Yahweh** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "as Yahweh commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **departure after departure** - "from one place to another place"
