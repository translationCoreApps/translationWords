===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue and end the introduction that began in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:22:17|22:17]].

  * **Have I not written for you ... you?** - This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "You need to know that I have written for you ... you." (See:  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **thirty sayings** - Some translations read, "excellent sayings." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_textvariants]])
  * **to those who sent you** - This implies that the hearer is or will be one whom others send to gain and bring back information.
