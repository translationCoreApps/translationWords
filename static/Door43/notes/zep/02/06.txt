===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the seacoast will become ... for sheep pens** - Part of this verse's meaning is unclear and is translated in different ways by modern versions.
  * **sheep pens** - A sheep pen is a small area surrounded by a fence to keep the sheep together. 
  * **lie down** - means to sleep in a bed.
