===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The twenty-four elders continue praising God.
  * **General Information:** - The words "you" and "your" refer to God.

  * **were enraged** - "were extremely angry"
  * **your wrath has come** - Existing in the present is spoken of as having come. AT: "You are ready to show your anger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])  
  * **The time has come** - Existing in the present is spoken of as having come. AT: "The time is right" or "Now is the time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]). 
  * **the prophets, those who are believers, and those who feared your name** - This list explains what "your servants" means. These were not three completely different groups of people. The prophets also were believers and feared God's name. AT: "the prophets and the others who are believers and fear your name"
