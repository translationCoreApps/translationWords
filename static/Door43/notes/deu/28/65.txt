===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **there will be no rest for the bottoms of your feet** - Here the phrase "the bottoms of your feet" refer to the whole person. AT: "you will have to continually wander because you have no permanent home where you can rest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Yahweh will give you there a trembling heart, failing eyes, and a soul that mourns** - This is an idiom. AT: "Yahweh will cause you to be afraid, without hope, and sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Your life will hang in doubt before you** - This is an idiom. AT: "You will not know whether you will live or die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
