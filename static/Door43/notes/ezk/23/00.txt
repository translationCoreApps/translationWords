====== Ezekiel 23 General Notes ======


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

Israel and Judah have been unfaithful to God by worshiping idols. They are compared to two unfaithful wives who have repeatedly committed adultery. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:unfaithful]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
