===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So they searched** - "So the king's servants searched"
  * **within all the borders of Israel** - This is a generalization. AT: "all over the land of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **Abishag** - This is a woman's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shunammite** - a person from the city of Shunem (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the king** - "King David"
