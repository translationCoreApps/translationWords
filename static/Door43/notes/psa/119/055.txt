===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I think about your name** - Here the word "name" represents Yahweh himself. AT: "I think about you, Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I keep your law** - This is an idiom which means to obey the law. AT: "I obey your law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my practice** - "my habit"
  * **I have observed your instructions** - This is an idiom which means to follow or to obey those instructions. AT: "I have obeyed your instructions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
