====== Psalm 118 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 118 is a psalm of war and is about Christ. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Rejected stone ==
The author praised Yahweh for his rescuing him. God enabled him to overpower his enemies. The new Testament, and even Jesus himself, saw the rejected stone as a reference to the messiah's rejection by the Jewish leaders. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])
