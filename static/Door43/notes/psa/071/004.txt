===== translationNotes: =====

  * **out of the hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous** - Possible meanings are the word "hand" is a metonym 1) for power, AT: "from the power of the wicked, from the power of the unrighteous" or 2) for the person himself, AT: "from wicked people, from unrighteous ... people" or "so that wicked people and unrighteous ... people cannot harm me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous** - "wicked; rescue me out of the hand of the unrighteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **wicked ... unrighteous ... cruel** - These adjectives can be translated as noun phrases. AT: "wicked people ... unrighteous people ... cruel people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **you are my hope** - Hope itself is a metonym for the one in whom the psalmist hopes. AT: "you are the one whom I confidently expect to help me" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
