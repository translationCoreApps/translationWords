===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The  young servants continue to speak to the king. 

  * **Let the king ... the king's official ... pleases the king** - The servants spoke to the king in third person as a sign of respect. AT: "You should ... your official ... pleases you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **provinces** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **harem** - "place where a ruler's wives are kept" (UDB)
  * **the fortress** - This refers to a castle, stronghold or fortified city. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:2]].
  * **Susa** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Let them be put under the care of Hegai, the king's official, who is in charge of the women** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Let Hegai, the king's official, who is in charge of the women, take care of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Hegai** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **their cosmetics** - A "cosmetic" is a substance such as a cream, lotion, or powder that women usually put on their face or body to improve their appearance.
