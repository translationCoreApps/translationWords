===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer continues to describe what God did for the people of Israel.

  * **tent of Joseph** - "descendants of Joseph" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Joseph ... Ephraim** - Ephraim was Joseph's son.
  * **Judah ... Mount Zion** - Mount Zion was in the land where the tribe of Judah lived.
  * **like the heavens** - "high, like the heavens" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like the earth** - "firm and strong, like the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
