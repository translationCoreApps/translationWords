===== translationNotes: =====

  * **After this** - "After he bought the field"
  * **the cave of the field** - "the cave in the field"
  * **the field of Machpelah** - "the field in Machpelah"
  * **that is, Hebron** - Possible meanings are 1) Mamre was another name for Hebron or 2) Hebron was formerly called Mamre or 3) Mamre was very near the larger city of Hebron, so people usually called it Hebron.
  * **passed to Abraham as a property for a burial place from the sons of Heth** - "became Abraham's property for a burial ground when he bought it from the sons of Heth"
  * **the sons of Heth** - Here "sons" stands for those who descended from Heth. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:23:03|23:3]]. AT: "the descendants of Heth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
