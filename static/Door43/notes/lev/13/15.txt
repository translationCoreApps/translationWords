===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The priest must ... pronounce him unclean because the raw flesh is unclean** - The man whom other people must not touch is spoken of as if he were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **pronounce him unclean** - Here "him" refers to the person with the skin disease.
  * **raw flesh** - Translate these words in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:13:09|13:10]].
  * **infectious disease** - Translate these words in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:13:03|13:3]].
  * **the priest will pronounce that person to be clean** - The man whom other people may touch is spoken of as if he were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
