===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why is it that we are hearing them, each in our own language in which we were born?** - Possible meanings are 1) this is a rhetorical question that expresses how amazed they were or 2) this is a real question for which the people wanted an answer. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **in our own language in which we were born** - "in our own languages that we have learned from birth"
  * **Parthians ... Arabians** - This lists some of the many nations, regions, and cities from which they came. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **proselytes** - "converts to the Jewish religion"
