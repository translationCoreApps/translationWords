===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the chief of the bakers** - This refers to the leading person who made food for the king. See how this was translated in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:40:01|40:2]].
  * **I also had a dream, and** - "I also had a dream, and in my dream,"
  * **behold, three baskets of bread were on my head** - "there were three baskets of bread on my head!" The baker uses word "behold" here to show that he was surprised by what he saw in his dream and to alert Joseph to pay attention.
  * **baked goods for Pharaoh** - "baked foods for Pharaoh"
