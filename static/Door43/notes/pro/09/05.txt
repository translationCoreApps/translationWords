===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my food ... I have mixed** - This is still wisdom speaking like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **the wine I have mixed** - In ancient Israel, people often mixed wine with water. "the drinks that I have prepared".
  * **your untaught ways** - "your naive ways" or "your young and naive way of living"
  * **the way of understanding** - "the way of living that is full of understanding" or "the manner of living that shows you have understanding"
