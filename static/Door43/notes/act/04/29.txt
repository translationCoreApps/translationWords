===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The believers complete their prayer that they began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:23|4:24]].

  * **look upon their warnings** - Here the words "look upon" are a request for God to take notice of the way in which the Jewish leaders threatened the believers. AT: "notice how they threaten to punish us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Stretch out your hand to heal** - Here the word "hand" refers to God's power. This is a request for God to show how powerful he is. AT: "while you show your power by healing people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **through the name of your holy servant Jesus** - Here the word "name" refers to power and authority. AT: "through the power of your holy servant Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your holy servant Jesus** - "Jesus who serves you faithfully." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:27|4:27]].
  * **the place ... was shaken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the place ... shook" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they were all filled with the Holy Spirit** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Holy Spirit filled them all." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:02:01|2:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
