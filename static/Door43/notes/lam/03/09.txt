===== translationNotes: =====

  * **blocked my path** - "prevented me from escaping from him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **hewn stone** - stones that someone has cut into regular shapes that fit together and make the wall strong
  * **stone; he made my paths crooked** - Possible meanings are 1) "stone, and so I cannot go anywhere" or 2) "stone; every road I take leads nowhere"
  * **he turned aside my paths** - Possible meanings are 1) "he dragged me off the path" or 2) "he caused my path to turn in the wrong direction"
