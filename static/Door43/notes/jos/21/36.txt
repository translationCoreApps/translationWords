===== translationNotes: =====

  * **To the clans of Merari were given from the tribe of Reuben: Bezer** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The clans of Merari received from the tribe of Reuben Bezer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **four cities** - This refers to the total number of cities. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Bezer ... Jahaz ... Kedemoth ... Mephaath ... Ramoth** - names of cities (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Out of the tribe of Gad they were given Ramoth** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They received from the tribe of Gad Ramoth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Mahanaim** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
