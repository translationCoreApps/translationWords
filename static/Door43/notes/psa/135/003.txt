===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for it is pleasant to do so** - "because we get pleasure from praising his name"
  * **Yahweh has chosen Jacob** - "Jacob" here refers to his descendants, the people of Israel. AT: "Yahweh has chosen Jacob's descendants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Israel as his possession** - The beginning of the sentence can be stated clearly. AT: "he has chosen Israel to be his possession" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
