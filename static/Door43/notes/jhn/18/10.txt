===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Malchus** - Malchus is a male servant of the high priest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **sheath** - the cover for a sharp knife or sword, so the knife will not cut the owner
  * **Should I not drink the cup that the Father has given me?** - This remark appears in the form of a question to add emphasis to Jesus' statement. AT: "I must surely drink the cup that the Father has given to me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the cup** - Here "cup" is a metaphor that refers to the suffering that Jesus must endure. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
