===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the names of the people who signed the sealed document. 

  * **Bunni** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:09:03|9:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Azgad, Bebai** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:15|7:16-17]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Adonijah ... Azzur ... Nebai, Magpiash ... Hezir ... Jaddua** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Bigvai** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:06|7:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Adin, Ater, Hezekiah ... Hashum** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:19|7:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Hodiah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:08:06|8:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Bezai, Hariph** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:23|7:23]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anathoth** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:27|7:27]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Meshullam ... Meshezabel, Zadok** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:03|3:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
