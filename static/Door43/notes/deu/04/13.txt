===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel.

  * **He declared** - "Yahweh declared"
  * **to you** - Here "you" refers to the Israelites who were at Mount Horeb.
  * **in the land that you are crossing over to take possession of** - "after you have crossed over the Jordan River and taken possession of the land"
