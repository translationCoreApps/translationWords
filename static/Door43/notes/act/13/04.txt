===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the words "they," "They," and "their" refer to Barnabas and Silas.  

  * **So** - This word marks an event that happened because of a previous event. In this case, the previous event is Barnabas and Saul being set apart by the Holy Spirit.
  * **went down** - The phrase "went down" is used here because Seleucia is lower in elevation than Antioch.
  * **Seleucia** - a city by the sea
  * **city of Salamis** - The city of Salamis was on Cyprus Island.
  * **synagogues of the Jews** - Possible meanings are that 1) "there were multiple Jewish synagogues in the city of Salamis where Barnabas and Saul preached" or 2) "Barnabas and Saul started at the synagogue at Salamis and continued to preach in all the synagogues they found while they traveled around the Island of Cyprus."
  * **They also had John Mark as their assistant** - "John Mark went with them and was helping them" (UDB)
  * **assistant** - "helper"
