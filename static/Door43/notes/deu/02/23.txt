===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer finishes describing how God enabled Esau to conquer the land Esau is now inhabiting.

  * **Avvites ... Caphtorim** - These are names of people groups. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Caphtor** - This is the name of a place. It may be another name for the island of Crete located in the Mediterranean Sea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **destroyed them** - "destroyed the Avvim"
  * **lived in their place** - "lived where the Avvim had lived"
