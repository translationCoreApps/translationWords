===== translationNotes: =====


  * **A false witness ... breathes out lies** - See how you translated this whole phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:19:05|19:5]].
  * **luxury** - condition of wealth and pleasure
  * **much less for a slave** - "it is much less fitting for a slave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]]) 
  * **rule** - This means to "lead" or to "have authority over."
