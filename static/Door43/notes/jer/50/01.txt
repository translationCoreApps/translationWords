===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **by the hand of Jeremiah** - "through the action of Jeremiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **cause them to listen ... cause them to listen** - The repetition of this phrase emphasizes the importance of the command. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Bel is made ashamed. Merodach is dismayed. Its idols are put to shame; its images are dismayed.** - These lines mean the same thing and emphasize that Yahweh has disgraced the gods of Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Bel ... Merodach** - These are the two names for the chief god of Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
