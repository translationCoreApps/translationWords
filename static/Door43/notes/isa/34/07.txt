===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Wild oxen will be slaughtered along with them, and young bulls with the older ones** - This can be stated in active form. Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh will slaughter all the oxen and bulls along with the people or 2) this refers to the leaders of Edom that Yahweh will slaughter along with the common people like oxen and bulls. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Wild oxen will be slaughtered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I, Yahweh, will slaughter the wild oxen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
