===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the words "they" and "them" refer to the people in the house. The words "He" and "he" refer to Peter.
  * **General Information:** - Though Herod had killed James in [[:en:bible:notes:act:12:01|12:2]], there was more than one James.

  * **But Peter continued knocking** - The word "continued" means that Peter kept knocking the entire time those inside were talking.
  * **Report these things** - "Tell these things"
  * **the brothers** - "the other believers"
