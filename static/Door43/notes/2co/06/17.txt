===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul quotes portions from the Old Testament prophets, Isaiah and Ezekiel. 

  * **be set apart** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "set yourselves apart" or "allow me to set you apart" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Touch no unclean thing** - This can be stated in positive terms. AT: "Touch only things that are clean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
