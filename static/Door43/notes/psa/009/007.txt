===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh remains forever** - "Remain" probably represents sitting on the throne as king. AT: "Yahweh sits on his throne forever" or "Yahweh rules forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he has established his throne for justice** - The phrase "his throne" represents God's rule. Possible meanings are 1) "He rules in order to judge people" or "He rules over people justly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He will judge the world with righteousness ... he will execute judgment for the nations with fairness** - These two clauses express the same meaning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **He will judge the world with righteousness** - Here "the world" refers to all the people in the world. AT: "He will judge all the people of the world righteously" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
