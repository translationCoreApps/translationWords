===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the third hour** - "Third" is a ordinal number. This refers to nine o'clock in the morning. AT: "nine o'clock in the morning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **On a sign** - The soldiers attached this sign to the cross above Jesus. AT: "They attached to the cross above Jesus' head a sign on which" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **charge** - "accusation"
  * **robbers** - "armed thieves" 
  * **one on the right of him and one on his left** - This can be written more clearly. AT: "one on a cross on the right side of him and one on a cross on the left side of him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
