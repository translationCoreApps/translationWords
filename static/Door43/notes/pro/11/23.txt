===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one who sows seed** - "Sowing seed" represents preparing for the future. AT: "a person who prepares for the future" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **will accumulate even more** - "will gain even more"
  * **does not sow** - "Sow" represents preparing for a result. AT: "does not prepare" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
