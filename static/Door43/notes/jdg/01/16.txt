===== translationNotes: =====

  * **of Moses' father-in-law** - "of the father of Moses' wife"
  * **father-in-law the Kenite went up** - "father-in-law, who was one of the Ken people, went up"
  * **went up from the City of Palms ... into the wilderness** - "left the City of Palms ... and went into the wilderness"
  * **City of Palms** - This is another name for the city of Jericho. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Arad** - This is the name of a city in Canaan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the men of Simeon their brothers** - Here "brothers" means relatives who were in another tribe of Israel. 
  * **Zephath** - This is the name of a city in Canaan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The name of the city was called Hormah** - After the Israelites destroyed Zephath, they changed its name to "Hormah." The name "Hormah" means "complete destruction." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
