===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Which animal among all these does not know ... this?** - This question emphasizes the point that all the animals know that Yahweh has done this. It means, "Every animal knows ... this." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **the hand of Yahweh has done this** - Yahweh's hand represents his power. AT: "Yahweh has done this by his power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **In his hand is the life ... and the breath of all mankind** - Yahweh's hand represents his control or power. Things being in his hand represents his ability to control them. AT: "who controls the life of every living thing and gives breath to all mankind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **the breath of all mankind** - "Breath" represents life or ability to live. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
