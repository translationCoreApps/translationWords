===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **that are written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that I have written" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **this glorious and fearful name, Yahweh your God** - Here "name"refers to Yahweh himself. AT: "Yahweh your God who is glorious and awesome" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh will make your plagues terrible, and those of your descendants** - "Yahweh will send terrible plagues on you and your descendants" or "Yahweh will make sure that you and your descendants suffer from terrible plagues"
