===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses are exactly the same as [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:6-7]] and can probably be translated in the same way.

  * **Then it was the day** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:6]].
  * **the day when** - This is not a specific day. The gathering happened often. AT: "at the time when" or "one day when"
  * **sons of God** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:6]]. 
  * **present themselves before Yahweh** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:6]].
  * **From wandering on the earth, from going  back and forth on it** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:06|1:7]].
