===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the world** - In this passage, "the world" is a metonym for the people who oppose God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **keep them from the evil one** - This refers to Satan. AT: "protect them from Satan, the evil one" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Set them apart by the truth** - The purpose for setting them apart can be stated clearly. The phrase "by the truth" here represents by teaching the truth. AT: "Make them your own people by teaching them the truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Your word is truth** - "Your message is true" or "What you say is true"
