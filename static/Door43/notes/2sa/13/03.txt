===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jehonadab ... Shammah** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Why, son of the king, are you depressed every morning?** - This rhetorical question is used to emphasize how unhappy Amnon was. It can be translated as a statement. AT: "Because you are the king's son, you should be happy, not depressed every morning." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **depressed** - in an emotional state of extreme unhappiness
  * **Will you not tell me?** - Here the sentence is shortened and means "Will you not tell me why you are unhappy?" This rhetorical question can also be translated as a statement. AT: "Please tell me why you are unhappy." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
