==== translationNotes: =====

  * **Laish** - Translate translate the name of this town the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:18:07| 18:7]].
  * **Do you know that in these houses there are an ephod, ... and a cast metal figure?** - The five men asked this question to suggest and encourage the men that they should steal the idols. This question can be written as statement and include the implied information. AT: "These houses contain an ephod, household gods, a carved idol, and a cast idol. We should take them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
