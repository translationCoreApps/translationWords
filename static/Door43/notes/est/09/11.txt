===== translationNotes: =====

  * **five hundred men** - "500 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **ten sons** - "10 sons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **What then have they done in the rest of the king's provinces?** - The king uses this question to show that he believes that the Jews must have also killed many people in the other provinces. AT: "What they must have done in the rest of the king's provinces!" or "They must have killed many more in the rest of the king's provinces!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **what is your petition?** - The abstract noun "petition" can be expressed with the verb "ask for" or "want." AT: "what do you ask for?" or "what do you want?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **It will be granted you** - This can be expressed in active form. AT: "I will give you what you ask for" or "I will do for you what you ask" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **What is your request?** - The abstract noun "request" can be expressed with the verb "ask for" or "want." AT: "What do you ask for?" or "What do you want?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
