===== translationNotes: =====

  * **signs and wonders** - These two words mean basically the same thing and refer to the miraculous troubles that God had caused in Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **into your midst, Egypt** - The psalmist speaks as if the people of Egypt were listening to him. AT: "among you people of Egypt" or "among the people of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **against Pharaoh** - "to punish Pharaoh"
