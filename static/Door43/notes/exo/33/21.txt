===== translationNotes: =====

  * **See** - "Look" or "Listen" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you."
  * **you will see my back** - This is because Yahweh will be walking away from Moses.
  * **but my face will not be seen** - This can be expressed in active form. AT: "but you will not see my face" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
