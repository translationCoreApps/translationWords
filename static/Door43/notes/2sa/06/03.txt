===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David and the army of the nation of Israel move the ark of the covenant.

  * **Abinadab ... Uzzah ... Ahio** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **house of Israel** - This metonym stands for the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **tambourine** - a musical instrument like the head of a drum with pieces of metal around the side that sound when the instrument is shaken or hit (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **rattles** - musical instruments with many small, hard objects inside a hard shell, making rhythmic noise when shaken (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **cymbals** - two thin, round metal plates that are hit together to make a loud sound (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
