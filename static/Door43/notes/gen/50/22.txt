===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one hundred ten years** - "110 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Ephraim's children to the third generation** - "Ephraim's children and grandchildren" (UDB)
  * **Machir** - This is the name of Joseph's grandson. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who were placed on the knees of Joseph** - This expression means that Joseph adopted these children of Machir as his own children. This means they would have special inheritance rights from Joseph. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
