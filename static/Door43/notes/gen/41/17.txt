===== translationNotes: =====

  * **behold, I stood** - Pharaoh uses the word "behold" to make Joseph pay attention to surprising information. 
  * **bank of the Nile** - This is the higher ground along the edge of the Nile River. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:41:01|41:3]]. AT: "beside the Nile"
  * **Behold, seven cows** - Pharaoh uses the word "Behold" to make Joseph pay attention to surprising information. 
  * **fat and desirable** - "well-fed and healthy." See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:41:01|41:2]].
  * **grazed among the reeds** - "were eating the grass along the side of the river." See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:41:01|41:2]].
