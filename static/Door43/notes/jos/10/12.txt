===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Sun, be still at Gibeon, and moon, in the Valley of Aijalon** - Joshua is praying that Yahweh would make the progression of time stop on this day. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Sun ... moon** - Joshua commands the sun and moon as if these were people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Valley of Aijalon** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
