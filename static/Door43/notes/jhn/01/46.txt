===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nathaniel said to him** - "Nathaniel said to Philip"
  * **Can any good thing come out of Nazareth?** - This remark appears in the form of a question in order to add emphasis. AT: "No good thing can come out of Nazareth!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **in whom is no deceit** - This can be stated in a positive way. AT: "a completely truthful man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes|Litotes]])
