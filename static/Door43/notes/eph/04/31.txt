===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul finishes his instructions on what believers should not do and ends with what they must do.

  * **You must put away** - Possible meanings are 1) you must do it yourself or 2) "You must allow God to take away."
  * **put away all bitterness** - "stop being angry about the bad things others have done to you"
  * **rage** - times of intense anger
  * **anger** - the more general term for anger
  * **Be kind** - "Instead, you must be kind"
  * **tenderhearted** - "gentle" or "full of compassion"
