===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nahaliel ... Bamoth** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **Mount Pisgah** - This is the name of a mountain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **looks down on the wilderness** - This is an idiom. It is a way of saying that the mountain is high, and speaks of the mountain as if it were a person who looks down to see the wilderness below him. AT: "rises above the wilderness" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
