===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continue to tell Moses how the tabernacle was to be built.

  * **crossbars** - These are horizontal support beams that give stability to the structure.
  * **the back side of the tabernacle to the west** - The front of the tabernacle faced to the east.
