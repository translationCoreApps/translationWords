===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking using parallelisms for emphasis.

  * **I may be refreshed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I may feel better" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **open my lips** - Here the "lips" represent the mouth. AT: "open my mouth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **neither will I give honorific titles to any man** - "neither will I praise any man or give him titles of honor"
  * **my Maker** - "God who made me." This is a name referring to God.
  * **take me away** - This means that he would destroy him. AT: "destroy me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
