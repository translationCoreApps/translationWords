===== translationNotes: =====

  * **angry and very furious** - These words mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of his anger. AT: "incredibly angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **all those in Babylon** - "all the men in Babylon"
  * **So the decree went out** - The decree is spoken of as if it was alive and able to go out by itself. AT: "So the king issued a command" or "So the king gave a command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **all those who were known for their wisdom were to be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the soldiers were to kill all of the men who were known for their wisdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so they could be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "in order to kill them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
