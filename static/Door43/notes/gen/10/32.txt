===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the clans** - This refers back to all the people listed in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:10:01|10:1-31]].
  * **according to** - "listed by"
  * **From these the nations separated and went over the earth** - "From these clans the nations divided and spread over the earth" or "These clans divided from each other and formed the nations of the earth"
  * **after the flood** - This can be stated clearly or more explicitly. AT: "after the flood destroyed the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
