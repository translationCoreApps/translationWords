===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul asks the believers to pray for him and his companions.

  * **And now** - Paul uses the word "now" to mark a change in topic.
  * **brothers** - Here "brothers" means fellow Christians, including both men and women. AT: "brothers and sisters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **the word of the Lord may rush and be glorified** - Paul speaks of God's word spreading as if it were running from place to place. AT: "that more and more people will soon hear our message about our Lord Jesus and honor it" (UDB)  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that we may be delivered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that God may save us" or "that God may rescue us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **for not all have faith** - "for many people do not believe in Jesus"
  * **who will establish you** - "who will strengthen you"
  * **the evil one** - "Satan"
