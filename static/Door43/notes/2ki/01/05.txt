===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When the messengers returned to Ahaziah** - AT "So Elijah went to meet the messengers and told that to them, and they returned to the king instead of going to Ekron. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])"
  * **Is it because there is no God in Israel that you sent men to consult with Baal Zebub, the god of Ekron?** - AT: "You fools! You know there is a God in Israel, but you were acting as though you did not know when you sent men to consult Baal Zebub, the god of Ekron!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
