===== translationNotes: =====
  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **beginning from the last to the first** - You can make clear the understood information. AT: "begin with the workers I hired last, then pay the workers I hired first" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **who had been hired** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom the landowner hired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
