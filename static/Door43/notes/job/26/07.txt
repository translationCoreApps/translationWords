===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He stretches out the northern skies over the empty space** - The northern skies represent heaven, the place where God dwells with the beings he created to dwell there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He binds up the waters in his thick clouds** - The clouds are spoken of as if they were a large blanket in which God wraps the rainwater. AT: "He wraps up the water in his thick clouds. 
  * **but the clouds are not torn under them** - This can be stated actively. The word "them" refers to the waters. AT: "but the weight of the waters does not tear the clouds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
