====== 1 Samuel 05 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of the Ark of the Covenant in Philistia continues

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The Ark in the Philistine Cities ==

The Philistines took the Ark of God to Ashdod and put it in their idol temple but their idle fell down in front of it and the people got sick with bubonic plague. So they took it to Gath and the people of Gath began dying of the plague. So they took it to Ekron, but the people of Ekron were afraid of it too and did not want it there. So they decided to send it back to Israel. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

There are two slightly different idioms used in the same way: the  "hand was heavy" and the "hand was hard," both mean "was severely punished." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
