===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - When Jesus and his disciples get out of their boat at Bethsaida, Jesus heals a blind man.

  * **Bethsaida** - This is a town on the northern shores of the Sea of Galilee. See how you translated the name of this town in [[:en:bible:notes:mrk:06:45|6:45]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **to touch him** - It may be helpful to state why they wanted Jesus to touch the man. AT: "to touch him in order to heal him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he had spat on his eyes ... he asked him** - "Jesus had spat on the man's eyes ... Jesus asked the man"
