===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **unworked stones** - stones in their natural shape that no one has shaped with metal tools
  * **write on the stones** - the stones they were to set up on Mount Ebal and cover with plaster ([[:en:bible:notes:deu:27:01|27:2]], [[:en:bible:notes:deu:27:04|4]])
