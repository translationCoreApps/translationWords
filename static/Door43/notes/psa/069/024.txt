===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pour out your indignation on them** - God's demonstration of his anger is describes as if he poured it out on his enemies like water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **indignation** - "rage" or "anger"
  * **the fierceness of your anger** - "your burning anger" or "your strong anger"
  * **overtake them** - God's judgment on his enemies is described as if he ran after them and caught them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **be a desolation** - This abstract noun can be stated as an action. AT: "become abandoned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
