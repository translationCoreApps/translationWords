===== translationNotes: =====

  * **house of Onesiphorus** - Here "house" stands for the people who live there. AT: "family of Onesiphorus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Onesiphorus** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:2ti:01:15|1:16]].
  * **Erastus ... Trophimus ... Eubulus ... Pudens, Linus** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Miletus** - This is the name of a city to the south of Ephesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Do your best to come** - "Make a way to come"
  * **before winter** - "before the cold season"
  * **also Pudens, Linus, Claudia, and all the brothers** - This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "Pudens, Linus, Claudia, and all the brothers also greet you"
  * **Claudia** - This is a female name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **all the brothers** - Here "brothers" means all believers whether male or female. AT: "all the believers here" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **May the Lord be with your spirit** - "I pray that the Lord makes your spirit strong." Here "you" is singular and refers to Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **May grace be with you** - "I pray that the Lord shows his grace to all of you there." Here "you" is plural and refers to all the believers there with Timothy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **grace** - "his kindness" or "his favor"
