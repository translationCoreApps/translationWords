===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This marks a new section of the letter. Here the author praises God and gives a final prayer for his readers.
  * **the great shepherd of the sheep** - Christ in his role of leader and protector of those who believe in him is spoken of as if he were a shepherd of sheep. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **by the blood of an eternal covenant** - Here "blood" stands for the death of Jesus, which is the basis for the covenant that will last forever between God and all believers in Christ. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **perfect you with every good thing to do his will** - Here "perfect you" means "make you capable" or "equip you." AT: "make you capable of doing every good thing according to his will"
  * **May he work in us** - The word "us" refers to the author and the readers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **to whom be the glory forever** - "whom all people will praise forever"
