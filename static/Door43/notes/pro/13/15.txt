===== translationNotes: =====


  * **but the way of the treacherous is never-ending** - Here a person's behavior or conduct is spoken of as if it were a way or path that a person walks. A person being ruined by their own treachery is spoken of as if they are on a way or path that never ends. AT: "but the behavior of the treacherous will cause their own destruction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the treacherous** - This nominal adjective can be stated as an adjective. AT: "the treacherous person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **a fool parades his folly** - To "parade" means to display in front of everyone. AT: "a fool displays his foolishness to everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
