===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **Yahweh our God is one** - "Our God Yahweh is the one and only God"
  * **with all your heart, with all your soul, and with all your might** - These phrases combined emphasize that a person is supposed to love God completely and to be totally dedicated to him.
