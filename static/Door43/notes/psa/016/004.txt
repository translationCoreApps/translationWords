===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Their troubles will be ... gods** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The troubles of those who seek other gods will increase" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **pour out ... blood to their gods** - "make sacrifices to their gods" (UDB)
  * **lift up their names with my lips** - Lifting up "their names with my lips" represents praising the other gods or praying to them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
