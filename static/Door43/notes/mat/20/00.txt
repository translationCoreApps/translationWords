====== Matthew 20 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The parable of the landowner and his vineyard ==

Jesus tells this parable ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:20:01|20:1-16]]) to teach his disciples that God's standards about what is right are different from human standards.
