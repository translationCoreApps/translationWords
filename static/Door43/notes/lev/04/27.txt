===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh has commanded him not to be done** - All of the people of Israel were commanded not to sin. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh commanded the people not to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **his sin which he has committed is made known to him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he becomes aware of the sin he committed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
