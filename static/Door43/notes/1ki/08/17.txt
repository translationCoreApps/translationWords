===== translationNotes: =====

  * **it was in the heart of David my father ... it was in your heart ... for it to be in your heart** - What David desired is spoken of as if it were an item in a container and the heart as if it were a container. AT: "David my father desired ... you desired ... to desire to do that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for the name of Yahweh ... for my name** - The word "name" is a metonym for the person, and "for ... name" refers to worshiping the person. Similar words appear in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:03:01|3:2]]. AT: "in which people would worship Yahweh ... in which people will worship me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **In that it was in your heart** - "Because you desired"
  * **for it to be in your heart** - "by wanting to do that"
  * **one who will be born from your loins** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "one who will be your own offspring" or "one whom you yourself will father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
