===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God gave him another heart** - God enabled Samuel to think differently from the way he had thought before. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the Spirit of God rushed upon him** - Samuel speaks as if the Spirit of Yahweh were a person running up to Saul and taking complete control of him.
