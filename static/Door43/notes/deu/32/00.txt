====== Deuteronomy 32 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 31:1-43, which is a song.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Punishment ==
This chapter prophesies a time when Israel will be disobedient to Yahweh and he will be forced to punish them. This is meant to sere as a warning to the nation. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]])
