===== translationNotes: =====

  * **pleaded** - "begged"
  * **to put an end to the evil plan of Haman the Agagite** - "Put an end to" is an idiom meaning to stop something. AT: "to stop the evil plan of Haman the Agagite" or "to prevent the evil things from happening that Haman the Agagite had planned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the Agagite** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:est:03:01|3:1]]
  * **scheme that he had devised** - "the plot that he had invented" or "the plot that Haman invented"
  * **the king held out the golden scepter to Esther** - He did this to show that he was pleased with her. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:est:05:01|5:2]]
