===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **Again he went out** - "Again the landowner went out"
  * **the sixth hour and again the ninth hour** - The sixth hour is around noon. The ninth hour is around three in the afternoon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **did the same** - This means the landowner went to the marketplace and hired workers.
  * **the eleventh hour** - This is about five in the afternoon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **standing idle** - "not doing anything" or "not having any work"
