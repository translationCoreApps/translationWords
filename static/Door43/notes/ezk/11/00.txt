====== Ezekiel 11 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Safety ==

The people were trusting Jerusalem to keep them safe. But God was going to pull them out of Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]])


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

The people in Jerusalem used the metaphor "this city is the pot and we are the meat in the pot" meaning they were safe in Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
