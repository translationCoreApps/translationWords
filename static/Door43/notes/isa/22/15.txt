===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Shebna** - This is the name of the manager of the king's palace in Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who is over the house** - Here "house" represents those in the king's palace. AT: "who is in charge of all those who work in the palace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **What are you doing here and who gave you permission ... in the rock?** - Yahweh uses this question to scold Shebna. AT: "You have no right ... in the rock!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **cut out a tomb ... hewing out a grave ... carving out a resting place** - These three phrases all refer to making a burial tomb. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **on the heights** - The most important people in Israel had tombs in the highest places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
