===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must not mistreat any widow or fatherless child** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "You must treat all widows and fatherless children fairly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **widow** - "woman whose husband has died"
  * **fatherless child** - "orphan" or "child with no parents"
  * **I will kill you with the sword** - To be killed "with the sword" is a metonym that means a person will die violently, or perhaps fighting in a war. AT: "you will die a violent death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
