===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will walk securely** - To walk securely is to live one's life securely. AT: "I will live securely in God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **I seek your instructions** - To "seek" is to perceive, or gain understanding of God's instructions as if one can see them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **solemn commands before kings** - "serious commands to kings" or "instructions to kings"
