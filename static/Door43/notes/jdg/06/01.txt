===== translationNotes: =====

  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - Here "in the sight of Yahweh" represents what Yahweh considers or thinks about something. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:02:11|2:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the hand of Midian** - Here "Midian" represents the people of Midian. Also, "hand" represents control. AT: "the control of the people of Midian" or "the control of the Midianites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The power of Midian oppressed Israel** - Here "the power of Midian" refers to the people of Midian. AT: "The people of Midian were more powerful than the people of Israel and they oppressed them"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **dens** - places in the rocky cliffs that would provide shelter
