===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who is motivated by a willing heart** - This is an idiom that indicates a person's desire to give an offering. AT: "who wants to give an offering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **You must receive** - The word "you" refers to Moses and the leaders. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
