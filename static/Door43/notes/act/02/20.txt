===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter finishes quoting the prophet Joel.

  * **The sun will be turned to darkness** - This means that the sun will appear to be dark instead of light. AT: "The sun will become dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and the moon to blood** - This means that the moon will appear to be red like blood. AT: "and the moon will appear to be red" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **the great and remarkable day** - The words "great" and "remarkable" share similar meanings and emphasize the intensity of greatness. AT: "the very great day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **remarkable** - "unusual" or "important"
  * **everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the Lord will save everyone who calls on him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
