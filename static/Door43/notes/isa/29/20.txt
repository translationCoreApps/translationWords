===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For the ruthless will cease** - "For there will no longer be cruel people"
  * **and the scoffer will vanish** - "and the people who mock will disappear"
  * **All those who love to do evil will be eliminated** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will remove all those who love to do evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **who by a word make a man out to be an offender** - "who say that an innocent man is guilty of doing something wrong"
  * **They lay a snare for him who seeks justice at the gate and put the righteous down with empty lies** - This symbolizes trying to trap people as one would trap animals. AT: "They lie and try to stop those who want to do what is fair and right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
