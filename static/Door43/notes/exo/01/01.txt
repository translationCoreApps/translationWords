===== translationNotes: =====


  * **household** - This refers to all the people who live in a house together, usually a large family with servants. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who came out of the thigh of Jacob** - who were descendants of Jacob (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seventy** - "70" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Joseph was already in Egypt** - "Joseph lived in Egypt before his brothers"
