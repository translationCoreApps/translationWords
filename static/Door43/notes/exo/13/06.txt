  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel.

  * **No yeast may be seen with you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You may not have any yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **within any of your borders** - "inside any of the borders of your land"
