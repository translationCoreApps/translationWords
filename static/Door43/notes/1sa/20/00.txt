====== 1 Samuel 20 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jonathan warns David ==
David tells Jonathan that Saul is still trying to kill him. Jonathan promises to find out if this is true. When David misses dinner two consecutive days and Saul asks about him. When Jonathan said he went home for an annual sacrifice, Saul got angry at Jonathan and told him he would never be king unless they killed David and he threw his spear at Jonathan. Jonathan warned David to run away but first they both promised to always be friends. It was usually a king son who became king after his death in the ancient Near East. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]).


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==
David uses a powerful metaphor and an oath to impress Jonathan with what danger he was in: "But as truly as Yahweh lives, and as you live, there is but a step between me and death." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
