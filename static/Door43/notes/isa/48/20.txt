===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **his servant Jacob** - This refers to the descendants of Jacob. AT: "the people of Israel, his servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to the ends of the earth** - This means to the farthest places. AT: "to the farthest parts of the whole world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
