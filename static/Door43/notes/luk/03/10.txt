===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John begins to respond to questions that people in the crowd ask him.

  * **asking him, saying** - "asking him and said" or "asking John"
  * **answered and said to them** - "answered them, saying" or "answered them" or "said"
  * **do the same** - "share extra food just as you shared the extra tunic." This refers back to giving food to those in need. AT: "give food to someone who does not have any" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
