===== translationNotes: =====


  * **While the servants were waiting ... Ehud escaped** - This tells what happened before the servants opened the doors to the upper room and found the king dead. AT: "Meanwhile, as the servants were still waiting outside of the upper room ... Ehud escaped" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_events]])
  * **Seirah** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **When he arrived** - This could be made clearer. AT: "When he arrived in Seirah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
