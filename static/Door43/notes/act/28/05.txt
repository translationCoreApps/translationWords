===== translationNotes: =====

  * **shook the animal into the fire** - "shook his hand so that the snake fell from his hand into the fire"
  * **suffered no harm** - "Paul was not hurt at all"
  * **become inflamed with a fever** - Possible meanings are 1) his body would swell because of the snake venom or 2) he would become very hot with fever.
  * **nothing was unusual with him** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "everything about him was as it should be" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **they changed their minds** - To think differently about a situation is spoken of as if a person is changing his mind. AT: "they thought again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and said that he was a god** - This can be stated as a direct quotation. AT: "and said, 'This man must be a god'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **said that he was a god** - Perhaps there was a belief that someone who lived after a poisonous snake bite was divine or a god.
