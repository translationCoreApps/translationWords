===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Those who are coming after us** - The Babylonian army.
  * **given ourselves to** - AT: "made a treaty with" or "surrendered to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **to get enough food** - AT: "so that we would have food to eat"
  * **they are no more** - AT: "they have died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **we bear their iniquities** - AT: "we bear the punishment for their sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
