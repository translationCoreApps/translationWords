===== translationNotes: =====


  * **his daughter-in-law** - "his oldest son's wife"
  * **in your father's house** - This means her to live in here father's house. AT: "and live in your father's house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **until Shelah, my son, grows up** - Judah intends for Tamar to marry Shelah when he grows up. AT: "and when Shelah, my son, grows up, he can marry you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Shelah** - This is the name of one of Judah's sons. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:38:03|38:5]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **For he feared, "He might also die, just like his brothers** - Judah feared that if Shelah married Tamar he would also die like his brothers did. AT: "For he feared, "If he marries her he may also die like his brothers did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
