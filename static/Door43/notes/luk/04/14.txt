===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus returns to Galilee, teaches in the synagogue, and tells the people there that he is fulfilling scripture of Isaiah the prophet.

  * **Then Jesus returned** - This begins a new event in the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **in the power of the Spirit** - "and the Spirit was giving him power." God was with Jesus in a special way, enabling him to do things that humans usually could not.
  * **news about him spread** - Those who heard Jesus told other people about him, and then those other people told even more people about him. This can be stated in active form. AT: "people spread the news about Jesus" or "people told other people about Jesus" or "knowledge about him was passed on from person to person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **throughout the entire surrounding region** - This refers to the areas or places around Galilee.
  * **was praised by all** - "everyone said great things about him" or "all the people spoke about him in a good way"
