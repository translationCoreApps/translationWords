===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king** - David is speaking about himself. This can be stated clearly in the translation. AT: "I, the king of Israel" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **swears by him ... proud of him** - The word "him" refers to "God." 
  * **but the mouth of those who speak lies will be stopped up** - Here "the mouth" represents the whole person. This can be stated in active form. AT: "but God will silence the liars" or "but God will silence those who lie" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
