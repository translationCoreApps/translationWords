===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem as if it were a branch that he plants on the mountains of Israel and of other nations as if they were other trees. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **Then all the trees of the field will know that I am Yahweh** - Yahweh speaks of trees as if they are people who can know about him. The trees represent the nations and the people who live in them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I bring down the high trees and I raise up the low trees** - "I cut down the tall trees and I cause the little trees to grow"
  * **wither** - when a plant dries up and dies.
