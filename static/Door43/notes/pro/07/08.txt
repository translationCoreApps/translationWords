===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **her corner** - Here "her" refers to any female stranger, as referred to in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:07:04|07:05]]. She was standing at a certain corner, waiting for a suitable man to pass by. AT: "the corner where a female stranger was standing"
  * **corner** - This refers to where two roads meet. 
  * **twilight** - the time of day when it is getting darker and about to become night
