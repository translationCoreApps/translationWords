===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his word** - Here "word" is a metonym that stands for the message that Jesus proclaimed. AT: "his message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2;translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **world** - The "world" is a metonym that refers to all the people who live in the world. AT: "all the people of the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
