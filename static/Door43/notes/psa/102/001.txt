===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **the afflicted** - This refers to a man who is afflicted. AT: "the afflicted man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **overwhelmed** - "faint" or "weak"
  * **pours out his lament before Yahweh** - The way the author expresses his lament is compared to pouring out a liquid. AT: "freely expresses his grief to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
