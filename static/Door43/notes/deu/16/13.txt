===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Festival of Shelters** - Other names for this festival are "Feast of Tabernacles," "Festival of Booths," and "Feast of the Ingathering." During harvest time, farmers would set up temporary shelters in the field. This festival took place after the last harvest of the year. 
  * **seven days** - "7 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **within your gates** - Here the word "gates" represents cities or towns. AT: "within your towns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
