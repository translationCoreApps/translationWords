===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer speaks as a father teaching his child using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **do not forget my commands** - The word "commands" can be translated as a verb. AT: "do not forget what I command you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **keep my teachings in your heart** - This phrase says in positive terms what the previous phrase says in negative terms. Here the word "heart" represents the mind. The word "teachings" can be translated as a verb. AT: "always remember what I teach you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **length of days and years of life** - These two phrases share similar meanings and refer to living a long life. AT: "a long life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
