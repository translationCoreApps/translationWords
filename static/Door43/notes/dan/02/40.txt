===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Daniel continues talking to the king.

  * **There will be a fourth kingdom** - "There will be a kingdom number four" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **strong as iron** - The fourth kingdom is spoken of as being as strong as iron. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **It will shatter all these things and crush them** - This symbolic language means the fourth kingdom will defeat and replace the other kingdoms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **all these things** - "the previous kingdoms" (UDB)
