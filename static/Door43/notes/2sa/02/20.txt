===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Asahel** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Turn aside to your right or to your left ... turn aside** - "Stop chasing me ... stop chasing Abner"
  * **seize one of the young men and take his armor** - Abner was asking Asahel to kill and plunder another younger soldier who would not be as dangerous as Abner. He did not want to kill Asahel. AT: "fight with another soldier and take his equipment as plunder" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
