===== translationNotes: =====

  * **are they not written in the book of the events of the kings of Israel?** - This question is used to remind the reader that the acts of Jehoahaz are recorded in another book. See how this phrase is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:01:17|1:18]]. AT: "they are written in the book of the events of the kings of Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **slept with his ancestors** - This is a polite way of saying that he died. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
