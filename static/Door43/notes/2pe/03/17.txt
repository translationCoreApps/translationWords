===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter finishes instructing the believers and ends his letter.

  * **Therefore, beloved, since you know these things** - "These things" refers to the truths about the patience of God and the teachings of these false teachers.
  * **guard yourselves** - "protect yourselves"
  * **so that you are not led astray by the deceit of lawless people** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "so that lawless people do no lead you astray with their deceitful words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **led astray ... deceit** - Being persuaded to disobey God is spoken of as it were being led off the right path to follow. Deceitful or false teachings are spoken of as if they were someone who could lead another person off the right path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you lose your own faithfulness** - Faithfulness is spoken of as if it were a possession that believers could lose. AT: "you stop being faithful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **grow in the grace and knowledge** - "increase in the grace and knowledge"
  * **the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ** - You can translate "grace" and "knowledge" using verbal phrases. See how you translated similar phrases in [[:en:bible:notes:2pe:01:01|1:2]] and [[:en:bible:notes:2pe:01:08|1:8]] and [[:en:bible:notes:2pe:02:20|2:20]]. AT: "experience more and more our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ acting kindly toward you, and that you get to know him better and better" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]]).
