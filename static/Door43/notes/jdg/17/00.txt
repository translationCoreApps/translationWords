====== Judges 17 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter begins a section explaining how Israel came to have a king. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Idols and figures ==

According to the law of Moses, the Israelites were prohibited from making these wooden figures or cast metal idols. This was a form of idolatry. This practice was common in Canaan and it shows the influence the Israelites allowed these people to have on them. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses]])
