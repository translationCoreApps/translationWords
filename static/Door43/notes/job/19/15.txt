===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues to speak to his three friends.

  * **regard me** - "consider me"
  * **I am an alien in their sight** - Their sight represents their attitude toward him. AT: "they think of me as a foreigner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **but he gives me no answer** - The answer is a response to Job's call. AT: "but he does not respond to me" or "but he does not come to me" 
  * **although I entreat him with my mouth** - The phrase "with my mouth" refers Job speaking. AT: "even though I speak to him and plead with him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **entreat him** - "plead with him"
