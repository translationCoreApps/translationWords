===== translationNotes: =====

  * **there is no equal to him** - "no other creature is like Leviathan" 
  * **He sees everything that is proud** - "He is very, very proud"
  * **he is king over all the sons of pride** - Leviathan is spoken of as if he were a person who is able to be proud and become king. AT: "Leviathan has more reason to be proud than anyone else on earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
