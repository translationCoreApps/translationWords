===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A voice is heard in Ramah** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "I hear a voice in Ramah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It is Rachel weeping for her children** - Rachel was the wife of Jacob/Israel and so the mother of the tribes of Joseph and Benjamin. Her name is a metonym for the women of Israel who are crying because the Babylonians have killed their children or taken them away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **She refuses to be comforted over them, for they live no longer** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "She will not let anyone comfort her, for her children are dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
