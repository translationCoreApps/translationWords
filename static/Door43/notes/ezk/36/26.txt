===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Israel.

  * **I will give you a new heart and a new spirit in your innermost parts, and I will take away the heart of stone from your flesh. For I will give you a heart of flesh** - Yahweh causing the people to no longer be stubborn but to love and serve Yahweh is spoken of as if he will take away their old heart and give them a new heart and spirit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a new heart and a new spirit** - Here "heart" and "spirit" represent a person's thoughts, attitudes, and emotions. See how you translated "heart" and "spirit" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]]. 
  * **in your innermost parts** - "within you" or "inside of you"
  * **the heart of stone** - This speaks of people being stubborn as if their hearts were made of stone. See how you translated "stone heart" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]]. AT: "the heart that is as hard as stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your flesh** - "your body"
  * **a heart of flesh** - People being willing to serve Yahweh is spoken of as if their hearts were soft like flesh. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:19]]. AT: "a heart that is soft like flesh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **enable you to walk in my statutes** - A person acting or behaving in a certain way is spoken of is if it were a person walking. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:11:19|11:20]]. AT: "enable you to obey my statutes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
