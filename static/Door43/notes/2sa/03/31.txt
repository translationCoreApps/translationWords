===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The king wept and cried loudly** - The words "wept" and "cried loudly" mean basically the same thing and emphasize how much David mourned for Abner. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **all the people** - This phrase refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
