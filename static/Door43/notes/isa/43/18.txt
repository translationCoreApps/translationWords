===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
 
  * **Do not think about these former things, nor consider the things of long ago.** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize that they are not to worry about what happened in the past. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Look** - This is an idiom. AT: "Listen" or "Pay attention" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **do you not perceive it?** - Yahweh uses a question to teach the people of Israel. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "surely you have noticed it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
