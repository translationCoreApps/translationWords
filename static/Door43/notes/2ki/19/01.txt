===== translationNotes: =====

  * **house of Yahweh** - This is another way of saying "temple of Yahweh."
  * **Eliakim ... Shebna ... Isaiah ... Amoz** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **He sent Eliakim** - "Hezekiah sent Eliakim" 
  * **all covered with sackcloth** - "all wearing sackcloth"
