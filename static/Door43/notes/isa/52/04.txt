===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the beginning** - Here "beginning" refers to the start of Israel's history as they were first becoming a people.
  * **went down into Egypt** - "went to Egypt." It was common to use the phrase "went down" when speaking of traveling from Canaan to Egypt.
  * **Assyria has oppressed them** - Assyria refers to the people of Assyria. AT: "the people of Assyria have treated them badly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
