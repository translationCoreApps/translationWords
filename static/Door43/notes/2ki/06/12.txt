===== translationNotes: =====

  * **No** - The servant is saying that none of the king's soldiers are giving information to the king of Israel. AT: "It is none of us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **my master, king** - This refers to the king of Aram.
  * **the words that you speak in your own bedroom** - "what you say in the privacy of your own bedroom"
  * **I may send men and capture him** - The king plans to send the men to capture Elisha for him. The king does not plan to capture him himself. AT: "I may send men to capture him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **See** - This word is used here to draw the king's attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **he is in Dothan** - "Elisha is in Dothan"
  * **Dothan** - This is the name of a city. ([[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
