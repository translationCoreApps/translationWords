===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to bless the tribes of Israel; the blessings are short poems. He continues describing the tribe of Levi, which he began to do in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:33:08|33:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **accept** - "be pleased with" or "be pleased to accept"
  * **the work of his hands** - Here the word "hands" refers to the whole person. AT: "all the work that he does" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Shatter the loins of** - The loins were considered the center of strength. AT: "Take away the strength of" or "Utterly destroy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **rise up ... rise up** - This phrase is used twice as a metaphor. AT: "rise up to fight ... cause any more trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rise up against** - This is an idiom. AT: "fight against" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
