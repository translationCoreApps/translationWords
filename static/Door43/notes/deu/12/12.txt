===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **rejoice before Yahweh** - "rejoice in the presence of Yahweh"
  * **the Levites who are within your gate** - Here "gates" is a reference to the city itself. AT: "the Levites who live inside your city" or "the Levites living with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **because he has no portion or inheritance among you** - The fact that Yahweh would not give any of the land to the Levites is spoken of as if a father were not giving them an inheritance. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he has no portion** - Here "he" refers to Levi. Levi represents all of his descendants. AT: "they have no portion" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
