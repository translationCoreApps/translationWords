===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gray hair is a crown of glory** - The writer speaks of gray hair as if it were a crown. "Gray hair" is a metonym for old age. AT: "A person who has lived long enough to have gray hair is like one who wears a glorious crown on his head" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **it is gained** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "a person gains it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **one who rules his spirit** - The writer speaks of a person being able to control his own temper and emotions as if he ruled over his spirit like a king rules his people. AT: "one who controls his temper" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
