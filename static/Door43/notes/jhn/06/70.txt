===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verse 71 is not part of the main storyline as John comments on what Jesus said. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Did not I choose you, the twelve, and one of you is a devil?** - Jesus gives this remark in the form of a question to draw attention to the fact that one of the disciples will betray him. AT: "I chose you all myself, yet one of you is a servant of Satan!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
