===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the Israelites as if they are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
 
  * **when your herds ... gold increase** - These words are the end of the list of "when" clauses that begins with "when you eat" ([[:en:bible:notes:deu:08:11|8:12]]).
  * **your herds and your flocks** - "your herds of cattle and your flocks of sheep and goats"
  * **multiply** - "increase in number" or "become many"
  * **all that you have multiplies** - "you have many more things" or "you have many more possessions"
  * **that then your heart become lifted up ... you forget Yahweh** - Moses tells of two things that will happen if all the "when" statements ([[:en:bible:notes:deu:08:11|8:12-13]]) become true.
  * **your heart become lifted up** - This is an idiom. AT: "you might become proud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **who brought you out** - Moses begins to remind the Israelites of what they know about Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **out of the house of bondage** - This is a metaphor for the time they were slaves in Egypt. AT: "out from the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
