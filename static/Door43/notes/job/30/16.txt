===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now my life is pouring out from within me** - Job speaks as if his life were a liquid and his body were a container. He feels he is about to die. AT: "Now I am dying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **many days of suffering have laid hold on me** - Job speaks of his continuous suffering as if the days of suffering have grabbed hold of him. AT: "I suffer many days, and the suffering does not end" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) 
  * **my bones in me are pierced** - Job speaks of the pain in his bones as if is bones were being pierced. AT: "my bones ache terribly" or "I have sharp pain in my bones" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the pains that gnaw at me take no rest** - Job speaks of his constant pain as if it were alive and biting him and refused to rest. AT: "the pains that cause me to suffer do not stop" or "I am in constant pain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
