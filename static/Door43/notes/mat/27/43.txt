===== translationNotes: =====
  * **Connecting Statement:** - The Jewish leaders continue mocking Jesus.

  * **For he even said, 'I am the Son of God.'** - This is a quotation within a quotation. It can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "because Jesus said that he is the Son of God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotesinquotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **Son of God** - This is an important title for Jesus that describes his relationship to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **the robbers who were crucified with him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the robbers that the soldiers had crucified with Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
