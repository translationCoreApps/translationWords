===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Sin ... Rehob ... Zoan** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the wilderness of Sin** - The word "Sin" here is the Hebrew name of the wilderness. It is not the English word "sin." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_transliterate]])
  * **Hebron had been built seven years before Zoan in Egypt** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the Canaanites had built Hebron 7 years before the Egyptians built Zoan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Ahiman ... Sheshai ... Talmai** - These are names of clans that were named after their ancestors. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anak** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
