===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Whatever touches its meat will become holy** - This is an implied warning that no one besides the priests should touch the meat of the sin offering. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **if the blood is sprinkled on** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "if the blood sprinkles on" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the clay pot in which it is boiled must be broken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must break the clay pot in which you boiled the meat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **If it is boiled in a bronze pot, it must be scrubbed and rinsed clean in water** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "If you boiled the meat in a bronze pot, then you must scrub the pot and rinse it with clean water" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
