===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Isaac made a feast for them, and they ate and drank** - Eating a meal together was a part of making a covenant with one another. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **for them** - Here "them" refers to "Abimelech, Ahuzzath, and Phicol"
  * **they ate** - Here "they" refers to Isaac, Abimelech, Ahuzzath, and Phicol. AT: "they all ate" (UDB)
  * **They rose early** - "They awoke early"
