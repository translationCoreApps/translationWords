===== translationNotes: =====

  * **pursued my soul** - "chased me to try to catch me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **has pushed me down to the ground** - "has completely defeated me" (see UDB)
  * **My spirit is overwhelmed within me** - "I am weak" or "I am extremely discouraged"
  * **my heart despairs** - "I no longer have any hope"
