===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May your hand help me** - The hand is a synecdoche for the whole person. AT: "Please help me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **have chosen** - "have chosen to obey"
  * **your rescue** - The abstract noun "rescue" can be stated as a verb. AT: "you to rescue me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **your law is my delight** - The abstract noun "delight" can be stated as a verb. "I very much enjoy obeying your law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
