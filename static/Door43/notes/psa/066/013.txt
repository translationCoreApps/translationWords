===== translationNotes: =====

  * **which my lips promised and my mouth spoke** - Here "lips" and "mouth" refer to the words of promise that were spoken. AT: "which I promised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **aroma of rams** - "smell of the smoke of sacrificed rams"
