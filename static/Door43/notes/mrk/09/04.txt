===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Elijah with Moses** - It may be helpful to state who these men are. AT: "two prophets who had lived long ago, Moses and Elijah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they were talking** - The word "they" refers to Elijah and Moses.
  * **Peter answered and said to Jesus** - "Peter said to Jesus." Here the word "answered" is used to introduce Peter into the conversation. Peter was not answering a question. 
  * **us** - This word refers to Peter, James, and John. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **shelters** - "tents." This refers to simple temporary dwellings.
  * **For he did not know what to say, for they were terrified** - This parenthetical sentence tells background information about Peter, James, and John. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **terrified** - "very frightened" or "very afraid"
