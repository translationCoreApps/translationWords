===== translationNotes: =====

  * **day by day** - "each day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **from the first day to the last** - The implied information is that it was during the entire week of the festival. AT: "from the first day to the last day of the week" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **They kept the festival** - "They made a feast" or "They celebrated the festival" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **on the eighth day** - "on day 8" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **solemn assembly** - This was a special religious gathering.
  * **in obedience to the decree** - The implied information is that "the decree" was the command of Yahweh about how the Festival of Shelters was to end. AT: "as God had commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
