===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who finds wisdom** - The writer speaks of "wisdom" as if it were an object that one finds. AT: "The one who attains wisdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **what silver will give in return** - This refers to the profit that one can make from trading or investing silver.
