===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zechariah son of Jeroboam reigned over Israel in Samaria for six months** - AT: "Zechariah son of Jeroboam was king of Israel in Samaria for 6 months."
  * **Zechariah son of Jeroboam** - this Jeroboam is referring to Jeroboam II, who ruled from 786-756 BC; he is also referred to as Joash.
  * **He did what was evil** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:03:01|3:2]].
  * **evil in the sight of Yahweh** - "evil according to Yahweh" or "evil before Yahweh" or "Yahweh condemns what he did and declares it to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Jeroboam son of Nebat** - this Jeroboam is referring to Jeroboam I, who ruled 922-901 BC
  * **who had caused Israel to sin** - "Jeroboam did many bad things, and he caused the people of Israel to sin with him."
