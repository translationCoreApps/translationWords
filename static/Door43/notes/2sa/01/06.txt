===== translationNotes: =====

  * **By chance I happened to be** - This statement emphasizes that the man did not plan to meet Saul.
  * **Saul was leaning on his spear** - Possible meanings are 1) Saul was weak and using the spear to support himself or 2) Saul was attempting to kill himself by falling on his own spear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
