===== translationNotes: =====



  * **shearing his sheep** - "shaving the wool off his sheep" 
  * **greet him in my name** - "greet him as I would greet him if I were there"
  * **Live in prosperity** - "I desire that you may live in prosperity" The abstract noun "prosperity" can be translated as a verb. AT: "I desire that you possess many good things as long as you live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Peace to you and peace to your house, and peace be to all that you have** - "I desire that peace may come to you, your household and your possessions"
