===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May my lips pour out praise** - The psalmist speaks of his lips as if they were a container and as if they were his whole being. AT: "I desire to praise you much" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **my tongue sing** - The psalmist speaks of his tongue either 1) as if it were a person or 2) as a synecdoche for his whole being. AT: "me sing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
