===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David speaks to the people of Jabesh Gilead.

  * **this thing** - They buried Saul.
  * **let your hands be strong** - Here "hands" refers to the people of Jabesh Gilead. AT: "be strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **anointed me king** - In this symbolic act, they poured oil on David's head to show that he was selected to be the king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
