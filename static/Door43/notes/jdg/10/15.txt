  * **foreign gods among them** - You can make explicit the implicit meaning of this statement. AT: "foreign gods whose images they possessed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Yahweh could bear Israel's misery no longer** - Here Israel refers to the people of Israel. AT: "And Yahweh did not want the people of Israel to suffer any longer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
