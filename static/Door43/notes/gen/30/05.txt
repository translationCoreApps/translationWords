===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bilhah** - This is the name of Rachel's female servant. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:29:28|29:29]].
  * **bore Jacob a son** - "gave birth to a son for Jacob"
  * **she called his name** - "Rachel gave him the name" 
  * **called his name Dan** - Translators may also add a footnote that says "The name Dan means 'he judged.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
