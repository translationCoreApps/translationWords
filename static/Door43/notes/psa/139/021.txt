===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do I not hate those, Yahweh, who hate you? Do I not despise those who rise up against you?** - These two sentences have similar meanings. The second one strengthens the thought in the first. Both of these questions actually make statements. (See UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **rise up against** - rebel against
