===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **gives them over to you** - Moses speaks of victory as if it were a physical object one person could give to another. AT: "enables you to defeat them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you defeat them** - Here "them" refers to the seven nations from [[:en:bible:notes:deu:07:01|7:1]].
