===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly, truly** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:01:49|1:51]].
  * **he who hears my word** - Here "word" is a metonym that represents the message of Jesus. AT: "anyone who hears my message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will not be condemned** - This can be stated positively. AT: "will be judged to be innocent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
