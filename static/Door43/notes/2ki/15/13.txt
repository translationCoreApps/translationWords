===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the thirty-ninth year of Azariah king of Judah** - It can be stated clearly that this is the thirty-ninth year of his reign. AT: "in year 39 of the reign of Azariah king of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Shallum ... Jabesh** - These are the names of two men. See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:15:10|15:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Menahem ... Gadi** - These are the names of two men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **he reigned only one month in Samaria** - Samaria is the city that he lived in when was king of Israel. AT: "Shallum lived in Samaria and reigned over Israel for only one month" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **became king in his place** - The phrase "in his place" is a metaphor meaning "instead of him." AT: "became king instead of Shallum" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
