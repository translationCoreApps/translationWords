===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jesus found a young donkey and sat on it** - Here John gives background information that Jesus secures a donkey. He implies that Jesus will ride the donkey into Jerusalem. AT: "he found a young donkey and sat on it, riding into the city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **as it was written** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "as the prophets wrote in the Scripture" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **daughter of Zion** - "Daughter of Zion" is a metonym that refers to the people of Jerusalem. AT: "you people of Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
