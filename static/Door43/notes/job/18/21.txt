===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Bildad continues to describe the wicked person.

  * **such are the homes of unrighteous people** - "that is what happens to the homes of unrighteous people." Bildad uses this phrase to conclude his comments on what happens to a wicked person.
  * **the places of those who do not know God** - AT: "such are the places of those who do not know God" or "and that is where people who do not know God belong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
