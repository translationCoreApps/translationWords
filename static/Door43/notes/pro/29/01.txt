===== translationNotes: =====


  * **stiffens his neck** - "resists the rebukes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **will be broken in a moment** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will suddenly break him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **beyond healing** - "and no one will be able to heal him." Sickness is a metaphor for any kind of bad situation. AT: "and no one will be able to help him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the people sigh** - The people let out long, loud breaths that show that they are weary and sad. AT: "the people will be weary and sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
