===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that he collapsed on the face of his father** - The term "he collapsed" is an idiom for being overcome. AT: "that he fell on his father in grief" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his servants the physicians** - "his servants who took care of dead bodies"
  * **to embalm his father** - To "embalm" is a special way of preserving a dead body before it is buried. AT: "to prepare his father's body for burial" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **They took forty days** - "They took 40 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seventy days** - "70 days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
