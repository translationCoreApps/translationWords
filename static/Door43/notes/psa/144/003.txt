===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh, what is man that you take notice of him or the son of man that you think about him?** - These questions can be translated as statements. AT: "Man is so small compared to everything else you have made that I am surprised that you take notice of man and that you think about the son of man." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **man ... son of man** - two words for human beings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **like a breath ... like a passing shadow** - The writer compares humans to these thing to emphasize how short their lives are. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
