===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The beast was captured and with him the false prophet** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The rider on the white horse captured the beast and the false prophet" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the mark of the beast** - This was an identifying mark that indicated that the person who received it worshiped the beast. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:13:15|13:17]].
  * **The two of them were thrown alive** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God threw the beast and the false prophet alive" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the fiery lake of burning sulfur** - "the lake of fire that burns with sulfur" (UDB) or "place full of fire that burns with sulfur"
