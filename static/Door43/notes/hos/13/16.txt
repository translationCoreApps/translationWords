===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **Samaria will be guilty, for she has rebelled against her God** - Here "Samaria" refers to the people in the city of Samaria who are guilty of rebelling against God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They will fall** - The people will die. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **by the sword** - In warfare (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their young children will be dashed to pieces, and their pregnant women will be ripped open** - These phrases can be put into the active voice. AT: "the enemy will dash their young children to pieces, and they will rip open the pregnant women" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
