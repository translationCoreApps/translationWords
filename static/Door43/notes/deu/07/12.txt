===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words. He speaks as if the Israelites are one person in verse 12 and to all of them as a group in verse 13. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **and multiply you** - "and increase the number of your people"
  * **the fruit of your body** - This is an idiom for "your children." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the fruit of your ground** - This is an idiom for "your crops." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the multiplication of your cattle** - "your cattle so they will become many"
