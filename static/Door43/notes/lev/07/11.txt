===== translationNotes: =====


  * **of cakes made without yeast, but mixed with oil** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "of cakes he made without yeast but mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cakes ... mixed with oil** - Here "cakes" refers to a thick bread.
  * **of cakes made without yeast, but spread with oil** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "of thin cakes he made without yeast but spread with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cakes ... spread with oil** - Here "cakes" refers to a thin bread.
  * **of cakes made with fine flour that is mixed with oil** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "of cakes he made with fine flour mixed with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cakes made with fine flour** - Here cakes refers to a thick bread. Apparently, it is similar to the first type of bread except it is made with the finest flour.
