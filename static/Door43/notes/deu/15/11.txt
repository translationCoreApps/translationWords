===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For the poor will never cease to exist in the land** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "For there will always be poor people in the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **For the poor** - The nominal adjective "the poor" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "For poor people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **I command you and say, 'You must surely open your hand ... in your land.'** - This has a quotation within a quotation. A direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "I command you to open your hand ... in your land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]]) (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotesinquotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **open your hand to your brother, to your needy, and to your poor** - A person who is willing to help someone else is spoken of as if his hand were open. AT: "help your fellow Israelite, those who are needy, and those who are poor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your brother, to your needy, and to your poor** - The words "needy" and "poor" mean basically the same thing and emphasize that these are people who cannot help themselves. AT: "help your fellow Israelites who cannot help themselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
