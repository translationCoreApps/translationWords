===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **made from two tenths of an ephah. They must be made from fine flour and baked with yeast** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that you have made from two tenths of an ephah of flour and then baked with yeast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **two tenths of an ephah** - This is approximately 4.5 liters. AT: "four and a half liters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **and producing a sweet aroma for Yahweh** - The Lord's pleasure with the aroma represents his pleasure with the person who burns the offering. AT: "and Yahweh will be pleased with you" or "that pleases the Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
