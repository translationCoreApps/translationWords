===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **When Saul saw David** - The conversation in 17:55-56 happened before David killed Goliath. AT: "When Saul had seen David" or "Earlier when Saul saw David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_events]])
  * **go out against the Philistine** - "go to fight against the Philistine"
  * **whose son is this youth** - "who is this youth's father"
  * **As you live** - This was a way of swearing that what he was about to say was true. 
  * **whose son the boy is** - "who the boy's father is"
