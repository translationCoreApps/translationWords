===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they will all be one ... just as you, Father, are in me, and I am in you ... they will also be in us** - Those who trust in Jesus become united with the Father and the Son when they believe.
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **the world** - Here the "the world" is a metonym that refers to the people who do not yet know God. AT: "the people who do not know God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
