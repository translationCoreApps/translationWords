===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **Do not be irritated because of evildoers** - "Do not let wicked people upset you"
  * **dry up as the grass ... wither as the green plants** - Evildoers are spoken of as if they are grass and plants that dry up and dies in the heat of the summer. These two similes mean both mean they will die. AT: "die" or "come to an end" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
