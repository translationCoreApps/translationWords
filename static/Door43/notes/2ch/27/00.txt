====== 2 Chronicles 27 General Notes ======



=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Trust in Yahweh ==
Jotham was able to conquer the people of Ammon because he obeyed the Lord. For Yahweh, trust in Yahweh is the key to victory in battle. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]])
