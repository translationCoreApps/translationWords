===== translationNotes: =====

  * **By you I have been supported from the womb** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You have supported me from the womb" or "You have taken care of me ever since I came out of my mother's womb" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you are he** - "you are the one"
  * **I am an example to many people** - "Many people see how I live and want to live as I do"
