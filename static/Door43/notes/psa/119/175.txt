===== translationNotes: =====

  * **may your righteous decrees help me** - The psalmist speaks of Yahweh's decrees as if they were a person who could help him. AT: "may I listen to your righteous decrees and so become wise and strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I have wandered off like a lost sheep** - "I have left your way like a sheep that has left its flock" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **seek your servant** - "because I am your servant, come and search for me"
