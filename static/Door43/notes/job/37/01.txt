===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my heart trembles ... it is moved out of its place** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of his fear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **my heart trembles at this** - The word "this" refers to the storm in [[:en:bible:notes:job:36:32|36:33]].
  * **it is moved out of its place** - Elihu speaks of his heart beating violently as if it were to jump out of his chest. AT: "it moves out of its place" or "it beats violently" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the noise of his voice, the sound that goes out from his mouth** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. Elihu speaks of the thunder as if it is God's voice. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **to the borders of the earth** - Elihu speaks of the farthest places on the earth as if they were the earth's borders. AT: "everywhere in the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
