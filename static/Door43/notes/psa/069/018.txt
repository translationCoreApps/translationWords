===== translationNotes: =====

  * **redeem me** - The writer asks God to redeem him as if the writer were a slave whose freedom God could purchase. AT: "free me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ransom me** - The writer asks God to ransom him as if the writer were a captive whose release could be paid for with money. AT: "rescue me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my rebuke, my shame, and my dishonor** - These abstract nouns can be stated as actions. AT: "how people have rebuked me, shamed me, and dishonored me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **my adversaries are all before you** - Here "before you" means that God sees and knows all about them. AT: "you know who all my enemies are" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
