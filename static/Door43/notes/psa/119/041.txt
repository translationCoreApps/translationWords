===== translationNotes: =====

  * **VAV** - This is the name of the sixth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 41-48 begins with this letter.
  * **give me your unfailing love** - This is a request. AT: "please give me your unfailing love" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **a reply** - "an answer" or "a response"
