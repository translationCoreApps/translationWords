===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He opened** - The word "He" refers to Yahweh.
  * **thick darkness was under his feet** - Though Yahweh does not actually have feet, human characteristics are given to him. AT: "thick darkness was beneath him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **wings of the wind** - Here David speaks of the wind as if it had wings like an angel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
