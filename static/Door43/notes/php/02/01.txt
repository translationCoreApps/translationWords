===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul advises the believers to have unity and humility and reminds them of Christ's example.

  * **I suppose** - "I believe it is true"
  * **that there is encouragement in Christ** - "that Christ has encouraged you "
  * **that there is comfort from his love** - "that his love has given you comfort"
  * **that there is fellowship of the Spirit** - "that you have fellowship with the Spirit"
  * **that there are tender mercies and compassions** - "that you have experienced many of God's acts of tender mercy and compassion"
  * **Make my joy full** - Paul speaks here of joy as if it were a liquid filling a container. AT: "Cause me to rejoice greatly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
