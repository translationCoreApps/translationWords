===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Lord, why have you caused trouble for this people?** - This question shows how disappointed he was that the Egyptians were treating the Israelites even more harshly now. AT: "Lord, I am sorry that you have caused this trouble for this people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Why did you send me in the first place?** - This question shows how disappointed Moses was that God had sent him to Egypt. AT: "I wish you had never sent me here!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **to speak to him in your name** - The word "name" here represents the message of God. AT: "to give him your message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
