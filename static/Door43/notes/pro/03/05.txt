===== translationNotes: =====


  * **all your heart** - Here the word "heart" represents the inner person. AT: "your whole being" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **do not lean on your own understanding** - The writer speaks of relying on one's own understanding as if "understanding" were an object on which a person can lean. AT: "do not rely on your own understanding" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in all your ways** - The writer speaks of a person's actions as if they were paths on which the person walks. AT: "in everything you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he will make your paths straight** - The writer speaks of Yahweh making a person's actions prosperous as if that person's actions were paths on which he walks and which Yahweh makes free of obstacles. AT: "he will give you success" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
