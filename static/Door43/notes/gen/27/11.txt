===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I am a smooth man** - "I am a man with smooth skin" or "I am not hairy"
  * **and I will seem to him as a deceiver** - "and he will think that I am a liar" or "he will know that I am deceiving him"
  * **I will bring a curse upon me and not a blessing** - Being cursed or blessed is spoken of as if a curse and a blessing are objects that are placed on a person. AT: "Then because of this, he will curse me and not bless me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
