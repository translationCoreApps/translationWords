===== translationNotes: =====

  * **its lamp is the Lamb** - Here the glory of Jesus, the Lamb, is spoken of as if it were a lamp that gives light to the city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Its gates will not be shut** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "No one will shut the gates" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
