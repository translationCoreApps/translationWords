===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The following events happen immediately after the three disciples witness Jesus' transfiguration.

  * **As they** - "As Jesus and the disciples"
  * **the Son of Man** - Jesus is speaking about himself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Why then do the scribes say that Elijah must come first?** - The disciples are referring to the belief that Elijah will come back to life and return to the people of Israel before the Messiah comes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
