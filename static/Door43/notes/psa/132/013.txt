===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - "He", "my" and "I" refer to Yahweh and "her" refers to Zion. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])

  * **Zion ... her ... her** - The author writes as though the city of Zion were a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **he has desired her for his seat** - Here "seat" represents either 1) his throne where he would rule, or 2) the place where he would stay. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **he has desired her for his seat** - "Yahweh has desired Zion for Yahweh's seat." This shows the actual participants. 
  * **my resting place** - The place God chose for people to worship him is spoken of as if it were a place where he rests or lives eternally. AT: "the place where I stay" or "my tabernacle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
