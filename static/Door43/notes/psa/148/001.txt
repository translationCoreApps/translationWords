  * **Praise Yahweh, you in the heavens ... you in the heights** - "Praise Yahweh, you in the heavens ... you in the sky." These two lines are parallel, with phrase "the heights" meaning the same thing as "the heavens" in the previous line. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
