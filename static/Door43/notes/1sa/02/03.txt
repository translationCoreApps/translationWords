===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hannah continues to recite a song to Yahweh. She speaks as if other people were listening to her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **no arrogance** - "no arrogant words"
  * **by him actions are weighed** - This can be stated in the active voice. AT: "he weighs people's actions" or "he understands why people act as they do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The bow of the mighty men are broken** - Possible meanings are 1) the bows themselves are broken or 2) the men who carry the bows are prevented from acting. AT: "Mighty bowmen are kept from acting"
  * **The bow of the mighty men are broken** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Yahweh breaks the bows of the mighty men" or "Yahweh can make even the strongest of people weak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **those who stumble put on strength like a belt** - This metaphor means they will no longer stumble, but their strength will remain with them as tightly as a belt. AT: "but he will make those who stumble strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **put on ... a belt** - This is the general term for putting something around one's waist to prepare for work.
