===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Asaph speaks of God as if God were a soldier returning down a mountain from winning a great battle.

  * **You shine brightly** - Shining brightly is a metaphor for being great. AT: "You show how extremely great you are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The bravehearted were plundered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Your people killed your enemies' brave soldiers and then took away all their possessions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fell asleep** - "died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
