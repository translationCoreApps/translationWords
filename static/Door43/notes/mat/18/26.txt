===== translationNotes: =====

  * **fell down, bowed down** - This shows that the servant approached the king in the most humble way possible. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **before him** - "before the king" 
  * **he was moved with compassion** - "he felt compassion for the servant"
  * **released him** - "let him go"
