===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gaal ... Ebed** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **had confidence in him** - The word "confidence" is an abstract noun that can be stated with the verb "trust." AT: "trusted him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **They went out into the field** - Here "They" refers to Gaal and his relatives and the men of Shechem.
  * **they trampled on them** - They did this to squeeze out the grape juice to make wine with it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **trampled** - "crushed" or "stomped"
  * **in the house** - Here "house" represents a temple. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
