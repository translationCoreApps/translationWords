===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their work** - This refers to the work of the people roused by God in the previous verse.
  * **everyone whose spirit God stirred to go** - Stirring the spirit is a metonym for making someone want to act. Similar words appear in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:01:01|1:1]]. AT: "everyone whom God had made want to go" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
