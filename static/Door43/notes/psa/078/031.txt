===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then** - while the food was still in their mouths ([[:en:bible:notes:psa:078:029|78:31]])
  * **God's anger attacked them** - "God was angry and attacked them." See how "his anger attacked Israel" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:078:021|78:21]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **brought down** - "killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **did not believe his wonderful deeds** - The word "deeds" is a metonym for God, who did the deeds. What they did not believe can be stated clearly. AT: "did not believe that he would take care of them even though he had done such wonderful deeds" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
