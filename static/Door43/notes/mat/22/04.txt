===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues telling a parable. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **servants, saying, 'Tell them who are invited** - This direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. Also, this can be stated in active form. AT: "servants, ordering them to tell those whom he invited" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **See** - "Look" or "Listen" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you"
  * **My oxen and fattened calves have been killed** - It is implied that the animals are cooked and ready to eat. This can be stated in active form. AT: "My servants have killed and cooked my oxen and my fattened calves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **My oxen and fattened calves** - "My best oxen and calves for eating"
