===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to purify them** - Here "them" refers to the Levites.
  * **Sprinkle the water of atonement on them** - Moses sprinkling water on them was symbolic of their atonement. AT: "Sprinkle on their the water that symbolizes atonement" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **wash their clothes** - The Levites are supposed to wash their own clothes. AT: "then make them wash their clothes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **a young bull and its grain offering** - When offering a young bull, a grain offering was usually required to accompany it. 
  * **of fine flour mingled with oil** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "of fine flour that they have mingled with oil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
