===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Every night and day** - This is a merism meaning "all the time." AT: "Continually, both night and day," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **cut himself with sharp stones** - Often times when a person is possessed by a demon, the demon will cause the person to do self-destructive things, such as cutting himself. 
  * **When he saw Jesus from a distance** - When the man first saw Jesus, Jesus would have been getting out of the boat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **bowed down** - This means that he knelt down before Jesus out of reverence and respect, not out of worship.
