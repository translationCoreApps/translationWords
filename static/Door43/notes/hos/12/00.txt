====== Hosea 12 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jacob ==

There is a parallel between Jacob and the northern kingdom of Israel. In the same way Jacob returned to Yahweh, the northern kingdom should repent of their idolatry and turn back to Yahweh. (See: [[::en:obe:kt:repent]])
