===== translationNotes: =====

  * **AYIN** - This is the name of the sixteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 121-128 begins with this letter.
  * **do not abandon me to my oppressors** - "do not allow people to oppress me"
  * **Guarantee the welfare of your servant** - The writer speaks about himself as "your servant." AT: "Guarantee my welfare" or "Help and protect me, your servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **the proud** - This nominal adjective can be stated as an adjective. AT: "those who are proud" or "proud people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
