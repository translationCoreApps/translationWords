===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For I have taken** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh.
  * **that was raised and presented to me** - Yahweh instructs the people to make sure that the priest presented their offerings to him. This can be translated in active form. AT: "that the priest raised and presented to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **made by the people of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that the people of Israel make" or "that the people of Israel give" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
