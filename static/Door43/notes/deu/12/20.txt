===== translationNotes: =====

  * **enlarges your borders** - "enlarges your territory" or "gives you even more land"
  * **you say, 'I will eat flesh,' because of your desire to eat meat** - This is a quotation within a quotation. This direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "and you decide that you want to eat meat" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotesinquotes]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **as your soul desires** - Here "soul" refers to the whole person. AT: "as you want" or "as you crave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
