===== translationNotes: =====


  * **fifteenth day of the seventh month** - This is near the beginning of October on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Festival of Shelters** - This is a celebration during which the people of Israel lived in temporary shelters for seven days as a way to remember the time they spent living in the wilderness after they left Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
