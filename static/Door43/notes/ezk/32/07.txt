===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Pharaoh what he will do to Egypt.

  * **when I extinguish you** - It was common to speak of a person's life as if it were a lamp that could be put out. AT: "When I destroy you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will cover the heavens** - Yahweh speaks of making the stars dark as if he were putting a cover over the sky. AT: "I will make everything in the sky go dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will put darkness over your land** - Darkness is spoken of as if it were something that someone could spread over everything. AT: "I will make it dark in your land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **this is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - Yahweh speaks of himself by name to express the certainty of what he is declaring. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]]. AT: "this is what the Lord Yahweh has declared" or "this is what I, the Lord Yahweh, have declared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
