===== translationNotes: =====

  * **were of one heart and soul** - Here the word "heart" refers to the thoughts and the word "soul" refers to the emotions. Together they refer to the total person. AT: "thought the same way and wanted the same things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **had all things in common** - "shared their belongings with one another." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:act:02:43|2:44]].
  * **great grace was upon them all** - Possible meanings are: 1) that God was greatly blessing the believers or 2) that the people in Jerusalem held the believers in very high esteem.
