===== translationNotes: =====

  * **David said** - "David asked" or "David prayed" (UDB)
  * **your servant has indeed heard ... as your servant has heard ... tell your servant** - David speaks as if he were someone else to show his respect for Yahweh. AT: "I have indeed heard ... as I have heard ... tell me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **surrender me into his hand** - Here "hand" represents power or control. AT: "give me over to Saul" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **destroy** - "defeat completely"
