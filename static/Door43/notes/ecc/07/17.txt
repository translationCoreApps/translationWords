===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why should you die before your time?** - "There is no reason for you to die sooner than you should." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **take hold of this wisdom** - "commit yourself to this wisdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you should not let go of righteousness** - "you should not stop trying to be righteous" or "you should keep trying to be righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will meet all his obligations** - "will do everything that God expects of him."
