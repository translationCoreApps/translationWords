====== Exodus 35 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sacrifice ==
All of the people offered sacrifices to Yahweh. This was a form of worship and a sign of repentance from making the golden calf idol. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:worship]] and [[:en:obe:kt:repent]])
