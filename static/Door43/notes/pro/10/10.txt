===== translationNotes: =====

  * **will be thrown down** - A person who is ruined is spoken of as if he were thrown down. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The mouth of one** - "The words of a person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **is a water spring of life** - This person's speech is spoken of as if it preserved living animals or people, as a water spring would do in a dry land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the mouth of the wicked** - "the words of a wicked person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the mouth of the wicked covers up violence** - That is, the wicked person says harmless things, but plans to violent things against other people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the mouth of the wicked** - This expressions stands what the wicked person says. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **covers up violence** - This expression represent the idea of hiding violence, that is, hiding one's plan to do violence to another person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]
