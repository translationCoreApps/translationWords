===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel.

  * **Another of its borders is also** - "The western border of the Reubenite and Gadite territory is"
  * **Chinnereth** - "the Sea of Chinnereth." This is the same place as "the Sea of Galilee" or "the Lake of Gennesaret." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mount Pisgah** - This is the name of a mountain in the northern part of the Abarim mountain range. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
