===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **all the statutes and the decrees** - These are the statutes and decrees Moses will give in Deuteronomy 12-26.
  * **I set before you today** - This does not mean these are new. Moses is reviewing the same statutes and decrees that he gave 40 years earlier.
  * **I set before you** - God's statutes and decrees, which Moses is telling the people, are spoken of as if they were objects that Moses is setting before the people. AT: "I am giving to you" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
