===== translationNotes: =====

  * **TAV** - This is the name of the twenty-second letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 169-176 begins with this letter.
  * **give me understanding into your word** - The psalmist speaks of the ability to understand as if it were a solid object. AT: "help me understand your word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **May my plea come before you** - The psalmist speaks of the words he speaks in prayer as if they were people who want to speak to a king. AT: "May you hear my prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
