===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul prays for the Ephesian believers and praises God for the power that believers have through Christ.

  * **I have not stopped thanking God** - Paul uses "not stopped" to emphasize that he continues to thank God. AT: "I continue to thank God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
