====== Psalm 089 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 89 is a worship and a deliverance psalm. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's promises ==
God is mighty and has promised David an eternal kingdom. But now that kingdom is being destroyed. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:promise]] and [[:en:obe:kt:eternity]])

== Parallelism ==
This Psalm exhibits the kind of parallelism where the second line often emphasizes the first line. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
