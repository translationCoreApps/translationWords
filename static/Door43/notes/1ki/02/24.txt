===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - King Solomon executes Adonijah.

  * **set me on the throne** - The word "throne" refers to Solomon's authority to rule that was given by Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who has made me a house** - Here "house" refers to descendants that Yahweh gave to King Solomon, who would continue to reign after him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
