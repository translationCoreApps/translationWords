===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the Philistines came up again** - They "came up" because the Philistines lived at a lower elevation than David's stronghold.
  * **Valley of Rephaim** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **balsam woods** - "Balsam" is a type of tree, and the "woods" describe many balsam trees growing together. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
