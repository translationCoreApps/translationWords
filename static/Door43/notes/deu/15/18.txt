===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It must not seem difficult for you to let him go free from you** - This means that they are not resent it when they let someone go free. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Be pleased when you let him go free" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **given twice the value of a hired person** - This means the owner only had to pay have the amount for this slave to work than he would for a person he hired to do a job.
  * **a hired person** - This is a person who works for pay.
