===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He must certainly be put to death** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must execute him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **You must not ... allow him to reside on his own property** - This implied that the man would have left the city of refuge and returned home. AT: "You must not ... allow him to leave the city of refuge and return home to live on his own property" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in this way** - "by accepting a ransom"
