===== translationWords: =====


===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When times are good ... when times are bad** - "When good things happen ... when bad things happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **live happily in that good** - "be happy about those good things"
  * **side by side** - "at the same time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **anything that is coming after him** - Possible meanings are 1) "anything that happens in the future" or 2) "anything that happens in the world after he dies" or 3) "anything that happens to him after he dies."
