===== translationNotes: =====


  * **as of first importance** - Possible meanings are 1) as the most important of many things or 2) as the first in time (UDB).
  * **for our sins** - "to pay for our sins" or "so that God could forgive our sins"
  * **according to the scriptures** - Paul is referring to the writings of the Old Testament. 
  * **he was buried** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they buried him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he was raised** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God raised him" or "he rose" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
