===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he who learns from correction** - The word "correction" can be translated as a verb. AT: "he who learns when someone corrects him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **prudent** - "wise"
  * **the earnings of the wicked person give** - The word "earnings" can be translated as a verbal phrase. AT: "the wealth that a wicked person earns gives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
