===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brutish** - "violent and stupid"
  * **When the wicked sprout like the grass** - This compares evil people to grass, which grows quickly and in many places. AT: "When evil people appear quickly and seem to be everywhere, like grass" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **they are doomed to eternal destruction** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "God has decided that he will destroy them completely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
