====== Psalm 076 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 76 is a worship psalm.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== Yahweh's greatness ==
God is great. He has conquered all the nations surrounding Israel. Everyone should bring him gifts.
