===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel as if he were speaking to one man, so the words "you" and "your" are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **you go out** - "you who are soldiers go out" (UDB)
  * **you have a desire for her** - Use a polite phrase for "you want to sleep with her."
  * **wish to take her for yourself as a wife** - "want to marry her"
  * **she will shave her head** - "she will shave the hair off of her head"
  * **cut her nails** - "cut her fingernails"
