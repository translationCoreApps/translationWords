===== translationNotes: =====

  * **seventy** - "70" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **made all the evil of the men of Shechem turn back on their own heads** - "Evil turn back on their heads" is an idiom. AT: "punished the men of Shechem for all the evil they had done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **on them came the curse of Jotham son of Jerub Baal** - This is an idiom. AT: "the curse of Jotham son of Jerub Baal happened to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Jerub Baal** - This is another name for Gideon. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:31|6:32]].
