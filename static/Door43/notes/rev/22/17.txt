===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This verse is a response to what Jesus said.

  * **the Bride** - Believers are spoken of as if they were a bride about to be married to her groom, Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Come!** - Possible meanings are 1) that this is an invitation for people to come and drink the water of life or 2) that this is a polite request for Jesus to return. AT: "Come and drink!" or "Please come!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Whoever is thirsty ... the water of life** - A person's desire for eternal life is spoken of as if it were thirst and of that person receiving eternal life as if he were drinking life-giving water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the water of life** - Eternal life is spoken of as if it were a drink of life-giving water. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:21:05|21:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
