====== Psalm 069 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 69 is a psalm of deliverance. The author prayed to be saved from his enemies and for them to be punished. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:save]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Deliverance ==
The author prayed God to rescue him from his enemies. They showed him no mercy and he asked God to show them no mercy. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:mercy]])
