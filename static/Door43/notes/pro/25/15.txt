===== translationNotes: =====

  * **With patience a ruler can be persuaded** - These words can be translated in active form. AT: "Someone who is patient can persuade a ruler" or "Someone who is patient can speak to a ruler and change his mind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a soft tongue can break a bone** - The word "tongue" is a metonym for the words the person speaks using the tongue. The word "bone" is a metaphor for strong opposition. AT: "gentle speech can overcome strong opposition" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
