===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - God continues telling Moses what to tell the people of Israel. The word  "you" refers to the people.

  * **call to mind** - This is an idiom. AT: "remember" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I am Yahweh your God** - This clause is repeated for emphasis.
