===== translationNotes: =====

  * **ask for it in the depths or in the height above** - Yahweh uses the word "depths" and "height" to mean Ahaz could ask him for anything. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **depths ... height** - The abstract nouns "depths" and "height" can be translated with prepositions. "places far below you ... places far above you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **will not ask** - "will not ask Yahweh for a sign" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
