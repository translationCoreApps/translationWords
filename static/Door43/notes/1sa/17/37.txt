===== translationNotes: =====

  * **from the paw of the lion and from the paw of the bear** - Bears and lions use their paws when they attack, so the word "paw" represents the attack. AT: "from the attack of the lion and from the attack of the bear" or "from the lion and the bear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the hand of this Philistine** - The Philistine's hand represents his power in fighting. AT: "the power of this Philistine" or "the Philistine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **coat of chainmail** - a flexible piece of body armor covered with protective scales or small plates
