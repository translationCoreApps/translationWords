===== translationNotes: =====

  * **beyond the Jordan** - Jesus had been on the west side of the Jordan River. AT: "to the east side of the Jordan River" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **and he stayed there** - Jesus remained on the east side of Jordan for a short period of time. AT: "Jesus stayed there for several days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **John indeed did no signs, but all the things that John has said about this man are true** - "It is true that John did no signs, but he certainly did speak the truth about this man, who does signs."
  * **signs** - These are miracles that prove that something is true or that give someone credibility.
  * **believed in** - Here "believed in" means accepted or trusted what Jesus said was true.
