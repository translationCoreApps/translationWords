====== 2 Chronicles 18 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Jehoshaphat as king continues in this chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

The will of God is accomplished, even when people try to avoid it. Ahab tried to disguise himself to hide from the soldiers of Aram but an arrow still hit and killed him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:willofgod]])
