===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the ark came to rest there** - "the people of Israel placed the ark there"
  * **the tabernacle, the tent of meeting** - Possible meanings are 1) that "the tent of meeting" specifies the other name for "the tabernacle" (UDB) or 2) "the sanctuary of the tent of meeting." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **They fulfilled their duties according to the instructions given to them** - AT: "The singers obeyed David's instructions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
