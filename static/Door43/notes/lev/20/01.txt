===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gives any of his children to Molech** - This can be made explicit. AT: "kills any of his children as a sacrifice to Molech" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **must certainly be put to death. The people in the land must stone him with stones** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the people in the land must stone him to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
