===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **with my whole heart** - Here the word "heart" represents the person's entire inner being and emotions. AT: "with all that I am" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in the assembly of the upright, in their gathering** - These two phrases mean that same thing and may be combined if necessary. AT: "in the gathering of upright people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **eagerly awaited by all those who desire them** - The word "them" refers to the "works of Yahweh." This can be stated in active form. AT: "all those who desire the works of Yahweh eagerly await them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **eagerly awaited** - Possible meanings are 1) "sought after" or 2) "studied."
