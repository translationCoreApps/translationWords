===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **walk in my statutes** - Here "walk" is used to mean "live" or "obey." AT: "continually obey all my statutes" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **do justice** - This refers to obeying and carrying out God's laws or decrees.
  * **keep all my commandments and walk in them** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the importance of obeying God's commandments. AT "carefully obey all I told you to do" (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **confirm my promise** - God would confirm his promise by doing what he promised to do, or fulfilling his promise.
  * **I will live** - AT: "My Spirit will dwell in the temple"
