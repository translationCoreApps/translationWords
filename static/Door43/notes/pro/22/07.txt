===== translationNotes: =====


  * **borrows ... lends** - "borrows money ... lends money"
  * **He who sows wickedness will reap trouble** - "A person who does bad things will get in trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the rod of his fury** - AT: "his fierce anger" or "becoming very angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will be of no use** - "will not help him"
