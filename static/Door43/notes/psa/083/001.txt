===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A song. A psalm of Asaph** - "This is a psalm that Asaph wrote"
  * **and remain unmoved** - "and do nothing to help us"
  * **Look, your enemies are making a commotion** - Here "making a commotion" means they are rioting and rebelling. AT: "Look, your enemies are rebelling against you"
  * **those who hate you have raised their heads** - The phrase "raised their heads" is way of saying they are rebelling against God. AT: "those who hate you are defying you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
