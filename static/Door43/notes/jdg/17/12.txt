===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Micah set apart the Levite** - Here "set apart" means that Micah "dedicated" or "ordained" him. AT: "Micah dedicated the Levite" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **was in Micah's house** - Here living in Micah's house is spoken of as "being" in his house. AT: "lived in Micah's house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
