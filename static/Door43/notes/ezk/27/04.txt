===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **General Information:** - "Your" and "you" in these verses refer to Tyre.
  * **General Information:** - The city of Tyre is described here and in the following verses as if it were a beautiful ship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **Your borders** - "Your boundaries"
  * **heart of the seas** - "middle of the seas"
  * **planks** - long, flat pieces of wood; thick boards
  * **a mast** - a large pole on a ship that holds up the sails of the ship
