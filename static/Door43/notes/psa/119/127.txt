===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I carefully follow all your instructions** - A person carefully obeying all of God's instructions is spoken of as if as if the instructions were leading the person and the person follows behind. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **every path of falsehood** - People doing what is evil is spoken of as if they are walking on the wrong path. AT: "all the bad ways some people live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
