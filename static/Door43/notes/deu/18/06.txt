===== translationNotes: =====

  * **desires with all his soul** - This is an idiom. AT: "strongly desires" or "really wants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **then he must serve in the name of Yahweh his God** - Here "name" represents Yahweh and his authority. AT: "then he must serve Yahweh his God as a priest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who stand there before Yahweh** - "who serve in the sanctuary in the presence of Yahweh"
  * **his family's inheritance** - This is what the priest would inherit from his father.
