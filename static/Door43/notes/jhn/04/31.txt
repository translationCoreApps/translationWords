===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the meantime** - "While the woman was going into town"
  * **the disciples were urging him** - "the disciples were telling Jesus" or "the disciples were encouraging Jesus"
  * **I have food to eat that you do not know about** - Here Jesus is not talking about literal "food," but is preparing his disciples for a spiritual lesson in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:04:34]].
  * **No one has brought him anything to eat, have they?** - The disciples think Jesus is talking about literal "food." They begin asking each other this question, expecting a "no" response. AT: "Surely no one brought him any food while we were in town!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
