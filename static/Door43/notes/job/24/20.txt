===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Job continues speaking.

  * **The womb** - This refers to the mother. AT: "The mother" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the worm will feed sweetly on him** - This means that he will die and worms will eat his body. AT: "the worm will enjoy eating his dead body" or "he will die and then his body will be eaten by worms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will be remembered no more** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one will remember him anymore" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **wickedness will be broken like a tree** - God's destruction of the wicked man is describes as if he were cutting down a tree. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will destroy the wicked as if he were a tree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **The wicked one devours** - This metaphor emphasizes how ruthless the wicked man is. AT: "Just like a wild animal kills its prey, so the wicked person harms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the barren women who have not borne children** - The people of that day considered that a woman who was barren was cursed by God. Therefore, this represents the most unfortunate women. 
  * **widow** - a woman whose husband has died
