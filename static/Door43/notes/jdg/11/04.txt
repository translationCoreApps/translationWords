===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Some days later** - "Some time later" (UDB) 
  * **made war against Israel** - The phrase "made war" means that they attacked Israel and were at war with them. Here "Israel" refers to the people of Israel. AT: "attacked the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **that we may fight with** - "so that we can fight against"
