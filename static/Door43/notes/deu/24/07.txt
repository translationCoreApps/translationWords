===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **If a man is found kidnapping** - This is an idiom for "If a man kidnaps." It can be stated in active form. AT: "If you find a man kidnapping" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **kidnapping** - using physical force to take an innocent person away from his home and imprison him
  * **any of his brothers from among the people of Israel** - "any of his fellow Israelites"
  * **that thief must die** - "then other Israelites should kill that thief as a punishment for what he did"
  * **and you will remove the evil from among you** - The adjective "evil" can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "and you must remove from among the Israelites the person who does this evil thing" or "and you must execute this evil person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
