===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This marks a new section of the letter. Here the author gives his final comments to his audience.
  * **brothers** - This refers to all the believers to whom he is writing whether male or female. AT: "fellow believers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **bear with the word of encouragement** - "patiently consider what I have just written to encourage you" (UDB)
  * **the word of encouragement** - Here "word" stands for a message. AT: "the encouraging message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **has been set free** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "is no longer in prison" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
