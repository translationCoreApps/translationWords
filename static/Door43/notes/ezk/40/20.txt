===== translationNotes: =====

  * **chambers** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:7]].
  * **portico** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]]. 
  * **measured the same** - "had the same measurement"
  * **fifty cubits** - about twenty-seven meters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **cubits** - Translate these "long" cubits as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **twenty-five cubits** - about 13.5 meters
  * **the main gate** - the gate that was on the eastern side of the temple
