===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeremiah took ... Jeremiah's dictation** - Jeremiah speaks of himself by name for reasons that are not clear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **Baruch wrote on it at Jeremiah's dictation** - The abstract noun "dictation" can be translated as a verb. See how similar words are translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:36:04|36:4]]. AT: "while Jeremiah was speaking, Baruch wrote on it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **burned by Jehoiakim king of Judah** - "that Jehoiakim king of Judah had burned in the fire"
  * **Furthermore, many other similar words were added to this scroll** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Furthermore, Jeremiah and Baruch added to this scroll many more words which were similar to the words that had been in the first scroll." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
