===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your protected ones** - "those whom you protect." This refers to the Israelites.
  * **the name of Israel will no longer be remembered** - Here "name" represents a reputation or the memory of someone. This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one will ever remember the Israelites existed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **They have schemed together with one strategy** - "Your enemies have agreed together with one plan"
  * **against you they have made an alliance** - "they have joined together against you"
