===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This message** - This refers to God giving the believers the Holy Spirit through Jesus in [[:en:bible:notes:tit:03:06|3:7]].
  * **may be intent on the good works** - "may seek to do the good works"
  * **that he put before them** - Paul speaks about good deeds as if they were objects that God could place in front of people. AT: "that God prepared for them to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
