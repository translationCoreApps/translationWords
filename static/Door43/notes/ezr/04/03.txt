===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jeshua** - man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:03|2:06]].
  * **It is not you, but we who must build** - Possible meanings are 1) the Jewish leader felt that Cyrus had authorized only them to build the temple or 2) building the temple was the exclusive work of the Jews and no non-Jew would be permitted to contribute to the work.
