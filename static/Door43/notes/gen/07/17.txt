===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Verses 13-18 repeat for a third time and give more detail about how Noah went into the ark with his family and the animals in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:07:01|7:1-5]]. This is not a new event.

  * **and the water increased** - This happened during the forty days while the water kept coming. "and the water became very deep"
  * **and lifted up the ark** - "and it caused the ark to float"
  * **It rose up from upon the earth** - "The ark rose up high over the ground" or "The ark floated on top of the deep water"
  * **The water came in torrents** - "The water came very fast and powerfully"
