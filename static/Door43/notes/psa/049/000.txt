====== Psalm 049 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 49 is a wisdom psalm. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:wise]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Riches ==
Riches do not last forever. They can not save a person from death and they can not be taken from a person when they die. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:forever]] and [[:en:obe:kt:save]])
