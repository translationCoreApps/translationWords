===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pilate said to them** - "Pilate said to the crowd"
  * **satisfy** - "please"
  * **He scourged Jesus** - Pilate did not actually scourge Jesus but rather his soldiers did. 
  * **scourged** - "flogged." This means to beat with an especially painful whip.
  * **then handed him over to be crucified** - Pilate told his soliders to take Jesus away to crucify him. This can be stated in active form. AT: "told his soldiers to take him away and crucify him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
