===== translationNotes: =====

  * **at the word of the king that had been brought to her by the officials** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when the king's officials told her about his command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **at the word** - Here "word" refers to the entire command. AT: "at the command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **his rage burned within him** - The intensity of the king's anger is spoken of as if it was a fire that burned inside him. AT: "his rage was as intense as a fire inside him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
