====== Isaiah 18 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of chapter 18, which is an important quotation.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Cush ==
This chapter records prophecies against the nation of Cush. They are being punished by Yahweh. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphors ==
This chapter uses many metaphors to describe the destruction coming to the people of Cush. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
