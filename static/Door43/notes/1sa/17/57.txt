===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in his hand** - Here "his" refers to David.
  * **Whose son are you** - "Who is your father"
  * **I am the son of your servant Jesse the Bethlehemite** - "My father is your servant Jesse, the Bethlehemite"
  * **your servant Jesse** - David referred to his father as "your servant" to show that his father was faithful to King Saul.
