===== translationNotes: =====

  * **she will be unclean** - A woman whom other people must not touch because she is bleeding from her womb is spoken of as if she were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **during the days of her monthly period** - This refers to the time of the month when a woman bleeds from her womb. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **the flesh of a baby boy's foreskin must be circumcised** - Only the priest could perform this action. This can be translated in active form. AT: "a priest must circumcise the baby boy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
