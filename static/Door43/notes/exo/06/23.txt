===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nadab ... Ithamar** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Phinehas** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **These were the heads of the fathers' houses** - The word "heads" here represents family leaders. AT: "These were the leaders of the families" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
