====== Psalm 101 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 101 is a psalm asking God's help in living as a person should.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Encouragement ==
The psalmist wanted to encourage all the good people in the land and restrain all the evil people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])
