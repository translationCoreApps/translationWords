===== translationNotes: =====

  * **For I received from the Lord what I also passed on to you, that the Lord** - "For it was from the Lord that I heard what I told you, and it was this: the Lord"
  * **on the night when he was betrayed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "on the night that Judas Iscariot betrayed him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he broke it** - "he pulled pieces from it"
  * **This is my body** - "The bread I am holding is my body"
