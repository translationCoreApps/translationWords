===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Jether** - This is the name of Gideon's son. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **For as the man is, so is his strength** - This is an idiom. AT: "It is a job for a man to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **crescent** - This is a curved shape with two points. This shape occurs when the moon is mostly covered in shadow.
  * **ornaments** - "decorations"
