===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God who confirms us with you** - Possible meanings are 1) "God who confirms our relationship with each other because we are in Christ" or 2) "God who confirms both our and your relationship with Christ."
  * **he commissioned us** - Possible meanings are 1) "he commissioned us to preach the gospel" (UDB) or 2) "he anointed us to be his people."
  * **He set his seal on us** - This means that God has marked believers as his own possession. AT: "He marked us as his own possession" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **gave us the Spirit in our hearts** - Here the word "hearts" refers to the innermost part of a person. AT: "gave us the Spirit to live within each of us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the Spirit ... as a guarantee** - The Spirit is spoken of as if he was a partial downpayment toward eternal life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
