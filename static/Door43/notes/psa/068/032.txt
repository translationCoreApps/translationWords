===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you kingdoms of the earth** - Here "kingdoms" refers to the citizens of the kingdoms. AT: "you people who are citizens of kingdoms all over the world" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **To him who rides on the heaven of heavens** - The word "Sing" is implied as the beginning of this verse. Here God is described as if he rides in a chariot in the sky. AT: "Sing to God who rides across the sky in his chariot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he lifts up his voice with power** - This idiom means he speaks powerfully. AT: "he shouts powerfully" or "he speaks loudly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
