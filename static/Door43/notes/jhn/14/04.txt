===== translationNotes: =====

  * **how can we know the way?** - "how can we know how to get there?"
  * **the way** - This is a metaphor that has these possible meanings 1) "the way to God" or 2) "the one who takes people to God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the truth** - This is a metaphor that has these possible meanings 1) "the true person" or 2) "the one who speaks true words about God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the life** - This is a metaphor that means Jesus can give life to people. AT: "the one who can make people alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **no one comes to the Father except through me** - People can come to God and live with him only by trusting Jesus. AT: "No one can come to the Father and live with him unless he comes through me." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
