===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hannah recites a song to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **My heart exults** - "I have great joy"
  * **in Yahweh** - "because of who Yahweh is" or "because Yahweh is so great"
  * **My horn is exalted** - A horn is a symbol of strength. "I am now strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
