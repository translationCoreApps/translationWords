===== translationNotes: =====

  * **signet ring** - a special ring that could be used to imprint the king's official seal on a proclamation
  * **I will see that the money is given back to you** - The meaning of this phrase is not clear. Possible meanings are 1) "I will return the money to you" or 2) "Take the money and give it to the men just as you have said." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
