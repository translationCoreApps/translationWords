===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This ends Paul's time in Caesarea.
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "we" refers to Luke, Paul and those traveling with them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive "We"]])
  * **General Information:** - The word "They" refers to some of the disciples from Caesarea. 

  * **They brought with them a man** - "Among them was a man"
  * **Mnason, a man from Cyprus** - Mnason is a man from the island of Cyprus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **an early disciple** - This means Mnason was one of the first to believe in Jesus.
