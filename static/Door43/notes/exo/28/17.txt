===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The colors of the precious stones mentioned here may be different from those in the UDB.

  * **precious stones** - "valuable gems" or "treasured gems." See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:03|25:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **ruby ... jasper** - These are precious stones. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **sapphire** - This is a gemstone that is blue in color. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:24:09|24:10]].
  * **onyx** - This is a valuable stone that has layers of white and black, red or brown. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:03|25:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **They must be mounted in gold settings** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must mount them in gold settings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
