===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Paul makes closing remarks to the believers at Thessalonica.

  * **May the Lord of peace himself give you** - You can make explicit that this is Paul's prayer for the Thessalonians. AT: "I pray that the Lord of peace himself gives you ... " (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the Lord of peace himself** - Here "himself" emphasizes that the Lord will personally give peace to believers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **This is my greeting, Paul, with my own hand** - "I, Paul, write this greeting with my own hand"
  * **This is how I write** - Paul makes it clear that this letter is from him and is not a forgery.
