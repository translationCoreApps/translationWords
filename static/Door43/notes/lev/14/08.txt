===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh tells Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone is cleansed of a skin disease.

  * **The person who is being cleansed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "The person whom the priest is cleansing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **then he will be clean** - The person whom other people may touch is spoken of as if he were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
