===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I truly know that this is so** - "I know that what you say is true"
  * **this is so** - Here the word "this" refers to what Bildad said.
  * **how can a person be in the right with God?** - "how can anyone be innocent before God?"
  * **argue** - "dispute" 
  * **he cannot answer him once in a thousand times** - "Once in a thousand times" is an idiom that means "at all." Possible meanings are 1) "he cannot give any answer to God" or 2) "God will not answer him at all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a thousand times** - "1,000 times"
