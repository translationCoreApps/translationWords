===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Peter completes his speech to the Jewish religious rulers that he began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:04:08|4:8]].
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "we" refers to Peter as well as those to whom he is speaking. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])

  * **Jesus Christ is the stone ... which has been made the head cornerstone** - Peter is quoting from the Psalms. This is a metaphor that means the religious leaders, like builders, rejected Jesus, but God will made him the most important in his kingdom, as a cornerstone in a building is important. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **head** - Here the word "head" means "most important" or "vital."
  * **you as builders despised** - "you as builders rejected" or "you as builders rejected as worth nothing"
  * **There is no salvation in any other person** - This can be stated positively. AT: "He is the only person who is able to save" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **no other name under heaven, that is given among men** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no other name under heaven that God has given among men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **no other name under heaven, that is given among men, by which** - The phrase "name ... that is given among men" refers to the person of Jesus. AT: "no other person under heaven, who is given among men, by whom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **under heaven** - This is a way of referring to everywhere in the world. AT: "in the world" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **by which we must be saved** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "which can save us" or "who can save us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
