===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gideon made an ephod out of the earrings** - "Gideon used the gold from the earrings to make an ephod"
  * **Ophrah** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **all Israel prostituted themselves by worshiping it there** - This speaks of worshiping a false god as if it were prostitution. AT: "the Israelites sinned against Yahweh by worshiping the ephod there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all Israel** - Here "all" is an exaggeration to emphasize that very many worshiped the garment. AT: "very many people in Israel worshiped the garment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **It became a trap for Gideon and for those in his house** - This speaks of Gideon and his family being tempted to worship the ephod as if the ephod were a hunter's snare that would trap them. AT: "It became a temptation for Gideon and his family" or "Gideon and his family sinned by worshiping it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **for those in his house** - Here "his house" represents Gideon's family. AT: "for his family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **So Midian was subdued before the people of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "So Yahweh subdued the Midianites before the people of Israel" or "So Yahweh helped the Israelites defeat the Midianites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they did not raise their heads up again** - This is an idiom. AT: "they did not attack Israel again" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **So the land had peace** - Here "land" represents the people of Israel. AT: "So the Israelites lived peacefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **in the days of Gideon** - Here "the days" refers to his lifetime. AT: "during the life of Gideon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
