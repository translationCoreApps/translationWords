====== Psalm 086 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 86 is a deliverance psalm. He is praying for deliverance from his many enemies. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's help ==
God has blessed the psalmist and is so loving to him. He has been one of God's servants. Now he needs God to save him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]], [[:en:obe:kt:love]] and [[:en:obe:kt:save]])
