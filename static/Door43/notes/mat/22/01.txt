===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - To rebuke the religious leaders and to illustrate their unbelief, Jesus tells a parable about a marriage feast. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **to them** - "to the people"
  * **The kingdom of heaven is like** - This is the beginning of a parable. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:13:24|13:24]]. 
  * **those who had been invited** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the people the king had invited" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
