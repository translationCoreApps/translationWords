===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer stops telling the story and gives background information so the reader can understand what happens next. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **you will anoint him to be prince** - The term prince is used here instead of king. This is the man whom God has chosen to be king of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **the land of Benjamin** - "the land where people from the tribe of Benjamin live"
  * **from the hand of the Philistines** - Here the word "hand" is a metonym for control. AT: "from the control of the Philistines" or "so the Philistines will no longer control them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **For I have looked on my people with pity** - "My people are suffering and I want to help them"
