===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David seeks Yahweh's direction in dealing with the raid on his people.

  * **David prayed to Yahweh for direction** - The abstract noun "direction" can be translated as a clause. AT: "David prayed that Yahweh would tell David what David should do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **pursue** - "chase" or "follow"
