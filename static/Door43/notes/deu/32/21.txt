===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. He continues to quote Yahweh's words. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **They have made me jealous** - Here "me" refers to Yahweh.
  * **what is not god** - "what are false gods"
  * **worthless things** - You may need to make explicit what the "things" are. AT: "worthless idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **those who are not a people** - "people who do not belong to one people group"
  * **foolish nation** - Translate "foolish" as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:32:05|32:6]].
