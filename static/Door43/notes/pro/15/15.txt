===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All the days of oppressed people are miserable** - "Oppressed people are miserable all of their days"
  * **a cheerful heart has an unending feast** - Here the word "heart" represents the person. The writer speaks of a cheerful person enjoying life as if that person were celebrating a feast that does not end. AT: "the cheerful person enjoys life, as if he were celebrating an unending feast" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **an unending feast** - "a feast that never ends"
  * **confusion** - "anxiety"
