===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Look, you** - "Pay attention to what I am going to tell you. You"
  * **you will sleep with your fathers** - This is a polite way to say "you will die." AT: "you will die and join your ancestors who have died before you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **will rise up and act like a prostitute** - Being a prostitute is a metaphor for worshiping gods other than Yahweh. AT: "will start to be unfaithful to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
