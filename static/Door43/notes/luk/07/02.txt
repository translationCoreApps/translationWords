===== translationNotes: =====


  * **who was highly regarded by him** - "whom the centurion valued" or "whom he respected"
  * **asked him earnestly** - "pleaded with him" or "begged him"
  * **He is worthy** - "The centurion is worthy"
  * **our nation** - "our people." This refers to the Jewish people.
