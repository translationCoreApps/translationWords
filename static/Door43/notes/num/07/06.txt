===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gershon ... Merari** - Translate these men's names the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:17|3:17]].
  * **because of what their work needed** - "because it was what they needed to do their work"
  * **in the care of Ithamar son of Aaron the priest** - "under the supervision of Ithamar son of Aaron the priest" or "Ithamar son of Aaron the priest oversaw their work" 
  * **Ithamar** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:01|1:2]].
  * **He did this because** - The word "he" refers to Moses.
  * **because of what their work required** - "because it was what they needed to do their work"
