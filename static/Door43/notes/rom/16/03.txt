===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Priscilla and Aquila** - Priscilla was the wife of Aquila. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **my fellow workers in Christ Jesus** - Paul's "fellow workers" are people who also tell others about Jesus. AT: "who work with me to tell people about Christ Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Greet the church that is in their house** - "Greet the believers who meet in their house to worship"
  * **Epaenetus** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **firstfruit of Asia to Christ** - Paul speaks of Epaenetus as if he were a fruit that he harvested. AT: "first person in Asia to believe in Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
