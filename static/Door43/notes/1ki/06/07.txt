===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the house** - Here "house" refers to God's house, the temple.
  * **stones prepared at the quarry** - A quarry is where people cut large stones from a mountain and shape them with tools so they are smooth. After they prepared the stones at the quarry, they brought them to the temple.
  * **ground level ... middle level ... third level** - There were a total of three floors. Some languages call these "ground floor," "first floor," and "second floor."
