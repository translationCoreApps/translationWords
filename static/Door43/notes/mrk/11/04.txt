===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They went** - "The two disciples went"
  * **colt** - This refers to a young donkey that is large enough to carry a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mrk:11:01|11:2]].
  * **They spoke** - "They responded"
  * **as Jesus told them** - "as Jesus had told them to respond." This refers to how Jesus had told them to respond to people's questions about taking the colt.
  * **let them go their way** - This means that they allowed them to continue doing what they were doing. AT: "let them take the donkey with them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
