===== translationNotes: =====

  * **If this report reaches the governor** - "If the governor hears that you were asleep when Jesus' disciples took his body"
  * **the governor** - "Pilate" ([[:en:bible:notes:mat:27:01|27:2]])
  * **we will persuade him and take any worries away from you** - "do not worry. We will talk to him so that he does not punish you."
  * **did as they had been instructed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "did what the priests had told them to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **This report spread widely among the Jews and continues even today** - "Many Jews heard this report and continue to tell others about it even today"
  * **even today** - This refers to the time Matthew wrote the book.
