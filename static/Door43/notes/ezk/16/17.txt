===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem as if the city were his unfaithful wife. The city represents the people who live there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

  * **male figures** - "statues of men" or "idols that look like men"
  * **you did with them as a prostitute would do** - Possible meanings are 1) this is a polite way to say the young woman was sleeping with the male figures. AT: "you slept with them" or 2) this is a metaphor in which Yahweh speaks of her worshiping the figures as if she were sleeping with them. AT: "you worshiped them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **made with fine flour, oil, and honey** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "bread that I made with fine flour, oil, and honey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **set before them** - "placed as an offering in front of them"
