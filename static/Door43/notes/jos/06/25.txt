===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She lives in Israel** - The word "She" refers to Rahab and represents her descendants. AT: "Her descendants live in Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to this day** - "now" or "even today." Rahab's descendants are still living in Israel as the original writer writes this story.
