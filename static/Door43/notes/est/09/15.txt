===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the fourteenth day of the month of Adar** - "Adar" is the name of the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourteenth day is near the beginning of March. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **provinces** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **seventy-five thousand** - "75,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
