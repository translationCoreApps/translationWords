===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues to teach his disciples as they walk to the Mount of Olives.
  * **General Information:** - In verse 31, Jesus quotes the prophet Zechariah to show that in order to fulfill prophecy, all of his disciples will leave him.

  * **hymn** - a song of praise to God
  * **fall away** - "leave me"
  * **for it is written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for the prophet Zechariah wrote long ago in the scriptures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I will strike** - Here "I" refers to God. It is implied that God will cause or allow people to harm and kill Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the shepherd ... sheep of the flock** - These are metaphors that refer to Jesus and the disciples. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the sheep of the flock will be scattered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they will scatter all the sheep of the flock" (UDB) or  "the sheep of the flock will run off in all directions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **after I am raised up** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "after God raises me up"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
