===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they asked each other, "What is this? A new teaching with authority?** - The people's response can be written with statements instead of rhetorical questions. AT: "they said to each other, "This is amazing! He gives a new teaching, and he speaks with authority!"(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **He even commands** - The word "He" refers to Jesus.
