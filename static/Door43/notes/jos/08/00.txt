=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sin brought defeat ==

Taking what was to be destroyed  was great sin. Because of their sin, Yahweh withheld victory from the Israelites. Sin brought defeat in battle. Because of their repentance, Yahweh brought victory to Israel in Ai. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]] and [[:en:obe:kt:repent]])

== Built an altar ==
Altars were commonly built in the ancient Near East to commemorate important events. Several altars are built  in the Book of Joshua.
