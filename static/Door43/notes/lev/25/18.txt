===== translationNotes: =====

  * **obey my decrees, keep my laws, and carry them out** - All of these phrases mean basically the same thing. They emphasize that the people must obey everything Yahweh says. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **you will eat your fill** - This means that they would eat enough until their stomach was full. AT: "you will eat till you are full" or "you will eat plenty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
