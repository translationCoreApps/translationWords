===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the Tishbite** - This is the name of a people group from Tishbe. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Tishbe** - This is the name of a town in the region of Gilead. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **As Yahweh, the God of Israel lives** - This phrase is an oath to emphasize that what he will say is true.
  * **before whom I stand** - This is an idiom that means "to serve." AT: "whom I serve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **dew** - drops of water that form on the plants during the night
