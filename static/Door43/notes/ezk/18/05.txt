===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has not eaten on the mountains** - People often worshiped and offered sacrifices to pagan gods on the mountains. It is implied that this person has not participated in these kinds of pagan rituals. AT: "he has not eaten meat sacrificed to idols on the mountains" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he has not lifted up his eyes to the idols** - The idiom "lifted up his eyes" refers to looking toward something. With reference to idols, this gesture represents worshiping or praying to idols. AT: "he has not worshiped the idols" or "he has not prayed to the idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **the house of Israel** - The word "house" is a metonym for the family that lives in the house, in this case the Israelites, the descendants of Jacob over many years. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:1]]. AT: "the Israelite people group" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **approached a woman during her monthly period** - It is implicit that he has not approached a woman in order to sleep with her. AT: "approached a woman to sleep with her during her monthly period" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
