===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh's hand was heavy upon** - "Yahweh severely judged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **tumors** - Possible meanings are 1) painful swelling under the skin or 2) hemorrhoids.
  * **both Ashdod and its territories** - The name of the town is a metonym for the people living in the town. "both the people of Ashdod and the people in the land surrounding Ashdod" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **realized** - "understood"
  * **ark of the God of Israel** - Possible meanings are 1) they were speaking the proper name of the God of Israel or 2) they believed that Israel worshiped one of many gods, "ark of the god of Israel."
