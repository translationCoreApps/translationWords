===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She went against the wall** - This was an attempt to escape from the angel of Yahweh in the road.
  * **She went** - Sometimes animals are referred to as "her" or "she." AT: "It went"
  * **pinned Balaam's foot against it** - "pushed Balaam's foot against it" or "hurt Balaam's foot against it"
