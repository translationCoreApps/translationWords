===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul reminds believers of the gifts that Christ gives believers to use in the church, which is the whole body of believers.
  * **General Information:** - The quote here is from a song that King David wrote.

  * **To each one of us has been given a gift** - This can be stated using an active form. AT: "God has given a gift to each one of us" or "God gave a gift to each believer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **When he ascended the heights** - "When Christ went up into heaven"
