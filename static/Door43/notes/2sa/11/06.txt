===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then David sent** - Here the word "sent" means that David sent a messenger. AT: "Then David sent a messenger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **how Joab was, how the army was doing, and how the war was going** - David was asking if Joab and the army were well and about the progress of the war. AT: "if Joab was well, if other soldiers were well, and how the war was progressing" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Go down to your house** - Possible meanings for the phrase "go down" are 1) Uriah's house was located at a lower elevation than the king's palace or 2) Uriah's house was of lesser importance than the king's palace. AT: "Go to your house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **wash your feet** - This phrase is a metonym for returning home to rest for the night after working all day. AT: "rest for the night" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the king sent a gift for Uriah** - David sent someone to bring the gift to Uriah. This can be stated clearly. AT: "the king sent someone to take a gift to Uriah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
