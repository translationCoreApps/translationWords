===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Israel was defeated** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they defeated Israel" The word "Israel" is a metonym for "the army of Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The ark of God was taken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The Philistines also took the ark of God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
