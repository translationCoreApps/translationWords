===== translationNotes: =====

  * **make himself unclean** - A person who is not acceptable for God's purposes is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **among his people** - "among the Israelites"
  * **virgin** - This can also be translated as "young woman"
