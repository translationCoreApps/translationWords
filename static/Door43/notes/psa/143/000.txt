====== Psalm 143 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 143 is a deliverance psalm. The author prays for deliverance from his enemies. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's help ==
The author prayed for help from Yahweh against his enemies because he is God's servant.
