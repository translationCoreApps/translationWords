===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all its utensils** - These are all the pots, pans, shovels, and forks used at the altar.
  * **the washbasin** - This is a bronze basin that was located between the altar and the tabernacle.
  * **its base** - This is a bronze stand on which was placed the washbasin.
