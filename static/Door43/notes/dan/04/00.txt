====== Daniel 04 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 4:3, 34-35, which is are poetic.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The king becomes insane ==

The king went insane until he realized that Yahweh was the ruler over everyone, including him.
