===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **A hand touched me** - This implies metonymy, where a person's hand represents that person, probably the man whom Daniel saw in [[:en:bible:notes:dan:10:04|10:5-6]]. AT: "Someone touched me with his hand" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Daniel, man greatly treasured** - This can also be stated in active form. AT: "Daniel, you whom God greatly treasures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **greatly treasured** - much valued and loved
