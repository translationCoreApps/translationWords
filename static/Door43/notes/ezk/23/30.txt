===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues his metaphor in which he speaks of the cities of Jerusalem and Samaria as if they were two prostitutes. This metaphor continues through [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:23:33|23:34]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **These things will be done to you in your acting** - "These things will be done to you because you have acted." This can be stated in active form. AT: "These things will happen to you because you have acted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **became unclean with their idols** - She became unclean by worshiping the idols. AT: "became unclean by worshiping their idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **acting like a prostitute, lusting after nations** - The reason this phrase says Oholibah acts like a prostitute with the nations is because the men she has slept with are symbols of the wealth and power of these nations. AT: "acting like a prostitute, lusting after men of other nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **unclean** - A person who God considers spiritually unacceptable or defiled is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **so I will put her cup of punishment into your hand** - This refers to Oholibah's punishment that she will receive as if it were a cup of wine. AT: "so I will cause you to be punished in the same way as your sister" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
