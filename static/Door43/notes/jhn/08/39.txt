===== translationNotes: =====

  * **father** - "forefather"
  * **Abraham did not do this** - "Abraham never tried to kill anyone who told him the true revelation from God"
  * **You do the works of your father** - Jesus implies that their father is the devil. AT: "No! You are doing the things that your real father did" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **We were not born in sexual immorality** - Here the Jewish leaders imply that Jesus does not know who his real father is. AT: "We do not know about you, but we are not illegitimate children" (UDB) or "We were all born from proper marriages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **we have one Father: God** - Here the Jewish leaders claim God as their spiritual Father. This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
