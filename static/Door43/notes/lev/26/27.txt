===== translationNotes: =====

  * **If you do not listen to me** - Listening represents obeying what he has said. AT: "If you do not obey me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to walk against me** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against someone represents opposing him or fighting against him. AT: "to oppose me" or "to fight against me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will walk against you** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against someone represents opposing him or fighting against him. AT: "I will oppose you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will punish you even seven more times as much** - Here "seven more times" is not literal. It means Yahweh will increase the severity of his punishment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
