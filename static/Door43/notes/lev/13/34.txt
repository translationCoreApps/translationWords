===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone has a skin disease."

  * **the disease** - Here "the disease" refers to the disease on the person's head or chin.
  * **the priest must pronounce him clean ... he will be clean** - The man whom other people may touch is spoken of as if he were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
