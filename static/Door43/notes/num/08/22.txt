===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to do their service** - The word "service," an abstract noun, can be expressed as a verb. AT: "to serve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **before Aaron and before Aaron's sons** - "in the presence of Aaron and his sons" 
  * **This was** - "What they did was"
  * **They treated all the Levites in this way** - "They did to the Levites what Yahweh commanded." The word "They" refers to the people of Israel.
