===== translationNotes: =====

  * **conspired against Nadab** - "secretly plotted to kill King Nadab"
  * **Gibbethon** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Nadab and all Israel** - "The phrase "all Israel" is a generalization that represents the  many soldiers of Israel. AT: "Nadab and the many soldiers of Israel" or "Nadab and the army of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **were laying siege to Gibbethon** - "were surrounding Gibbethon, so that the people of Gibbethon would surrender to them"
  * **became king in his place** - The phrase "in his place" is a metaphor meaning "instead of him." AT: "became king instead of Nadab" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
