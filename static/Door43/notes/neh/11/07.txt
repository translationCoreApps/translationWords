===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the provincial officers who lived in Jerusalem. 

  * **These are the descendants** - "These are some of the descendants." Your language may need to specify that this is not a list of every descendant.
