===== translationWords: =====


===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This continues the decree that King Artaxerxes gave to Ezra.

  * **the objects that were freely given to you** - This can be expressed in active form. AT: "the objects that we have freely given to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Place the objects ... before him** - The word "him" refers to God. 
  * **for the service of the house of your God** - The abstract noun "service" can be expressed with the verbs "serve" or "use" "work" or "worship." AT: "to use in the house of your God" or "to serve in the house of your God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **treasury** - a secure place where money is stored
