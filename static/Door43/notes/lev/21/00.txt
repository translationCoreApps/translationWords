====== Leviticus 21 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Priests must be holy ==

Priests must be separate from the rest of the world. Priest should not do anything that would the unclean. They also cannot be unclean by birth or marry an unclean woman. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:priest]], [[:en:obe:kt:holy]] and [[:en:obe:kt:unclean]])
