===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Nehemiah is continuing to recount the number of people who returned from exile.

  * **the heads of ancestors' families** - AT: "heads of the clans" or "heads of the families"
  * **darics of gold** - A daric was a small gold coin that people in the Persian Empire used. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **basins** - large bowls
  * **garments** - items of clothing
  * **minas of silver** - A mina is 600 grams. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
