===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Make two silver trumpets** - This means that Yahweh commanded Moses to have someone make the trumpet. He did not make them himself. AT: "Tell someone to make two silver trumpets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **You must use the trumpets** - Moses will not blow the trumpets himself, but he will command the priests to blow them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
