===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You must bless the people of Israel** - "You" is plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **May Yahweh bless you and keep you** - Here "you" is singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **keep you** - This is an idiom. Here "keep" means to "protect." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
