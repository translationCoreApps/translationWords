===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The Levites continue their prayer before all the people.

  * **Ur of the Chaldees** - "Ur, where the Chaldean people group lived"
  * **You found his heart was faithful before you** - The heart, the inner being of the person, represents the person. AT: "You saw that he was completely faithful to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Canaanites ... Hittites ... Amorites ... Perizzites ... Jebusites ... Girgashites** - people group names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
