===== translationNotes: =====

  * **told him what Elijah said** - "Obadiah told Ahab what Elijah had told him to say"
  * **Is it you? You are the one who brings trouble to Israel!** - Ahab asks the question for emphasis to be certain about Elijah's identity. AT: "So here you are. You are the troublemaker of Israel!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
