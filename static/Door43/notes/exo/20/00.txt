====== Exodus 20 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The instructions recorded in this chapter are commonly known as the "ten commandments." 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Covenant ==
Yahweh's covenant faithfulness is now based on the covenant he made with Abraham as well as the covenant he is making with Moses. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:covenantfaith]] and [[:en:obe:kt:covenant]])
