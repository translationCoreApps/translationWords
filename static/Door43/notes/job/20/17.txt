===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Zophar continues speaking to Job.

  * **enjoy the streams, the torrents of honey and butter** - The wicked person will not enjoy the good things that God gives his people, even good food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **streams** - small rivers
  * **neglected** - "ignored"
