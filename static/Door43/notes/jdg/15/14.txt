===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When he came** - Samson was not travelling alone, he was being led by the men who had tied him with ropes. AT: "When they came" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Lehi** - This is the name of a town in Judah. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:15:09|15:9]].
  * **came on him with power** - The phrase "came on" means that Yahweh's Spirit influenced Samson. In this case, he made him very strong. AT: "made Samson very strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The ropes on his arms became like burnt flax** - Samson easily broken the ropes that bound his hands. The author describes how easily he broke the ropes by saying it was as if they had become burnt flax. AT: "He snapped the ropes on his arms as easily as if they had been stalks of burned flax" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **flax** - fibers from the flax plant used for making threads and cloth
