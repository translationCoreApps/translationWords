===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **You will write them on the doorposts of your house and on your city gates** - Translate these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:06:08|6:9]].
  * **that your days and the days of your children may be multiplied** - Here "days" represents a longer period of time. This can be stated in active form. AT: "that Yahweh may cause you and your children to live a long time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to your ancestors** - This refers to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.
  * **as long as the heavens are above the earth** - This compares how long the people could stay in the land to how high the sky is above the earth. This is a way of saying "forever." This can be stated as a new sentence. AT: "The people of Israel will live in the land forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
