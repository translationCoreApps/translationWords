===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh gives Joshua a series of commands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_imperative]])

  * **Be strong and courageous** - Yahweh commands Joshua overcome his fears with courage. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_imperative]])
  * **Do not turn from it to the right or to the left** - This can be stated as a positive command. AT: "Follow it exactly" or "Follow them exactly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_imperative]])
  * **be successful** - "achieve your goal" or "reach your goal"
