===== translationNotes: =====

  * **broken my heart** - This idiom means a person is extremely sad. AT: "offended me deeply" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I am full of heaviness** - The writer's great sadness is spoken of as if he was full of a heavy weight. AT: "I am heavy with sorrow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to take pity** - to feel sorrow or sadness
  * **They gave me poison for my food** - This is probably figurative. The   food that people gave to the writer was so bad that it tasted like poison. AT: "They gave me food that tasted like poison" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
