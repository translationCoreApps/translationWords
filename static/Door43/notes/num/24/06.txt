===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Balaam continues to prophesy under the control of the Spirit of God.

  * **Like valleys they spread out** - Balaam speaks of the Israelies as if they were numerous enough to cover entire valleys. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like gardens by the riverside** - Balaam speaks of the Israelies as if they were well-watered gardens that produce an abundant harvest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like aloes planted by Yahweh** - Aloes are plants with a pleasant smell that grow well even in dry conditions. Balaam speaks of the Israelies as if they would thrive and be pleasant like aloe plants. This can be stated in active form. AT: "aloes which Yahweh has planted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **like cedars beside the waters** - Cedar trees were the largest trees in Israel. Balaam speaks of the Israelies as if they grew as large as well-watered cedar trees. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
