===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the days of Joiakim these were the priests, the leaders of the families: Meraiah was the leader of Seraiah ... Joseph was the leader of Shebaniah** - This means there was a representative relative for each family.
  * **Joiakim ... Meraiah ... Seraiah, Hananiah ... Jeremiah, Meshullam ... Ezra, Jehohanan ... Amariah ... Jonathan ... Malluch ... Joseph ... Shebaniah** - These are male names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
