===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus tells a parable about a landowner who hires workers, to illustrate how God will reward those who belong to the kingdom of heaven. 

  * **For the kingdom of heaven is like** - This is the beginning of a parable. See how you translated the introduction to the parable in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:13:24|13:24]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])
  * **After he had agreed** - "After the landowner had agreed"
  * **one denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **he sent them into his vineyard** - "he sent them to work in his vineyard"
