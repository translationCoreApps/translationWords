===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The wilderness and the Arabah will be glad; and the desert will rejoice** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. Isaiah describes these places as a person who is glad because they see Yahweh's glory. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the glory of Lebanon will be given to it, the splendor of Carmel and Sharon** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will make the desert as beautiful as Lebanon, Carmel, and Sharon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the glory of Yahweh, the splendor of our God** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
