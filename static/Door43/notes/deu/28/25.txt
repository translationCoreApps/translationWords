===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **Yahweh will cause you to be struck down before your enemies** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will cause your enemies to strike you down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to be struck down before** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:28:07|28:7]].
  * **will flee before them seven ways** - This means the Israelites will be afraid and panic and run away from their enemies. See similar wording in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:28:07|28:7]]. AT: "you will flee in seven directions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seven ways** - This is an idiom. AT: "in many different directions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **You will be tossed to and fro among all the kingdoms** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The peoples of the other nations will drive you from one nation to another" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
