===== translationNotes: =====

  * **which I have given** - God had decided that the land of Canaan would belong to the people of Israel, but they had not yet moved into it. AT: "which I have decided to give" or "which I will soon give" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pastforfuture]]) 
  * **Each man must be a leader among them** - "Each man whom you send must be a leader among his tribe"
