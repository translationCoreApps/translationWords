===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Joshua gathered all the tribes of Israel** - Joshua's summons to the tribes is spoken of as if he gathered them together in a basket. AT: "Joshua asked all the tribes of Israel to meet with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **presented themselves before** - "came and stood in front of" or "came before"
  * **long ago** - "many years ago"
  * **This is what** - Joshua begins to quote what Yahweh had said previously. The quotation continues until the end of verse 13.
  * **Terah ... Nahor** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
