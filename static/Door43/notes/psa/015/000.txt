====== Psalm 015 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 15 is a wisdom psalm telling how people who honor God should live. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:wise]] and [[:en:obe:kt:life]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Godliness ==
This psalm outlines a person who is godly and sees others from a godly perspective. There are several good actions mentioned. Such a person treats his neighbor right, does not slander him, does not take a bribe or interest from him, and keeps his promises even when it is difficult to do so. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:godly]], [[:en:obe:kt:good]] and [[:en:obe:kt:promise]])

== Moral Conditions for Worship ==

Scholars have noted that this Psalm contains a list of ten different conditions for those who want to participate in worship at the temple. The list consists of three positives in verse 2, 3 negatives in verse 3, two positives in verse 4, followed by two negatives in verse 5. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])
