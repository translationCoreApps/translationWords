===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is a list of people who had returned to Israel from various Babylonian cities but could not prove their heritage. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **Tel Melah, Tel Charsha, Kerub, Addan, and Immer** - These are the names of places in Babylon that no longer exist. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Delaiah, Tobiah, and Nekoda** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
