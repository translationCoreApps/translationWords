===== translationNotes: =====

  * **How sweet are your words to my taste, yes, sweeter than honey to my mouth!** - The writer delighting in what God says is spoken of as if God's words were food that tasted sweet to the writer. AT: "Your words are good and delightful!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I gain discernment** - The abstract noun "discernment" can be stated as a verb. AT: "I learn to discern what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **therefore I hate every false way** - Evil behavior is spoken of as if it were a false way or road. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
