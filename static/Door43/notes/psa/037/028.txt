===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They are preserved forever** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will protect them forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will be cut off** - The destruction of the wicked is spoken of as if they were a branch of plant that was cut off and thrown away. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:037:008|37:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the righteous** - This refers to the people who are righteous. AT: "the righteous people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **will inherit the land** - The possession of the land is spoken of as if it had been received as an inheritance. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:037:008|37:9]]. AT: "will receive the land as their own possession" or "will be allowed to live safely in the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The mouth of the righteous person** - Here "mouth" represents the whole person who speaks. AT: "The righteous person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **speaks wisdom** - The abstract noun "wisdom" can be stated as an action. AT: "gives wise advice to others" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **increases justice** - The abstract noun "justice" can be stated as an action. AT: "encourages other people to live rightly" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
