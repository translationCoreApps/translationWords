===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - John begins to describe his vision of the new Jerusalem.

  * **I saw** - Here "I" refers to John.
  * **like a bride adorned for her husband** - This compares the new Jerusalem to a bride, who makes herself beautiful for her bridegroom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
