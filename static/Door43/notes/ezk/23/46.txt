===== translationNotes: =====

  * **raise up a company** - The phrase "raise up" is an idiom. AT: "gather a large group of people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **against them and give them** - "against Jerusalem and Samaria and give them" 
  * **give them up** - Yahweh gives up the responsibility to take care of them and allows them to suffer.
  * **to be terrorized and plundered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "for the company to terrorize and plunder them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cut them down** - This is an idiom. AT: "kill them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
