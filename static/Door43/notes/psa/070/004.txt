===== translationNotes: =====

  * **seek you** - Seeking God represents either 1) asking God for help or 2) thinking about God and obeying him. AT: "ask you for help" or "think about you and obey you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **rejoice and be glad** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize the intensity of gladness. AT: "rejoice greatly" or "be very glad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) 
  * **love your salvation** - The abstract noun "salvation" can be stated as an action. AT: "love you because you saved them" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **always say** - This is an exaggeration in order to emphasize the importance of praising God often. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **May God be praised** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Let everyone praise God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **poor and needy** - Here the words "poor" and "needy" mean basically the same thing and emphasize that he is unable to help himself. AT: "very needy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **hurry to me** - The writer speaks of God as if he is running to the writer in order to help him. AT: "come quickly to help me!" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you are my help and you rescue me** - Here the phrase "you rescue me" explains how God is his "help." AT: "you help me by rescuing me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **do not delay** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "please come quickly" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
