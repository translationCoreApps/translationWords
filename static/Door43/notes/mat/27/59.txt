===== translationNotes: =====

  * **linen** - a fine, costly cloth
  * **that he had cut into the rock** - It is implied that Joseph had workers who cut the tomb into the rock. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Then he rolled a large stone** - Most likely Joseph had other people there to help him roll the stone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **opposite the tomb** - "across from the tomb"
