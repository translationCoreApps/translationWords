===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **You must not lend on interest to your fellow Israelite** - "If you lend something to your fellow Israelite, you must not make him pay you back more than he borrowed"
  * **lend on interest** - to lend to somebody and force that person to pay back more than was lent
  * **interest of money ... anything that is lent on interest** - "you must not charge interest when you lend somebody money, food, or anything else"
  * **all that you put your hand to** - an idiom for "all that you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
