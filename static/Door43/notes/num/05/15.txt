===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a tenth** - This is one part out of ten equal parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
  * **a tenth of an ephah** - This can be written in modern measurements. AT: "a tenth of an ephah (which is about 2 liters)" or "2 liters " (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **a grain offering of jealousy** - "a grain offering for jealousy" 
  * **indicator of sin** - An "indicator" is something that shows evidence of something. In this case, he made the offering to determine whether his wife had committed adultery or not.
