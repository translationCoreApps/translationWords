===== translationNotes: =====

  * **revealed themselves to the garrison of the Philistines** - "allowed the Philistine soldiers to see them"
  * **the garrison** - the army camp
  * **coming out of the holes where they have hidden themselves** - The Philistines implied that the Hebrews had been hiding in holes in the ground like animals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **we will show you something** - This is an idiom that means "we will teach you a lesson." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **has given them into the hand of Israel** - Here "hand" refers to power to defeat the Philistines. AT: "will enable Israel to defeat them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
