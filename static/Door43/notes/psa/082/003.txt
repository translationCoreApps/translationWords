===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the poor and fatherless** - Here "the poor" and "fatherless" are nominal adjectives. They can be stated as adjectives. AT: "those who are poor and those who are orphans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **maintain the rights of the** - "do what is right for the "
  * **the afflicted and destitute** - Here "the afflicted" and "destitute" are nominal adjectives. They can be stated as adjectives. AT: "those who are suffering and those who have nothing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **the poor and needy ... the wicked** - These are all nominal adjectives. They can be stated as adjectives. AT: "those who are poor and those who are needy ... those who are wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **take them out of the hand of the wicked** - Here the word "hand" represents power or control. AT: "stop the wicked from harming them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
