===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Bezalel's work crew continues to make the priestly garments.

  * **as Yahweh had commanded Moses** - "just as Yahweh told Moses to do." See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:39:01|39:01]].
  * **it was made of one piece with the ephod, made of fine twined linen** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they made it as one piece with the ephod with fine twisted linen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
