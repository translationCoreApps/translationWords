===== translationNotes: =====

  * **rejoice with great joy** - "rejoice greatly"
  * **So the joy of Jerusalem could be heard far away** - "Jerusalem" is a metonym for "the sound that the people of Jerusalem made." This can be translated in active form. AT: "people far away from Jerusalem could hear the sound that the people of Jerusalem made as they celebrated" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
