===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **infectious diseases, with fever, with inflammation** - These all refer to diseases that cause people to grow weak and die. "diseases and burning fevers that will make you weak"
  * **with drought** - "with lack of rain"
  * **mildew** - mold that grows on crops and causes them to rot
  * **These will pursue you** - Moses speaks of the bad things that would happen to the Israelitesmas if they were people or animals who would chase after the Israelites. AT: "You will suffer from them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
