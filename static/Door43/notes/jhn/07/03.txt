===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brothers** - This refers to the actual younger brothers of Jesus, the sons of Mary and Joseph.
  * **the works that you do** - The word "works" refers to the miracles that Jesus had performed.
  * **he himself** - The word "himself" is a reflexive pronoun that emphasizes the word "he." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
  * **the world** - Here "the world" is a metonym for all of the people in the world. AT: "all people" or "everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
