===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Behold!** - AT: "Look!" or "Listen!" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you!"
  * **I am bringing Nebuchadnezzar** - God is using Nebuchadnezzar to bring about his will.
  * **king of kings** - "most powerful king"
  * **a great many people** - This exclamation emphasizes the great size of Nebuchadnezzar's army.
  * **He will kill your daughters** - Here the word "He" refers to Nebuchadnezzar and is a metonym for is army. AT: "His army will kill your daughters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
