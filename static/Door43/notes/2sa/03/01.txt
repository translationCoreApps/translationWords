===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here Samuel gives information about the war between David and the supporters of Saul. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **house** - Here "house" is used to mean "supporters." 
  * **grew stronger and stronger** - This metaphor means the number of people supporting David increased. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **grew weaker and weaker** - This metaphor means the number of people supporting Saul's family decreased. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
