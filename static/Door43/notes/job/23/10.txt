===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Job continues speaking.

  * **he knows the way that I take** - Job's actions are spoken of as a path that he walks. AT: "God knows what I do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will come out like gold** - Job believes that the test will prove that he is as pure as refined gold. AT: "he will see that I am as pure as gold whose impurities have all been burned out" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **My foot has held fast to his steps** - Here "My foot" refer to Job AT: "I have followed the path he has shown me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I have kept to his way** - Job's obedience is spoken of as if he was walking in a path that God showed him. AT: "I have done what he told me to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **turned not aside** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "followed it precisely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **I have not gone back from** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "obeyed what he commanded" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **of his lips** - This phrase refers to the message that God spoke. AT: "that he spoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I have treasured up in my heart** - Here "my heart" refers to Job's inner being. AT: I have stored in my inner being" or "I constantly think of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the words of his mouth** - This refers to God's speech. AT: "what he said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
