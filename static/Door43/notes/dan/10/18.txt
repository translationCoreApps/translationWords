===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the one with an appearance of a man** - "the one who looked like a human"
  * **Be strong now, be strong** - The words "be strong" are repeated for emphasis.
  * **man greatly treasured** - This can also be stated in active form. AT: "you whom God greatly treasures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I was strengthened** - This can also be stated in active form. AT: "I became strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
