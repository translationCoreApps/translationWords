===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Any meat that touches an unclean thing must not be eaten** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "No one may eat meat that touches something unclean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **an unclean thing** - Something that Yahweh has stated is unfit to touch or eat is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **It must be burned** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "You must burn it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **anyone who is clean** - A person who is acceptable for God's purposes is spoken of as if the person were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **unclean person** - A person who is not acceptable for God's purposes is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that person must be cut off from his people** - A person being excluded from his community is spoken of as if he had been cut off from his people, as one would cut a piece of cloth or cut a branch from a tree. This can be translated in active form. AT: "that person may no longer live among his people" or "you must separate that person from his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
