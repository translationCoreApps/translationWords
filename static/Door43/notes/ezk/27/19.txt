===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **General Information:** - The words "you" and "your" in these verses refer to Tyre.

  * **Dan ... Javan ... Uzzal ... Dedan ... Arabia ... Kedar** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Dan and Javan** - This is a metonym for the people of Dan and Javan. AT: "The people of Dan and Javan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **wrought iron** - a form of iron that can be shaped with a hammer
  * **cinnamon** - This is a type of spice that comes from the bark of a tree. "Cassia" is another name for this spice. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **calamus** - a type of grass that people used as perfume and for medicine (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **Dedan was** - This is a metonym for the people of Dedan. AT: "The people of Dedan were" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **saddle blankets** - A saddle blanket is a piece of cloth that people place on a horse beneath a saddle or seat.
  * **Arabia** - This is a metonym for the people of Arabia. AT: "The people of Arabia" or "The Arabians" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
