===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The person who composed this psalm continues to speak.

  * **The cords of death surrounded me** - The writer speaks of death as if it were a person who could capture him and bind him with ropes. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:018:004|18:4]]. AT: "I felt like I was about to die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the snares of Sheol confronted me** - The writer speaks of "Sheol," the place of the dead, as if it were a person who could trap him with snares. AT: "I felt as if I were ready to enter the grave" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **called on the name of Yahweh** - Here the word "name" represents Yahweh himself. AT: "called out to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **rescue my life** - Here the word "life" represents the person. AT: "rescue me" or "keep me from dying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
