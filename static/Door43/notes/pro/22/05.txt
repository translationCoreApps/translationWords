===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Thorns and snares lie in the path of the perverse** - The writer speaks of the way perverse people live as if it were a path on which the perverse will have trouble because of the natural "thorns" and man-made "snares." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **snares** - traps to catch animals
  * **the perverse** - This nominal adjective can be translated as a noun phrase. AT: "perverse people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **whoever guards his life** - A person doing what he needs to do so he can live a long time is spoken of as if that person were keeping thieves away from a physical object. AT: "people who want to live a long time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the way he should go** - How a person lives is spoken of as if it were a path on which he walks. AT: "how he should live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
