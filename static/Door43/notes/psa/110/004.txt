===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You are** - Yahweh is speaking to the lord.
  * **will not change** - This means that he will not change what he has said. AT: "will not change his mind" or "will not change what he has said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])   
  * **after the manner of Melchizedek** - The missing words may be added. AT: "after the manner that Melchizedek was priest" or "in the same way that Melchizedek was priest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
