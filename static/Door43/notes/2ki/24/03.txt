===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It was certainly at the mouth of Yahweh** - Some versions have, "It was certainly because of the wrath of Yahweh," which is just as good a reading of the original text. If translators have access to versions in major languages in their region, they should probably follow their choice.
  * **at the mouth of Yahweh** - Here "mouth" represents Yahweh's command. AT: "as Yahweh had commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])  
  * **remove them out of his sight** - "get rid of them" or "destroy them"
  * **innocent blood that he shed** - Blood is a metonym for innocent life, and shedding blood is a metonym for killing innocent people. AT: "innocent people whom he killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he filled Jerusalem with innocent blood** - Blood is a metonym for innocent life, and shedding blood is a metonym for killing innocent people. AT: "he killed many innocent people in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
