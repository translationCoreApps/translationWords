===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ben Hadad said** - It is understood that Ben Hadad is speaking to his soldiers. AT: "Ben Hadad said to his soldiers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Whether they have ... take them** - Here "they" and "them" refer to the Israelite army.
  * **So the young officers ... the army followed** - "The young Israelite officers ... the Israelite army followed"
