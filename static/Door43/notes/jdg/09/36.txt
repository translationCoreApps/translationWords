===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gaal** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|9:26]].
  * **Zebul** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:28|9:28]].
  * **You are seeing the shadows on the hills like they are men** - Zebul is trying to confuse Gaal and keep him from preparing for battle. AT: "That is not people, it is only shadows on the hills"
  * **one unit** - "1 group" or "1 troop" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
