===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He brought the Israelites out with silver and gold** - When the Israelites left Egypt they took silver and gold with them. AT: "He brought the Isrealites out of Egypt with silver and gold in their possession" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **none of his tribes stumbled on the way** - No one was left behind. This can be stated positively AT: "all of his tribes was able to make the journey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **Egypt was glad** - Here "Egypt" refers to the people who live in Egypt. AT: The people of Egypt were glad (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He spread a cloud for a covering** - Here the psalmist describes Yahweh placing a cloud in the sky as if he were spreading out a garment. The cloud was a "covering" to protect them from the sun. AT: "He placed a cloud in the sky to protect them from the sun and heat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **made a fire to light up the night** - Yahweh placed a pillar of fire in the sky to give light during the night. AT: "placed a fire it the sky to light up the night" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
