===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **A psalm of praise. Of David** - "This is a psalm of praise that David wrote."
  * **extol you** - "tell people how wonderful you are"
  * **bless your name** - The word "name" is a metonym for Yahweh himself. See how "may his glorious name be blessed" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:072:018|72:19]]. AT: "bless you" or "do what makes you happy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **praise your name** - The word "name" is a metonym for Yahweh himself. AT: "praise you" or "tell people how great you are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
