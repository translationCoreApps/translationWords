===== translationNotes: =====

  * **We have heard** - The word "we" refers to Yahweh. He often refers to himself as "we."
  * **a trembling voice of dread and not of peace** - AT: Possible meanings are 1)"people cry out in fear because there was no peace" or 2) "you cry out ... peace."
