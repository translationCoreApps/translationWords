===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the beginning of a new part of the story where Jesus begins to teach the crowds, using parables, about the kingdom of heaven.

  * **On that day** - These events happened on the same day as those in the previous chapter. (See: [[:en:obe:other: biblicaltimeday]])
  * **sat beside the sea** - It is implied that he sat down to teach the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **out of the house** - It is not mentioned at whose house Jesus was staying.
  * **so he got into a boat** - It is implied that Jesus got into a boat because it would make it easier to teach the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **a boat** - This was probably an open, wooden fishing boat with a sail. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
