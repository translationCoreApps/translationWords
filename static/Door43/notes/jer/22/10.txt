===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh has shifted from addressing the King of Judah and is now speaking to a general audience. 

  * **the one who is dead** - This is singular, and refers to King Josiah who had been killed.
  * **him who is about to go away** - This refers to King Jehoahaz who was taken into exile in Egypt.
  * **never return and see his native land** - "never come back and see the land of Israel again" or "never again see his homeland again"
