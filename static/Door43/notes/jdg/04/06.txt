===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer of Judges refers to men, a city, a mountain, and a river by their names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **Barak ... Abinoam** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Mount Tabor** - This is the name of a mountain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **ten thousand men** - "10,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **I will draw out** - Here "I" refers to God.
  * **draw out Sisera** - Here "Sisera" represents his army. AT: "draw out Sisera and his army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **draw out** - to cause people to come away from a safe location
  * **Sisera ... Jabin** - Translate these names the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01|4:2]].
  * **Kishon** - This is the name of a river. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
