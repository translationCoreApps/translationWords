===== translationNotes: =====


  * **followed in all the way that his father had walked in** - "walked in all the ways that his father walked in." The way a person lives is spoken of as if that person were walking on a path. AT: "lived completely the way his father had lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He abandoned Yahweh** - "He went away from Yahweh" or "He paid no more attention to Yahweh"
  * **conspired against him** - "made plans and worked together to harm him"
