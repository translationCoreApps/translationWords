===== translationNotes: =====

  * **What have I to do with you?** - AT: "I have nothing to do with you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **As Yahweh of hosts lives** - "As I know that Yahweh of hosts lives"
  * **before whom I stand** - AT: "and I am ready to serve him"
  * **were it not for the fact that I honor the presence of Jehoshaphat ... I would not pay any attention to you** - AT: "I pay attention to you ... only because I honor the presence of Jehoshaphat." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **I honor the presence of Jehoshaphat** - Elijah respects the man, King Jehoshaphat, not just that he is present.
  * **I would not pay any attention to you, or even look at you** - AT: "I would not have anything at all to do with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
