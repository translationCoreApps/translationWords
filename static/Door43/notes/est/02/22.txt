===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When the matter was revealed to Mordecai** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "When Mordecai learned about what they were planning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in the name of Mordecai** - This is an idiom. AT: "on behalf of Mordecai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **The report was investigated and confirmed, and both the men were hanged** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The king investigated and confirmed the report, and ordered his servants to hang both men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a gallows** - This was a structure used for killing people by tying one end of a rope around the top of the structure and the other end of the rope around their necks and hanging them from it. AT: "a frame for hanging people"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **This account was written** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They recorded this account" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
