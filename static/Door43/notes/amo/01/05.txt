===== translationNotes: =====

  * **cut off the man** - Here "cut off" means either "destroy" or "drive away." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the man who holds the scepter in Beth Eden** - Some modern versions interpret this to be the same man who lives in Biqat Aven. 
  * **the man who lives in Biqat Aven** - Some modern versions interpret this to mean, "The people who live in Biqat Aven." The versions that have "the man" usually interpret this expression to mean the king.
  * **Biqat Aven ... Beth Eden ... Kir** - names of cities (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Eden** - different from the Garden of Eden
  * **the man who holds the scepter** - the king or chief (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
