===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the tabernacle was set up** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "So the people set up the tabernacle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the first day of the first month** - This refers to exactly one year after God rescued his people from Egypt. This happens around the middle of March on Western calendars. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:40:01|40:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **in the second year** - This is the second year after Yahweh brought his people out of Egypt. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Moses set up** - Moses was the leader. The people helped him set up the tabernacle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **posts** - a strong piece of wood set upright and used as a support
