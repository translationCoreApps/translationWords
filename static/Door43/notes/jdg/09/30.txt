===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zebul** - Translate this name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:28|9:28]].
  * **heard the words of Gaal son of Ebed** - Here "words" represents what was said. AT: "heard what Gaal son of Ebed said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Gaal ... Ebed** - Translate these names as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:09:26|9:26]].
  * **his anger was kindled** - Becoming angry is spoken of as if a fire were starting. AT: "he became very angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in order to deceive** - Zebul is deceiving Gaal and the people of Shechem. AT: "secretly"
  * **they are stirring up the city against you** - This speaks of the people of the city becoming upset as if they were liquid in a pot moving around. AT: "they are persuading the people of the city to rebel against you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the city** - Here "city" represents the people of the city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
