===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They took up** - Possible meaning are 1) "The disciples took up" or 2) "The people took up." 
  * **broken pieces of bread, twelve baskets full** - "twelve baskets full of broken pieces of bread"
  * **twelve baskets** - "12 baskets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **five thousand men** - "5,000 men"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **There were five thousand men who ate the loaves** - The number of women and children was not counted. If it would not be understood that women and children were present, it can be made explicit. AT: "And there were five thousand men who ate the loaves. They did not even count the women and children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
