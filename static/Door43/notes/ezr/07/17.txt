===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The king desires to please God with the money they bring back.

  * **So buy in full** - The king authorizes the purchase of animals and food for sacrifices to God.
  * **Do with the rest ... whatever seems good to you ... to please your God** - The king grants Ezra freedom to use the money as he sees fit to please God.
