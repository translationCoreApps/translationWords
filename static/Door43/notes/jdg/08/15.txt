===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zebah and Zalmunna** - Translate these names as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:04|8:5]].
  * **Have you already conquered Zebah and Zalmunna?** - Gideon quotes the people of Succoth as using a question to mock him. AT: "You have not yet conquered Zebah and Zalmunna." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Gideon took ... he punished ... he pulled** - Here "Gideon" represents his soldiers. AT: "Gideon and his soldiers took ... they punished ... they pulled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **thorns and briers** - These are sharp, pointed pieces on vines or tree limbs that stick out and can cut people and animals. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:06|8:7]].
  * **Penuel** - Translate the name of this city as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:08:08|8:8]].
