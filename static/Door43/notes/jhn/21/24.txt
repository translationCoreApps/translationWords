===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of the Gospel of John. Here the author, the Apostle John, gives a closing comment about himself and what he has written in this book. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_endofstory]])

  * **the disciple** - "the disciple John"
  * **who testifies about these things** - Here "testifies" means that he personally sees something. AT: "who has seen all these things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **we know** - Here "we" refers to those who trust in Jesus. AT: "we who trust in Jesus know" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **If each one were written down** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "If someone wrote down all of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **even the world itself could not contain the books** - Jesus did many more miracles than what people could write about in many books. Here John uses exaggeration is used for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **the books that would be written** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "the books that people could write about what he did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
