===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Be glad in Yahweh** - Here "in Yahweh" refers to what Yahweh has done for them. "Be glad because of what Yahweh has done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you righteous** - This refers to people. AT: "you righteous people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **shout for joy** - "shout joyfully" or "shout because of joy"
  * **who are upright in heart** - Here "heart" refers to the person. AT: "people who are upright" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
