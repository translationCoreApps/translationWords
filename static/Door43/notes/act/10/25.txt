===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when Peter entered** - "when Peter entered the house"
  * **bowed down at his feet to worship him** - Although bowing was common in that culture, here Cornelius bowed to Peter as an act of worship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **Stand up! I too am a man** - This was a mild rebuke or correction to Cornelius not to worship Peter. AT: "Stop doing that! I am only a man, as you are"
