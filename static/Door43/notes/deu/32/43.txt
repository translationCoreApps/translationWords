===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the end of Moses' song. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Rejoice, you nations** - Moses addresses the peoples of all the nations as if they were there listening. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **for he will avenge the blood of his servants; he will render vengeance on his enemies** - Here "blood of his servants" represents the lives of his innocent servants who were killed. AT: "for he will take revenge on his enemies, who killed his servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
