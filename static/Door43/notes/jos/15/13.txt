===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Kiriath Arba ... Debir ... Kiriath Sepher** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Arba ... Anak ... Sheshai ... Ahiman ... Talmai** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the three sons of Anak: Sheshai, Ahiman and Talmai, descendants of Anak** - These names represent clans of people who were descendants of Sheshai, Ahiman, and Talmai. The words "sons" and "descendants" in this context mean the same thing. AT: "the three clans, Sheshai, Ahiman, and Talmai, who were descendants of Anak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **He went up from there against** - "He went up from there to fight against"
