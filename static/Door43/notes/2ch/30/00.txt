=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Hezekiah continues in this chapter.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Passover ==
Hezekiah invited everyone in Judah and in Israel to come to Jerusalem and celebrate the Passover. It had not been celebrated since King Solomon's day. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:passover]])
