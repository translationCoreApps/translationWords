  * **the descendants of Lot** - This means the people of the nations of Moab and Ammon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Selah** - This may be a musical term that tells people how to sing or play their instruments here. Some translations write the Hebrew word, and some translations do not include it.
