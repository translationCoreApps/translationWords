===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the narrator tells background information about Deborah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **Deborah** - This is the name of a woman (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Lappidoth** - This is the name of a man (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **judge** - God appointed judges to lead the Israelites in times of trouble. Often the judges rescued them from their enemies.
  * **palm of Deborah** - This tree was named after Deborah.
