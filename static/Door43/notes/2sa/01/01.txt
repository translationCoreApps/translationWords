===== translationNotes: =====

  * **On the third day** - "After three days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **with his clothes torn and with dirt on his head** - In this culture, tearing one's own clothes and putting dirt on one's head was an act of mourning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **he lay facedown on the ground and prostrated himself** - This was an act of showing submission to David, who was now the king of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
