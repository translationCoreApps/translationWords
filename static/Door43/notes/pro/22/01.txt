===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A good name is ... and favor is better** - These two lines say basically the same thing and are repeated to emphasize how important this is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **A good name is to be chosen over great riches** - "A person should choose a good reputation over great riches" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **have this in common** - "share the fact that"
