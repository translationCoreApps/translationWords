===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **By the rivers of Babylon** - "Next to one of the rivers near Babylon"
  * **we sat ... we thought ... we hung** - The writer does not include the readers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **On the poplars there** - Poplar trees do not grow in Israel. "Poplars" may here stand for all the trees in Babylon. AT: "On the trees in Babylon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
