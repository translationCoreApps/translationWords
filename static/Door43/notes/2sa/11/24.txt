===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their shooters shot** - "their shooters shot arrows"
  * **some of the king's servants were killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they killed some of the king's servants" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **your servant Uriah the Hittite was killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they killed your servant Uriah the Hittite" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **king's servants** - Here "servants" refers to soldiers, not slaves, because soldiers were servants of the king.
  * **for the sword devours one as well as another** - Here "the sword" refers to a person who kills someone with sword. Also, killing someone with a sword is spoken of as if the sword were "eating" the people. AT: "for one man can be killed by a sword the same as another man" or "for any man can die in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Make your battle even stronger** - "Fight even stronger"
