===== translationNotes: =====

  * **One of them** - "One of the Cretans" or "Someone from the people of Crete"
  * **a wise man of theirs** - Possible meanings are 1) a prophet or 2) a poet or philosopher or 3) a teacher.
  * **Cretans are unceasing liars** - "Cretans lie all the time." This is an exaggeration for many cretans habitually lie. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **bad and dangerous animals** - This metaphor compares the Cretans to dangerous beasts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **lazy bellies** - an idiom for people who do nothing but eat too much food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **so correct them severely** - "therefore tell them strongly that they are wrong"
  * **that they may be sound in the faith** - "so they will have a healthy faith" or "so their faith may be true"
