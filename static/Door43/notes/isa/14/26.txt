===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This may be Isaiah speaking or it may be Yahweh speaking

  * **this is the hand that is raised over all the nations** - Here "hand" refers to Yahweh's power. AT: "this is my power to punish all the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who will stop him?** - This question emphasizes that no one can stop Yahweh. AT: "there is no one who can stop him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Yahweh of hosts** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
