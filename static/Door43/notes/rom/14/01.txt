===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul encourages believers to remember that they are answerable to God.

  * **weak in faith** - This refers to those who felt guilty over eating and drinking certain things.
  * **without giving judgment about arguments** - "and do not condemn them for their opinions"
  * **One person has faith to eat anything** - Here "faith" refers to doing what a person believes God is telling him to do. 
  * **another who is weak eats only vegetables** - This describes a person who believes God does not want him to eat meat.
