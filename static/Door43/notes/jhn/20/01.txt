===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the third day after Jesus was buried. 

  * **first day of the week** - "Sunday"
  * **she saw the stone rolled away** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "she saw that someone had rolled away the stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **disciple whom Jesus loved** - This phrase appears to be the way that John refers to himself throughout his book. Here the word "love" refers to brotherly love or love for a friend or family member. 
  * **They took away the Lord out from the tomb** - Mary Magdalene thinks that someone has stolen the Lord's body. AT: "Someone has taken the Lord's body out of the tomb" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
