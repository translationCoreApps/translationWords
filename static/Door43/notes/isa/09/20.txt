===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They will grab food on the right hand ... on the left hand** - This expression means that the people will grab food wherever they can find it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **Each will even eat the flesh of his own arm** - Here "his own arm" means "the person who helps him," "his neighbor." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
