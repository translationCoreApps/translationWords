===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He said to them** - "Moses said to the people of Israel"
  * **Fix your mind on** - This is an idiom. AT: "Pay attention to" or "Think about" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I have witnessed to you** - Possible meanings are 1) "I have testified to you," referring to what Yahweh has said he will do to the Israelites if they disobey, or 2) "I have commanded you," referring to what Yahweh was commanding them to do.
  * **your children** - "your children and descendants"
  * **this is** - "this law is"
  * **no trivial matter** - This litotes can be stated positively. AT: "something very important" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **because it is your life** - "because you will live if you obey it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **prolong your days** - Long days are a metaphor for a long life. AT: "be able to live a long time." Translate these words as they are in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:04:25|4:26]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
