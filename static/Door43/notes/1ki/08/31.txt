===== translationNotes: =====

  * **is required to swear** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "someone requires him to swear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **upon his own head** - Here "head" refers to the whole person. AT: "on him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **to reward him for his righteousness** - "to give him what he deserves because he is innocent"
