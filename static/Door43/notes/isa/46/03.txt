===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Listen to me** - Here "me" refers to Yahweh.
  * **who have been carried by me from before your birth, carried from the womb** - Yahweh speaks of the nation of Israel as if it were a person, and of the nation's beginning as if it were its birth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **who have been carried by me** - Yahweh speaks of helping and rescuing the people of Israel as if he were carrying them. This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom I have carried" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **Even to your old age I am he, and until your hair is gray I will carry you** - Yahweh speaks of the nation of Israel becoming very old as if it were an old man with gray hair. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
