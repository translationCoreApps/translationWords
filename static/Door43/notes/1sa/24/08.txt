===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why do you listen to the men who say, 'See, David is seeking your harm?** - A rhetorical question containing the desired response. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "You should not listen to someone who says, 'See, David is seeking to harm you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
