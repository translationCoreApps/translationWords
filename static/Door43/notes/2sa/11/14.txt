===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sent it by the hand of Uriah** - The phrase "the hand of Uriah" refers to Uriah himself. AT: "sent Uriah himself to deliver it to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **very front of the most intense battle** - "very front of the battle line where the fighting is the worst" 
  * **withdraw from him** - "command the soldiers to back away from him"
  * **he may be hit and killed** - "he may be wounded and killed"
