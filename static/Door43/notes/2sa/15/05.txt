===== translationNotes: =====

  * **take hold of him and kiss him** - "greet him as a friend and as an equal" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **all Israel** - This metonym refers to the people of the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Absalom stole the hearts** - This sentence means Absalom convinced the men to be loyal to him rather than to David. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
