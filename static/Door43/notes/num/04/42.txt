===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The descendants of Merari were counted** - This can be stated in active form.  AT: "Moses and Aaron counted the descendants of Merari" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from thirty to fifty years old** - "from 30 to 50 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **everyone who would join the company** - Here the word "would" does not mean that the men "chose" to join the company but rather that they were "assigned" to the company. AT: "everyone who was assigned to join the company" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **join the company to serve in the tent of meeting** - The word "company" refers to the rest of the people working in the tent of meeting. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:04:01|4:3]].
  * **counted by their clans** - This can be stated in active form.  AT: "whom Moses and Aaron counted by their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
