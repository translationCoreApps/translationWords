===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ten parcels of land were assigned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua assigned ten parcels of land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ten parcels** - "Ten portions"
  * **received an inheritance** - The land is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "received land as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The land of Gilead was assigned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua assigned the land of Gilead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
