===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In these verses, Nehemiah lists the names of the priests who signed the sealed document. (See [[:en:bible:notes:neh:10:01|10:1]])

  * **Hattush** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:08|3:10]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shebaniah ... Meshullam** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:09:03|9:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Malluch ... Obadiah, Daniel, Ginnethon ... Abijah, Mijamin, Maaziah, Bilgai** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Harim** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:11|3:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Meremoth** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:03|3:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Baruch** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:20|3:20]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shemaiah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:28|3:29]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **These were the priests** - This refers to the previous list of men who signed the document. AT: "These were the names of the priests who signed the document" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
