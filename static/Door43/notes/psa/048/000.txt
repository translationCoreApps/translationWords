====== Psalm 048 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 48 is a psalm of praise for Jerusalem.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Jerusalem ==
Jerusalem has mighty defenses. Foreign nations will be amazed. It is something to remember and testify about. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:testimony]])

It is helpful to know that "Mount Zion" is often used to refer to the city of Jerusalem.
