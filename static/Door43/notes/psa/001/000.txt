
====== Psalm 001 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 1 is usually considered to be a "wisdom psalm," which gives advice to those who hear it read or sung. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:wise]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Blessed ==
A main theme in this psalm is what it means to be blessed. Here "blessed" refers to anyone who is well off because of a good relationship to God. The contrasting theme is that there are ungodly people, those who refuse to honor God. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]] and [[:en:obe:kt:ungodly]])

== People who opposed Yahweh ==

There are several words in this psalm for those who oppose God: "the wicked" (those who do evil), "sinners" (those who ignore God's law), and "mockers" (those who mock God and the righteous people). (See: [[:en::obe:kt:evil]], [[:en:obe:kt:sin]], [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses]] and [[:en:obe:kt:righteous]])
