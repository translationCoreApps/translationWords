===== translationNotes: =====

  * **spread out the earth above the waters** - The ancient Israelites thought that the earth's dry land was on top of the sea. AT: "placed the earth over the waters"
  * **great lights** - This is a reference to the sources of light for the earth, especially the sun and the moon. AT: "the sun and the moon and the stars"
