===== translationWords: =====
 

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **She said** - "Rebekah said" or "the young woman said"
  * **to him** - "to the servant"
  * **I am the daughter of Bethuel son of Milcah, whom she bore to Nahor** - "Bethuel is my father, and his parents are Milcah and Nahor"
  * **We have plenty of both straw and feed** - It is understood that the straw and feed are for the camels. You can make clear this understood information. AT: "We have plenty of straw and feed for the camels" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **for you to spend the night** - "for you to stay tonight" or "where you can stay for the night"
  * **for you** - Here "you" refers to the servant and those traveling with him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
