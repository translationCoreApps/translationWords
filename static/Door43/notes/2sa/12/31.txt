===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He brought out the people** - David did not bring out the people himself; he commanded his soldiers to bring them out. AT: "David commanded his soldiers to bring out the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **saws, iron picks, and axes** - These are tools to cut wood or break up the ground.
  * **brick kilns** - ovens where bricks are dried and hardened
  * **all the cities of the people of Ammon** - This refers to the people in the cities. AT: "all the people of the cites of Ammon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
