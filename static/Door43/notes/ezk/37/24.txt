===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel.

  * **David my servant** - Yahweh said "David" to refer to a descendant of David. See how you translated "my servant David" in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:34:22|34:23]]. AT: "A descendant of my servant David"(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **one shepherd over them** - This speaks of king ruling a people as if he were a shepherd leading sheep. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:34:22|34:23]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **over them** - "over the people of Israel"
  * **will walk according to my decrees** - This speaks of acting or behaving in a certain way as if it were a person walking. AT: "will live as I have commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **had stayed** - "had lived"
