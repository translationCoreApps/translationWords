===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When the mocker is punished** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "When someone punishes the mocker" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **naive** - "inexperienced, immature"
  * **the mocker** - "the person who mocks others"
  * **when the wise person is instructed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "when someone instructs the wise person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lays hold of knowledge** - Here knowledge is spoken of as if it were an object that someone could grasp and keep for himself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The righteous** - Possible meanings are 1) any righteous person or 2) "Yahweh the one who is righteous."
  * **watches** - "pays careful attention to"
  * **he brings wicked people to disaster** - Here disaster is spoken of as if it were a place that someone could be brought to. AT: "he destroys them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
