===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul advises the believers to have unity and humility and reminds them of Christ's example.

  * **If there is any encouragement in Christ** - "If Christ has encouraged you "
  * **if there is any comfort from his love** - "if his love has given you any comfort"
  * **if there is any fellowship of the Spirit** - "if you have fellowship with the Spirit"
  * **if there are any tender mercies and compassions** - "if you have experienced many of God's acts of tender mercy and compassion"
  * **make my joy full** - Paul speaks here of joy as if it were a liquid filling a container. AT: "cause me to rejoice greatly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
