===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **If any of your exiled people are in the farthest places** - "Even those of your exiled people who are in the farthest places"
  * **under the heavens** - "under the sky" or "on the earth"
