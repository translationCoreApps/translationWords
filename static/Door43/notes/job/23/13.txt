===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Job continues speaking.

  * **But he is one of a kind, who can turn him back?** - Job uses this question to emphasize that God is unique. AT: "But there is no one like him, and nobody can make him change his mind." or "But he alone is God, and no one can influence him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **What he desires, he does** - "He accomplishes whatever he wants to do"
  * **he carries out his decree against me** - "he is doing to me what he said he would do"
  * **there are many like them** - "he has many similar plans for me"
