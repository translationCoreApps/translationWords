===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **Yahweh is my light** - Here "light" represents life. AT: "Yahweh is the source of my life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **whom should I fear?** - This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should fear. AT: "I will not be afraid of anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Yahweh is my life's refuge** - This speaks about Yahweh as if he were a place where people can go for safety. AT: "Yahweh is the one who keeps me safe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **whom should I dread?** - This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should be afraid of. AT: "I will not dread anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
