===== translationNotes: =====

  * **two hundred** - 200 (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **loaves** - A loaf is like a cake. You may need to make explicit what the loaves consisted of. AT: "loaves of bread" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **already prepared** - "that someone had already cooked" or "ready for someone to cook"
  * **measures** - This is a generic term as none is given. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **parched grain** - "grain that someone had cooked"
  * **clusters of raisins** - "cakes of raisins" or "masses of raisins"
