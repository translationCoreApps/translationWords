===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer is speaking again.

  * **judge the earth** - Here "earth" represents the people. AT: "judge the people of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for you have an inheritance in all the nations** - "for all the nations are your inheritance." Yahweh taking all the people as his own and ruling over them is spoken of as if the nations were a possession that he inherited. AT: "for you rule over all the people of every nation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all the nations** - Here "nations" represents the people of the nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
