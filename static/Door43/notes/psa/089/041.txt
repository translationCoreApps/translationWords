===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to his neighbors** - Here "neighbors" means people of nearby nations.
  * **raised the right hand of his enemies** - Here "right hand" represents power. To "raise the right hand" means that Yahweh has made his enemies strong enough to defeat God's chosen king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **turn back the edge of his sword** - Here "sword" represents the king's power in battle. To turn the sword back represents making the king unable to win in battle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the edge of his sword** - Here "edge" represents the whole sword. AT: "his sword" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **have not made him stand when in battle** - Here "stand" represents being victorious in battle. AT: "You have not helped him to be victorious in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
