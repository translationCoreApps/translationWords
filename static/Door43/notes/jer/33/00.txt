== Covenant faithfulness ==
Despite the great punishment Judah is facing and Israel has undergone, there is hope. This hope is based in the covenant faithfulness of Yahweh. Ultimately, he will restore his people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:covenantfaith]], [[:en:obe:kt:hope]], [[:en:obe:kt:restore]] and [[:en:obe:kt:peopleofgod]])
