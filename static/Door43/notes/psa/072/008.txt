===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May he have dominion** - "May the king have dominion"
  * **from sea to sea, and from the River to the ends of the earth** - Both of these expressions refer to the whole earth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **from sea to sea** - from the Dead Sea and the Sea of Chinnereth in the east to the Mediterranean Sea in the west.
  * **the River** - "the Euphrates River," which the Israelites would travel to on land by going north
  * **the ends of the earth** - as far as people could travel on land by different routes to the south. The Israelites spoke of the earth as if it were a flat surface with ends. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **lick the dust** - This is a metaphor for extreme humiliation. AT: "do everything they can so he will allow them to live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Tarshish** - the name of a place (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **render** - "give"
  * **offer gifts** - "give gifts"
  * **Seba** - This is the name of a country. It is not the same country as Sheba. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
