===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

  * **for them to serve as holders for the crossbars** - "which will hold the crossbars" or "because they will hold the crossbars"
  * **crossbars** - These are horizontal support beams that give stability to the structure. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:26|26:26]].
  * **you were shown on the mountain** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that I have shown you here on this mountain" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
