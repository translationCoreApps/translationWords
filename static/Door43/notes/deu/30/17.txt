===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the Israelites. as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **if your heart turns away ... but instead are drawn away and bow down to other gods and worship them** - Here "heart" refers to the whole person. This can be stated in active form. AT: "if you stop being loyal to God ... and the people living in the land convince you to bow down and worship other gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to you today that you ... you will not prolong your days** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as a group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **prolong your days** - Long days are a metaphor for a long life. AT: "be able to live a long time" Translate these words as they are in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:04:25|4:26]].(See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
