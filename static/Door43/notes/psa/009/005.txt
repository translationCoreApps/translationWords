===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you have blotted out their name forever and ever** - Causing people to be forgotten is spoken of as blotting out their name.  AT: "you have caused them to be forgotten as if their name was blotted out" or "no one will ever remember them again" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **blotted out** - "erased"
  * **The enemy crumbled like ruins** - The enemy is spoken of as if it were a city full of broken down buildings. AT: "Our enemies were destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **when you overthrew their cities** - "when you destroyed their cities"
  * **All remembrance of them has perished** - Remembrance is spoken of as if it were a living being that could die. AT: "All remembrance of them has stopped" or "There is no longer any remembrance of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **All remembrance of them has perished** - Remembrance can be expressed with the verb "remember." AT: "No one remembers them at all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
