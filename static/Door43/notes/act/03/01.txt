===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - One day Peter and John go to the temple. 
  * **General Information:** - Verse 2 gives background information about the lame man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
 
  * **into the temple** - They did not go into the temple building where only the priests were allowed. AT: "to the temple courtyard" (UDB) or "into the temple area" 
  * **A certain man, lame from birth, was carried every day and was laid** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Every day, people carried a certain man, lame from birth, and laid him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **lame** - unable to walk
  * **the door of the temple called Beautiful** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the door of the temple, which people called Beautiful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **door** - "gate"
