===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I have broken Yahweh's commandment** - This is an idiom that means he had disobeyed the commandment. AT: "I have disobeyed what Yahweh commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **because I was afraid of the people** - This is the reason Saul gives for not obeying God. AT: "because I was afraid of the soldiers"
  * **obeyed their voice** - Here "voice" refers to what the soldiers asked Saul to do. AT: "did what they asked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **return with me** - Saul and Samuel were apparently talking in private away from the other people.
