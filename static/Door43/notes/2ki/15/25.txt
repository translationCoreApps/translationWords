===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pekah ... Remaliah** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **conspired against him** - "secretly planned to kill Pekahiah"
  * **fifty men** - "50 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Argob ... Arieh** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the citadel of the king's palace** - "the fortified part of the king's palace" or "the safe place in the king's palace"
  * **became king in his place** - The phrase "in his place" is a metaphor meaning "instead of him." AT: "became king instead of Pekahiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they are written in the book of the events of the kings of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you can read about them in the book of the events of the kings of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
