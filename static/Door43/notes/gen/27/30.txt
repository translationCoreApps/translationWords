===== translationNotes: =====

  * **had scarcely gone out from the presence of Isaac his father** - "had just left the tent of Isaac his father"
  * **delicious food** - "tasty meat that I love." See how this was translated in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:27:03|27:3]].
  * **some of your son's game** - Here "your son's" was a polite way of Esau referring to his own food he prepared. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **your son's game** - The word "game" refers to wild animals that people hunt to eat. See how "game" was translated in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:27:05|27:7]].
  * **bless me** - This refers to the formal blessing a father pronounces on his children.
