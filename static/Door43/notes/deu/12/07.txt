===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It is there** - This refers to the place that Yahweh will choose for the children of Israel to worship.
  * **rejoice about everything that you have put your hand to** - Here "put your hand to" represents the whole person and work that he has done. AT: "rejoice about all the work you have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
