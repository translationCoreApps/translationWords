===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the first month** - "In month one" (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **which is the month of Nisan** - "Nisan" is the name of the first month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of March and the first part of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in the twelfth year of King Ahasuerus** - "in year number 12 of King Ahasuerus" or "when King Ahasuerus had reigned for about twelve years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the Pur—that is the lot—was thrown** - "they cast the Pur—that is the lot—" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the twelfth month** - "month twelve" (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the month of Adar** - "Adar" is the name of the twelfth and last month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part of February and the first part of March on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
