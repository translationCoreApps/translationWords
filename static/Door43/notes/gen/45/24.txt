===== translationNotes: =====

  * **do not quarrel** - Possible meanings include 1) "do not argue" and 2) "do not become afraid"
  * **went up out of Egypt** - It was common to use the word "up" when speaking of traveling from Egypt to Canaan.
  * **he is ruler over all the land of Egypt** - Here "land of Egypt" stands for the people of Egypt. AT: "he rules all the people of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **His heart was astonished** - Here "heart" stands for the whole person. AT: "and he was astonished" or "he was very surprised" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **he could not believe what they told him** - "he did not accept that what they said was true"
