===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So I went up ... and I turned back** - The other men with Nehemiah also followed him. AT: "So we went up ... and we turned back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **by the Valley Gate** - "through the Valley Gate" 
  * **the rest who did the work** - This refers to the men who would later rebuild the walls. AT: "the others who would later do the work of rebuilding the walls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
