===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So** - The writer tells the result of getting understanding and discretion.
  * **you will walk in the way ... follow the paths** - A person's conduct is spoken of as if it were walking on a path. AT: "you will live in the way ... follow the example" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the wicked will be cut off from the land** - The writer speaks of Yahweh removing people from the land as if he were cutting the people off, like a person might cut a branch from a tree. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will remove the wicked from the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the wicked ... the faithless** - These are nominal adjectives that can be stated as adjectives. AT: "those who are wicked ... those who are faithless" or "wicked people ... faithless people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **the faithless will be cut off from it** - The writer speaks of Yahweh removing people from the land as if he were cutting the people off, like a person might cut a branch from a tree. This can be stated in active form. AT: "he will remove the faithless from it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
