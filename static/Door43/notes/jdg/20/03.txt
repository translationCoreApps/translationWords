===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the author of the book tells background information about what the people of Benjamin knew.
  * **had gone up to Mizpah** - Mizpah was located high in the mountains.
  * **to spend the night** - "for the night" or "to stay for a night"
