===== translationNotes: =====

  * **have I asked of Yahweh** - "I have asked Yahweh to let me do"
  * **I will seek that** - A person really wanting something and continually asking God for it is spoken of as if he were seeking to find something. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **to see the beauty of Yahweh** - The wonderful character of God is spoken of as if it were physical beauty. AT: "to see how wonderful Yahweh is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to meditate in his temple** - Possible meanings are 1) "to ask God what he wants me to do" or 2) "to think carefully about God in his temple."
