===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here Jesus quotes the prophet Isaiah, who had written scripture many years before.

  * **with their lips** - Here "lips" is a metonym for speaking. AT: "by what they say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]
  * **but their heart is far from me** - Here "heart" refers to a person's thoughts or emotions. This is a way of saying the people are not truly devoted to God. AT: "but they do not really love me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Empty worship they offer me** - "They offer me useless worship" or "They worship me in vain"
