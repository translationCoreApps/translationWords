===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Instruction from your mouth is** - Here "mouth" represents what God says. AT: "The instructions you have spoken are" or "Your instructions are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **than thousands of pieces of gold and silver** - This can be stated more generally. AT: "than a large amount of wealth" or "all the money in the world"
