===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a spring** - A place where water flows naturally out of the ground.
  * **for their sin and impurity** - AT: "to cleanse their sin and impurity"
  * **this is the declaration of Yahweh of hosts** - This phrase is often translated as "Yahweh says" in the UDB. You can translate this concept whichever way is best in your language. This phrase is used many times in Zechariah.
  * **cut off the names of the idols from the land** - AT: "erase idol worship throughout the land"
  * **I will also cause the false prophets and their unclean spirit to go out of the land** - AT: "I will also remove the false prophets and their impure spirits from the land."
