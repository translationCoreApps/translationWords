===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in which the mildew has been found** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "in which they found the mildew" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **an unclean place** - A place that is unfit for people to occupy or to be used for God's purposes is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
