===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brought him under an oath** - "made him swear an oath"
  * **so the kingdom might become lowly and not lift itself up** - The kingdom being small and weak is spoken of as if it were lowly and being large and powerful as if it lifted itself up. AT: "so the kingdom might remain small and not become great" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the land will survive** - Here the word "land" represents the kingdom of Judah and the people in it. AT: "the kingdom will continue to exist" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
