===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **But this is a people robbed and plundered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "But the enemy has robbed and plundered this people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **robbed and plundered** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize how badly the enemy had plundered them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **held captive in prisons** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and they are prisoners" or "and the enemy has put them in prisons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
