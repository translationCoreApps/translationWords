===== translationNotes: =====

  * **offerings for your vows** - "offerings to fulfill vows" or "vow offerings"
  * **the blood of your sacrifices will be poured out** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the priest will pour out the blood of the sacrifice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you will eat the flesh** - God's law specifies which parts of the animal are for the burnt offering, which parts are for the priest and which parts are for the offerer to eat. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "you will eat some of the meat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
