===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I give thanks for your fellowship in the gospel** - Paul is expressing thanks to God that the Philippians are also preaching the gospel. AT: "I give thanks to God that you are proclaiming the gospel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I am confident** - "I am sure"
  * **he who began** - "God, who began"
