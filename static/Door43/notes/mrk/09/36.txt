===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in their midst** - "among them" (UDB). The word "their" refers to the crowd.
  * **He took him in his arms** - This means that he hugged the child or picked him up and placed him on his lap.
  * **such a child** - "a child like this"
  * **in my name** - This means to do something because of love for Jesus. AT: "because they love me" (UDB) or "for my sake" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the one who sent me** - This refers to God who has sent him to earth. AT: "God who has sent me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
