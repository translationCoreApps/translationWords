===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Do not withhold good** - "Do not withhold good things" or "Do not withhold good actions"
  * **when it is in your power to act** - "when you are able to help"
  * **when you have the money with you** - "when you have the money with you now." The meaning here is that the person has the money to help today, but tells his neighbor to come back tomorrow.
