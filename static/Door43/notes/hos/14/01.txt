===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **for you have fallen because of your iniquity** - Sinning is spoken of here as if it were falling. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Take with you words** - This probably means words of confession and praise. "Confess your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the fruit of our lips** - What a person says is called the fruit of his lips. AT: "our words and songs of praise." Modern versions have different translations of this difficult passage. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
