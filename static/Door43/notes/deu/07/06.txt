===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses tells the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **you are a nation that is set apart** - Yahweh choosing the people of Israel to belong to him in a special way is spoken of as if Yahweh set them apart from all other nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that are on the face of the earth** - This is an idiom. AT: "that live on the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
