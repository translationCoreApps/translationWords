===== translationNotes: =====


  * **howling like dogs** - The psalmist speaks of his enemies threatening to attack people as if they were dogs howling, growling, or barking at people. AT: "threatening to attack us" or "threatening to attack us like wild dogs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **satisfied** - "content"
