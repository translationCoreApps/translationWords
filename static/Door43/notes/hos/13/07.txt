===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **like a lion ... like a leopard ... as a bear ... as a lion ... as a wild beast** - These are wild animals that attack and kill other animals. Yahweh continues to say that he will destroy his people for their sins. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **as a bear that is robbed of her cubs** - The words "would attack" are left out because they are understood. This can be stated in active form. AT: "like a bear would attack an animal that takes her cubs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as a lion** - The words "would devour them" are left out because they are understood. AT: "as a lion would devour them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
