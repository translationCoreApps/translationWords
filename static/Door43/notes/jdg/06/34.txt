===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came over Gideon** - This is an idiom. AT: "took control of Gideon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **clan of Abiezer** - Translate the name of this people group as you did in [[ en:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **so they might follow him** - The words "to battle" are understood. AT: "so they might follow him to battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **and they too, were called out to follow him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "calling them out to follow him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to Asher, Zebulun, and Naphtali** - These all represent the people of each tribe. AT: "to the tribes of Asher, Zebulun, and Naphtali" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
