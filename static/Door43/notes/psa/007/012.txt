===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God will sharpen his sword and will prepare his bow for battle** - In verses 12 and 13, David speaks of God deciding to punish the wicked as if God were a warrior preparing to fight against them with weapons.  AT: "God will take action against him like a warrior who sharpens his sword and prepares his bow for battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
