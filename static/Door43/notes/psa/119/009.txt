===== translationNotes: =====

  * **BETH** - This is the name of the second letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 9-16 begins with this letter.
  * **How can a young person keep his path pure?** - This question is used to introduce a new value to the word of God. AT: "This is how a young person can keep his path pure" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **keep his path pure** - The writer refers to a way of living according to God's law as a path kept clear of obstructions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **With my whole heart** - The heart refers to all of one's emotions, feelings, desires, and will.
  * **I seek you** - Here this means "I try to serve you."
  * **Do not let me stray from your commandments** - To stray from God's commandments is like that of one who strays off a path. AT: "Do not let me disobey your commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
