===== translationNotes: =====

  * **beyond the Jordan** - This refers to the land across the Jordan River, to the east of Israel. Moses was east of the Jordan when he spoke to the Israelites. AT: "east of the Jordan"
  * **Suph ... Paran ... Tophel ... Laban ... Hazeroth ... Di Zahab** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **It is eleven days' journey from Horeb ... to Kadesh Barnea** - "It takes eleven days to walk from Horeb ... to Kadesh Barnea"
  * **Mount Seir** - This is a mountainous area south of the Dead Sea. The area is also called "Edom." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **eleven** - "11" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Numbers]])
