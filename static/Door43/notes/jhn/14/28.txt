===== translationNotes: =====

  * **loved** - This kind of love comes from God and desires the good of others, even when it does not benefit oneself. This kind of love cares for others, no matter what they do. 
  * **I am going to the Father** - Here Jesus implies that he will return to his Father. AT: "I am going back to the Father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the Father is greater than I** - Here Jesus implies that the Father has greater authority than the Son while the Son is on the earth. AT: "the Father has greater authority than I have here."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
