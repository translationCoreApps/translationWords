===== translationNotes: =====

  * **do you see what the elders of the house of Israel are doing in the dark?** - God is commanding Ezekiel to look at what the elders are doing. AT: "look at what the elders of the house of Israel are doing in the dark." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the house of Israel** - The word "house" is a metonym for the family that lives in the house, in this case the Israelites, the descendants of Jacob over many years. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01|3:1]]. AT: "the Israelites" or "the Israelite people group" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the hidden chamber of his idol** - "the room where no one can see him worship his idol" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
