===== translationNotes: =====

  * **returned a pledge** - God is impressed by lenders who return the pledge back to the borrower before the borrower actually pays the lender back.
  * **gives his food to the hungry. and that he covers the naked with clothes** - God is impressed by those who are generous towards those who lack food and cloths.
