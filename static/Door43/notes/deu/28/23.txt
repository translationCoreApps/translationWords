===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **skies ... will be bronze** - Moses speaks of the skies being like bronze because there will be no rain. AT: "skies ... will give no rain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **earth ... will be iron** - Moses speaks of the earth being like iron because no crops will grow. AT: "nothing will grow from the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **make the rain of your land into powder and dust** - "instead of rain, there will be sandstorms"
  * **until you are destroyed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "until it destroys you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
