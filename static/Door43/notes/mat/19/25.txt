===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they were very astonished** - "the disciples were amazed." It is implied that they were astonished because they believed having riches was proof that God approved of someone. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Who then can be saved?** - The disciples use a question to emphasize their surprise. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Then there is no one whom God will save!" or "Then there is no one who will receive eternal life!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **we have left everything** - "we have left all our wealth" or "we have given up all our possessions"
  * **What then will we have?** - "What good thing will God give us?"
