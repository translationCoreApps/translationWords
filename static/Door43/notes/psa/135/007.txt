===== translationNotes: =====
  * **bringing the wind out of his storehouse** - A storehouse is a place where things are kept for future use. This imagery shows God's power to control the wind. AT: "causing the wind to blow by his power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
