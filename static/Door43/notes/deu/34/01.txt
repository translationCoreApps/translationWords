  * **Mount Nebo** - This is the highest point of Mount Pisgah which is in the northern part of the Abarim mountain range. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:32:48|32:49]].
  * **Pisgah** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:03:17|3:17]].
  * **the City of Palms** - This is another name for Jericho. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
