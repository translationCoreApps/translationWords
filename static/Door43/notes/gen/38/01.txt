===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about at that time that Judah** - This introduces a new part of the story that focuses on Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])

  * **whose name was Shua** - Shua is a Canaanite woman who married Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
