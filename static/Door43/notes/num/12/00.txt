====== Numbers 12 General Notes ======

=== Structure and formatting ===
Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 12:6-8, which is an important quotation.

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Idiom ==

God used the idiom "mouth to mouth" meaning "speaking directly with both people present." This indicated that Moses was more than just a prophet and greater than other prophets. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]])
