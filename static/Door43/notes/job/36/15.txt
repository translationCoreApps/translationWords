===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he opens their ears** - Elihu speaks of God causing a person to listen as if God were opening their ears. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:job:36:10|36:10]]. AT: "he causes them to listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **into a broad place where there is no hardship** - Elihu speaks of living without trouble as if it were being in a wide-open space where there were no hardships. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **where your table would be set with food full of fatness** - Elihu speaks of living prosperously as if it were having one's table filled with the best foods. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your table would be set** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "your servants would set your table" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **food full of fatness** - Meat that had plenty of fat on it was a sign of prosperity because the animals were healthy and well-fed. AT: "the very best food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
