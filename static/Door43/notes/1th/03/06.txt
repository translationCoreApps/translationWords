===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul tells his readers about Timothy's report after he returned from visiting them.

  * **came to us** - The word "us" refers to Paul and Silvanus. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **the good news of your faith** - It is understood that this refers to faith in Christ. This can be made explicit. AT: "a good report of your faith"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you always have good memories** - When they think of Paul, they always have good thoughts about him.
  * **you long to see us** - "you desire to see us"
  * **brothers** - Here "brothers" means fellow Christians.
  * **because of your faith** - This refers to faith in Christ. This can be made explicit. AT: "because of your faith in Christ" (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in all our distress and affliction** - The word "affliction" explains why they are in "distress." AT: "in all our distress caused by our afflictions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
