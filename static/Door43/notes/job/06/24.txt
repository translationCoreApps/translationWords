===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Teach me ... make me** - These verbs "teach" and "make" are second person plural and are spoken to his friends. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **and I will hold my peace** - This is an idiom. AT: "and I will be silent" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **How painful are truthful words** - Possible meanings are 1) The truthful words are painful to hear or 2) "When people speak what is true, that truth will not hurt the person who hears it."
  * **But your arguments, how do they actually rebuke me?** - Job is using this question to rebuke his friends and to emphasize that what they are saying does not apply to him. this question can be written as a statement. AT: "Your reasons for rebuking me do not apply to me even though you sternly correct me." "But your arguments against me are not true, so they do not actually rebuke me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **your arguments** - "your reasons" or "your claims"
