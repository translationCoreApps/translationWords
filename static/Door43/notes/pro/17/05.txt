===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the poor** - This refers to poor people. AT: "those who are poor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **his Maker** - This is a name that refers to Yahweh. This is also an abstract noun that can be written as a verb. AT: "the one who made him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **at misfortune** - This refers to the misfortune of others. AT: "at others' misfortune" or "at other peoples' troubles" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **are the crown of** - This speaks of older peoples' grandchildren being a sign of honor for them as if their grandchildren were a crown. AT: "bring honor and respect to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the aged** - This refers to older people. AT: "those who are older" or "older people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
