===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to devour my flesh** - Destroying someone completely is spoken of as if it were devouring a person's flesh. He did not mean that they wanted to eat his body. AT: "to destroy me"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my adversaries and my enemies** - These words mean the same thing. These are the evildoers who came near to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **stumbled and fell** - This represents the writer's enemies failing to fulfill their plans to harm the writer. AT: "did not succeed" or "failed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Though an army encamps against me** - "though an army surrounds me" or "though an army puts its tents around me"
  * **my heart will not fear** - Here "heart" represents the whole person. AT: "I will not be afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **though war rises up** - The writer enemies are spoken of as if they themselves were a war. AT: "though my enemies come to fight me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will remain confident** - "I will continue to trust God to help me"
