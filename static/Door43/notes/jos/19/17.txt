===== translationNotes: =====

  * **fourth casting** - number four in a series (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **casting of lots** - A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:19:01|19:1]].
  * **Chesulloth ... Shunem ... Hapharaim ... Shion ... Anaharath** - These are names of cities given to Issachar's tribe. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
