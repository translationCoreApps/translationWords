===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Eliphaz continues speaking to Job.

  * **the plans of wicked people are far from me** - The idiom "far from me" means Eliphaz rejects them. AT: "but I will not listen to their wicked plans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **see their fate** - "know what will happen to the wicked"
  * **laugh them to scorn** - "mock the wicked people"
  * **those who rose up against us are cut off** - Here "those who rose up" refers to the wicked people. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God has destroyed the wicked people who harmed us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
