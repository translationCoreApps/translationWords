===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the part of the story about Zacchaeus. This verse tells us what Jesus did after this part of the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_endofstory]])

  * **When he had said these things** - "When Jesus had said these things"
  * **going up to Jerusalem** - Jerusalem was higher than Jericho, so it was normal for Israelites to speak of going up to Jerusalem.
