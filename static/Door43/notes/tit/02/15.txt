===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Speak of and encourage these things** - "Teach these things and encourage the hearers to do these things"
  * **Give correction with all authority** - This statement can be made explicit. AT: "Correct with all authority those people who do not do these things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Let no one** - "Do not allow anyone to"
  * **disregard you** - This statement can be made explicit. AT: "refuse to listen to your words" or "refuse to respect you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
