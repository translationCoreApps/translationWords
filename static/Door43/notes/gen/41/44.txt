===== translationNotes: =====
 
 
  * **I am Pharaoh, and apart from you** - Pharaoh is emphasizing his authority. AT: "As Pharaoh, I command that apart from you"
  * **apart from you, no man will lift his hand or his foot in all the land of Egypt** - Here "hand" and "foot" stand for a person's actions. AT: "no person in Egypt will do anything without your permission" or "every person in Egypt must ask your permission before they do anything" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **no man** - Here "man" refers to any person in general, whether male or female. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_gendernotations]])
  * **Zaphenath Paneah** - Translators may add the following footnote: The name Zaphenath Paneah means "a revealer of secrets." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **He gave him Asenath, the daughter of Potiphera priest of On, as a wife** - Priests in Egypt were the highest and most privileged caste. This marriage signifies Joseph's place of honor and privilege. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **gave him Asenath** - "Asenath" is the name of the woman whom Pharaoh gave to Joseph as his wife. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **daughter of Potiphera** - "Potiphera" is the father of Asenath. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **priest of On** - On is a city, also called Heliopolis, which was "the City of the Sun" and the center of worship of the sun god Ra. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
  * **Joseph went out over the land of Egypt** - Joseph traveled over the land to supervise the preparations for the coming drought.
