===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hosea the prophet is speaking.

  * **Who is wise that he may understand these things? Who understands these things so that he may know them?** - The prophet uses these questions to say that wise people will understand and listen to what has been said to them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **For the ways of Yahweh are right, and the righteous will walk in them** - Yahweh's commandments are spoken of as ways to walk in. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] 
  * **stumble** - Disobeying Yahweh is spoken of as if it were stumbling while walking. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]
