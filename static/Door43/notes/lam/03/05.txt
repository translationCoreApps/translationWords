===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He built up siege works against me** - Siege works are ramps of dirt that enable the attacking army to cross over the city walls and invade the city. AT: "He surrounded me as if I were a city under attack." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **surrounded me with bitterness and hardship** - AT: "surrounded me with things that are painful and hard to bear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **he shut out my prayer** - AT: "he refuses to hear my prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
