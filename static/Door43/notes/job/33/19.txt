===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Elihu continues speaking to Job.

  * **Man is punished also** - AT: "God also punishes a person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so that his life abhors food, and his soul abhors delicacies** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing, that the person is in so much pain that he cannot even eat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **abhors delicacies** - "hates even his favorite food"
