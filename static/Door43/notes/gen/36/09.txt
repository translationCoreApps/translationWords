===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These were the descendants of Esau** - This sentence introduces the account of Esau's descendants in Genesis 36:9-43. AT: "This is an account of the descendants of Esau" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the hill country of Seir** - This means that they lived in the hill country of Seir. The full meaning of this may be made explicit. AT: "who lived in the hill country of Seir" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Eliphaz ... Reuel** - These are names of Esau's sons. See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:36:04|36:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Adah ... Basemath** - These are names of Esau's wives. See how you translated these names in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:36:01|36:2-3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, and Kenaz ... Amalek** - These are names of the sons of Eliphaz. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Timna** - This is the name of Eliphaz's concubine. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
