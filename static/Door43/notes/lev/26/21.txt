===== translationNotes: =====

  * **walk against me** - Walking represents behavior. Walking against God represents opposing him or rebelling against him. AT: "rebel against me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will bring seven times more blows on you** - The noun "blow" can be expressed with the verb "hit." AT: "I will hit you seven times as much." Blows represent disasters that God would send as punishment. AT: "I will cause seven times as many disasters to come against you" or "I will punish you seven times more severely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will bring seven times more blows on you** - The number seven is a symbol of completeness and shows that the punishment would be very severe.
  * **in proportion to your sins** - The noun "sins" can be expressed with the verb "sin." AT: "according to how much you have sinned"  (See: (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **which will steal your children** - Stealing represents attacking or attacking and dragging them away. AT: "which will attack your children" or "which will drag your children away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **So your roads will become deserted** - Deserted means that there is no one there. AT: "So no one will travel on your roads"
