===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the noise of the guard** - This refers to the noise made by all the soldiers.
  * **she came to the people in the house of Yahweh** - "she came to where the people had gathered at the temple"
  * **She looked, and, behold, the king was standing** - "When she arrived, she was surprised to see King Joash standing"
  * **by the pillar** - "by one of the pillars of the temple"
  * **as the custom was** - "which was the usual place for the king to stand"
  * **the trumpeters** - "the people who played the trumpets"
  * **Athaliah tore her clothes** - She tore her clothes to express that she was very upset and angry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **Treason! Treason!** - "You are traitors! You have betrayed me!" (UDB)
