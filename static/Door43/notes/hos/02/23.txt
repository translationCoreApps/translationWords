===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking to Hosea about what he will do for Israel.

  * **I will plant her for myself in the land** - When God makes his people safe and prosperous in their land again, they are spoken of if they were agricultural crops. AT: "I will take care of the Israelite people as a farmer plants his crops and takes care of them" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Lo Ruhamah** - This name means "no mercy." The translator may choose to represent this meaning as the name. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:01:06|1:6]]. AT: "No Mercy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Lo Ammi** - This name means "not my people." The translator may choose to represent this meaning as the name. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:01:08|1:9]]. AT: "Not My People" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ammi Attah** - This name means "you are my people." The translator may choose to represent this meaning as the name. AT: "You Are My People" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
