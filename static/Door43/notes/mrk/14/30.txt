===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly I say to you** - This indicates that the statement that follows is especially true and important. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mrk:03:28|3:28]].
  * **rooster crows** - The rooster is a bird that calls out very early in the morning. The loud sound he makes is "crowing."
  * **twice** - "two times" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **you will deny me** - "you will say that you do not know me"
  * **If I must die** - "Even if I must die"
  * **They all made the same promise** - This means that all of the disciples said the same thing that Peter said.
