===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a high place** - "a high mountain"
  * **in an instant of time** - "in an instant" or "instantly"
  * **they have been given to me** - This can be stated in active form. Possibly meanings are that "them" refers to 1) the authority and splendor of the kingdoms or 2) the kingdoms. AT: "God has given them to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **if you will bow down ... worship me** - These two phrases are very similar. They can be combined. AT: "if you will bow down in worship to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **it will be yours** - "I will give you all these kingdoms, with their splendor"
