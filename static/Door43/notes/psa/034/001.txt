===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **pretended to be insane** - "acted like a crazy person"
  * **before Abimelech** - This refers to a specific historical event that the Hebrews knew well. AT: "when he was in Abimelech's house" or "when he was Abimelech's prisoner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **drove him out** - "forcefully sent him away"
  * **his praise will always be in my mouth** - Here "in my mouth" refers to David speaking about Yahweh. AT: "I will always praise him out loud" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
