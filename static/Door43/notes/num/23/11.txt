===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Balak** - This is the king of Moab. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:22:02|22:2]].
  * **What have you done to me?** - Balak uses this question to scold Balaam. This rhetorical question can be translated as a statement. AT: "I cannot believe you did this to me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **but look** - This emphasizes the shocking action that follows.
  * **Should I not be careful to say only what Yahweh puts in my mouth?** - Balaam uses this rhetorical question to defend his actions. This can be translated as a statement. AT: "I must be very careful to say only what Yahweh tells me to say." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **to say only what Yahweh puts in my mouth** - The message is spoken of as if it is something that God put into his mouth. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:num:22:38|22:38]]. AT: "to say only what Yahweh wants me to say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
