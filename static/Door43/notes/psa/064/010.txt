===== translationNotes: =====

  * **take refuge in him** - Going to Yahweh for protection is spoken of as taking refuge in him. AT: "go to him for protection" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **all the upright in heart will take pride in him** - Here "upright in heart" is an idiom that means godly or righteous. AT: "all the people who are godly will praise him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
