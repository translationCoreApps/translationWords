====== Ezekiel 13 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

== False prophets ==

God spoke against people who said they were prophesying but had not received any message from God. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]])

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

God said of people who encourage others to feel safe even when they continue to sin were not strengthening a wall but just putting whitewash on it to hide the imperfections. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
