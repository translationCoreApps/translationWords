===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they did not understand** - Possible meanings: 1) "the disciples did not understand" (UDB) or 2) "the crowd did not understand."
  * **this parable** - This is an illustration from the work of shepherds, using metaphors. The "shepherd" is a metaphor for Jesus. The "sheep" represent those who follow Jesus, and the "strangers" are the Jewish leaders, including the Pharisees, who try to deceive the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
