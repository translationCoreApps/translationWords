===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is part of the seventh bowl of God's wrath.

  * **the mountains were no longer found** - The inability to see any mountains is metonymy expressing the idea that no mountains existed any longer. AT: "there were no longer any mountains" (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a talent** - "34 kilograms" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]])
