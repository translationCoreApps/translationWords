===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This ends Paul and Barnabas' time in Antioch of Pisidia and they go to Iconium.
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "they" refers to Paul and Barnabas.

  * **the Jews** - This probably refers to the leaders of the Jews. AT: "the Jewish leaders" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **urged on** - "convinced" or "stirred up"
  * **the leading men** - "the most important men"
  * **These stirred up a persecution against Paul and Barnabas** - "They convinced the important men and women to persecute Paul and Barnabas"
  * **threw them out beyond the border of their city** - "removed Paul and Barnabas from their city"
  * **shook off the dust from their feet against them** - This was a symbolic act to indicate to the unbelieving people that God had rejected them and would punish them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **the disciples** - This probably refers to the new believers in the Antioch of Pisidia that Paul and Silas just left.
