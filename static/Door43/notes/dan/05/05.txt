===== translationNotes: =====

  * **At that moment** - "As soon as they did that" or "Suddenly" (UDB)
  * **plaster** - cement or mud that is spread on walls or ceilings to give them a smooth hard surface when it dries
  * **the king's face changed** - "his face became pale" (UDB). This was caused by his fear.
  * **his limbs** - "his legs"
  * **his knees were knocking together** - This was the result of his extreme fear.
