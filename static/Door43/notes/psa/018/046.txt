===== translationNotes: =====

  * **may my rock be praised** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he is my rock and he should be praised" or "may people praise my rock" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my rock** - Here the writer speaks of Yahweh's protection as if he were a rock that prevented his enemies from reaching him. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:018:002|18:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **May the God of my salvation be exalted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "May people exalt the God of my salvation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the God of my salvation** - This means that it is God who has rescued him. AT: "the God who rescued me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the God who executes vengeance for me** - To "execute vengeance" means to punish people for their evil actions. AT: "the God who punishes people for the evil things they have done to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
