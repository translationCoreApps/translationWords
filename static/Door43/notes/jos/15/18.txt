===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Achsah came to Othniel** - This is an idiom that refers to Achsah becoming Othniel's wife. AT: "when Achsah became Othniel's wife" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **she urged him to ask her father for a field** - This can be translated as direct speech. AT: "she urged him, 'Ask my father to give me a field'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
