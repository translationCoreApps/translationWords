===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Redeemer** - Translate this word in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:41:14|41:14]].
  * **he who formed you from the womb** - Yahweh speaks of creating the nation of Israel as if it were forming the nation as a baby in its mother's womb. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:44:01|44:2]]. AT: "he who made you, as I form a baby in the womb" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who alone stretched out the heavens** - Yahweh speaks of creating the heavens as if they were fabric which he stretched out. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:42:05|42:5]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **omens** - These are signs that people used to attempt to predict the future.
  * **the empty talkers** - This refers to people who say things that are meaningless.
