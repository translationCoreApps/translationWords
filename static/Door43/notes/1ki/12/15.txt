===== translationNotes: =====

  * **it was a turn of events brought about by Yahweh** - This is an idiom and can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh caused things to happen like this" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Ahijah ... Jeroboam ... Nebat** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shilonite** - This is the name of a people group from the town of Shiloh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
