===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After Jesus and his disciples leave Capernaum, Jesus reminds the Pharisees, as well as his disciples, what God really expects in marriage and divorce.

  * **Jesus left that place** - Jesus' disciples were traveling with him. They were leaving Capernaum. AT: "Jesus and his disciples left Capernaum" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **and to the area beyond the Jordan River** - "that was beyond the Jordan River" or "that was across the Jordan River" 
  * **He was teaching them again** - The word "them" refers to the crowds.
  * **he was accustomed to do** - "was his custom" or "he usually did"
  * **What did Moses command you** - Moses gave the law to their ancestors, which they now were also supposed to follow. AT: "What did Moses command your ancestors about this"
  * **a certificate of divorce** - This was a paper saying that the woman was no longer his wife.
