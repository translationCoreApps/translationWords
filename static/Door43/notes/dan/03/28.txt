===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they set aside my command** - Not obeying the king's command is spoken of as if they had physically moved it away from them. AT: "they ignored my command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they gave up their bodies** - This phrase refers to the three men's willingness to die for what they believed. AT: "they were willing to die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **prostrate themselves to** - "stretch themselves out on the ground face down in honor of"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **any god except their God** - "any other god except their God"
