===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Then they will know** - This can also be stated as the writer making a request to God. AT: "Cause them to know" (UDB)
  * **are the Most High over all the earth** - God ruling over everything in the earth is spoken of as if he were elevated higher than everything else. AT: "are supreme, and you rule all things on the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
