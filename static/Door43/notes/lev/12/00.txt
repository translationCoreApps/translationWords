====== Leviticus 12 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===
== Menstruation ==
A woman was considered to be unclean after she began her monthly period and after having a baby. This was because all blood was considered to be unclean. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:unclean]] and [[:en:obe:kt:blood]])
