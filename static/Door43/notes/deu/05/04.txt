===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **face to face** - Use your language's idiom for two people who are close to each other and looking at each other when they are speaking to each other. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **at that time** - Moses is referring to an event that happened about 40 years earlier.
  * **out of the house of slavery** - Here the metonym "house of slavery" refers to Egypt where the people of Israel had been slaves. AT: "out of the place where you were slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
