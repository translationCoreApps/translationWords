===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Kohathites** - This refers to the descendants of Kohath. Translate this the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:27|3:27]].
  * **The armies under the banner of Ephraim's descendants** - This refers to the armies of the tribes under the division of Ephraim: Ephraim, Manasseh, and Benjamin. 
  * **Elishama son of Ammihud** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:10|1:10]].
  * **Gamaliel son of Pedahzur** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:10|1:10]].
  * **Abidan son of Gideoni** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:10|1:11]].
