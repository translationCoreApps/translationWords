===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Some details are given about the structure of the Palace of the Forest of Lebanon.

  * **The house was roofed with cedar that rested on beams** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The carpenters built a roof from cedar planks and attached them to beams" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **beams** - long pieces of strong wood usually used to support a roof
  * **were made square** - "had rectangular frames" (UDB)
