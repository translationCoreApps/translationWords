===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He will require** - Here "He" refers to the priest.
  * **all the inside walls of the house to be scraped** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "that the owner scrapes all the inside walls" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **the contaminated material that is scraped off** - This refers to the material with mildew on it. This can be translated in active form. AT: "the contaminated material that they scraped off" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the unclean place** - A place that is unfit for people to occupy or to be used for God's purposes is spoken of as if it were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the stones that were removed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the stones that they removed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they must use new clay to plaster the house** - "they must cover the stones with new clay"
