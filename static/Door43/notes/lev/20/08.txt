===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

  * **You must keep my commands and carry them out** - The words "keep" and "carry out" mean the same thing in order to emphasize that the people must obey God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **must surely be put to death** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must surely put to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
