===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The man** - Some translations say "Adam."
  * **called his wife's name Eve** - "gave his wife the name Eve" or "named his wife Eve" (UDB)
  * **Eve** - Translators may write a footnote saying "The name Eve sounds like the Hebrew word that means 'living.'"
  * **all the living** - The word "living" refers to people. AT: "all the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **garments of skins** - "clothing made out of animal skins"
