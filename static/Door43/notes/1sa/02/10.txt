===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Hannah continues to recite a song to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Those who oppose Yahweh will be broken** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will break those who oppose him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **broken to pieces** - This idiom means "defeated." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **the ends of the earth** - This is an idiom that means everywhere. AT: "the whole earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **exalt the horn of his anointed** - A horn is a symbol of strength. AT: "he will make the leader he has chosen stronger than his enemies" Similar words appear in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:02:01|2:1]].
  * **his anointed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the one he has anointed" or "the one on whom he has poured oil" The oil was a symbol that showed that Yahweh had chosen the person on whom they poured the oil to lead and rule over the people. AT: "the leader he has chosen"
