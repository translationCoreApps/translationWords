===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Be on your guard** - "Be ready for what people will do to you" (UDB)
  * **deliver you up to** - This means to arrest someone and then put them under someone else's control. AT: "arrest you and give you over to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **you will be beaten** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "people will beat you" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You will stand before** - This means to be put on trial and judged. AT: "You will be put on trial before" or "You will be brought to trial and judged by" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **for my sake** - "because of me" or "on account of me"
  * **, as a testimony to them** - This means they will testify about Jesus. This can be made clear. AT: "and testify to them about me" or "and you will tell them about me" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **But the gospel must first be preached to all the nations** - Jesus is still speaking about things that must happen before the end comes. This can be made clear. AT: "But the gospel must first be preached to all the nations before the end will come" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
