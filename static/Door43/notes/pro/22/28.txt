===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - These verses continue the "thirty sayings" ([[:en:bible:notes:pro:22:20|22:20]]).

  * **ancient** - very old
  * **boundary stone** - a large stone that shows where one person's land ends and another person's land begins
  * **fathers** - ancestors
  * **Do you see a man skilled at his work?** - This rhetorical question is actually a command. AT: "Think of someone you know who is skilled at his work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **stand before** - serve, become a servant of. Kings and other important people will think so highly of him that they will use his services. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
