===== translationNotes: =====

  * **keep the Passover** - Here the word "keep" means to observe. AT: "observe the Passover" or "celebrate the Passover" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the second month on the fourteenth day** - "the 2nd month on the 14th day" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **at evening** - "at sunset"
  * **They must eat it** - "They must eat the Passover lamb"
  * **with bread without yeast** - "with bread made without yeast" or "with bread that contains no yeast"
  * **bitter herbs** - These are small plants that have a strong and usually bad taste.
  * **or break any of its bones** - "and they must not break any of its bones"
