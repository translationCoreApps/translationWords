===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will be with you** - Here "be with you" is an idiom that means Yahweh will help and bless Gideon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **as one man** - You can state the full meaning explicitly. AT: "as easily as if you were fighting only one man" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **set it before you** - "place it in front of you"
