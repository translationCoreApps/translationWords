===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He looked around** - "Jesus looked around"
  * **was grieved** - "was deeply saddened" 
  * **their hardness of heart** - This metaphor describes how the Pharisees were unwilling to have compassion on the man with the withered hand. AT: "they were unwilling to have compassion on the man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Stretch out your hand** - "Reach out with your hand"
  * **his hand was restored** - This can be stated with an active form. AT: "Jesus restored his hand" or "Jesus made his hand the way it was before" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **began to plot** - "began to make a plan"
  * **the Herodians** - This is the name of an informal political party that supported Herod Antipas.
  * **how they might put him to death** - "how they might kill Jesus"
