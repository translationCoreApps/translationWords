===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking.

  * **the year of Yahweh's favor, the day of vengeance** - Both of these phrases refer to the same span of time. "Year" and "day" are both specific examples that represent the greater whole. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **year of Yahweh's favor** - "time when Yahweh will act kindly toward his people" (UDB)
