===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who rules in Zion** - "who lives in Jerusalem"
  * **tell the nations** - Here "the nations" represent all of the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **For the God who avenges bloodshed remembers** - What he remembers can be stated clearly. AT: "For the God who avenges bloodshed remembers those who were killed" or "For God remembers those who were killed and he punishes the killers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he does not forget the cry** - "He does not ignore the cry"
