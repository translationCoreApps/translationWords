===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main teaching. Here Moses begins listing the amount of plunder and how much went to the solders, to the people, and to Yahweh.
  * **seventy-two thousand oxen, sixty-one thousand donkeys, and thirty-two thousand women** - "72,000 oxen, 61,000 donkeys, and 32,000 women" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **women who had never slept with any man** - Refers to women who are virgins. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
