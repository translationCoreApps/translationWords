===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the princes of Israel.

  * **In the first month on the fourteenth day of the month** - This is the first month of the Hebrew calendar. The fourteenth day is near the beginning of April. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **for you** - The word "you" is plural and refers to the prince and the rest of the people of the house of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
