===== translationNotes: =====

  * **afflict the nation** - Here the metonym "nation" refers to the people of the nation. AT: "afflict the people of the nation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They crush** - The psalmist speaks of powerful people treating powerless people badly as if it were crushing them or breaking them in pieces. AT: "They completely destroy" or "They greatly harm." See how "break in pieces" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:072:004|72:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the widow** - "women whose husbands have died"
  * **the fatherless** - "children without fathers"
  * **the God of Jacob does not take notice of it** - "the God of Israel does not see what we do"
