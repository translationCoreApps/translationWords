====== Judges 19 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The sin of Benjamin  ==
People from a village of the tribe of Benjamin raped a visitor's wife to death. This was very evil, especially in the ancient Near East. The people of Israel considered mistreatment of a guest one of the worst crimes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])
