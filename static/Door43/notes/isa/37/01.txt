===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about that** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **he tore his clothes, covered himself with sackcloth** - This is a sign of mourning and distress. AT: "he tore his clothes and covered himself with sackcloth because he was very distressed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **Eliakim ... Shebna** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:03]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **over the household** - This is an idiom that means that he was in charge of the affairs of the palace household. AT: "in charge of the palace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **all covered with sackcloth** - This is a sign of mourning and distress. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
