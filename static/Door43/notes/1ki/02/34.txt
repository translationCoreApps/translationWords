===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Benaiah kills Joab and becomes the commander of King Solomon's army.

  * **He was buried in his own house** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "They buried Joab in his own house"
  * **in his own house** - The house is a metonym for the land on which the house stood. The Israelites buried people out of doors in graves or tombs. AT: "where his family lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
