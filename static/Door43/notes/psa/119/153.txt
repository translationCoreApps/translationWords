===== translationNotes: =====

  * **RESH** - This is the name of the twentieth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 153-160 begins with this letter.
  * **Look on my affliction** - The psalmist speaks of affliction as if it were an object that people could see. AT: "Look at how much I am suffering" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Plead my cause** - "Defend me against those who accuse me"
  * **keep me** - "protect my life" or "give me life"
