===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A greedy man** - a person who selfishly wants more things, money or food than what he needs
  * **stirs up conflict** - The action of the greedy man is spoken of as if he were stirring up or awakening conflict. AT: "causes conflict" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **One who trusts in his own heart** - Depending on one's self is spoken of as trusting in one's own heart. AT: "The person who relies on himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **whoever** - "any person who"
  * **walks in wisdom** - Possible meanings are 1) this is an idiom that means to live wisely. AT: "lives wisely" or 2) this is an idiom that means to follow the teachings of wise people. AT: "follows wise teachings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
