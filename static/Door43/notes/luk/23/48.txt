===== translationNotes: =====

  * **multitudes** - "crowd of people"
  * **who came together** - "who gathered together"
  * **witness this sight** - "see this event" or "observe what was happening"
  * **things that were done** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "what had happened" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **returned** - "returned to their homes" (UDB)
  * **beating their breasts** - This was a symbol of sorrow and regret. AT: "hitting their own chests to show that they were sorrowful" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **his acquaintances** - "those who knew Jesus" or "those who had met Jesus"
  * **followed him** - "traveled with Jesus"
  * **at a distance** - "some distance away from Jesus"
  * **these things** - "what happened"
