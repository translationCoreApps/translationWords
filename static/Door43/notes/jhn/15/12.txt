===== translationNotes: =====

  * **No one has a love greater than this** - This kind of love comes from God and desires the good of others, even when it does not benefit oneself. This kind of love cares for others, no matter what they do. AT: "You can have no greater love than this." (UDB) 
  * **life** - This refers to physical life.
