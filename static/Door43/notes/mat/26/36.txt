===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This begins the account of Jesus praying in Gethsemane.

  * **and began to become sorrowful** - "and he became very sad"
  * **My soul is deeply sorrowful** - Here "soul" refers to the whole person. AT: "I am very sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **even to death** - This is an idiom. AT: "I feel like I could even die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
