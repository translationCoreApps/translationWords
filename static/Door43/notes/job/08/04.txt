===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for he has handed them over to their sins** - "it is because God has allowed the power of their sin to control them" or "he punished them with the consequences of their sins."
  * **But suppose you diligently sought God and presented your request to the Almighty** - Both of these phrases refer to Job asking God for help, or pleading with God for mercy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **diligently sought God** - "earnestly asked God for help"
