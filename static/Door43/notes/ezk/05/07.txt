===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Israel and Judah.

  * **Because you are more troublesome than** - AT: "because your sinfulness is worse than" or "because you are more unruly than"
  * **that surround you** - AT: "that are all around you."
  * **have not walked in my statutes** - "have not lived according to my statutes" or "have not obeyed my statutes"
  * **or acted according to my decrees** - This phrase means basically the same thing as the phrase before it and emphasizes the people's failure to obey Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Behold!** - AT: "Look!" or "Listen!" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you!"
  * **I will execute judgments within your midst** - AT: "I will perform judgments among you" or "I will judge you in various ways" or "I will punish you" (UDB).
