===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hilkiah** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:18:16|18:18]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the book of the law** - Most likely these laws were written on a scroll. A scroll was a type of book made of one long, rolled-up sheet of papyrus or leather.
  * **given it into the hand of the workmen** - Here "hand" represents the workmen as a whole. AT: "given it to the workmen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
