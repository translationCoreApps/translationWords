===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the boat** - It can be assumed that this boat belongs to James and John. AT: "in their boat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **mending the nets** - "repairing the nets"
  * **called them** - It may be helpful to state clearly why Jesus called to James and John. AT: "called them to come with him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **hired servants** - "servants who worked for them"
  * **they followed him** - James and John went with Jesus.
