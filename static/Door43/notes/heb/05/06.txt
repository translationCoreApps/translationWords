===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This prophecy is from a Psalm of David.

  * **he also says** - To whom God is speaking can be stated clearly. AT: "he also says to Christ" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **in another place** - "in another place in the scriptures"
  * **after the manner of Melchizedek** - This means that Christ as a priest has things in common with Melchizedek as a priest. AT: "in the same way that Melchizedek was a priest"
