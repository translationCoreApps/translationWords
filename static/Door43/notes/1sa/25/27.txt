===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Abigail continues to tell David not to be angry.

  * **this present** - The "present" was actually many different things. "these gifts"
  * **your servant ... my master ... my master ... the trespass of your servant ... my master ... my master** - Abigail speaks of herself and David as if they are two different people to show that she respects David. AT: "I ... you ... you ... my trespass ... you ... you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **let it be given to the young men** - Abigail uses the passive to show her respect for David. This can be translated in active form. AT: "please give it to the young men" or "please let my master give it to the young men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh will certainly make my master a sure house** - Here "house" is an idiom used for "descendants." AT: "Yahweh will ensure that my master always has a descendant who serves as king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **fighting the battles of Yahweh** - "fighting against Yahweh's enemies"
  * **evil will not be found in you** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "no one will ever see you do any evil thing" or "you will never do anything evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
