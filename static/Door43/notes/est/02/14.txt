===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This continues the background information that began in [[:en:bible:notes:est:02:12|2:12]] about the customs for the women who became the king's wives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **in the morning** - It is implied that this is the following morning. This information can be made clear. AT: "the next morning" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **second house** - "a different house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **custody** - "supervision" or "protection" (UDB)
  * **Shaashgaz** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
