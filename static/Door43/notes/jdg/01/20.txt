===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Hebron was given to Caleb (like Moses had said)** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Moses had given Hebron to Caleb" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **three sons of Anak** - The leaders of the people groups are used to refer to the entire group. AT: "three sons of Anak and their people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Anak** - This is the name of a man. Anak and his descendants were famous for being very tall. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **to this day** - "until now." This refers to the time the book of Judges was written.
