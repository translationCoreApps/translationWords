===== translationNotes: =====

  * **So the peaceful pastures will be devastated** - Here "pastures" stands for the entire nation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will be devastated** - This can be put into active form. AT: "he will devastate the entire nation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **angry wrath** - The fact that Yahweh is angry is spoken of as if it were an object. AT: "Yahweh is angry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Like a young lion, he has left his den, for their land will become a horror** - Unlike other references where God is viewed as a protective lion toward Israel, in this passage God acts like a lion in order to punish Israel. AT: "Yahweh is coming like a young lion to make the people's land a horror" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **their land will become a horror** - Here "horror" stands for a quality. AT: "their land will become horrible" or "their land will become something fearful to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the oppressor's anger** - This refers to the anger of Israel's enemies.
