===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

  * **sinned against Yahweh** - "we have rebelled against Yahweh by disobeying him"
  * **we will follow** - "we will obey"
  * **to attack the hill country** - Here the metonym "hill country" represents the people that live there. AT: "to attack the people who live in the hill country" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **for I will not be with you, and you will be defeated by your enemies** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "your enemies will defeat you because I will not be with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
