===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh raised up someone** - Yahweh appointing someone to do a special work for him is spoken of as if Yahweh raised or lifted up the person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Othniel ... Kenaz** - Translate these names the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:01:11|1:13]].
  * **empowered him** - This phrase means that Yahweh helped Othniel to have and develop the qualities he needed to be a great leader.
  * **he judged Israel** - Here "judged" means he led the people of Israel.
  * **he went out to war** - Here "he" refers to Othniel who represents the army of Israel. AT: "Othniel and the Israelite soldiers went to fight against the army of Cushan Rishathaim" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Yahweh gave him victory over Cushan Rishathaim king of Aram** - Here "Cushan Rishathaim" represents his army. AT: "Yahweh helped the Israelite army defeat the army of Cushan Rishathaim king of Aram" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **The hand of Othniel** - Here "hand" is a metonym for army. AT: "The army of Othniel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The land had peace** - "The land" is used to refer to the people who lived in the land. AT: "The people lived peacefully" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **forty years** - "40 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
