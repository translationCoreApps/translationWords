===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He killed** - "Aaron killed"
  * **Aaron's sons gave him the blood** - It is implied that the blood was in a bowl. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **inner parts** - This is the stomach and intestines. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:01:07|1:9]].
  * **kidneys ... liver** - Translate these words as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:03:03|3:4]].
