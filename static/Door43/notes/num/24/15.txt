===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Balaam son of Beor** - Beor was Balaam's father. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:22:05|22:5]].
  * **whose eyes are wide open** - This idiom means he sees and understands clearly. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:24:02|24:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **who has knowledge from the Most High** - The abstract term "knowledge" can be stated as an action. AT: "who knows things that God Most High has revealed to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **bows down** - This is a sign of submission to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
