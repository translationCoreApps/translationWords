===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nebuchadnezzar made a gold statue ... He set it up** - That Nebuchadnezzar's men made and set up a statue by his command is spoken of as if Nebuchadnezzar had made and set up a statue. AT: "Nebuchadnezzar commanded his men to make a gold statue ... They set it up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sixty cubits tall and six cubits wide** - "about 27 meters tall and almost 3 meters wide" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **Plain of Dura** - This is a location within the kingdom of Babylon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **provincial governors ... regional governors ... local governors** - These are officials who have authority over different sizes of territory.
  * **treasurers** - These officials are in charge of money.
