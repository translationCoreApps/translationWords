===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Many shepherds have destroyed my vineyard** - Here Yahweh speaks of his land and his people being destroy by armies as if it were a vineyard that shepherds had destroyed. AT: "My people and my land are like a vineyard that many shepherds have destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Many shepherds have destroyed** - Here the "shepherds" refer to their sheep. AT: "Many shepherds have allowed their sheep to destroy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They have stomped all over** - "They have crushed under their feet"
  * **my portion of land** - "the ground I planted" or "my field"
  * **All the land has been made desolate** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "All the land is desolate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **takes it to heart** - This is an idiom. AT: "cares" or "pays any attention" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
