===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the rulers and authorities in the heavenly places would come to know the many-sided nature of the wisdom of God** - "God will make his great wisdom known to the rulers and authorities in the heavenly places through the Church"
  * **rulers and authorities** - These words share similar meanings. Paul uses them together to emphasize that every spiritual being will know God's wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **in the heavenly places** - "in the supernatural world." The word "heavenly" refers to the place where God is. See how this is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:eph:01:03|1:3]].
  * **the many-sided nature of the wisdom of God** - God's complex wisdom (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **according to the eternal plan** - "in keeping with the eternal plan" or "consistent with the eternal plan"
