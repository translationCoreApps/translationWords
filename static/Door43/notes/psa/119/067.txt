===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Before I was afflicted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Before you afflicted me" or "Before you punished me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I went astray** - Disobeying God is spoken of as if the person walked on the wrong path. AT: "I disobeyed you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I observe your word** - Here "word" represents commandments. AT: "I obey your commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
