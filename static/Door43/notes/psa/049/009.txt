===== translationNotes: =====

  * **so that his body should not decay** - This refers to the body decomposing in the grave.
  * **he will see decay** - The writer speaks of experiencing something as if it were seeing that thing. AT: "he will die and his body will decay" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Wise men die; the fool and the brute alike perish** - The author refers to all people by referring to those who have the most and least wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **brute** - This means a person who is stupid or unintelligent.
