===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The words "him" and "his" refer to Peter.

  * **Behold** - This word alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **by him** - "next to him" or "beside him"
  * **in the cell** - "in the prison room"
  * **He struck Peter** - "The angel tapped Peter" or "The angel poked Peter." Peter was evidently sleeping deeply enough that this was required to wake him.
  * **his chains fell off from his hands** - The angel caused the chains to fall from Peter without touching them. 
  * **Peter did so** - "Peter did what the angel told him to do" or "Peter obeyed"
