===== translationNotes: =====

  * **establish my covenant with you** - "make a covenant between you and me"
  * **with you** - with Noah
  * **You will come into the ark** - "You will enter the ark." Some translations say "You will go into the ark."
  * **Of every living creature of all flesh, two of every kind you must bring into the ark** - "You must bring into the ark two of every kind of living creature"
  * **creature** - an animal God created
  * **all flesh** - Translate these words as in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:06:11|6:12]].
