  * **The sons of Caleb ... Mareshah** - Different versions understand the relationships among these people in different ways. Some interpreters believe that "the father of Hebron ... the father of Raham, the father of Jorkeam ... the father of Shammai" means "the founder of the clan of Hebron," etc. Some versions might employ this interpretation.
