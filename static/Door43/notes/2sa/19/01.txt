===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Joab was told** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Someone told Joab" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Look, the king is weeping** - This word is used here to draw someone's attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen, the king is weeping" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **So the victory that day was turned into mourning for all the army** - This means that the whole army mourned instead of celebrating. AT "So instead of celebrating victory that day, the whole army mourned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
