===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he will not be put to death** - Here "put to death" is an idiom that refers to kill. This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will not put him to death" or "I will not kill him" or "I will certainly keep him alive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
