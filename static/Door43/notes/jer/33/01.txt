===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **the word of Yahweh came to Jeremiah a second time** - The phrase "the word of Yahweh came" is an idiom that is used to introduce something that God told his prophets or his people. Similar words appear in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:13|1:13]]. AT: "Yahweh spoke to Jeremiah a second time" or "Yahweh gave Jeremiah this second message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **came to Jeremiah** - It is not clear why Jeremiah is referring to himself by name here. There is no need to translate using first person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **who forms in order to establish** - who creates things so he can make them exist forever
  * **he was still shut within** - "he was still a prisoner in"
  * **the courtyard of the guard** - This was an open area attached to the king's palace that was surrounded by buildings and in which they kept prisoners. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:32:01|32:2]].
