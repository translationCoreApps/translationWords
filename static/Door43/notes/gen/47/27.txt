===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They were fruitful and multiplied greatly** - The word "multiplied" explains how they were "fruitful." AT: "They had very many children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **were fruitful** - Here "fruitful" means to prosper or to have children. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **seventeen years** - "17 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **so the years of Jacob's life were one hundred forty-seven years** - "so Jacob lived to be 147 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
