===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The brothers** - This refers to those people who were either ministering with or to Paul. 
  * **brothers** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:php:01:12|1:12]].
  * **every believer ... All the believers** - Some versions translate this using "holy person" and "holy people." 
  * **especially those of Caesar's household** - This refers to servants who worked in Caesar's palace. "especially the fellow believers who work in the palace of Caesar" (UDB)
  * **with your spirit** - Paul refers to the believers by using the word "spirit," which is what enables humans to relate to God. AT: "with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
