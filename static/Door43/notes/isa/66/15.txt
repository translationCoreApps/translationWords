===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking to God's faithful people.

  * **coming with fire** - Yahweh's appearances in the Old Testament are often accompanied by fire that represents Yahweh's anger and judgment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **like the windstorm** - Storms represent Yahweh's powerful actions to make his judgment effective. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **with his sword** - "Sword" is one weapon that represents all of warfare and killing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Those killed by Yahweh will be many** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will kill many people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
