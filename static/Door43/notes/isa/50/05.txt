===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The servant of Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **has opened my ear** - This means enabling a person to hear. AT: "has enabled me to hear him speaking" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and I was not rebellious, nor did I turn away backward** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. AT: "and I obeyed what he said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **I did not hide my face from acts of shame and spitting** - Hiding one's face means to protect oneself. AT: "I gave myself to those who mocked me and spat at me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
