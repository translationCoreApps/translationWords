===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - As they leave the temple area, Jesus tells his disciples what will happen in the future to the wonderful temple that Herod the Great has built.


  * **the wonderful stones and wonderful buildings** - The "stones" refer to the stones that the buildings were built with. AT: "the wonderful buildings and the wonderful stones that they are made of" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Do you see these great buildings? Not one stone** - This question is used to draw attention to the buildings. This can be written as a statement. AT: "Look at these great buildings! Not one stone" or "You see these great buildings now, but not one stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Not one stone will be left on another which will not be torn down** - It is implied that enemy soldiers will tear down the stones. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Not one stone will remain on top of another, for enemy soldiers will come and destroy these buildings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
