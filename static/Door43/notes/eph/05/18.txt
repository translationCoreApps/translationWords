===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul ends his instructions on how all believers should live.

  * **And do not get drunk with wine** - "And you should not get drunk from drinking wine"
  * **Instead, be filled with the Holy Spirit** - "Instead you should be controlled with the Holy Spirit"
  * **psalms and hymns and spiritual songs** - Possible meanings are that 1) Paul is using these words as a merism for "all sorts of songs to praise God" or 2) Paul is listing specific forms of music. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **psalms** - Possible meanings are 1) quotations from the Book of Psalms or 2) any worship song.
  * **hymns** - songs that praise the Father or the Son
  * **spiritual songs** - Possible meanings are 1) these are songs about God or 2) these are songs given by the Holy Spirit or 3) this word is the same as "hymns" and needs no separate word.
