===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **song of ascents** - Possible meanings are 1) "song people sang while going up to Jerusalem for a celebration" or 2) "song people sang while going up the steps into the temple" or 3) "song whose words are like steps." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **Those who trust in Yahweh are like Mount Zion, unshakable, forever enduring** - People who trust in Yahweh are spoken of as if they they were Mount Zion. Mountains are unable to be moved.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **As the mountains surround Jerusalem, so Yahweh surrounds his people** - Yahweh's protection is spoken of as if he was the mountains surrounding Jerusalem. Jerusalem was surrounded by several mountains, which protected it from attack. AT: "As the hills that surround Jerusalem protect it, so Yahweh protects his people" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **now and forever** - Here, this phrase means "always." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]]) 
  * **The scepter of wickedness** - Here the scepter of wickedness represents the rule of wicked people. AT: "Wicked people" or "Wicked rulers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
