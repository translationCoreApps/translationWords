===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues to speak to his three friends.

  * **He has put my brothers far from me** - Being "far from" someone represents being unwilling to relate to him or help him. AT: "God has caused my brothers to stay away from me" or "God caused my brothers to refuse to help me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my acquaintances are wholly alienated from me** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "my acquaintances have alienated themselves from me" or "my friends treat me like a stranger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **My kinsfolk have failed me** - "My relatives have left me without help"
  * **my close friends** - "my intimate friends"
  * **have forgotten me** - This means that they refuse to treat him according to how he and they had related to each other in the past. It implies that they abandon him. AT: "have abandoned me" or "neglect me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
