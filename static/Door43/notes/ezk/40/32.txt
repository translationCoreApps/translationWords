===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to the inner courtyard by the eastern way** - "to the east side of the inner courtyard"
  * **cubits** - Translate these "long" cubits as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **fifty cubits** - about twenty-seven meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **twenty-five cubits** - about 13.5 meters
  * **Its portico faced the outer courtyard** - Here "faced" is an idiom that means it was oriented in that direction. AT: "Its portico opened toward the outer courtyard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
