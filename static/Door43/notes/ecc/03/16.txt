===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wickedness exists where there should be justice, and in place of righteousness wickedness is often found** - These two phrases mean the same thing and emphasize how common wicked behavior is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **wickedness is often found** - AT: "people often find wickedness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I said in my heart** - AT: "I said to myself" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **every matter and every deed** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and refer to every action that people do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
