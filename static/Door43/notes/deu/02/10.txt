===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer begins to give background information about the people of the land. These words are not part of Moses' speech to the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **The Emim lived ... call them the Emim** - These words give background information about the Emim people, who had lived in the land before the Moabites. Your language may have a special way to mark background information. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
  * **Emim ... Rephaim** - These are names of people groups that were considered giants. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anakim** - These are descendants of the Anak people who were very large and fierce. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:01:26|1:28]].
