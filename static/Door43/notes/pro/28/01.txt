===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Because of the transgression of a land** - The abstract noun "transgression" can be translated as a verb. AT: "Because of how a land transgresses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the transgression of a land** - This is a metonym for the sins of the people living in a land. AT: "the transgression of the people of a land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **with a man of understanding and knowledge** - The implied information is that this man is a ruler or leader. The abstract nouns "understanding" and "knowledge" can be translated as verbs. AT: "with a man who understands and knows how to rule" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
