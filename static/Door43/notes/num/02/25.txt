===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses where each tribe and its army will camp around the tent of meeting. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **the divisions of the camp of Dan** - The this refers to the divisions of Dan, Asher, and Naphthali that are under the standard of Dan. AT: "the divisions that camp under the standard of Dan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **Ahiezer son of Ammishaddai** - Translate this man's name the say way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:12|1:12]].
  * **62,700** - This modifies men. AT: "62,700 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
