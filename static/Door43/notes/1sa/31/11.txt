  * **all night** - "throughout the whole night"
  * **a tamarisk tree** - This is a type of tree. AT: "a large shade tree" Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:22:06|22:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
