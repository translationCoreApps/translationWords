===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Memucan** - Translate his name as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:13|1:14]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **province** - A "province" is a large area into which some countries are divided for the purposes of government. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:01:01|1:1]].
  * **every man should be master of his own household** - "all men should have complete authority over their wives and their children" (UDB)
  * **This decree was given** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "They wrote this decree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
