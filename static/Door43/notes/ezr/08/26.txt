===== translationNotes: =====

  * **650 silver talents** - "650 talents of silver." You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "22,000 kilograms of silver" or twenty-two thousand kilograms of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **one hundred talents of silver objects** - You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "3,400 kilograms of silver objects" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **one hundred gold talents** - You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "3,400 kilograms of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bweight]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **one thousand darics** - A "daric" was a small gold coin that people in the Persian Empire used. You can translate this in terms of the number of coins or their weight. AT: "one thousand Persian gold coins" or "eight and one half kilograms of gold" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **bronze vessels** - Bronze is a mixture of copper and another metal. It is stronger than pure copper.
