===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh is far away from wicked people** - The writer speaks of Yahweh not listening to wicked people as if he were physically far away from them. AT: "Yahweh does not listen to wicked people" or "Yahweh does not answer wicked people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The light of the eyes** - The writer speaks of an expression of joy on one's face as if the person's eyes emitted light. AT: "A cheerful expression" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **brings joy to the heart** - The word "heart" represents the person. Possible meanings are 1) the person who has a cheerful expression becomes joyful or 2) other people become joyful when they see someone with a cheerful expression. AT: "causes a person to be joyful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **good news is health to the body** - Here the word "body" represents the person. AT: "receiving good news makes a person feel good" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
