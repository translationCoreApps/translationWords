===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now when the time came** - This introduces a new part of the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **daughter of Abihail, the uncle of Mordecai, who had taken her as his own daughter** - This background information reminds the reader of Esther's relationship to Mordecai. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **Abihail** - "Abihail" was Esther's father and Mordecai's uncle. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **she did not ask for anything but what** - This can be stated in positive form. AT: "she asked only for what"
  * **Hegai** - Translate this man's name as in [[:en:bible:notes:est:02:03|2:3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **received the favor of all** - This is an idiom. AT: "pleased all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the tenth month, which is the month of Tebeth** - "Tebeth" is the name of the tenth month of the Hebrew calendar. It is during the last part December and the first part January on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **seventh year** - "year number 7" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
