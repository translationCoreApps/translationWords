===== translationNotes: =====

  * **When Peter came to himself** - "When Peter became fully awake and alert" or "When Peter became aware that what had happened was real"
  * **delivered me out of the hand of Herod** - Here "the hand of Herod" refers to "Herod's hold" or "Herod's plans." AT: "rescued me from the harm Herod had planned for me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all the expectation of the people of the Jews** - Here "the people of the Jews" probably referred mainly to the Jewish leaders. AT: "all that the Jewish leaders thought would happen to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **realized this** - He became aware that God had rescued him.
  * **John whose surname was Mark** - "John who was also called Mark"
