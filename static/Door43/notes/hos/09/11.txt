===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking.

  * **their glory will fly away like a bird** - The people of Ephraim, or the northern kingdom of Israel, will lose everything that makes other nations respect them. Their glory will quickly disappear, as a bird flies away. AT: "their glory will be like a bird that flies away from them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **when I turn away from them** - When God stops helping the northern kingdom, it will be as if he physically turned away from them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
