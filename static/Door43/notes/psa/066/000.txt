====== Psalm 066 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 66 is a psalm of praise.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh saves ==
Just as God brought Israel through the wilderness, he took the author through hard times but saved him. The author will worship God by sacrificing animals to him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:save]])
