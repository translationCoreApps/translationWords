===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the four living creatures** - "living being" or "living thing." See how you translated "living creatures" in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:06|4:6]]
  * **seven golden bowls full of the wrath of God** - The image of the wine in the bowls can be stated clearly. The word "wrath" here refers to punishment. The wine is a symbol for punishment. AT: "seven gold bowls full of the wine that represents God's wrath" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **until the seven plagues of the seven angels were completed** - "until the seven angels finished sending the seven plagues to the earth"
