===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak a poetic song to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **For Yahweh will give justice to his people** - The abstract noun "justice" can be translated as a verb. AT: "For Yahweh will do what is just for his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **he will pity his servants** - "he will feel that he needs to help his servants"
