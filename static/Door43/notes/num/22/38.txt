===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do I now have any power to say anything?** - Balaam uses this rhetorical question to tell Balak that he will not be able to do everything that Balak asks him to do. This can be translated as a statement. AT: "But I have no power to say anything I want" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
  * **the words that God puts into my mouth** - Here "words" refers to the message from God. The message is spoken of as if it is something that God put into his mouth. AT: "the message that God wants me to say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Kiriath Huzoth** - This is the name of a town. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **some meat** - "some of the meat from the sacrifices"
