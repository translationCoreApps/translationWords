===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The land must be divided** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must divide the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **these men** - This refers to all of the men that were counted in their clans, beginning in [[:en:bible:notes:num:26:05|26:5]].
  * **according to the number of their names** - "by the number of people in each clan"
