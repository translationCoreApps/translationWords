===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A gossip** - This means someone who gossips a lot.
  * **you should not associate with** - AT: "you should not be friends with"
  * **curses** - This means to express a desire that bad things will happen to someone.
  * **his lamp will be snuffed out in the middle of darkness** - AT: "his life will end like when someone snuffs out a lamp and is left in the dark" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
