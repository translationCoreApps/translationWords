===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All the paths of Yahweh are steadfast love and faithfulness** - The word "paths" is a metaphor for ways. AT: "Yahweh always loves us because of his covenant and he is always trustworthy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For your name's sake** - The phrase "your name" here refers to Yahweh's reputation. AT: "For your reputation" or "So that people will honor you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **pardon my sin, for it is great** - The abstract noun "sin" can be stated as "sinned." AT: "please forgive me, for I have sinned much" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
