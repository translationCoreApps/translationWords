===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **the myrtle ... the pines and the cypress box trees** - These are types of trees. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **the hand of Yahweh has done this** - Here the word "hand" represents Yahweh himself. AT: "Yahweh has done this" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
