===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who join themselves to Yahweh** - This refers to non-Hebrews who have joined Yahweh's people.
  * **who love the name of Yahweh** - "name" represents the person of Yahweh. AT: "who love Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **holy mountain** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:11:08|11:9]].
  * **will be accepted on my altar** - This can be stated in acive form. AT: "I will accept on my altar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my house will be called a house of prayer** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "my house will be a house of prayer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
