===== translationNotes: =====

  * **After him their countrymen repaired ... repaired another section** - These phrases refer to repairing the wall. AT: "Next to him their countrymen repaired the wall ... repaired another section of the wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **After him** - "Next to him" 
  * **Bavvai ... Henadad ... Ezer ... Jeshua** - These are names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Bavvai son of Henadad, the ruler** - Bavvai was the ruler, not Henadad.
  * **Keilah ... Mizpah** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ezer son of Jeshua, the ruler** - Ezer was the ruler, not Jeshua.
  * **that faced the ascent to the armory** - "in front of the steps that went up to the armory" (UDB)
  * **armory** - the place where weapons are kept
