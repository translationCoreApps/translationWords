===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The writer Luke, Paul, and his companions continue their travels. 
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "we" refers to the writer Luke, Paul, and those traveling with them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive|Exclusive "We"]])

  * **we took a straight course to the city of Cos** - "we went straight to the city of Cos" or "we went directly to the city of Cos"
  * **city of Cos** - Cos is a Greek island off the coast of modern day Turkey in the South Aegean Sea region. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **city of Rhodes** - Rhodes is a Greek island off the coast of modern day Turkey in the South Aegean Sea region south of Cos and northeast of Crete. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **city of Patara** - Patara is a city on the southwest coast of modern day Turkey south of the Aegean Sea in the Mediterranean Sea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|How to Translate Names]])
  * **When we found a ship crossing over to Phoenicia** - Here "a ship crossing over" stands for the crew that would sail the ship. AT: "When we found a ship with a crew sailing over to Phoenicia" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a ship crossing over** - Here "crossing" does not mean it was presently crossing but that it would be crossing to Phoenicia soon. AT: "a ship that would be going across the water" or "a ship that would be going"
