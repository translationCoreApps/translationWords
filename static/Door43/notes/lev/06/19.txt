===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when each son is anointed** - It is implied that they will be anointed when they become priests. This can be stated in active form. AT: "when he anoints each son, ordaining them as priests" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **a tenth part of an ephah** - An ephah is 22 liters. One tenth of an ephah is about 2 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **a tenth** - This is one part of ten equal parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_fraction]])
