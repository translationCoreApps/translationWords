===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Better is a poor person** - "It is better to be a poor person"
  * **who walks in his integrity** - This is an idiom. Here walking refers to living. AT: "who lives in his integrity" or "who lives an honest life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **is perverse in speech** - The word "speech" may be expressed as a verb. AT: "speaks perversely" or "speaks in an evil way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **to have desire without knowledge** - This refers to people trying to do something without the knowledge for how to correctly do it. AT: "to work hard without knowing what you are doing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the one who runs too fast misses the path** - This speaks of a person doing something too quickly and making mistakes as if running too quickly and missing the path. AT: "the one who acts too quickly makes mistakes" or "the one who acts too quickly makes poor choices" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
