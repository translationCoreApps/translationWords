===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul tells believers how to live under their rulers.

  * **Let every soul be obedient to** - Here "soul" is a synecdoche for the whole person. "Every Christian should obey" or "Everyone should obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **higher authorities** - "government officials" (UDB)
  * **for** - "because"
  * **there is no authority unless it comes from God** - "all authority comes from God"
  * **And the authorities that exist have been appointed by God** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "And the people who are in authority are there because God put them there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that authority** - "that government authority" or "the authority that God placed in power"
  * **those who oppose it will receive judgment on themselves** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "God will judge those who oppose government authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
