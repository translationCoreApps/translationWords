===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Terrors overtake him** - Here "overtake him" represents suddenly happening to him. Possible meanings are that "terrors" 1) is a metonym for things that cause people to be afraid. AT: "Terrifying things suddenly happen to him" or 2) fear. AT: "He suddenly becomes terrified"
  * **like waters** - The word "waters" refers to a flood. Floods can happen very suddenly when people do not expect them, and they are dangerous and frightening. AT: "like a flood" or "like waters that rise up suddenly"  (See:  and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a storm takes him away** - "a violent wind blows him away"
  * **it sweeps him out of his place** - Job speaks of the wind blowing the wicked man out of his house as if the wind were a person sweeping dust out of a house with a broom. AT: "the wind sweeps him out of his place like a woman who sweeps dirt out of a house" or "the wind easily blows him out of his place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **his place** - "his home"
