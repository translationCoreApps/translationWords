===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all day long** - He does not literally cry out all day long. This emphasizing that he continually cries out. AT: "continually" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **Make your servant glad** - David refers to himself as "your servant," that is, God's servant. AT: "Make me, your servant" or "Make me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
