===== translationNotes: =====

  * **hymn** - A hymn is a type of song. It was traditional for them to sing an Old Testament psalm.
  * **Jesus said to them** - "Jesus said to his disciples"
  * **will fall away** - This is an idiom that means leave. AT: "will leave me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will strike** - "kill." Here "I" refers to God. 
  * **the sheep will be scattered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will scatter the sheep" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
