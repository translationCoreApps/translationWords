===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This is the end of the taunt song that the Israelites will sing to the king of Babylon. 

  * **all of them lie down in honor** - This means that their bodies were buried in an honorable way. AT: "all the kings who have died are buried in an honorable way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **But you are cast out of your grave** - Being thrown out of the grave represents not being buried. AT: "But you are not buried. Your body is left on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **like a branch thrown away** - A branch thrown away represents something worthless. AT: "like a worthless branch that is tossed aside" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **The dead cover you like a garment** - This represents many dead bodies being on top of his body. AT: "The bodies of dead people completely cover your body" or "The bodies of dead soldiers are piled on top of your body"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **those pierced by the sword** - This tells who "The dead" are at the beginning of the sentence. Being pierced by the sword represents being killed in battle. AT: "those who were killed in battle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **who go down to the stones of the pit** - The pit refers either to hell, or to a large hole in the ground where many dead bodies are simply dumped.
  * **You will not join them in burial** - The word "them" refers to the other kings who died and were buried properly. Joining them in burial represents being buried as they were. AT: "you will never be buried as other kings were buried" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The offspring of evildoers will never be mentioned again** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one will ever speak again about the descendants of evildoers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
