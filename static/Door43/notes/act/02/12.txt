===== translationNotes: =====

  * **amazed and perplexed** - These two words share similar meanings. Together they emphasize that the people could not understand what was happening. AT: "surprised and confused" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **They are full of new wine** - Some people accuse the believers of having drunk too much wine. AT: "They are drunk" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **new wine** - This refers to wine that is in the process of fermentation.
