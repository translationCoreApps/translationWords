===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Are the hands of Zebah and Zalmunna now in your hand?** - The leaders use a question to emphasize that the Israelites have not yet captured Zebah and Zalumnna. AT: "You have not captured Zebah and Zalmunna yet." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Are the hands of Zebah and Zalmunna** - Here "hands" refer to the whole body. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])  
  * **now in your hand** - Here "hand" represents power or control. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Why should we give bread to your army?** - The leaders use a question to emphasize that they have no reason to give bread to the Israelites. AT: "We see no reason to give bread to your army." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **I will tear up your flesh with the desert thorns and briers** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "I will make whips out of desert thorns and briers and use them to beat you and cut you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **thorns and briers** - sharp, pointed pieces on vines or tree limbs that stick out and can cut people and animals
