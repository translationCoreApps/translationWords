===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Wild oxen will be slaughtered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I, Yahweh, will slaughter the wild oxen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Their land will be drunk with blood** - This describes the amount of blood that will soak into the ground by comparing the land to a drunk person. AT: "Their land will be soaked with blood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **their dust made fat with fatness** - Here "dust" means the dirt on the ground. This describes the amount of fat that will soak into the dirt by comparing it to a person that has become fat from eating so much animal fat. AT: "the dirt will be full of the fat of the animals" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
