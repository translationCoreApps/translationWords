===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hush!** - "Be quiet!" or "Be silent!"
  * **Do not be grieved** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Do not grieve" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **celebrate with great joy** - The abstract noun "joy" can be expressed as a verb. AT: "rejoice greatly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the words that were made known to them** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the words that he declared to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
