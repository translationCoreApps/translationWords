===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the word "we" refers to Peter and John but not to those whom they are addressing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])

  * **Whether it is right in the sight of God** - Here the phrase "in the sight of God" refers to God's opinion. AT: "Whether God thinks it is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
