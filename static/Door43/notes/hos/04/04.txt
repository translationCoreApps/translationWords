===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh is speaking about Israel.

  * **lawsuit** - a complaint by one person against another person in a court of law
  * **do not let anyone accuse anyone else** - No one should accuse another person of anything because everyone is guilty of something. 
  * **You priests will stumble** - Here "stumble" means to disobey God or even to stop trusting him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will destroy your mother** - Here "mother" refers to the nation of Israel. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:hos:02:02|2:2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
