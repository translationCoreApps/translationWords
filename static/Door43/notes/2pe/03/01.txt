===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Peter begins to talk about the last days.

  * **to stir up your sincere mind** - Peter speaks of causing his readers to think about these things as if he were waking them from sleep. AT: "to cause you to think pure thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the words spoken in the past by the holy prophets** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the words that the holy prophets spoke in the past" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the command of our Lord and Savior given through your apostles** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the command of our Lord and Savior, which your apostles gave to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
