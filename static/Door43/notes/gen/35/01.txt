===== translationNotes: =====

  * **go up to Bethel** - The phrase "go up" is used because Bethel is higher in elevation than Shechem.
  * **Build an altar there to God** - God speaks about himself in the third person. AT: "Build an altar there to me, your God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **said to his household** - "said to his family"
  * **Put away the foreign gods that are among you** - "Throw away your idols" or "Get rid of your false gods"
  * **purify yourselves, and change your clothes** - This was the custom of cleansing oneself morally and physically before going to worship God.
  * **change your clothes** - Putting on a new clothes was a sign that they had made themselves clean before approaching God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **in the day of my distress** - Possible meanings for "day" are 1) the day when Jacob fled from Esau or 2) "day" stands for a period of time when Jacob was distressed. AT: "when I was in a difficult situation" or "when I was in trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
