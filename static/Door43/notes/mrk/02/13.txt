===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus is teaching the crowd beside the Sea of Galilee, and he calls Levi to follow him.

  * **the lake** - This is the Sea of Galilee, which is also known as the Lake of Gennesaret.
  * **the crowd came to him** - "the people went where he was"
  * **Levi son of Alphaeus** - Alpheus is Levi's father. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
