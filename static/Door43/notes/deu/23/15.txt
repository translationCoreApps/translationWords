===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **a slave who has escaped from his master** - The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "a slave from another country who escaped from his master and came to Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] )
  * **Let him live with you** - "Let the slave live among your people"
