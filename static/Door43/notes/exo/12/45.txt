===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The food must be eaten in one house** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Each Israelite family must eat the food in one house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **you must not break any bone of it** - "you must not break any of its bones." Here the word "it" refers to the lamb which the Israelite family will eat.
