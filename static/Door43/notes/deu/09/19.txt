===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what happened in the past.

  * **I was afraid of the anger and hot displeasure with which Yahweh was angry enough against you to destroy you** - The words "anger and hot displeasure" are a metonym for what Yahweh would do because he was angry and displeased. AT: "Yahweh was angry at you—he was extremely displeased with you—he was angry enough to destroy you, and so I was afraid of what he would do"
