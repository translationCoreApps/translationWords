===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he was told** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "someone told him" or "they told him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **three thousand** - 3,000 (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **chosen men** - men whom he had chosen because they were the best men he could find
  * **wilderness of Engedi** - Engedi is a place where there is water with dry land all around it located in Israel west of the Dead Sea.
