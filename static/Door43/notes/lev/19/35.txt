===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not use false measures** - This prohibits the practice of intentionally using instruments that give inaccurate readings when measuring things.
  * **ephah** - This was a measurement for grain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **hin** - This was a measurement for liquids. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **must obey ... and do them** - These phrases mean the same thing and emphasize the command for obedience. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
