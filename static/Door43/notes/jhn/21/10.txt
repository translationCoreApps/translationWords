===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **Simon Peter then went up** - Here "went up" means Simon Peter had to go back to the boat. AT: "So Simon Peter went back to the boat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]]) 
  * **and drew the net to land** - "and pulled the net to the shore"
  * **the net was not torn** - You can translate this as an active form. AT: "the net did not break" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
