===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking to the people of Judah.

  * **the bread of adversity and the water of affliction** - Here "bread" and "water" make up the diet of a very poor person. The whole phrase represents the hard times and poverty of the people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your teacher** - This refers to Yahweh.
  * **you will see your teacher with your own eyes** - Here "eyes" represents the whole person. AT: "you yourselves will see your teacher" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Your ears will hear** - Here "ears" represents the whole person. AT: "You will hear" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **a word behind you saying** - Here "word" represents the person speaking. AT: "him speaking behind you saying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **This is the way, walk in it** - How Yahweh wants his people to behave is spoken of as if it were a way or path. To obey Yahweh is spoken of as if it were a person walking on his path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **when you turn to the right or when you turn to the left** - Disobeying Yahweh is spoken of as if the person turned left or right off of Yahweh's path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
