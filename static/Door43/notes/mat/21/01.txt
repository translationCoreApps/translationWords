===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - This begins the account of Jesus' entry into Jerusalem. Here he gives his disciples instructions about what they are to do.

  * **Bethphage** - This is a village near Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **a donkey tied up** - You can state this in active form. AT: "a donkey that someone has tied up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **tied up there** - You can make explicit how the donkey is tied. AT: "tied up there to a post" or "tied up there to a tree" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **colt** - "a young male donkey"
