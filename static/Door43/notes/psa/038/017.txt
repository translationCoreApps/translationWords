===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I am about to stumble** - Possible meanings for this metaphor are 1) "I am so sick that I am about to die" or 2) "I will soon be ruined." It may be best not to interpret the metaphor in the text. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I am in constant pain** - "I am always in pain"
