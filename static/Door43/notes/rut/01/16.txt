===== translationNotes: =====

  * **where you stay** - "where you live"
  * **your people will be my people** - Ruth is referring to Naomi's people, the Israelites. AT: "I will consider the people of your country as being my own people" or "I will consider your relatives as my own relatives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Where you die, I will die** - This refers to Ruth's desire to spend the rest of her life living in the same place and town as Naomi. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **May Yahweh punish me, and even more, if** - This was a common Jewish expression that means "I ask God to punish me if I do not do what I say." Many languages have idioms with similar meanings that could be used here. AT: "God forbid if" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **she stopped arguing with her** - "Naomi stopped arguing with Ruth"
