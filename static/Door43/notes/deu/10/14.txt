===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the Israelites as if they are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **Behold** - The word "behold" emphasizes that what is said next is important. "Pay attention" or "Look" or "Listen" 
  * **heaven ... earth** - These words show two extremes, and are combined to mean that all things everywhere belong to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **the heaven of heavens** - This refers to the highest places in the heavens. Everything in the heavens belong to God.
  * **chose you** - Here the word "you" refers to all the Israelites and is plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
