===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the dead or with spirits** - Possible meanings are 1) that "the dead" and "spirits" are two different things or 2) that this is a doublet that means "the spirits of dead people" (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Do not seek them out, or they will defile you** - "Do not seek those people out. If you do, they will defile you"
