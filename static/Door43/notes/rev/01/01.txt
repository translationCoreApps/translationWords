===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is an introduction to the book of Revelation. It explains that it is a revelation from Jesus Christ and it gives a blessing to those who read it.

  * **his servants** - This refers to people who believe in Christ.
  * **what must soon take place** - "the events that must happen soon"
  * **made it known** - "communicated it"
  * **to his servant John** - John wrote this book and was referring to himself here.  AT: "to me, John, his servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **the word of God** - Here it refers to the messages that God gave John.
  * **the one who reads aloud** - This does not refer to a specific person. It refers to anyone who reads it aloud. AT: "anyone who reads aloud"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **obey what is written in it** - "believe what is written in it and obey the commands in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the time is near** - "the things that must happen will soon happen"
