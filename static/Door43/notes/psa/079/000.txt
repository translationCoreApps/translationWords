====== Psalm 079 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 79 is a deliverance psalm and a prayer for revenge against their enemies. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]] and [[:en:obe:kt:avenge]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Destruction ==
Jerusalem has been destroyed and its people killed. God should destroy these enemy nations who do not pray to him.
