===== translationNotes: =====

  * **deep and wide** - "it is very deep and very wide." The depth and width of the sea emphasizes how large it is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **teeming** - "overflowing"
  * **innumerable** - "uncountable"
  * **both small and great** - This means creatures of all sizes. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **The ships travel there** - "The ships travel on the sea"
