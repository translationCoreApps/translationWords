===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verse 32 there is a break from the main storyline as the author tells us background information about how Jesus' predicted how he would die. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **The Jews said to him** - Here "Jews" is a synecdoche for the Jewish leaders who opposed Jesus and arrested him. AT: "The Jewish leaders said to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **It is not lawful for us to put any man to death** - According to Roman law, the Jews could not put a man to death. AT: "According to Roman law, we cannot put a person to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **so that the word of Jesus would be fulfilled** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "in order to fulfill what Jesus had said earlier" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to indicate by what kind of death he would die** - "regarding how he would die"
