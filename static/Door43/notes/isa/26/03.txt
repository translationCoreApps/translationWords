===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The mind that is stayed on you** - Here "mind" represents a person's thoughts. Also "you" refers to Yahweh. The phrase "The mind that is stayed on you" is an idiom that means "The person who continually thinks about you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Yah, Yahweh** - Yah is another name for Yahweh.
  * **Yahweh, is an everlasting rock** - Yahweh having the power to protect his people is spoken of as if he were a tall rock where people could go to escape from their enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
