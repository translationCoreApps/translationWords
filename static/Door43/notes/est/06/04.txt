===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Haman** - Translate this man's name as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:est:03:01|3:1]]
  * **the outer courtyard** - "the first courtyard from the outside"
  * **hanging Mordecai** - It can be made clear what the purpose of hanging him was. AT: "killing Mordecai by hanging him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the gallows he set up for him** - The gallows was a structure that was used to kill people by hanging them from it. See how you translated "gallows" in [[:en:bible:notes:est:05:14|5:14]]. AT: "the structure Haman set up for hanging Mordecai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **set up** - "built"
  * **What should be done for the man whom the king takes pleasure in honoring** - Here the king speaks of himself in the third person. This can be stated in first person and in active form. AT: "What should I do for the man whom I take pleasure in honoring" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the king takes pleasure in honoring** - Taking pleasure in doing something is an idiom for being glad to do something or wanting to do something. AT: "the king is glad to honor" or "the king wants to honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **said in his heart** - The heart represents the thoughts and attitudes. AT: "thought" or "said to himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Whom would the king take pleasure in honoring more than me?** - This can be expressed as a statement. "Surely there is no one whom the king would take pleasure in honoring more than me!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
