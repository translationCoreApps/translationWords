===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck down before you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "cause you to defeat the armies that attack you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but will flee before you seven ways** - "but they will run away from you in seven directions" (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **seven ways** - "in many different directions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Yahweh will command the blessing to come on you in your barns** - Moses describes Yahweh blessing the Israelites as if Yahweh were commanding a person to attack them by surprise. AT: "When Yahweh blesses you, you will be surprised by how much grain you have in your barns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **and in all that you put your hand to** - This is an idiom. AT: "and everything you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
