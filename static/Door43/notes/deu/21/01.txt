===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel.

  * **If someone is found killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "If someone finds a person whom someone else has killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **lying in the field** - The dead person is lying in the field.
  * **and it is not known who has attacked him** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and nobody knows who attacked him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they must measure to the cities** - "they must measure the distance to the cities"
  * **him who has been killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "him whom someone has killed" or "the dead body" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
