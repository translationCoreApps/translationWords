===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer uses similes to describe God's total destruction of Israel's enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

  * **make them like the whirling dust, like chaff before the wind** - Both statements speak of God destroying his enemies as if he were a strong wind easily blowing them away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **like the fire that burns the forest, and like the flame that sets the mountains on fire** - Both statements speak of God's punishment as if it were a fire, and the enemies of God are things that burn in the fire. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Chase them with your strong wind, and terrify them with your windstorm** - Both statements ask God to destroy the enemies with storms. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
