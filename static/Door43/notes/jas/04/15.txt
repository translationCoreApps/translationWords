===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Instead, you should say** - "Instead, your attitude should be"
  * **we will live** - The word "we" does not directly refer to James or his audience but is an example of how James' audience should behave.
  * **do this or that** - "do what we have planned to do"
  * **for anyone who knows to do good but does not do it, for him it is sin** - Anyone who fails to do the good he knows he should do is guilty of sin.
