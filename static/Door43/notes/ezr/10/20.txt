===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Ezra continues to list the men who married non-Jewish women.

  * **Immer ... Pashhur** - names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:36|2:37]].
  * **Hanani ... Elioenai ... Nethanel ... Elasah** - names of men (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zebadiah** - name of a man. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:08|8:08]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Harim** - name of a man. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:31|2:32]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Maaseiah** - name of a man. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:10:18|10:18]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Shemaiah ... Jehiel** - names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:12|8:13]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jozabad** - name of a man. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:33|8:33]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
