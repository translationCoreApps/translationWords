===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh's enemies will be like the glory of the pastures** - The writer compares Yahweh's enemies and the flowers blooming in the fields. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **be consumed ... disappear in the smoke** - The writer speaks of the destruction of the wicked as if they were the grass in the fields that is burned off after the harvest. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh will destroy them as fire turns the grass of the field in to smoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **is generous and gives** - These mean the same thing, and emphasize the generosity of the righteous. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
