===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The psalmist is speaking to all of God's people, so you should use the plural command form if your language has one. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])

  * **a new song** - Possible meanings are 1) "a song you have never sung before" or 2) a song no one has ever sung before.
  * **sing his praise** - "praise him with songs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
