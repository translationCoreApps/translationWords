===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This verse continues to give background information that the writer inserted to explain to the readers how the ambush defeated the Benjamites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **would turn from the battle** - "would retreat from the fight"
  * **they are defeated before us** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "we have defeated them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
