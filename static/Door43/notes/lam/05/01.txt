===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - A new poem begins. See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]].

  * **Remember** - "Think about" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **look and see our disgrace** - "look at the shameful state we are in"
  * **Our inheritance has been turned over to strangers** - "You have allowed strangers to take possession of our inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **our houses to foreigners** - "you have allowed foreigners to take possession of our houses" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **We have become orphans ... our mothers are like widows** - The people of Jerusalem have no one to protect them because the men have either died in battle or have gone into exile. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **We must pay silver for the water we drink** - "We have to pay silver to our enemies in order to drink our own water"
  * **we must pay silver to get our own wood** - "our enemies sell us our own wood" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
