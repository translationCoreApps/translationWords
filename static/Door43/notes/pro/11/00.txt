====== Proverbs 11 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Chapter 11 continues the section of the book which is attributed to Solomon and is filled mainly with individual proverbs. 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Themes ==

There are individual proverbs that run along common themes, often using contrasting elements: wise/foolish, money, lazy/diligent, truth telling, wicked/righteous, sluggard, pride/humility, integrity/crookedness. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:wise]], [[:en:obe:kt:foolish]], [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and  [[:en:obe:kt:righteous]])
