===== translationNotes: =====


  * **with a false heart** - Here "heart" represents intentions or plans. AT: "she planned to deceive someone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **She was loud and wayward** - "She talked loudly and acted in the ways she wished to" 
  * **her feet did not stay at home** - The phrase "her feet" represent the woman. AT: "she did not stay at home" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **she waited in ambush** - Here the woman is spoken of as if she were preparing to physically trap a person or an animal. Also, the idea of trapping someone here represents persuading someone to commit sin. AT: "she waited to trap someone" or "she waited to find someone she could persuade to sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
