===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Pi Hahiroth ... Migdol ... Baal Zephon** - These are towns on Egypt's eastern border. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **You are to camp** - Here the word "You" is plural and refers to Moses and the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **Pharaoh will say about the Israelites, 'They are wandering in the land. The wilderness has closed in on them.'** - This can be stated as an indirect quote. AT: "Pharaoh will say the Israelites are wandering in the land and the wilderness has closed in on them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **The wilderness has closed in on them** - Pharaoh speaks of the wilderness as a person who has trapped the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
