===== translationNotes: =====

  * **brought among his family members a Midianite woman** - It is implied that he brought her among his camp to have sex with her. The full meaning of this statement can be made clear. AT: "brought a Midianite woman to the Israelite camp to sleep with her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in the sight of Moses and all the community of the people of Israel** - Here "in the sight of" is an idiom that means they heard about it, or found out about it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Eleazar** - This is the name of Aaron's son. See how you translated it in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:01|3:2]].
