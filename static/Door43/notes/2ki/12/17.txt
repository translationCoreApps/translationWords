===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Hazael king of Aram attacked ... Hazael then turned to attack** - This refers to Hazael's army as well as to Hazael. AT: "Hazael king of Aram and his army attacked ... Then they turned to attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Hazael** - This is the name of the king of the country of Syria. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **took it** - "defeated and took control of it"
  * **Jehoshaphat and Jehoram and Ahaziah, his fathers** - These men were previous kings of Judah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **set apart** - "dedicated"
  * **the gold that was found in the storerooms** - "the gold that was stored in the storerooms"
  * **Then Hazael went away from Jerusalem** - The gifts Joash gave Hazael convinced him not to attack Jerusalem. This can be made explicit. AT: "So Hazael stopped attacking Jerusalem and left"
