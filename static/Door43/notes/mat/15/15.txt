===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus explains the parable that he told in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:15:12|15:13-14]].

  * **to us** - "to us disciples"
  * **Are you also still without understanding?** - Jesus uses a question to rebuke the disciples for not understanding the parable. Also, the word "you" is emphasized. Jesus cannot believe his own disciples do not understand. AT: "I am disappointed that you, my disciples, still do not understand what I teach!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Do you not see ... into the latrine?** - Jesus uses a question to rebuke the disciples for not understanding the parable. AT: "Surely you understand ... into the latrine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the mouth passes** - "the mouth goes"
  * **latrine** - This a polite term for the place where people bury body waste.
