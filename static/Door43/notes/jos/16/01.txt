===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the tribe of Joseph** - The "tribe of Joseph" consisted of the tribes of Joseph's two sons, Manasseh and Ephraim. Since half of the tribe of Manasseh had settled east of the Jordan, this phrase refers to the tribe of Ephraim and the other half of the tribe of Manasseh. AT: "the tribe of Ephraim and the other half of the tribe of Manasseh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Luz ... Ataroth** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Archites** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
