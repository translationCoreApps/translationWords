===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Your servant used to keep his father's sheep** - David spoke of himself as "your servant" to show respect to Saul. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:17:31|17:32]]. AT: "I, your servant, used to keep my father's sheep"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **used to keep his father's sheep** - "used to take care of his father's sheep"
  * **a bear** - A bear is a large animal with thick fur and long claws and that walks on four legs but can stand on two legs as a person does.
  * **chased after him and attacked him** - Here "him" refers to the lion or bear. Some languages would use the word "it" instead of "him."
  * **and rescued it out of his mouth** - Here "it" refers to the lamb.
  * **he rose up against me** - Here "rose up against" is an idiom that refers to attacking. AT: "it attacked me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **caught him by his beard** - The "beard" refers to the lion's mane or the hair on the bear's face.
