===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the unfaithful people of Israel.

  * **that have been assigned to you** - This can be stated in active form. The exact meaning in Hebrew is unclear. AT: "that is your lot" or "that is your inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **In these things should I take pleasure?** - Yahweh uses a question to scold the people. AT: "Certainly, these things do not please me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
