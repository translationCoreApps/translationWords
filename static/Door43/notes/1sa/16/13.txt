===== translationNotes: =====
  * **the horn of oil** - The term "horn" was sometimes used to refer to a "flask" that was shaped like a horn and was used for holding water or oil. A flask of oil was used for anointing a king. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:16:01|16:1]].
  * **Samuel rose up and went** - It is implied that he rose up after they sat down to eat. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The Spirit of Yahweh rushed on David** - The phrase "rushed on" means Yahweh's Spirit influenced David. In this case it means he enabled David to fulfill whatever Yahweh wanted him to do. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:10:05|10:6]].
