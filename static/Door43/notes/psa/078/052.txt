===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer continues to describe what God did for the people of Israel.

  * **like sheep ... like a flock** - The writer speaks about the Israelites as if they were sheep. This means God cared for and protected the people like a shepherd does his sheep. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **overwhelmed** - "completely covered"
