===== translationNotes: =====


  * **who worship you** - Here the word "worship" implies shouting and blowing horns. These were common acts of worship during Israelite festivals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they walk** - Here people living their lives is spoken of as if they were walking. AT: "they live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in the light of your face** - The writer speaks of Yahweh acting favorably towards them as if Yahweh's face shone a light on them. AT: "knowing that you act favorably towards them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **in your name** - Here "name" represents the person. AT: "in you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in your righteousness they exalt you** - The abstract noun "righteousness" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "they exalt you because you always do what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
