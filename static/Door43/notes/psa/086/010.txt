===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wonderful things** - "very good things that amaze me"
  * **you only are God** - "you are the only God"
  * **Teach me your ways, Yahweh. Then I will walk in your truth** - How God wants people to live is spoken of as if it were his ways or roads. A person who obeys what God wants is spoken of as if he were walking on God's way or road. AT: "Teach me your truth, Yahweh. Then I will obey what you say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Unite my heart to reverence you** - Here "heart" represents a person's thoughts, emotions, and motives. Asking God to cause a person to respect him completely is spoken of as if the person's heart is in many pieces and God unites them together. AT: "Cause me to respect you sincerely with all my heart" or "Cause me to respect you sincerely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will praise you with my whole heart** - Here "heart" represents a person's thoughts, emotions, and motives. AT: "I will praise you completely and sincerely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will glorify your name** - Here "name" represents the whole person. AT: "I will glorify you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
