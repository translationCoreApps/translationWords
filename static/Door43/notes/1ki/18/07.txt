===== translationNotes: =====

  * **master Elijah** - Here the word "master" is used as a term of respect. 
  * **Go tell your master, 'Look, Elijah is here.'** - Here the word "master" refers to Ahab.
  * **Look** - The word "Look" here adds emphasis to what follows.
