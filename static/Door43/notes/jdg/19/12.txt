===== translationNotes: =====

  * **turn aside into** - This means to take a break from their journey and stop at a place along the route. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:19:10|19:11]]. AT: "stop at" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Come, let us** - This is an idiom used to make a suggestion. AT: "I suggest that we" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
