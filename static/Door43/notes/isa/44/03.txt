===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **I will pour water on the thirsty ground, and flowing streams on the dry ground** - Yahweh speaks of giving his Spirit to the people of Israel as if he were causing rain to fall and streams to flow on dry ground. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the thirsty ground** - Dry ground is spoken of as if it were a thirsty person. AT: "the dry ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **I will pour my Spirit on your offspring** - Yahweh speaks of giving his Spirit to the people as if his Spirit were liquid that he pours out on them. AT: "I will give my Spirit to your offspring" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and my blessing on your children** - The verb may be supplied from the previous phrase. AT: "and I will pour out my blessing on your children" or "and I will give my blessing to your children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **They will spring up among the grass, like willows by the streams of water** - The people of Israel being prosperous and multiplying is spoken of as if they were plants that grow because they have plenty of water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **willows** - A willow is a tree with thin branches that grows near the water.
