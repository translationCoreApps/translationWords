===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zilpah** - This is the name of Leah's female servant. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:29:23|29:24]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who were born to him in Paddan Aram** - It is implied that this does not include Benjamin who was born in the land of Canaan near Bethlehem. It only mentions Paddan Aram since that is where most of them were born. The full meaning of this can be made explicit. AT: "who were born to him in Paddan Aram, except Benjamin who was born in the land of Canaan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Jacob came to Isaac** - Here "came" can be stated as "went." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **Mamre** - This was another name for the city of Hebron. It may have been named after Mamre, the friend of Abraham who lived there. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:13:16|13:18]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Kiriath Arba** - This is the name of a city. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:23:01|23:02]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
