===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - God continues speaking to Moses.

  * **You must put these** - Here "these" refers to the parts of the sacrifice mentioned in the previous verses.
  * **it will be an offering made to me by fire** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "burn it as an offering to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
