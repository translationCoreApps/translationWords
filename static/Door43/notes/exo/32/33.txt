===== translationNotes: =====

  * **that person I will blot out of my book** - The phrase "that person" represents "that person's name." AT: "I will erase that person's name from my book" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **my book** - This refers to the book of Yahweh that Moses spoke of in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:32:30|32:32]].
  * **But on the day that I punish them, I will punish them** - On the day that God decides to punish them, it will be clear that it is God who is judging them.
  * **Yahweh sent a plague on the people** - This plague may have been a serious illness. AT: "Yahweh made the people very sick"
  * **they had made the calf, the one that Aaron made** - Even though Aaron made the calf, the people were also guilty because they told Aaron to do it. AT: "they told Aaron to make the calf"
