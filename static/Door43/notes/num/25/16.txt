===== translationNotes: =====

  * **with their deceitfulness** - The abstract noun "deceitfulness" can be stated as a verb. AT: "by deceiving you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **They led you into evil** - "They persuaded you to do this evil thing"
  * **in the case of Peor ... in the matter of Peor** - Both of these phrases mean that these things happened at Mount Peor. 
  * **Peor** - Peor was the name of a mountain. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:num:23:28|23:28]].
  * **who was killed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "whom Phinehas killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
