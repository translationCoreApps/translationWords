===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Bishlam ... Mithredath ... Tabeel ... Shimshai** - names of men (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **The letter** - This is the letter spoken of in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:04:04|4:6]].
  * **Aramaic** - the language used in that area at the time for official business
  * **and translated** - into Persian
  * **Rehum** - man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:02:01]].
