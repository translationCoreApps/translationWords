===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Indeed, look at your enemies, Yahweh** - Many texts do not have these words.
  * **They will perish** - "They will die" or "You will kill them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **All those who do evil will be scattered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you will scatter all those who do evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will be scattered** - Many texts read, "have been scattered."
  * **be scattered** - "driven away"
