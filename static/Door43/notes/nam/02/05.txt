===== translationNotes: =====

  * **dash you to pieces** - Translate this the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:nam:02:01|2:1]].
  * **calls his officers** - Possible meaning: 1) "summons his officers" or 2) "thinks of his officers"
  * **they ... their ... they** - the soldiers who will attack Nineveh
  * **in their march** - "as they march"
