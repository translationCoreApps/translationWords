====== 2 Kings 10 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

God's prophecy against the family of Ahab was fulfilled. Jehu killed all of Ahab's descendants and all the worshipers of Baal. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]] and [[:en:obe:kt:fulfill]])
