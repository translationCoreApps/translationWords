===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **satisfy you in regions where there is no water** - "Water" represents all they need for an abundant life even when their surroundings do not have enough. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You will be like a watered garden** - "A watered garden" represents abundance and plenty so they will have all they need. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like a spring of water, whose waters never fail** - "A spring of water" represents a source of abundance in a land where water is precious. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
