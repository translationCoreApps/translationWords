===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David's song to Yahweh continues. He uses parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **From the lightning before him ... lightning bolts and dispersed them** - David continues describing Yahweh, whom he compares to a storm, coming to save him from his enemies. This emphasizes God's power and anger towards David's enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **From the lightning before him coals of fire fell** - Possible meanings are 1) "Out of his bright light he sent burning coals" or 2) "From his brightness he sent lightning"
  * **The Most High shouted. He shot arrows** - David describes Yahweh doing these actions a person would do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **He shot arrows ... lightning bolts** - David compares the lightning from Yahweh's storm to arrows that a soldier would use. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
