===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Consecrate yourselves** - This refers to a special preparation of being religiously clean before Yahweh.
  * **Yahweh will do wonders** - Yahweh will be doing miracles for all to see and experience.
  * **Take up the ark** - This is referring to the levites picking up the ark for the purpose of carrying it from one location to another.
