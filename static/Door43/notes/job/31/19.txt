===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Job continues describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment (described in [[:en:bible:notes:job:31:22|31:22]]), but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])

  * **if his heart has not blessed me because he has not been warmed with the wool of my sheep,** - AT: "I have given such people woolen clothing that has kept them warm, and so they have blessed me; but if I had not done that," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I have lifted up my hand against** - AT: "I have threatened to harm" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **in the city gate** - This is where the important men of the city would gather to make decisions.
