===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their wives ... sons ... daughters** - belonging to David and his men
  * **it was burned, and their wives ... daughters were taken captive** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "they saw that someone had burned it and taken their wives ... captive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the people that were with him** - These were mainly his army of men.
