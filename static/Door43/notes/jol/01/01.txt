===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - God speaks through Joel to the people of Israel using poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **the word of Yahweh that came** - "the word that Yahweh God spoke" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this.
  * **Pethuel** - Joel's father (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Has this ever before happened in your days or in the days of your ancestors?** - AT: "This has never happened before either to you or your ancestors" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
