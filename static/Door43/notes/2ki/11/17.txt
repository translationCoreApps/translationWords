===== translationNotes: =====

  * **also between the king and the people** - "also made a covenant between the king and the people"
  * **all the people of the land** - This is an exaggeration to show that a large group of people tore down the temple of Baal. AT: a large number of the people of the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **the house of Baal** - "the temple of Baal"
  * **Mattan** - This is the name of a male priest. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
