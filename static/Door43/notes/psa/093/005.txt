===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **solemn** - very serious
  * **are very trustworthy** - "always stay the same" or "never change" (UDB)
  * **holiness adorns your house** - The psalmist speaks of Yahweh's house as if it were a woman wearing beautiful clothing or jewelry and of Yahweh's holiness as the beautiful clothing or jewelry. AT: "your house is beautiful because you are holy" or "your holiness makes your house beautiful the way beautiful clothes and jewelry make a woman more beautiful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **your house** - the temple in Jerusalem
  * **adorns** - makes beautiful
