===== translationNotes: =====

  * **within your gates** - Here "gates" represents the city or town. AT: "within your community" or "inside your city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the unclean ... persons** - A person who is not acceptable for God's purposes is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the clean persons** - A person who is acceptable for God's purposes is spoken of as if the person were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a gazelle or a deer** - These are wild animals with long thin legs that can run quickly. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:12:15|12:15]].
  * **you must not eat its blood** - "you must not eat the meat with blood in it"
