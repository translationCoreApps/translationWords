===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah continues speaking.

  * **enter the gardens** - This is a place where people would go to worship idols. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the one in the middle** - This expression is unclear. Some modern versions understand it to refer to the leader of the idol worshipers.
  * **Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:14:21|14:22]].
