===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jeremiah continues to list all the nations that were under God's judgment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **the sea** - This is a reference to the Mediterranean Sea.
  * **the ones who cut the hair on the sides of their heads** - This is probably a reference to Arabians who lived in the desert, people who cut their hair short in order to honor a pagan god. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
