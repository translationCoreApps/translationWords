===== translationNotes: =====

  * **gnats** - small flying insects like flies but smaller
  * **swarms** - large flying groups
  * **hail** - ice that falls from the sky like rain
  * **He destroyed ... he broke** - God caused the hail, rain, and lightning to ruin the vines and trees. AT: "He caused it to destroy ... and to break" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
