===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Are you still sleeping and taking your rest?** - Jesus uses a question to scold the disciples for going to sleep. AT: "I am disappointed that you are still sleeping and resting!" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the hour is at hand** - This is an idiom. AT: "the time has come" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and the Son of Man is being betrayed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and someone is betraying the Son of Man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the Son of Man** - Jesus is speaking about himself in the third person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **into the hands of sinners** - Here "hands" refers to power or control. AT: "and is handing him over to the power of sinners" or "and is giving him over to sinners" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **Look** - "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you"
