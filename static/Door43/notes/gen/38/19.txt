===== translationNotes: =====

  * **veil** - This was a very thin material used to cover a woman's head and face. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:38:12|38:14]].
  * **clothing of her widowhood** - "clothing that widows wear." See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:38:12|38:14]].
  * **from the flock** - "from his flock"
  * **Adullamite** - "Adullam" is the name of a village where Hiram lived. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:38:01|38:1]]. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:38:01|38:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **receive the pledge** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "take back the pledge"(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from the woman's hand** - Here "hand" emphasizes that they were in here possession. The woman's hand refers to the woman. AT: "from the woman" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
