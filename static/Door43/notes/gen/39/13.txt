===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It came about ... that she called** - "Then ... she called." The phrase "it came about" is used here to mark the next event in the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **and had fled outside** - "and had quickly ran out of the house"
  * **the men of her house** - "the men who worked in her house"
  * **See** - "Listen." Potiphar's wife uses this word to get the servants' attention.
  * **He came in to me to sleep with me** - Here Potiphar's wife is accusing Joseph of trying to seize her and sleep with her. 
  * **It came about when he heard me scream, that he** - "When he heard me scream, he." The phrase "it came about" is used here to mark the next event in the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
