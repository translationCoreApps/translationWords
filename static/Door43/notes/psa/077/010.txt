===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I said ... the right hand of the Most High** - Possible meanings are 1) "I said to myself ... the right hand of the Most High" or 2) "I said to God ... the right hand of you, the Most High"
  * **the changing of the right hand of the Most High toward us** - The "right hand" is a metonym for power. AT: "the Most High no longer uses his power to help us"
