===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **a song at the dedication of the temple** - "This song was sung when the temple was dedicated"
  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **you have raised me up** - The writer speaks of God rescuing him and keeping him from dying as if God had drawn him up from a deep well. AT: "you rescued me" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **brought up my soul from Sheol** - Since "Sheol" was the place where dead people go, it refers to death. AT: "kept me from dying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **brought up my soul** - Here "my soul" refers to the writer. AT: "brought me up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **from going down to the grave** - The "grave" represents death. AT: "from dying" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
