===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He dreamed** - "Jacob had a dream"
  * **set up on the earth** - "with the bottom of it touching the ground"
  * **reached to heaven** - This refers to the place where God lives.
  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **Yahweh stood above it** - Possible meanings are 1) "Yahweh was standing at the top of the stairway" or 2) "Yahweh was standing next to Jacob"
  * **Abraham your father** - Here "father" means "ancestor." AT: "Abraham your ancestor" or "Abraham your forefather"
