===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the word "him" refers to Paul.
  * **General Information:** - The word "we" is inclusive and refers to Paul and his companions including Luke, the author of Acts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **General Information:** - The first use of "us" refers to the people in Macedonia. The second use of "us" refers to Paul and his companions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **General Information:** - The word "them" refers to the people of Macedonia.

  * **A vision appeared to Paul** - "Paul saw a vision from God" or "Paul had a vision from God"
  * **calling him** - "begging him" or "inviting him"
  * **Come over into Macedonia** - The phrase "Come over" is used because Macedonia is across the sea from Troas.
