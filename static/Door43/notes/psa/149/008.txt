===== translationNotes: =====

  * **chains** - These are made of heavy metal and restrict the movement of prisoners.
  * **shackles** - a pair of chains or bands with a chain between that limits the movement of both a person's hands or feet.
  * **execute the judgment that is written** - do what Yahweh had Moses write that people should do with those who are guilty (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
