===== translationNotes: =====

  * **rose up and went from his dead wife** - "got up and left his wife's body"
  * **the sons of Heth** - Here "sons" stands for those who descended from Heth. AT: "the descendants of Heth" (UDB) or "the Hittites" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **among you** - This idea may be expressed in terms of location. "in your country" or "here"
  * **Please grant me a property** - "Sell me some land" or "Allow me to buy a piece of land"
  * **my dead** - The nominal adjective "dead" can be stated as an adjective or a verb. AT: "my dead wife" or "my wife who has died" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
