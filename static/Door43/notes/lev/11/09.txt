===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

  * **fins** - the thin, flat part that the fish uses to move through the water
  * **scales** - the small plates that cover the body of the fish
  * **all living creatures that do not have fins and scales in the ocean or rivers** - "all creatures that live in the ocean or rivers that do not have fins and scales"
  * **they must be detested by you** - Yahweh uses a strong prohibition to command the people that they must not eat these creatures. This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must detest them" or "you must completely reject them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
