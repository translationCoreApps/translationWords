===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called his relatives to eat a meal** - Eating a meal together was part of making the covenant with one another. The full meaning of this may be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Early in the morning ... returned home** - Verse 55 is the first verse of chapter 32 in the original Hebrew text, but the last verse of chapter 31 in most modern Bibles. We suggest that you follow the numbering of Bibles in your national language.
  * **blessed** - This means expressing a desire for positive and beneficial things to happen to someone.
