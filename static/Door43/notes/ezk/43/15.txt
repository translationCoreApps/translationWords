===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The hearth** - the place where sacrifices were either cooked or burnt up with fire
  * **four cubits ... twelve cubits ... fourteen cubits ... a half cubit ... a cubit** - Each long cubit was about 54 centimeters. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:5]]. AT: "about 2.2 meters ... about 6.5 meters ... about 7.6 meters ... about 27 centimeters ... about 54 centimeters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **horns** - The horns were the parts of the altar at the four corners of the altar that stuck up above the rest of the altar.
