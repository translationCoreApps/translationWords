===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they were making their hearts glad** - The phrase "making their hearts glad" is an idiom that means to have a good time with someone else. AT: "they were have a good time together" (UDB) or "they were enjoying themselves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **some men of ... surrounded the house** - Some men stood on all sides of the house.
