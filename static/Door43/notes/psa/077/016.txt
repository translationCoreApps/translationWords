===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The waters saw you ... they were afraid ... the depths trembled** - Asaph is speaking of the water as if it were a person who saw something that terrified him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **depths** - "deepest water"
  * **The clouds poured down water** - Asaph is speaking of the clouds as if they were people pouring water out of containers. AT: "Much rain fell" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **your arrows flew about** - "the lightning you made flashed like arrows" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **flew** - Use your language's word for what an arrow does after someone shoots it.
