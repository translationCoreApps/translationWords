===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Can a throne of destruction be allied with you, one who creates injustice by statute?** - This question is used to make a point. AT: "A wicked rulers who makes unjust laws is not your friend" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **a throne of destruction** - "a wicked ruler" or "corrupt judge"
  * **conspire** - secretly plan with a person something harmful or illegal
  * **take the life of** - "kill" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
