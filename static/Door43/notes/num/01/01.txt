===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this. 
  * **the first day of the second month** - "the 1st day of the 2nd month." This is the second month of the Hebrew calendar. The first day is near the middle of April on Western calendars. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_hebrewmonths]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **the second year** - "the 2nd year" or "year 2" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **Count them by name** - This means to count the men by recording their names. AT: "Count them, recording each man's name" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **twenty years old** - "20 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **record the number of men in their armed groups** - This refers to assigning the men to their military divisions.
