===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Sherebiah ... Mahli ... Hashabiah ... Merari** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **by our God's good hand** - an idiom for expressing God's goodness or provision to them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a prudent man** - This is a man of understanding and wisdom.
  * **eighteen ... twenty** - 18, 20. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Jeshaiah** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:04|8:7]].
  * **officials** - People with specific authority within a government system.
