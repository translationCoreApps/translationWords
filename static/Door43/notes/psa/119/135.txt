===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Let your face shine on your servant** - The writer speaks of Yahweh acting favorably towards him as if Yahweh's face shone a light on him. AT: "Act favorably towards your servant" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Streams of tears** - The writer speaks of his many tears as if they were streams of water. AT: "Many tears" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **observe your law** - "obey your law"
