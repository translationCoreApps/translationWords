===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the narrator tells us why the gates of Jericho are closed and locked up. 
  * **I have delivered Jericho into your hand, its king, and its trained soldiers** - Yahweh is telling Joshua that he will certainly do this by saying that he has already done it. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pastforfuture]])
  * **into your hand** - This means "into your control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
