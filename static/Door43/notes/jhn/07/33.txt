===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I am still with you for a short amount of time** - "I will remain with you for only a short period of time"
  * **then I go to him who sent me** - Here Jesus refers to God the Father, who sent him. 
  * **where I go, you will not be able to come** - "you will not be able to come to the place where I am"
