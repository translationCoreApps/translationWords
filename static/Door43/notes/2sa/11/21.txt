===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Who killed Abimelech son of Jerubbesheth?** - Joab says that David my rebuke him by asking these rhetorical questions. This can be written as a statement. AT: "Remember how Abimelech son of Jerubbesheth was killed!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Abimelech son of Jerubbesheth** - This is the name of a man. His father is also know by the name Gideon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Did not a woman cast an upper millstone on him from the wall, so that he died at Thebez?** - Joab says that David my rebuke him by asking these rhetorical questions. This can be written as a statement. AT: "Remember he died at Thebez when a woman cast an upper millstone on him from the top of the wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **from the wall** - "from the top of the city wall"
  * **millstone** - a heavy stone that would roll, used to crush grain for making bread 
  * **Thebez** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Why did you go so near the wall?** - Joab says that David my rebuke him by asking these rhetorical questions. This can be written as a statement. AT: "You should not have gone so near the wall!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
