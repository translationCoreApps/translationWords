  * **Repay no one evil for evil** - "Do not do evil things to someone who has done evil things to you"
  * **Do good things in the sight of all people** - "Do things that everyone considers to be good"
  * **as much as it lies with you, be at peace with all people** - "do whatever you can to live in peace with everyone"
