===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Arpachshad** - Arpachshad was one of Shem's sons.
  * **Peleg** - Translators may add a footnote note that says: "The name Peleg means 'division.'"
  * **the earth was divided** - This can be made active. AT: "the people of the earth divided themselves" or "the people of the earth separated from one another" or "God divided the people of the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
