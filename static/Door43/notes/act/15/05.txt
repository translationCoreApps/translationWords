===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul and Barnabas are now in Jerusalem to meet with the apostles and elders there.
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "them" refers to non-Jewish believers who were not circumcised and did not keep the Old Testament laws of God.

  * **But certain men** - Here Luke contrasts those who believe that salvation is only in Jesus to others who believe salvation is by Jesus yet also believe that circumcision is required for salvation.
  * **to keep the law of Moses** - "to obey the law of Moses"
  * **to consider this matter** - The church leaders decided to discuss whether or not Gentiles needed to be circumcised and obey the Law of Moses in order for God to save them from their sins.
