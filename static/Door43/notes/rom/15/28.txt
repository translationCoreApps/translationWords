===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and have certified this fruit to them** - Paul speaks of the money he is taking to Jerusalem as if it were fruit. AT: "and have safely delivered this offering to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I know that, when I come to you, I will come in the fullness of the blessing of Christ** - This phrase means that Christ will bless Paul and the Roman believers. AT: "And I know that when I visit you, Christ will abundantly bless us" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
