===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not sweep me away with sinners** - God destroying someone is spoken of as if he were sweeping them away like dust. AT: "Do not destroy me along with sinners" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **or my life** - The word "sweep" is understood. AT: "or sweep my life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **people who are bloodthirsty** - The word "bloodthirsty" represents a person who wants to kill other people. AT: "people who are eager to shed others' blood" or "people who are murderers" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **in whose hands there is a plot** - The phrase "in whose hands" is a way of referring to a person who is ready to do something. AT: "people who are ready to do wicked things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **a plot** - "a wicked plan"
