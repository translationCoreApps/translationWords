===== translationNotes: =====

  * **who are cursed** - Possible meanings are that 1) God will curse them or 2) they deserve God's punishment. AT: "who deserve your punishment"
  * **who wander from your commandments** - The writer speaks of disobeying God's commandments as if they were a path that can be wandered off from. AT: "who go away from the path of your commandments" or "who disobey your commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Spare me from** - "Keep me from" or "Rescue me from"
  * **and humiliation** - "and feeling ashamed and foolish"
