===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They came** - "Jesus and his disciples came"
  * **began to cast out the sellers and the buyers in the temple** - Jesus is driving these people out of the temple. This can be written clearly. AT: "began to drive the sellers and buyers out of the temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the sellers and the buyers** - "the people who were buying and selling"
