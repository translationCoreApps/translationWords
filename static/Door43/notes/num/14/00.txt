====== Numbers 14 General Notes ======

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===
== Rhetorical Questions ==
The author uses several rhetorical questions in this chapter. These questions indicate that people did not have faith in Yahweh. Yahweh also uses rhetorical questions to show the people's lack of faith. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:obe:kt:faith]])
