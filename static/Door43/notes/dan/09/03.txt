===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I turned my face to the Lord God** - "I focused my attention on the Lord God" or "I directed my thoughts toward the Lord God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I made confession of our sins** - "I confessed our sins"
  * **keeps the covenant and is faithful to love those** - "does what he said he would do in his covenant and he keeps his promise to love those"
