===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will place my tabernacle among you** - "I will put my dwelling place among you"
  * **I will not detest you** - "I will accept you"
  * **I will walk among you** - Walking among them represents living with them. "I will live with you"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I have broken the bars of your yoke** - God speaks of their slavery as if they had to wear a yoke that animals wear in order to do hard work. Breaking the bars of the yoke represents setting them free. AT: "I have set you free from the hard labor they made you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
