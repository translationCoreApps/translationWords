===== translationNotes: =====

  * **one of the sons of the prophets** - "a member of the group of prophets"
  * **by the word of Yahweh** - "the word" is metonymy for the message. AT: "as Yahweh had told him to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you have not obeyed the voice of Yahweh** - "The voice" is metonymy for what Yahweh commanded. AT: "you have not obeyed Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
