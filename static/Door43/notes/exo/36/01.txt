===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues speaking to the people.

  * **Bezalel** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:01|31:1-2]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Oholiab** - This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:31:06|31:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **every who was wise of heart** - Here "wise in heart" refers to the person who is wise. AT: "every wise person" or "every person who has wisdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **in whom Yahweh has put skill and understanding** - Here skill and understanding are spoken of as if they are something that Yahweh can place inside a person. AT: "who Yahweh has given skill and understanding" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
