===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here the words "them" and "they" refer to those who were sick.

  * **God was doing mighty deeds by the hands of Paul** - Here "hands" stands for Paul's whole person. AT: "God was causing Paul to do miracles" or "God was doing miracles through Paul" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **even handkerchiefs and aprons that had touched him were taken to the sick and** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "when they took to sick people even handkerchiefs and aprons that had touched Paul"
  * **even handkerchiefs and aprons that had touched him** - Possible meanings are 1) these were cloth items that Paul had touched or 2)  these were cloth items that Paul had worn or used. 
  * **handkerchiefs** - cloths worn around the head
  * **aprons** - clothing worn on the front of the body to protect the clothes of people
  * **the sick** - This refers to sick people. AT: "sick people" or "those who were sick" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **their illnesses were cured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "those who were sick became healthy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
