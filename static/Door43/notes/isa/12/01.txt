===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Here Isaiah continues to describe what it will be like when the king that God has chosen rules. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:11:01|11:1-2]])

  * **On that day** - It can be stated clearly what time is being referred to. AT: "At that time" or "When the king rules" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **your wrath has turned away** - God's wrath is spoken of as if it were a person that could turn away and leave. It means that God has stopped being angry. AT: "You are no longer angry with me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **God is my salvation** - God causing someone's salvation is spoken of as if God were that salvation. The abstract noun "salvation" can be expressed with the noun "savior" or the verb "save". AT: "God causes my salvation" or "God is my savior" or "God is the one who saves me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Yahweh is my strength** - God causing someone to be strong is spoken of as if Yahweh were their strength. AT: "Yahweh makes me strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and song** - The word "song" here represents what a person sings about. AT: "and the one I joyfully sing about" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He has become my salvation** - "He has saved me"
