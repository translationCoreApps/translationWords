===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone has a skin disease.

  * **a dull white** - "a faded white"
  * **rash** - Translate this word in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:13:05|13:6]].
  * **He is clean** - The person whom other people may touch is spoken of as if he were physically clean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
