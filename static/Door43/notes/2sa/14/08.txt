===== translationNotes: =====

  * **may the guilt be on me and on my father's family** - Here there is information that is implict. The "guilt" refers back to the brother killing his brother. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The king and his throne** - Here the word "throne" refers to anyone who later becomes king in David's place. AT: "The king and his descendants" or "The king and his family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
