===== translationNotes: =====

  * **praise your name** - Here "name" represents Yahweh. AT: "praise you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **things planned long ago** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "things you planned long ago" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in perfect faithfulness** - The abstract noun "faithfulness" can be stated as "faithful." AT: "because you are perfectly faithful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the city** - This is not referring to a certain city. It means cities in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **a fortress of strangers** - "a fortress belonging to foreigners"
  * **a city of ruthless nations** - Here "city" and "nations" represent the people who live there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
