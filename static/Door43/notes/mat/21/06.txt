  * **cloaks** - These were outer clothing or long coats.
  * **crowd spread their cloaks on the road, and others cut branches from the trees and spread them in the road** - These are ways to show honor to Jesus as he was entering Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
