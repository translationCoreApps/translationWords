===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There remains yet the youngest** - "There is still my youngest son"
  * **we will not sit down** - It is understood that they were waiting to sit down and eat. AT: "we will not sit down to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Now ... appearance** - The word "Now" is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here the narrator tells about a new person in the story.
  * **this son was ruddy** - The word "ruddy" means David was healthy looking.
