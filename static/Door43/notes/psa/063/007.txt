===== translationNotes: =====

  * **in the shadow of your wings I rejoice** - The writer speaks of God as if he were a bird protecting its young under his wings. AT: "I rejoice because you protect me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I cling to you** - "I need you" or "I depend on you"
  * **your right hand supports me** - Here, the right hand is used as a symbol of strength and power. AT: "you support me" or "you lift me up" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
