===== translationNotes: =====

  * **second time** - "2nd time" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
  * **on my side** - To "be on someone's side" means to be loyal to them and supportive of them. AT: "loyal to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **listen** - to hear and obey
  * **to my voice** - Here Jehu's "voice" refer to what he says. AT: "to what I say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you must take the heads ... and come to me** - It is implied that they are to bring the heads of the Ahab's descendant and present them to Jehu. AT: "you must take the heads ... and bring them to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **you must take the heads of the men of your master's descendants** - This refers to killing them and removing their heads. AT: "kill your master's descendants and cut off their heads" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **seventy in number ... seventy persons** - "70 in number ... 70 persons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **who were bringing them up** - This means that they were supervising them and teaching them. AT: "who were raising them" or "who were supervising them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **and sent them to Jehu** - This means that they sent people to deliver the baskets to Jehu. AT: "and sent people to take them to Jehu" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
