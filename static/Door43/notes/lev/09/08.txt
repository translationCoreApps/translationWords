===== translationNotes: =====

  * **sons of Aaron presented the blood to him** - This implies that they caught the blood in a bowl as the blood drained from the animal. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **horns of the altar** - This refers to the corners of the altar. They are shaped like the horns of an ox. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:04:06|4:7]].
  * **at the base of the altar** - "at the bottom of the altar"
