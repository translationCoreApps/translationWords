===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

  * **Yahweh your God ... who went before you** - Moses reminds them of all the ways Yahweh had gone ahead of Israel during all their travels in the past.
  * **make camp** - "put up your tents"
