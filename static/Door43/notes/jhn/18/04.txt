===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus begins to speak with the soldiers, officers, and Pharisees. 

  * **Then Jesus, who knew all the things that were happening to him** - "Then Jesus, who knew everything that was about to happen to him"
  * **Jesus of Nazareth** - "Jesus, the man from Nazareth" 
  * **I am** - The word "he" is implied in the text. AT: "I am he" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **who betrayed him** - "who handed him over"
