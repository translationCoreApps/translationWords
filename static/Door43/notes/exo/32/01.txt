===== translationNotes: =====


  * **the people saw** - Understanding something is spoken of as seeing it. AT: "the people realized"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Come, make us an idol** - The word "come" strengthens the force of the command following it. The people were demanding that Aaron make an idol for them.
  * **go before us** - "lead us" or "be our leader"
  * **bring them to me** - The word "them" refers to the golden rings.
