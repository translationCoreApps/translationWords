===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All authority has been given to me** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "My Father has given me all authority" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **in heaven and on earth** - Here "heaven" and "earth" are used together to mean everyone and everything in heaven and earth. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **of all the nations** - Here "nations" refers to the people. AT: "of all the people in every nation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **into the name** - Here "name" refers to authority. AT: "by the authority" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Father ... Son** - These are important titles that describe the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
