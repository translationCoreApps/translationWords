===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the third year of his reign** - "After he had ruled for 2 years" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **The army** - This may refer to the leaders of the army. AT: "The officers of the army" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the wealth of the splendor of his kingdom** - These words have similar meaning and emphasize how great his kingdom was. AT: "the great wealth of his kingdom" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **the honor of the glory of his greatness** - These words have similar meaning and emphasize how great he was. AT: "the splendor of his greatness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **180** - "one hundred and eighty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
