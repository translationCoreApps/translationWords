===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In 10:37 is a quotation from the prophet Isaiah in the Old Testament.

  * **do not throw away your confidence, which has a great reward** - Rejecting a promise is spoken of as if it were throwing away something worthless or useless. AT: "do not stop confidently trusting in God, who will reward you greatly" or "continue to trust completely in God, who will reward you greatly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **For in a very little while** - You can make this explicit. AT: "As God said in the scriptures, 'For in a very little while" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **in a very little while** - "very soon"
