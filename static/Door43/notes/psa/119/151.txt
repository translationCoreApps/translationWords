===== translationNotes: =====

  * **all your commandments are trustworthy** - The abstract noun "trustworthy" can be translated using the verb "trust." AT: "I can trust your commandments" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **covenant decree** - Other possible meanings are "testimonies" or "laws." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:078:005|78:5]]
  * **you had set them in place** - The psalmist speaks of Yahweh's commands as if they were posts set in the ground. AT: "given them for people to obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
