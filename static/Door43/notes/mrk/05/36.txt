===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The UDB uses a verse bridge to combine verses 37 and 38. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_versebridge]])

  * **Just believe** - If necessary, you can state what Jesus is commanding Jairus to believe. AT: "Just believe I can make you daughter live" 
  * **He did not ... he saw** - In these verses the word "he" refers to Jesus.
  * **to accompany him** - "to come with him." It may be helpful to state where they were going. AT: "to accompany him to Jairus' house" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **a lot of weeping and wailing** - This phrase describes the commotion that Jesus saw. The words "weeping" and "wailing" share similar meanings and together they add emphasis. It is also a metonym for the people who were doing it. AT: "people who were weeping as they cried loudly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
