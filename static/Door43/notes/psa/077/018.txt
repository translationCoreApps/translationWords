===== translationNotes: =====

  * **thunderous voice** - "voice, which was as loud as thunder" or "very loud"
  * **lightning lit up the world** - "lightning lit up everything all around" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **Your path ... your way ... your footprints** - Asaph is speaking of Yahweh as if Yahweh were a person with feet. While this is personification, it should be translated literally if possible. [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]
  * **your footprints were not seen** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one saw your footprints"
  * **by the hand of** - "by the work of"
