===== translationNotes: =====

  * **will be thirsty again** - "will need to drink water again" 
  * **the water that I will give him will become a fountain of water in him** - Here the word "fountain" is a metaphor for life-giving water. AT: "the water that I will give him will become like a spring of water in him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **eternal life** - Here "life" refers to the "spiritual life" that only God can give.
