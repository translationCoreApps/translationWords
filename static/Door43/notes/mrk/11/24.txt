===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Therefore I say to you** - "So I tell you" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_connectingwords]])
  * **it will be yours** - It is understood that this will happen because God will provide what you ask for. This can be stated clearly. AT: "God will do it for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When you stand and pray** - It is common in Hebrew culture to stand when praying to God. AT: "When you pray" (UDB) 
  * **whatever you have against anyone** - "whatever grudge you have against anyone." Here the word "whatever" refers to any grudge you hold against someone for sinning against you or any anger you have against someone.
