===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling a parable to Ezekiel about a cooking pot that represents Jerusalem. This parable continues through [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:24:14|24:14]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **until my fury rests from you** - This speaks of Yahweh punishing the people in his anger as if it were his "fury" that were punishing them. AT: "until I am finished punishing you and am no longer furious with you" or "until I have punished you and I am longer angry with you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
