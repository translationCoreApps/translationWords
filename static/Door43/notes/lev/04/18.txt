===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He will put** - "The priest will put"
  * **horns of the altar** - This refers to the corners of the altar. They are shaped like the horns of an ox. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:04:06|4:7]].
  * **he will pour out all the blood** - "he will pour out the rest of the blood"
  * **all the fat from it and burn it** - "all the fat from the bull and burn the fat"
