===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Whenever the ark set out** - Here the ark is spoken of as if were a person travelling. The ark was really being carried by men. AT: "Whenever the people carrying the ark set out" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **Rise up, Yahweh** - Here the phrase "rise up" is a request for Yahweh to act, in this case Moses is asking him to scatter their enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Make those who hate you run from you** - Here Moses speaks about Yahweh causing their enemies to flee from the people of Israel as if they were fleeing from Yahweh himself. AT: "Make those who hate you run away from your ark and your people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Whenever the ark stopped** - Here the ark is spoken of as if were a person travelling. The ark was really being carried by men. AT: "Whenever the people carrying the ark stopped" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **many tens of thousands** - This refers to people. "many tens of thousands of people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
