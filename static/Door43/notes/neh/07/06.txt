===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These are the people of the province** - "These are the descendants of this region"
  * **who went up** - "who returned" or "who came up"
  * **whom Nebuchadnezzar the king of Babylon took into exile** - "whom Nebuchadnezzar, Ruler of Babylon took away from their home country"
  * **Nebuchadnezzar ... Zerubbabel ... Jeshua ... Nehemiah ... Azariah ... Raamiah ... Nahamani ... Mordecai ... Bilshan ... Mispereth ... Bigvai ... Nehum ... Baanah** - These are the names of males. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]). 
  * **The number of the men** - A census had been taken when the Israelites first returned to Jerusalem after the exile. The numbers represent how many men belonged to each family group. This sentence introduces the information in the next verse.
