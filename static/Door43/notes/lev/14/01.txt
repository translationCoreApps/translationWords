===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh tells Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone is cleansed of a skin disease.

  * **the day of his cleansing** - This refers to the day on which the priest declares the person to be ritually clean.
  * **He must be brought to the priest** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Someone must bring him to the priest" or "He must go to the priest" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
