===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The wage ... the profit** - These terms normally refer to the money a worker earns. Here they represents the results of either doing what is right or doing what is wrong. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **There is a path to life for the one who follows discipline** - "The person who obeys wise instruction will have a long and happy life"
  * **but the one who rejects correction is led astray** - "but the one who does not obey wise instruction will not have a good life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
