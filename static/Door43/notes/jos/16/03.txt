===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Japhletites** - This is the name of a people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Lower Beth Horon ... Gezer** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the tribes of Joseph, Manasseh and Ephraim** - "the tribes of Manasseh and Ephraim, the sons of Joseph"
  * **received their inheritance** - The land that the tribes of Manasseh and Ephraim possessed is spoken of as if it were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "received this land as their inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
