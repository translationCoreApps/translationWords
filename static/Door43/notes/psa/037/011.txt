===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the meek** - This refers to the people who are meek. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **will inherit the land** - The possession of the land is spoken of it had been received as an inheritance. AT: "will receive the land as their own possession" or "will be allowed to live safely in the land." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:037:008|37:9]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **The wicked man** - This does not refer to a specific man. It refers to wicked men in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **the righteous** - This refers to the people who are righteous. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **gnashes his teeth against him** - The wicked man hates the righteous people so much that he grinds his teeth together to show his anger. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **his day is coming** - It is implied that "his day" will be a day judgment. AT: "the day is coming when Yahweh will judge and punish him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
