===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He executes justice** - "He decides matters fairly" (UDB)
  * **for the oppressed** - This nominal adjective can be stated as adjectives. AT: "for oppressed people" or "for those whom other people oppress" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **to the hungry** - This nominal adjective can be stated as adjectives. AT: "to hungry people" or "to those who are hungry" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **opens the eyes of the blind** - Causing a blind person to see is spoken of as if Yahweh were opening the person's eyes. AT: "causes the blind to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the blind** - This nominal adjective can be stated as adjectives. AT: "blind people" or "those who are blind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **Yahweh raises up those who are bowed down** - Yahweh helping someone is spoken of as if he were physically helping someone to stand up. AT: "Yahweh helps those who are discouraged" or "Yahweh helps those who are weak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who are bowed down** - This action is a sign of distress or mourning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
