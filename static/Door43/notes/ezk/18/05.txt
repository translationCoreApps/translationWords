===== translationNotes: =====

  * **has not eaten on the mountains ... has not lifted up his eyes to the idols** - These phrases refer to idol worship. People ate the food at the mountain shrines to honor the idols. The phrase "lifted up his eyes" refers to looking at the idols and honoring them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
