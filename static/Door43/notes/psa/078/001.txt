===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **A maschil of Asaph** - "This is a maschil that Asaph wrote." See how "a psalm of Asaph" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:050:001|50:1]].
  * **A maschil** - This may refer to a style of music. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1]].
  * **my teaching** - "me as I teach you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the words of my mouth** - The word "mouth" is a metonym for the person who speaks them. AT: "my words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **open my mouth in parables** - "open my mouth and tell parables" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **sing about** - "tell." See how "pours out" is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:psa:019:001|19:2]].
  * **hidden things** - If your language has a word for sayings that are purposely difficult to understand, you might use it here.
