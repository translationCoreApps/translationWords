===== translationNotes: =====

  * **their grain offering and their drink offerings** - These offerings were to be offed with the bull, the ram, and the lambs. AT: "the grain offerings and the drink offerings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
  * **as were commanded** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "as Yahweh commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **its grain offering, and their drink offerings** - The grain offering was to be offered with the burnt offering and the drink offerings were to be offered with both the sin offering and the burnt offering. AT: "along with the grain offering and the drink offerings that accompany them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
