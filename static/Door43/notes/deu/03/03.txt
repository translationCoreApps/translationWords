===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and all his people were put under our control** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and Yahweh put all of Og's people under our control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **we struck him dead** - The word "him" is a metonym for all of his people. AT: "we killed all of his people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **not one of his people remained** - "all of his people were dead" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **there was not one city that we did not take** - "we took every one of the cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **sixty cities** - "60 cities" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the region of Argob** - This is the name of a region within Bashan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
