===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to tell the people of Israel Yahweh's words as if the Israelites are one person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **You will not test Yahweh** - Here "test" means to challenge Yahweh and force him to prove himself.
  * **Massah** - This is the name of a place in the desert. The translator may add a footnote that says: "The name 'Massah' means 'testing.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
