===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh is close** - Here "is close" means "ready to help." AT: "Yahweh is always ready to help" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the brokenhearted** - Deep sadness is spoken of as if the person's heart is broken. AT: "people who are very sad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **those who are crushed in spirit** - People who are deeply discouraged are spoken of as if their spirits are crushed. AT: "people who are deeply discouraged" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the righteous** - This refers to people who are righteous. AT: "the righteous people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **He keeps all his bones, not one of them will be broken** - Here "all his bones" is literal, but it also implies that Yahweh takes care of the entire person. AT: "He provides complete protection for him, he will not be harmed in any way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
