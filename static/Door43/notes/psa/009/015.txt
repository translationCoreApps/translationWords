===== translationNotes: =====



  * **The nations have sunk down into the pit that they made** - People dig pits in order to catch animals that fall into them. Here digging a pit represents making plans to destroy people. AT: "The nations are like people who dig a pit for others and then fall into it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **their feet are caught in the net that they hid** - People hide nets in order to catch animals that get caught in them. Here hiding a net represents making plans to destroy people. AT: "they are like people who hide a net and get trapped in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ensnared** - "trapped" or "caught." This represents being destroyed. AT: "destroyed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
