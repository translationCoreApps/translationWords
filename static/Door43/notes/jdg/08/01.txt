===== translationNotes: =====


  * **What is this you have done to us?** - The people of the tribe of Ephraim were rebuking Gideon with this rhetorical question for not including them in his army. This can be expressed as a statement. AT: "You have not treated us fairly." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **against Midian** - Here "Midian" represents the Midianite army. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **they argued with him with force** - "they argued angrily with him" or "they rebuked him severely"
