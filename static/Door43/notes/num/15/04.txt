===== translationNotes: =====

  * **a burnt offering** - This refers to the offerings spoken of in [[:en:bible:notes:num:15:04|15:3]].
  * **a tenth of an ephah** - An ephah is a unit of volume equal to about 22 liters. AT: "about 2 liters" or "two liters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **one-fourth of a hin** - A hin is a unit of volume equal to about 3.7 liters. AT: "about 1 liter" or "one liter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
