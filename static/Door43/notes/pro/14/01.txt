===== translationNotes: =====

  * **builds her house** - "builds up her house" or "makes her house better"
  * **house** - Possible meanings are 1) this may refer to her actual house, that is the building she lives in or 2) this may refer to her family. (UDB)
  * **with her own hands** - "Hands" represent the actions she does. AT: "by herself" or "by the way she behaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **The one who ... the one who** - "The person who ... the person who"
  * **walks uprightly** - "Walks" represents the conduct of life. AT: "conducts his life in a just and honest way" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **despises** - "grossly disrespects" or "indicates he hates"
  * **in his ways despises him** - The word "his" refers to the dishonest man and "him" refers to Yahweh.
