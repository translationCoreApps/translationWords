===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He made loops ... became united** - For 36:11-13 see how you translated many of these words in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:04|26:4-6]].
  * **curtain** - These were large, heavy sections of woven cloth that were used to form the covering and dividing walls of the tabernacle. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:01|26:01]].
  * **He made** - Here "he" refers to Bezalel, but it includes all the men working on the sanctuary.
  * **fifty gold clasps** - "50 gold clasps" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
