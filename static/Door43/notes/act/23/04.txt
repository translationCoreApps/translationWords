===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Is this how you insult God's high priest?** - The men use this question to scold Paul for what he has said in [[:en:bible:notes:act:23:01|23:03]]. AT: "Do not insult God's high priest!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **For it is written** - Paul is about to quote what Moses wrote in the law. This can be stated in active form. AT: "For Moses wrote in the law" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
