===== translationWords: =====

===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here Jesus starts to tell a new part of the story.
  * **while the bridegroom was delayed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "while the bridegroom was taking a long time to arrive" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **they all got sleepy** - "all ten virgins got sleepy"
  * **there was a cry** - "someone shouted"
