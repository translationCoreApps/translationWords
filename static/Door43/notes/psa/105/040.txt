===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and he brought quail** - It may be helpful to make clear that quail are small birds Yahweh sent for them to eat. AT: "and Yahweh sent small birds to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **with bread from heaven** - Yahweh caused manna, a type of bread, to fall from the sky. AT: "with bread that fell from the sky" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they flowed** - "the waters flowed"
  * **called to mind** - This means to remember. AT: "remembered" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
