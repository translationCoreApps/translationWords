===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to describe Eliakim, who will replace Shebna in the king's palace.

  * **I will fasten him, a peg in a secure place** - Eliakim's authority will be strong and secure in the king's palace. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he will become a seat of glory for his father's house** - Here "throne of glory" refers to a place of honor. AT: "Eliakim will bring honor to his family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They will hang on him all the glory of his father's house** - AT: "They will give honor to his whole family because of him"
  * **every small container from the cups to all the jugs** - This continues to speak of Eliakim as a peg. His offspring will be like cups that hang on it. His descendants will be honored because of him.
