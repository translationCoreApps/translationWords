===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre.
  * **General Information:** - The word "your" in these verses refers to Tyre.

  * **Beth Togarmah ... Rhodes** - These were the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **stallions** - strong male horses
  * **Merchandise was in your hand** - The metonym 'in your hand" means "in your possession." This can be stated in active form. AT: "You owned goods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **horn** - This refers to the tusks or elongated teeth of certain animals.
  * **ebony** - a dark brown to black hardwood that is very dense or heavy
