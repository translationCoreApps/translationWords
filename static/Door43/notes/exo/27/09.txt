===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There must be hangings ... courtyard** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must place hangings ... courtyard" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **hangings of fine twined linen** - A "hanging" was a large curtain made of cloth. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:26:36|26:36]]
  * **fine twined linen** - "finely twisted linen." This was cloth made from fine linen threads that someone twisted together to make a stronger thread
  * **one hundred cubits** - "44 meters" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **There must also be hooks ... posts** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must also attach hooks ... posts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **posts** - a strong piece of wood set upright and used as a support
