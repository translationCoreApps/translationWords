===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus concludes the parable about the servants and the talents. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])

  * **take away the talent** - The master is speaking to other servants.
  * **talent** - A "talent" was worth twenty years' wages. Avoid translating this into modern money. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:25:14|25:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]])
  * **who possesses** - It is implied that the person who possesses something also uses it wisely. AT: "who uses well what he has" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **more will be given** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will give him more" or "I will give him more" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **even more abundantly** - "even much more"
  * **from anyone who does not possess anything** - It is implied that the person does possess something but he does not use it wisely. AT: "from anyone does not use well what he has" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **will be taken away** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will take away" or "I will take away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **into the outer darkness, where there will be weeping and grinding of teeth** - "outside into the dark where there is only crying and grinding of teeth"
