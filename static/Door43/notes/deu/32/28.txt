===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. He continues to quote Yahweh's words. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Oh, that they were wise, that they understood this, that they would consider their coming fate** - Moses states something that he wishes were true, but he knows that they are not wise and do not understand that their disobedience will cause Yahweh to bring this disaster on them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
  * **their coming fate** - The abstract noun "fate" can be translated as a verb. AT: "what is going to happen to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
