===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Michal** - Michal was the daughter of King Saul and also David's first wife. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **she despised him in her heart** - "she looked at him with disdain" or "she sneered at him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **her heart** - The word "heart" here stands for Michal, in herself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
