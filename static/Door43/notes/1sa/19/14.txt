===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Saul acts out his desire to kill David.

  * **take David** - "take David back to Saul"
  * **she said** - Here "she" refers to Michal.
  * **Bring him up to me in the bed** - The reason why the men thought David was in bed can be made explicit. AT: "If he really is too sick to come to me, bring me the bed with him in it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
