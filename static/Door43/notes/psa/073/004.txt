===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Asaph begins to describe how he sometimes wants to complain to God about those who are "arrogant" and "wicked" ([[:en:bible:notes:psa:073:001|73:3]]).

  * **the burdens of other men** - The words "burdens" is a metonym for the hard work people do to carry them. AT: "the hard work that other men have to do"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they are not afflicted like other men** - "they do not suffer the way other people do"
