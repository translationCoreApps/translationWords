===== translationNotes: =====

  * **your prayer and your request** - The words "prayer" and "request" mean basically the same thing and emphasize that Yahweh recognized that Solomon's request was sincere. Similar words appear in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:08:27|8:28]]. AT: "your request" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **to put my name there forever** - The name is a metonym for the person. AT: "to dwell there and to claim possession of it forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **My eyes and my heart will be there** - The eyes and heart are synecdoche for the whole person. AT: "I will protect and care for it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
