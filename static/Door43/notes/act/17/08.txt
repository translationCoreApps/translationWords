===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The word "they" refers to the officials of the city."
  * **General Information:** - The word "them" refers to Jason and the other believers they had brought the city officials.

  * **disturbed** - "worried" or "anxious"
  * **made Jason and the rest pay money as security** - This money was paid to the city officials as a promise of good behavior; that money might be returned if all went well or it may be used to repair the damages brought on by bad behavior. AT: "had received a bond" or "had received money" 
  * **the rest** - The words "the rest" refers to other believers that the Jews brought before the officials.
