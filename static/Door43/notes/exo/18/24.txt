===== translationNotes: =====

  * **heads over the people** - Moses writes of the leaders of people as if they were the head of a body.  AT: "leaders of the people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **capable men** - What they were capable of doing can be stated clearly. AT: "men who were able to lead" or "men who were able to judge" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **circumstances** - "situations"
  * **The difficult cases they brought to Moses** - The author writes of telling Moses about the difficult cases as bringing him the difficult cases. AT: "the difficult cases they told Moses about" or "when there were difficult cases, they told Moses about them so that he would judge them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the small cases** - "the easy cases"
