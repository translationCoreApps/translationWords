===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Now the writer again talks to Yahweh.

  * **until a pit is dug for the wicked** - The psalmist speaks of Yahweh's punishing the wicked as if Yahweh were trapping an animal in a pit. This can be stated in active form. AT: "until you dig a pit for the wicked" or "until you destroy the wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
