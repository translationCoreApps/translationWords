====== Psalm 126 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 126 is a psalm of praise for Yahweh bringing captives home.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sorrow and joy ==
People may have sorrow now; but later it will turn into joy. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:joy]])
