===== translationNotes: =====

  * **As Ezra prayed and confessed ... threw himself down** - Ezra speaks of himself as if he were someone else. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pronouns]])
  * **threw himself down** - quickly went from standing to lying facedown
  * **before God's house** - in front of the temple
  * **Shekaniah** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:04|8:5]].
  * **Jehiel** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezr:08:08|8:9]].
  * **We have been unfaithful to our God** - This was made as a public confession, not as a boastful claim.
