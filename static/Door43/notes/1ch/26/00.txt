====== 1 Chronicles 26 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The preparation for the temple continues in this chapter. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])

This chapter indents some of the lines of 26:2-10, 25-28 because they are long lists.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Casting lots ==
The gate keepers cast lots to see which gate they would guard. Those who took care of the storage also cast lots.
