===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Paul talks about the ways in which Christ is excellent.

  * **He has rescued** - "God the Father has rescued"
  * **the dominion of darkness** - Darkness is a common picture in the scriptures for evil. AT: "the power of evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his beloved Son** - "God the Father's beloved Son, Jesus Christ"
  * **In his Son we have redemption** - Paul often speaks as if the believers were "in" Jesus Christ or "in" God. Here this expression can also be translated as "by means of his Son we have redemption."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the forgiveness of sins** - "; his Son forgives our sins" or "; the Father forgives us through his Son"
