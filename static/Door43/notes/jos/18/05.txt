===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Joshua is continuing his speech to the children of Israel.

  * **They will divide it** - "They will divide the land"
  * **Judah will remain** - "The tribe of Judah will remain"
  * **the house of Joseph** - Here the word "house" represents the descendants of Joseph. The phrase refers to the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh. AT: "the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
