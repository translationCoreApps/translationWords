===== translationNotes: =====

  * **dross** - the material in a metal that people do not want and they remove by heating the metal
  * **his throne will be established by doing** - The throne is a metonym for the power to rule. These words can be translated in active form. AT: "the king will establish his throne by doing" or "he will have the power to rule because he does" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
