===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It will be all right** - The woman states this, knowing this will be the case if her husband does as she requests. The full meaning of this statement can be made explicit. AT: "Everything will be all right if you do as I ask" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **she saddled a donkey** - The woman did not saddle the donkey, rather the servant would have saddled it for her. AT: "she had her servant saddle a donkey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
