===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - "Yahweh spoke his word." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:1]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:01]].
  * **Son of man, is the house of Israel, that rebellious house, not asking, 'What are you doing?** - God asks this question to Ezekiel to show that the people of Israel have noticed what Ezekiel is doing. AT: "Ezekiel, the people of Israel, the ones who disobey me, have noticed what you are doing." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **in whose midst they are** - AT: "who are among them"
