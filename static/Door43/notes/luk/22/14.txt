===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - this is the next event in the part of the story about the Passover. Jesus and his disciples are sitting to eat the Passover meal.

  * **When the time came** - "When it was time to eat the meal" (UDB)
  * **he sat down** - "Jesus sat down"
  * **I have greatly desired** - "I have wanted very much" (UDB)
  * **before I suffer** - Jesus is referring ahead to his death. The word for "suffer" here refers to an unusually difficult or painful experience.
  * **For I say to you** - Jesus uses this phrase to emphasize the importance of what he will say next.
  * **until it is fulfilled** - This can be stated in active form. Possible meanings are 1) until the purpose of the Passover Festival is accomplished. AT: "until God fulfills it" or "until God completes the purpose of the Passover Festival" or 2) "until we celebrate the final Passover Festival" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
