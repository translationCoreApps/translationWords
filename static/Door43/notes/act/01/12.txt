===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then they returned** - "The apostles returned"
  * **a Sabbath day's journey** - This refers to the distance which, according to Rabbinical tradition, a person was allowed to walk on a Sabbath day. AT: "about one kilometer away" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When they arrived** - "When they reached their destination." Verse 12 says they were returning to Jerusalem.
  * **the upper chamber** - "the room on the upper level of the house"
  * **They were all united as one** - This means that the apostles and believers there all shared a common commitment and purpose, and there was no strife among them.
  * **as they diligently continued in prayer** - This means that the disciples prayed together regularly and frequently.
