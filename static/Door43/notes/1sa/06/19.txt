===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they had looked into his ark** - Only the priests, who were descendants of Aaron, were permitted to look into the ark and only at certain times of the year.
  * **He killed seventy men** - "He killed 70 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Who is able to stand before Yahweh, this holy God?** - Possible meanings are 1) This is a rhetorical question, "There is no one who can resist Yahweh because he is so holy" or "Because Yahweh is holy, no one is good enough to stand in his presence," or 2) this is an information question to which they were seeking an answer, "We need to find someone to serve as a priest to Yahweh; who among us is qualified?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **to whom will he go up from us?** - This is an information question to which the people were seeking an answer. Possible meanings are 1) "to whom will Yahweh go up from us?" or 2) "to whom will the ark go up from us?"
