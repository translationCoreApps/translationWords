===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **For the chief musician** - "This is for the director of music to use in worship."
  * **A psalm of David** - Possible meanings are 1) David wrote the psalm or 2) the psalm is about David or 3) the psalm is in the style of David's psalms.
  * **In you, Yahweh, I take refuge** - Going to Yahweh for protection is spoken of as taking refuge in him. AT: "I go to you, Yahweh, for protection" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **never let me be humiliated** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "do not let others humiliate me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **be my rock of refuge, a stronghold to save me** - The phrase "be my rock of refuge" is a request for protection. The second phrase emphasizes the first phrase. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **rock of refuge** - Yahweh is spoke of as if he were a huge rock that would protect the writer from attack. AT: "like a huge rock on which I can be safe" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a stronghold to save me** - Yahweh is spoken of as if he were a strong fortress in which the writer would be protected from his enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
