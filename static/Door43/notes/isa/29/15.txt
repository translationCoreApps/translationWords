===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This may be Isaiah speaking or it may continue Yahweh's speech in 29:13-14.

  * **who deeply hide their plans** - "who completely hide their plans"
  * **deeds are in darkness** - "acts are done in secret"
  * **Who sees us, and who knows us?** - This question shows how strongly they believe that their acts are hidden. AT: "No one, not even Yahweh, sees us or knows what we are doing!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
