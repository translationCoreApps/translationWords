====== Nehemiah 11 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== The places where the Jews lived ==

Some people lived in Jerusalem, but most people were spread outside of the city. This was because there was land to farm and for animals outside of the city. The city and its walls provided all of the people with protection if others came to attack.
