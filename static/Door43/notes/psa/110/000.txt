====== Psalm 110 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 110 is a psalm honoring the king as he becomes king and also a prophecy about Christ. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:prophet]] and [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== King ==
The king will be able to conquer his enemies. Christ is seen as both high priest and king. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:highpriest]])
