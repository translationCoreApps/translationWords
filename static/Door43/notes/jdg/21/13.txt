===== translationNotes: =====

  * **were offering them peace** - The abstract noun "peace" can be stated as an action. AT: "wanted to make peace with them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **Jabesh Gilead** - This is the name of a city. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:21:08|21:8]].
  * **there were not enough women for all of them** - There were six hundred Benjamite men, and only four hundred women from Jabesh Gilead.
  * **made a division between the tribes of Israel** - "had caused the tribes of Israel not to be unified"
