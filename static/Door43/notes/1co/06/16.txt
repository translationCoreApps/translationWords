===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do you not know that ... her?** - Paul begins to teach the Corinthians by emphasizing a truth that they already know. "I want to remind you that ... her." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **he who is joined to a prostitute becomes one flesh with her** - This can also be stated in active form. AT: "when a man joins his body to the body of a prostitute, it is as if their bodies become one body" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **he who is joined to the Lord becomes one spirit with him** - This can also be stated in active form. AT: "when the Lord joins his spirit to the spirit of a person, it is as if their spirits become one spirit" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
