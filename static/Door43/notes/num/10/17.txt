===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Gershon ... Merari** - Translate this men's names the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:03:17|3:17]].
  * **the armies under the banner of Reuben's camp** - This refers to the armies of the tribes under the division of Reuben: Reuben, Simeon, and Gad.
  * **Elizur son of Shedeur** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:04|1:5]].
  * **Shelumiel son of Zurishaddai** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:04|1:6]].
  * **Eliasaph son of Deuel** - Translate this man's name the say way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:12|1:14]].
