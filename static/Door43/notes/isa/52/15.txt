===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking about his servant.

  * **startle** - to surprise suddenly, possibly from fear, making someone move without thinking about it. However, the Hebrew word translated "startle" here can also be translated as "sprinkle," which some versions do.
  * **That which they had not been told** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "That which no one had told them" or "Something no one had told them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
