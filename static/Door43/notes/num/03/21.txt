===== translationNotes: =====

  * **come from Gershon** - Here the author speaks of "descending" as if it were "coming." AT: "descend from Gershon" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Libnites ... Shimeites ... Gershonites** - "Libnites" and "Shimeites" are the name of clans, named after the head of their family. The "Gershonites" is the name of people who descended from Gershon. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **All the males from a month old and older were counted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Moses counted all the males from a month old and older" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **7,500** - "seventy-five hundred" or "seven thousand and five hundred" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
