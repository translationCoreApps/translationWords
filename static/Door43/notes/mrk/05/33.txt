===== translationNotes: =====


  * **fell down before him** - "knelt down before him." She knelt down before Jesus as an act of honor and submission.
  * **told him the whole truth** - The phrase "the whole truth" refers to how she had touched him and became well. AT: "told him the whole truth about how she had touched him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **Daughter** - Jesus was using this term figuratively to refer to the woman as a believer.
  * **your faith** - "your faith in me"
