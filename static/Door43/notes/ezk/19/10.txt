===== translationNotes: =====

 
  * **mother** - Yahweh describes the nation of Israel as though it was the mother of the princes of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a vine planted in your blood** - Possible meanings are that "blood" represents 1) the violence of the kings of Judah who had murdered people or 2) the plentiful wine of Judah which had become a symbol of prosperity. AT: "a vine planted by means of violence"  or "a vine planted in your prosperity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
