===== translationNotes: =====

  * **you will remain among wise people** - Possible meanings are 1) people will continue to consider you to be a wise person or 2) you will continue to enjoy the company of wise people.
  * **listens to correction** - The word "correction" can be translated with a verbal phrase. AT: "listens when others correct him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
