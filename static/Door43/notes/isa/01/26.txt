===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Isaiah speaks to the people of Judah in the form of a poem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **at the first ... at the beginning** - These are two ways of speaking of the first part or beginning of Israel's history, when Israel first became a nation.
  * **you will be called** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "people will call you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **the city of righteousness, a faithful town** - Here "city" and "town" refer to the people living in Jerusalem. AT: "the city where the people are righteous and loyal to God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
