===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the provincial officers who lived in Jerusalem. 

  * **And there was Maaseiah** - "and Maaseiah"
  * **the sons of Perez** - "the descendants of Perez"
  * **468. They were outstanding men.** - "468 valiant men" or "468 courageous men." See how you translated numbers in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].
  * **Maaseiah ... Baruch ... Col Hozeh ... Hazaiah ... Adaiah ... Joiarib ... Zechariah ... Shilonite ... Perez** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
