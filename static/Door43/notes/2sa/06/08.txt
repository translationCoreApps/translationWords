===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Perez Uzzah** - This is the name of a place. Translator may add a footnote that says, "The name 'Perez Uzzah' means 'the punishment of Uzzah.'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **to this day** - This means to the time when this was being written, not to the present time in the twenty-first century.
  * **How can the ark of Yahweh come to me?** - David uses this question to emphasize that he is afraid to take the ark to Jerusalem. AT: "I am too afraid to bring the ark of Yahweh with me to Jerusalem." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
