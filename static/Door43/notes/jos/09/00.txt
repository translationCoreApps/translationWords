====== Joshua 09 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Israel's mistake ==

Israel was deceived because "they did not consult with Yahweh for guidance." Instead of consulting Yahweh, they attempted to achieve victory under their own power.  This was sinful. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
