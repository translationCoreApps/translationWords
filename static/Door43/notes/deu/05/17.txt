===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel if they were one man, so all instances of "you" and "your" are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]]) 

  * **You will not commit adultery** - "You will not sleep with anyone other than your spouse"
  * **You will not** - "Never"
  * **You will not give false witness against your neighbor** - "You will not tell lies about someone"
