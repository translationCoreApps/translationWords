====== Isaiah 20 General Notes ======

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Naked and barefoot ==
This is an image used in this chapter to describe Assyria's victory over Egypt and Ethiopia. While they will be defeated, they will not be totally destroyed. Instead, they will be left with nothing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
