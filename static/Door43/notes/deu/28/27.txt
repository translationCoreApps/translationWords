===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **the boils of Egypt** - "the same skin disease with which I cursed the Egyptians"
  * **boils ... ulcers, scurvy, and itch** - These are different types of skin diseases.
  * **from which you cannot be healed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "from which no one will be able to heal you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **You will grope about at noonday like the blind grope in the darkness** - "You will be like blind people who grope in darkness even at noonday." The Israelites will have a difficult life even when everyone else is enjoying life. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **you will be always oppressed and robbed** - "stronger people will always oppress and rob you"
