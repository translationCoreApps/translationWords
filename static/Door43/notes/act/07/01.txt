===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The part of the story about Stephen, which began in [[:en:bible:notes:act:06:08|6:8]], continues. Stephen begins his response to the high priest and the council by talking about things that happened in Israel's history. Most of this history comes from Moses' writings.
  * **General Information:** - The word "our" includes both Steven, the Jewish council to whom he spoke, and the entire audience. The word "your" is singular refers to Abraham. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_yousingular]])

  * **Brothers and fathers, listen to me** - Stephen was being very respectful to the council in greeting them as extended family.
