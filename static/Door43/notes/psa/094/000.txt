====== Psalm 094 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 94 is a prayer requesting that evil people be punished. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Justice ==
Evil people expect God to ignore their injustices. But God knows what they are doing and will punish them for their evil deeds. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:justice]], [[:en:obe:kt:unjust]] and [[:en:obe:kt:works]])
