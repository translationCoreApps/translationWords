===== translationNotes: =====

  * **came** - Some languages may say "went."
  * **Led by the Spirit** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "As the Holy Spirit directed him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **into the temple** - "into the temple courtyard." Only priests could enter the temple building. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the parents** - "Jesus' parents"
  * **the custom of the law** - "the custom of the law of God"
  * **Now let your servant depart in peace** - "I am your servant; let me depart in peace." Simeon was referring to himself. 
  * **depart** - This is a euphemism meaning "die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **according to your word** - "as you have said" or "because you said I would"
