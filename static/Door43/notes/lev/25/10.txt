===== translationNotes: =====

  * **fiftieth year** - This is an ordinal number. AT: "year 50" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **a Jubilee for you** - The Jubilee was a year when the Jews had return land to its original owners and set slaves free. AT: "a year of restoration for you" or "a year for you to return land and free slaves"
  * **property and slaves must be returned** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must return property and slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
