===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Eliakim ... Hilkiah ... Shebna ... Joah ... Asaph** - Translate the names of these men the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:36:01|36:03]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **over the household** - This idiom means that he was in charge of the affairs of the palace household. AT: "in charge of the palace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **with their clothes torn** - Hezekiah's officials tore their clothes as a sign of mourning and distress. The meaning of this can be made clear.  AT: "with their clothes torn because they were extremely distressed" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
