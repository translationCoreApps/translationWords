===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The king had cast them** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped the king do this. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **plain of the Jordan** - "flat land near the Jordan River"
  * **Succoth ... Zarethan** - names of cities (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Solomon did not weigh all the utensils** - Solomon would not have done the work himself anyway. AT: "Solomon did not have Huram and his workers weigh all the utensils" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the weight of the bronze could not be measured** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "no one could measure the weight of the bronze" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
