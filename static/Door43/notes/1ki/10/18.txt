===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the king made** - It might be best to translate so that the reader understands that other people helped the king do this. AT: "the king's men made" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **throne of ivory** - Ivory is the hard, white substance from the tusks or teeth of large animals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
