===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues telling the Levites and the people what they must say.

  * **May the man be cursed** - This may be stated in active form. AT: "May Yahweh curse the man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **who removes his neighbor's landmark** - The full meaning of the statement can be made explicit. AT: "who takes land away from his neighbor by moving the markers on the borders of his land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
