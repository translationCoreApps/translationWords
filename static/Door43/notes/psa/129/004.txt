===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has cut the ropes of the wicked** - Israel is spoken of as being bound by evil people. AT: "he has freed us from our enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **May they all be put to shame and turned back** - Yahweh is not explicitly mentioned as the one who does these things. This can be stated in active form. AT: "May Yahweh shame them and defeat them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
