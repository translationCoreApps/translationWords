===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the new king of Israel.

  * **the nations for your inheritance ... the farthermost regions of the earth for your possession** - These phrases express very similar ideas.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the farthermost regions of the earth** - "the lands that are very far away" 
  * **You will break them with an iron rod; like a jar of a potter, you will smash them to pieces** - These phrases express very similar ideas.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **You will break them with an iron rod** - Defeating the nations is spoken of as breaking them, and his power is spoken of as an iron rod. AT: "You will defeat them completely by your power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **you will smash them to pieces** - Destroying nations is spoken of as if they could be smashed like a clay jar. AT: you will completely destroy them like a clay pot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **a jar of a potter** - A potter is a person who makes clay pots and jars. These are fragile and can be broken easily. AT: "a clay jar" or "a clay pot" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
