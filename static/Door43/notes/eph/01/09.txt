===== translationNotes: =====

  * **according to his desire** - "as he was pleased" (UDB)
  * **in Christ** - "by means of Christ"
  * **When the times are fulfilled for the completion of his plan** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "When it is the right time for him to finish doing everything he has planned to do" or "When God has finished doing all that he wanted to do to complete his plan" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
