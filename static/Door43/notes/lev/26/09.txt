===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I will look at you with favor** - "I will show you favor" or "I will bless you" (UDB)
  * **make you fruitful and multiply you** - These two phrase refer to God causing them to have many descendants so they become a large group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **make you fruitful** - God speaks of them having many children as if they were trees that bear a lot of fruit. AT: "cause you to have many children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **You will eat food stored a long time** - "You will have enough food stored to eat for a long time" or "you will have enough food to store and eat it for a long time"
