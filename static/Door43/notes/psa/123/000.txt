====== Psalm 123 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 123 is a psalm asking for deliverance from the contempt of proud people. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Trust ==
Just as a servant trusts his master to protect him, so the author is trusting God to stop proud people from insulting him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:trust]])
