===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The introductory "but" shows that a different eagle is being describe than the one before.

  * **another great eagle** - "another large eagle"
  * **Behold!** - The word "Behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **This vine turned its roots toward the eagle** - Yahweh speaks of the vine as if it were able to behave like a person and intentionally turned its roots toward the other eagle to receive nourishment from it. AT: "The roots of the vine grew toward the eagle" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **from the place it had been planted so it would be watered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "from the place that the first eagle had planted it so that it would have water" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **It had been planted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "The first eagle had planted the vine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
