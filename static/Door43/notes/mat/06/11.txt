===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - All instances of "we," "us," and "our" refer to the crowd that Jesus is addressing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])

  * **daily bread** - Here "bread" refers to food in general. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **debts** - A debt is what one person owes another. This is a metaphor for sins. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **debtors** - A debtor is a person who owes debts to another. This is a metaphor for sinners. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Do not bring us into temptation** - The word "temptation," an abstract noun, can be expressed as a verb. AT: "Do not let anything tempt us" or "Do not let anything cause us to desire to sin" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
