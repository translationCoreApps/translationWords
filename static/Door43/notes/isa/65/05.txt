===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to and about the people of Israel.

  * **These things are smoke in my nose** - Yahweh compares these people who are continually annoying him to smoke that irritates a person's breathing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a fire that burns all day long** - Yahweh compares the people of Israel to a slow-burning fire that sends out smoke without stopping as it irritates. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
