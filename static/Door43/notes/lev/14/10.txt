===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he must take** - Here "he" refers to the man who was cleansed.
  * **ephah** - One ephah is 22 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **log** - One log is 0.31 liters. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **the person who is to be cleansed** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the person he is cleansing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
