====== Psalm 032 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 32 is a psalm of confession of sin. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:confess]] and [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Confession of sin ==
This psalm tells of the sorrow of unconfessed sin; it also tells of the blessing of confession and of receiving God's forgiveness. It is such a blessing when God forgives sin. Trouble comes when sin is hidden and not confessed. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]] and [[:en:obe:kt:forgive]])
