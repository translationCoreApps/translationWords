===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to meet her** - "to meet the young woman"
  * **a little drink of water** - "a little water"
  * **pitcher** - This is a medium-size jar made of clay used for holding and pouring liquids. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:24:12|24:14]].
  * **my master** - "sir." Here the woman uses this term of respect to refer to the man, though she is not his slave.
  * **she quickly let down her pitcher on her hand** - "she quickly lowered her pitcher." She was carrying the pitcher on her shoulder. She had to lower it to get water for the servant.
