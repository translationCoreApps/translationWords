===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Nohah** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **they trampled them down** - The completed destruction of the Benjamites is spoken of as if the Israelites stomped on their bodies. AT: "they completely destroyed them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **killed them** - "were killing them while they were running"
  * **eighteen thousand** - "18,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **were distinguished in battle** - "had fought bravely in the battle"
