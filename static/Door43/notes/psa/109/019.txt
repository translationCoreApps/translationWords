===== translationNotes: =====

  * **May his curses be to him like the clothes he wears to cover himself** - People wore their clothes everyday. David speaks of him always being covered with his curses like he is covered by his clothing. AT: "Let his curses be on him every day like the garment he wears" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **like the belt he always wears** - This missing words may be added. AT: "and let his curses always be on him like the belt he always wears" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **May this be the reward** - "May these curses be the reward"
