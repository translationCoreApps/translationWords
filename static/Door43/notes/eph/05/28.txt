===== translationNotes: =====

  * **as their own bodies** - That people love their own bodies may be stated explicitly. AT: "as husbands love their own bodies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **he nourishes** - "he cares for"
  * **we are members of his body** - Possible meanings are 1) "we are members of his body of believers" or 2) believers fit together to form the body of Christ just as the parts of the human body fit together to form a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
