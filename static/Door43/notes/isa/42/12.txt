===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking.

  * **Let them give glory** - Here "them" refers to the people along the coastlands.
  * **Yahweh will go out as a warrior; as a man of war** - Yahweh is compared with a warrior who is ready to defeat his people's enemies. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **he will stir up his zeal** - Here "zeal" refers to the passion that a warrior experiences when he is about to fight a battle. Yahweh stimulating his zeal is spoken of as if he stirred it up like the wind stirs up waves of water. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
