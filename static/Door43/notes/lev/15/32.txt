===== translationNotes: =====

  * **These are the regulations** - "These are the things that must be done"
  * **makes him unclean ... an unclean woman** - People whom other people may not touch are spoken of as if they were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **who has a menstrual period** - "who is menstruating" or "who is bleeding from her womb"
