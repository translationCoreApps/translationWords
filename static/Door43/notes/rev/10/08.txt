===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John hears the voice from heaven, which he had heard in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:10:03|10:4]], speak to him again.

  * **The voice I heard from heaven** - The word "voice" refers to the speaker.  AT: "The one I heard speak from heaven" or "The one who had spoken to me from heaven" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I** - The word "I" here refers to John.
  * **He said to me** - "The angel said to me"
  * **bitter** - "sour" or "acid." This refers to a bad taste from the stomach after eating something that is not good.
  * **it will be as sweet as honey** - "It will taste as sweet as honey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
