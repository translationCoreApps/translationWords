===== translationNotes: =====

  * **are they not written ... Israel?** - This question is used to either inform or remind the readers that the information about Menahem is in this other book. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:01:17|1:18]]. AT: "they are written in The Book of the Events of the Kings of Israel." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Menahem slept with his ancestors** - Sleeping represents dying. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:10:34|10:35]]. AT: "Menahem died as his ancestors had" or "like his ancestors, Menahem died"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **Pekahiah** - This a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **became king in his place** - The phrase "in his place" is a metaphor meaning "instead of him." AT: "became king instead of Menahem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
