====== Psalm 085 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 85 is a worship psalm.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Blessings ==
God has given so many blessings. If the people stop their sinning then the land will be filled by the glory of God and his blessings. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:bless]], [[:en:obe:kt:sin]] and [[:en:obe:kt:glory]])

== Group Prayer ==
Note how this psalm uses the plural to show that it is a group praying.
