===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the framing** - This to the panels that they made by joining together smaller pieces of wood.
  * **crossbars** - These are support beams that give stability to the structure.
  * **posts** - A post is a strong piece of wood set upright and used as a support.
  * **bases** - The bases held the posts in place.
  * **hardware** - This means everything used to join the crossbars, posts, and bases together.
  * **with their sockets** - Here "their" refers to the "pillars and posts."
  * **sockets, pegs, and ropes** - These are all items used secure the pillars and posts into place.
