===== translationNotes: =====

  * **This is true** - "This" refers to the wicked man running at God from the previous verse.
  * **he has covered his face with his fat and gathered fat on his loins** - This wicked man is described as fat and weak, while believing himself to be strong enough to defeat God. AT: "he is weak with a fat face and fat loins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_irony]])
  * **which no man inhabits** - "which are abandoned" 
  * **heaps** - "ruins" or "dumps"
