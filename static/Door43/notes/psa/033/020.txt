===== translationNotes: =====

  * **We wait for Yahweh** - Here "wait" is an idiom that refers to trust. AT: "We trust in Yahweh" (UDB) or "We hope in Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he is our help and our shield** - Here Yahweh is spoken of as if he is a shield that protects soldiers in battle. AT: "he is our helper and protects us like a shield" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Our hearts rejoice** - Here "hearts" refer to the people. AT: "We rejoice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **in his holy name** - Here "holy name" refers to Yahweh's holy character. AT: "in his holy character" or "in him because he is holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
