===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Zadok ... Abiathar** - Translate these men's name the same as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:15:24|15:24-25]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **in such and such a way** - This phrase, meaning "like this," refers to what Ahithophel advised Absalom earlier in [[:en:bible:notes:2sa:17:01|17:1-3]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the fords of the Arabah** - A ford is a shallow part of a river where people can walk across. The Arabah is the land along both sides of the Jordan River.
  * **by all means** - This means to make sure that you do something. AT: "be sure to" or "make sure that you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the king will be swallowed up** - Here the king and his people being killed is describes as if they were "swallowed" by their enemy. AT: "the king will be killed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
