===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Why, Yahweh, do you stand far off? Why do you hide yourself in times of trouble?** - The speaker uses these questions to express his distress that God has not helped him. AT: "Yahweh, it seems as though you are far away from me and you hide from me whenever I am in trouble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **schemes** - "plans"
  * **the wicked person** - This refers to wicked people in general. AT: "wicked people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **his deepest desires** - The noun "desires" can be expressed with the verb "want. "AT: "the things that he wants very much to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the greedy** - "greedy people"
