====== Leviticus 15 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Bodily fluid ==
This chapter discusses fluids that come out of the body. These fluids caused a person to be unclean because of their potential to cause diseases. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:unclean]])

== Cleanliness ==
While these rules about cleanliness are intended to benefit the Israel and promote their health, they also were about making Israel into a separate and holy nation, different from the rest of the world. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:holy]])
