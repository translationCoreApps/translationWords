===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In these verses, Nehemiah continues to list the names of the people who signed the sealed document.

  * **Pelatiah ... Hoshea ... Pilha ... Shobek ... Hashabnah ... Ahiah ... Anan ... Harim ... Baanah** - These are the name of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Hanan** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:46|7:49]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anaiah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:08:04|8:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Anaiah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:08|3:8]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Hasshub ... Hallohesh** - These are the names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:11|3:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Rehum** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:16|3:17]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Maaseiah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:03:22|3:23]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Malluch, Harim** - These are the  names of men. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:10:04|10:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Baanah** - This is a man's name. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:06|7:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
