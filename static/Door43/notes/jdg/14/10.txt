===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Samson's father went down to where the woman was** - The phrase "went down" is used to describe Timnah which is lower in elevation than where Samson's father lives. AT: "Samson's father went to where the woman lived" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the custom of the young men** - It may be helpful to state that this was a marriage custom. AT: "the custom of young men who were getting married" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **thirty of their friends** - "30 of their friends" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
