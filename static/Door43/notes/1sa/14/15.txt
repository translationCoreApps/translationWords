===== translationNotes: =====

  * **There was a panic in the camp, in the field, and among the people** - The abstract noun "panic" can be stated as the adjective "very afraid." AT: "The Philistine soldiers in the camp and in the field and all the people with them became very afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **the raiders** - the Philistines who were raiding Israelite cities
  * **The earth quaked** - It may be helpful to state the cause. AT: "God caused the ground to shake" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
