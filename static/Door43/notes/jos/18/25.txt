===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer continues to list the cities that were in the land that the tribe of Benjamin received as an inheritance. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])

  * **This was the inheritance of Benjamin** - The land and cities that the tribe of Benjamin received are spoken of as if they were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land and the cities that the tribe of Benjamin received as an inheritance" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
