===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Give blessing to God ... let the sound of his praise be heard** - These two clauses have similar meanings. The idea is repeated to emphasize the importance of praising God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]) 
  * **our** - In these verses the word "our" includes David and the people he is speaking to. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **he does not permit our feet to slip** - The writer speaks of God's protection as preventing his people from falling in their pathway or off a cliff. AT:"he has not allowed us to fall into disaster" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
