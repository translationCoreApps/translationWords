===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Why do your disciples not walk according to the tradition of the elders, for they eat their bread with unwashed hands?** - The Pharisees and scribes asked this question to challenge Jesus' authority. This can be written as two statements. AT: "Your disciples disobey the traditions of our elders! They should wash their hands using our rituals." (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **bread** - This is a synecdoche, representing food in general. AT: "food" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
