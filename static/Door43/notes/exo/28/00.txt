====== Exodus 28 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Holy garments ==
Because Yahweh is holy, only the priests could approach him, and when they did they must be wearing specially made clothing. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:priest]] and [[:en:obe:kt:holy]])
