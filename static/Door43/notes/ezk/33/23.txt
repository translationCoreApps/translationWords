===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came** - This is an idiom that is used to introduce something that God told his prophets or his people. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]]. AT: "Yahweh spoke this message" or "Yahweh spoke these words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those ruins** - Possible meanings are 1) "those ruined buildings" or 2) "those ruined cities."
  * **he inherited the land** - Yahweh giving the land to Abraham is spoken of as if Abraham inherited the land. AT: "Yahweh gave him the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the land** - "the land of Israel"
  * **The land has been given to us** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh has given us the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **as a possession** - That abstract noun "possession" can be stated as "possess." AT: "so that we can possess it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
