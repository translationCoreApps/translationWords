===== translationNotes: =====

  * **over their heads** - "over the heads of the living creatures"
  * **was the likeness of a throne** - "was something that looked like a throne"
  * **the likeness of** - The abstract noun "likeness" can be translated as an adjective. AT: "something similar to" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **sapphire** - a very valuable stone that is clear blue and very shiny
  * **on the likeness of the throne was** - The abstract noun "likeness" can be translated as an adjective. AT: "on what was similar to a throne there was" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **a likeness like the appearance of a man** - The abstract noun "likeness" can be translated as an adjective. The abstract noun "appearance" can be translated as a verb. If you need to tell who this is, you should probably identify him as Yahweh ([[:en:bible:notes:ezk:01:01|1:3]]). AT: "something that looked similar to what appeared to be a man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
