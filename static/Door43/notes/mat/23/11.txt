===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he who is greatest among you** - "the person who is most important among you"
  * **among you** - Here "you" is plural and refers to Jesus' followers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **exalts himself** - "makes himself important"
  * **will be humbled** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will humble" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **will be exalted** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will make important" or "God will honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
