===== translationNotes: =====

  * **riches of his grace** - Paul speaks of God's grace as if it were material wealth. AT: "greatness of God's grace" or "abundance of God's grace" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He lavished this grace upon us** - "He gave us this great amount of grace" or  "He was extremely kind to us" 
  * **with all wisdom and understanding** - Possible meanings are 1) "because he has all wisdom and understanding" 2) "so that we might have great wisdom and understanding"
