===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - In verses 34-37 Nebuchadnezzar speaks in the first person to describe his response to God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])

  * **All the earth's inhabitants are considered by him to be as nothing** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "He considers all the earth's inhabitants as nothing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **All the earth's inhabitants** - "All the people on the earth"
  * **the army of heaven** - "the angel armies in heaven" (UDB)
  * **whatever suits his will** - "whatever satisfies his purpose" or "anything he wants to do"
  * **No one can stop him** - It may be helpful to add additional detail. AT: "When he decides to do something, no one can stop him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **No one can say to him, 'Why have you done this?'** - This can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "No one can question what he does." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
