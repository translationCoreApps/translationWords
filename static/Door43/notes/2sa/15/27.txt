===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Ahimaaz** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Abiathar** - Translate this man's name the same as you did in [[:
en:bible:notes:2sa:08:15|8:17]].
  * **Are you not a seer?** - This rhetorical question is used to rebuke Zadok and can be translated as a statement. AT: "You will be able to find out what is happening." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **See** - Here this word is an idiom and means for the listener to pay attention to what is said next. AT: "Listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]]) 
  * **until word comes from you** - This refers to him sending a messenger to the king. AT: "until you send a messenger to me to inform me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **to inform me** - Here the king implies he is to receive a message informing him about what is happening in Jerusalem. AT: "to tell me what is happening in Jerusalem" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
