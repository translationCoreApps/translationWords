===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Scripture continues to describe the rule of Amaziah, King of Judah. 

  * **But the high places were not taken away** - AT: "But he did not remove the high places of pagan worship that were built upon the hills"
  * **as soon as his rule was well established** - AT: "as soon as Amaziah securely established his royal rule and kingly authority"
