====== 1 Thessalonians 03 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Standing ==
In this chapter, standing is used as a way to illustrate steadfastness. This is a common way to describe steadfastness or faithfulness. The opposite of steadfastness is described as "shaking" in this chapter. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:faithful]])
