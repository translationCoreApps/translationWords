===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In those days** - This refers to a time period. What time period this refers to can be stated clearly. AT: "During that time" or "While Jotham was king of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Rezin** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Pekah ... Remaliah** - These are men's names. Pekah was the king of Israel. See how you translated them in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:15:37]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jotham slept with his ancestors** - Sleeping represents dying. AT: "Jotham died as his ancestors had" or "Like his ancestors, Jotham died"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **became king in his place** - The phrase "in his place" is a metaphor meaning "instead of him." AT: "became king instead of Jotham" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
