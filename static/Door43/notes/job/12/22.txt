===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He reveals deep things from darkness** - Revealing things represents either making them known. "Deep things from darkness" represent secrets that people do not know. AT: "He makes known secrets that people do not know"   (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **brings out to light the shadows where dead people are** - Bringing things out to light represents making them known. AT: "makes known the place where dead people are" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He enlarges nations** - "He causes the extent of the land of people groups to become bigger" or "He causes the nations to take more land"
  * **he also leads them along as prisoners** - God leading nations represents God causing enemy nations to lead them. The word "them" represents nations, which here represents the people of those nations. AT: "He also causes their enemies to lead them along as prisoners" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
