===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues the  quotation from the prophet Jeremiah.
 
  * **They will not teach each one his neighbor and each one his brother, saying, 'Know the Lord.'** - This direct quotation can be stated as an indirect quotation. AT: "They will not need to teach their neighbors or brothers to know me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **neighbor ... brother** - Both of these refer to fellow Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **Know the Lord ... all will know me** - "Know" here stands for acknowledge. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **to their acts of unrighteousness** - This stands for the people who committed these unrighteous acts. AT: "to those who did unrighteous acts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **I will not remember their sins any longer** - Here "remember" stands for "think about." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
