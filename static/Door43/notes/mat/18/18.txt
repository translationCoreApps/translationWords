===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Truly I say to you** - "I tell you the truth." This adds emphasis to what Jesus says next.
  * **you** - All occurrences of this word are plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **bind ... bound ... release ... released** - This metaphor means that God in heaven will approve of what Peter allows or forbids on earth. See how you translated these in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:16:19|16:19]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **will be bound ... will be released** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "God will bind ... God will release" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I say to you** - This adds emphasis to what Jesus says next.
  * **if two of you** - It is implied that Jesus means "if at least two of you" or "if two or more of you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **they ... them** - These refer to the "two of you." AT: "you ... you"
  * **my Father** - This is an important title for God that describes the relationship between God and Jesus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **two or three** - It is implied that Jesus means "two or more" or "at least two." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **are gathered** - "meet"
  * **in my name** - Here "name" refers to the entire person. AT: "because of me" or "because they are my disciples" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
