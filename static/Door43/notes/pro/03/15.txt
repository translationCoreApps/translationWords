===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The author speaks of wisdom as if it were a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])

  * **precious** - "valuable" or "costly"
  * **She has length of days in her right hand; in her left hand are riches and honor** - The writer speak of the benefits that one gains from having wisdom as if wisdom were a woman who held these qualities in her hands and offered them to people. AT: "Wisdom gives a person length of days and riches and honor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **length of days** - This idiom refers to a long life. AT: "long life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
