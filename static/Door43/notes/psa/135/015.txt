===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Those who make them are like them** - Being ignorant and powerless is spoken of as being like idols, which cannot speak, see, hear, or breathe. AT: "Those who make them are stupid and powerless like their idols" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **as is everyone who trusts in them** - "and so are those who trust in the idols"
