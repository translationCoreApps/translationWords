===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The leaders of Israel are counting the men, 20 years old and older, according to their tribes and families. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **These were Joseph's descendants** - The word "these" refers to all of the men descended from Joseph's sons, Ephraim and Manasseh. AT: "These were Joseph's descendants, descended from his sons Manasseh and Ephraim," (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **counted in each of their clans** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they counted them in each of their clans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
