===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Joiada ... Paseah and Meshullam ... Besodeiah ... Melatiah ... Jadon** - These are all names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **set its doors** - "installed its doors" or "put its doors in place"
  * **its bolts, and its bars** - "its locks and bars." These locked the gates securely. 
  * **Gibeonite ... Meronothite** - Gibeonites and Meronothites are people groups. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Gibeon and Mizpah** - These are names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Province Beyond the River** - This is the name of the province that was west of the Euphrates River. It was across the river from the city of Susa. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:02:07|2:7]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
