===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the man was healed** - "the man became healthy again"
  * **Now that day was a Sabbath** - "Now that day was God's Day of Rest"
  * **Now** - "Now" is used here to draw attention to background information. It emphasizes the fact that this event takes place on the Sabbath. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
