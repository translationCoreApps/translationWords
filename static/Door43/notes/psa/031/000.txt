====== Psalm 031 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 31 is a psalm of deliverance. The psalmist's enemies thought they had defeated him, but God protected him. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Abandonment ==
Even though everyone deserted and despised the psalmist, God did not abandon him.
