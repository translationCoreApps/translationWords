====== Psalm 040 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===
Psalm 40 is a psalm of praise. Many also see it as a messianic psalm, a psalm about Christ. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:christ]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sacrifices ==
God is not interested in sacrifices but wants people to obey him and fulfill his plans for them.
