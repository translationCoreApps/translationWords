===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Israel and Judah.

  * **Lord Yahweh** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:02:04|2:4]]. 
  * **Because you are more troublesome than** - "because your sinfulness is worse than" or "because you are more unruly than"
  * **that surround you** - "that are all around you."
  * **have not walked in my statutes** - Walking is a metaphor for the way a person lives. AT: "have not lived according to my statutes" or "have not obeyed my statutes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **or acted according to my decrees** - "or obeyed my decrees"
  * **Behold!** - "Look!" or "Listen!" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you!"
  * **I will execute judgments within your midst** - "I will judge you in various ways" or "I will punish you" (UDB).
