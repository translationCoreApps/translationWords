===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to bless the tribes of Israel; the blessings are short poems. He continues to describe the tribe of Joseph, which he began to do in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:33:13|33:13]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **The firstborn of an ox, majestic is he** - The ox is a metaphor for something large and strong. The word "firstborn" is a metaphor for honor. AT: "People will honor Joseph's descendants, who are many and powerful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **his horns are the horns of** - The horn is a metaphor for strength. AT: "he is as strong as" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **With them he will push** - Pushing with his horns is a metaphor for strength. AT: "He is so strong that he will push" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **ten thousands of Ephraim ... the thousands of Manasseh** - This means the tribe of Ephraim will be stronger than the tribe of Manasseh. AT: "the people of Ephraim, who number many times 10,000 ... the people of Manasseh, who number many times 1,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
