===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

  * **Then we turned and took our journey** - "Then we turned around and went"
  * **we went around Mount Seir for many days** - Possible meanings are 1) the Israelites traveled around the mountain called Seir for a very long time or 2) the Israelites wandered in a region called Mount Seir for a very long time.
  * **Mount Seir** - This is a mountainous area south of the Dead Sea. The area is also called "Edom." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:01:01|1:2]] (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **many days** - Some languages translate this as "many nights."
