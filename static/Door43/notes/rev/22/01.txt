===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John continues to describe the new Jerusalem as the angel shows it to him.

  * **showed me** - Here "me" refers to John.
  * **the river of the water of life** - "the river flowing with life-giving water"
  * **the water of life** - Eternal life is spoken of as if it were a drink of life-giving water. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:21:05|21:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the Lamb** - This is a young male sheep. Here is it used symbolically to refer to Christ. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:05:06|5:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
  * **the nations** - Here "nations" refers to the people who live in every nation. AT: "the people of all nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
