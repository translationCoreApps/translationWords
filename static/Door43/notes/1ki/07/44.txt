===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He made** - Here the word "He" refers to Huram and his helpers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|]])
  * **all the other implements** - "all the other tools"
  * **polished bronze** - bronze that was polished so that it would reflect light
