===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then the chief priests of the Jews said to Pilate** - The chief priests had to go back to Pilate's headquarters to protest to him about the words on the sign. AT: "The chief priests went back to Pilate and said" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **What I have written I have written** - Pilate implies that he will not change the words on the sign. AT: "I have written what I wanted to write, and I will not change it!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
