===== translationWords: =====


===== translationNotes: =====

  * **is not at his house** - "is not at home"
  * **full moon** - The moon is said to be full when it is a perfectly round disk, shining at its brightest.
  * **she turned him** - To persuade someone to act in a certain way is spoken of as if it were changing the direction that person was walking. AT: "she persuaded him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **her ... she ... him** - The female is the married woman who wants to sleep with "him," the young man.
  * **smooth lips** - Here "lips" represents what a person says. When a person flatters someone else by saying things that are not sincere, these words are spoken of as if they were a smooth object. AT: "flattering, deceiving words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **she misled him** - "she persuaded him to do something evil" AT: "she convinced him to sin with her"
