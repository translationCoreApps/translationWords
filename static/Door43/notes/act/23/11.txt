===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The following night** - This means the night after the day Paul went before the council. AT: "That night" (UDB)
  * **bear witness in Rome** - The words "about me" are understood. AT: "bear witness about me in Rome" or "testify about me in Rome" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
