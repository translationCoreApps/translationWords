===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - To rebuke the religious leaders and illustrate their unbelief, Jesus tells a parable about rebellious servants. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])

  * **a landowner** - "a person who owned a piece of property"
  * **a hedge** - "a wall" or "a fence"
  * **dug a winepress in it** - "dug a hole in the vineyard to press the grapes"
  * **rented it out to tenant farmers** - "put tenant farmers in charge of the vineyard." The owner still owned the vineyard.
  * **vine growers** - These were people who knew how to take care of vines and grapes.
