===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the people** - This refers to the people of Israel.
  * **gave a blast with the trumpets** - "sounded the trumpets loudly" or "blew into the ram's horn trumpets"
  * **Yahweh has given you** - Joshua is saying that Yahweh will definitely give them the city by saying that he has already given it to them. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pastforfuture]])
  * **given you** - The word "you" refers to the entire nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
