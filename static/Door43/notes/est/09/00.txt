====== Esther 09 General Notes ======

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Purim ==
The events of this chapter were so significant, the Jews celebrated these events every year after this. It is known as "Purim."

=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Ironic Situation ==
On the day that was supposed to bring great victory to the enemies of the Jews became a day of great victory for the Jews. This is a type of irony.
