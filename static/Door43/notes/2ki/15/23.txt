===== translationNotes: =====

  * **In the fiftieth year of Azariah king of Judah** - It can be stated clearly that this is the fiftieth year of his reign. AT: "In year 50 of the reign of Azariah king of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Pekahiah** - This is a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **what was evil in the sight of Yahweh** - The sight of Yahweh represents Yahweh's judgment. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:03:01|3:2]]. AT: "what was evil in Yahweh's judgment" or "what Yahweh considers to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **He did not leave behind the sins of Jeroboam son of Nebat** - Leaving behind sins represents refusing to do those sins. AT: "Pekahiah did not refuse to commit the sins of Jeroboam son of Nebat" or "He sinned as Jeroboam son of Nebat had sinned" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **he had caused Israel to sin** - Here the word "Israel" represents the people of the kingdom of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
