===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Beth Peor** - This was a town in Moab. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:03:28|3:29]].
  * **look at it with your eyes** - "see it for yourself"
  * **to this day** - This refers to the time that this was written or edited, not to the present time in the twenty-first century.
