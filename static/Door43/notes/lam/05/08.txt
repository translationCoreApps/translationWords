===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Slaves rule over us** - Possible meanings are 1) "Now the people who rule over us are themselves slaves to their own masters in Babylon" (UDB) or 2) "People who used to be slaves in Babylon now rule over us."
  * **to deliver us from their hand** - Here the word "hand" refers to power. AT: "to rescue us from their power" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **because of the sword in the wilderness** - AT: "because there are people in the wilderness who kill others with swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
