===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus shows himself again to the disciples at the Sea of Tiberias. Verses 2 and 3 tell us what happens in the story before Jesus appears. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **After these things** - "Some time later"
  * **with Thomas called Didymus** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "with Thomas whom we called Didymus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Didymus** - This is a male name that means "twin." See how this name is translated in [[:en:bible:notes:jhn:11:15|11:15]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
