===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The author closes with a blessing and greetings.

  * **Pray for us** - Here "us" refers to the author and his companions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
  * **we are persuaded that we have a clean conscience** - Here "clean" stands for being free from guilt. AT: "we are certain that we have no guilt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **that I will be returned to you sooner** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that God will quickly remove the things that stop my coming to you" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
