===== translationNotes: =====

  * **found the man from whom the demons had gone out** - "saw the man whom the demons had left"
  * **and in his right mind** - "and sane" or "and behaving normally"
  * **sitting at the feet of Jesus** - "sitting at the feet" is an idiom that means "sitting humbly nearby" or "sitting in front of." AT: "sitting on the ground in front of Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they were afraid** - "they were afraid of Jesus" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
