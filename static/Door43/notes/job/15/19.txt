===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to whom alone the land was given** - AT: "to whom alone God gave the land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **among whom no stranger ever passed** - AT: "whose knowledge no stranger ever corrupted"
  * **twists in pain** - This is either physical or emotional pain. AT: "suffers a lot." Other translations may read: "is overcome by fear"
  * **the number of years that are laid up** - AT: "all the years that God has prepared" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
