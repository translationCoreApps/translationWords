===== translationNotes: =====

  * **En Shemesh ... Geliloth ... Adummim ... Beth Arabah** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Stone of Bohan** - This was likely a large stone that someone set up as a landmark and named after the man, Bohan. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:15:05|15:6]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the shoulder of Beth Arabah** - Land that is in the form of a slope or ridge is spoken of as if it were a shoulder. AT: "the slope of Beth Arabah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
