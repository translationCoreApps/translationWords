===== translationNotes: =====

  * **turned aside** - This mean to take a break from their journey and stop in at a place along the route. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:19:10|19:11]]. AT: "stopped there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **And he went** - Here the word "he" refers to the Levite, but actually refers to the Levite along with his servant and concubine. AT: "And they went" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **took them into his house** - This phrase means for someone to invite them to spend the night in their home. AT: "invited them to stay in their house for that night" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
