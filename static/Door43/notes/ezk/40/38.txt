===== translationNotes: =====

  * **by each of the inner gateways** - "in each of the inner gates"
  * **they rinsed the burnt offerings** - Possible meanings for "they" are 1) the people who were bringing offerings or 2) the priests who killed the animals.
  * **the burnt offerings** - the animals that would be killed and burned as offerings
  * **There were two tables on each side of each portico** - there were four tablesâ€”two on each side of each portico
