===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Stephanas, Fortunatus, and Achaicus** - These men were either some of the first Corinthian believers or church elders who were co-workers with Paul.
  * **Stephanas, Fortunatus, and Achaicus** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **They have made up for your absence** - "They made up for the fact that you were not here."
  * **For they have refreshed my spirit** - Paul is saying he was encouraged by their visit.
