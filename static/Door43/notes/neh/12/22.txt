===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Eliashib ... Joiada, Johanan ... Jaddua** - names of men (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **during the reign of Darius** - Another possible meaning is "until the reign of Darius"
  * **recorded in the book of the annals** - This may refer to the Book of Chronicles. The scribes wrote the words in the verses above in a book that recorded the events of each day. 
  * **up to the days of Johanan son of Eliashib** - The records in the temple recorded only up until Johanan.
