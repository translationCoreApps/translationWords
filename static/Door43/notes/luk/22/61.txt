===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Turning, the Lord looked at Peter** - "The Lord turned and looked at Peter"
  * **the word of the Lord** - Jesus' "word" is here used to refer to everything that he said. AT: "Jesus' words" or "what Jesus had said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a rooster crows** - Roosters often crow just before the sun appears in the morning. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:luk:22:33|22:34]].
  * **today** - The Jewish day started at sundown and continued to the next evening. Jesus had spoken the previous evening about what would happen shortly before dawn or at dawn. AT: "tonight" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **deny me three times** - "deny three times that you know me"
  * **Going outside** - "Going out of the courtyard"
