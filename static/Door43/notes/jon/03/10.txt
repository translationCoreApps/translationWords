===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God saw what they did** - "God understood that they stopped doing evil actions"
  * **they turned from their evil ways** - The author speaks of the people stop sinning as if they turned their back to an object. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **God changed his mind about the punishment that he had said he would do to them** - "God changed his thinking about the punishment that he had said he would do to them" or "God decided not to punish them as he had said he would". (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and he did not do it** - what God was to do can be made explicit. AT: "and he did not punish them" or "and he did not destroy them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
