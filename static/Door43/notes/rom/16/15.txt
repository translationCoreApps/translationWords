===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Philologus ... Nereus ... Olympas** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **Julia** - The name of a woman. Julia was probably the wife of Philologus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **a holy kiss** - an expression of affection for fellow believers
  * **All the churches of Christ greet you** - Here Paul speaks in a general manner concerning the churches of Christ. AT: "The believers in all the churches in this area send their greetings to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
