===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Turn your ears** - Listening to or paying attention to someone is spoken of as if it were turning one's ears to someone. AT: "Pay attention" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **faithful love promised to David** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "faithful love that I promised to David" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **I have placed him** - Possible meanings are 1) this refers to what Yahweh did for King David in the past or 2) it refers to what God will do through one of David's descendants.
