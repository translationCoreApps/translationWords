===== translationNotes: =====

  * **lips that say what is right** - Here the word "lips" represents the person who speaks. AT: "a person who speaks the truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **A king's wrath is a messenger of death** - The writer speaks of an angry king causing someone to die as if the king's wrath were a messenger that he sends out to kill someone. AT: "An angry king can put people to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
