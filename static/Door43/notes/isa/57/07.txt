===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the unfaithful people of Israel.

  * **You prepared your bed** - This refers to sleeping with prostitutes as a part of worshiping false gods. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **on a high mountain** - People often went on top of hills and mountains to worship false gods. They thought those were the best places for worship. This might even refer to Jerusalem, too. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **You made a covenant with them** - "You made a contract with them" or "You bribed them"
