===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The men of the city** - "The leaders of the city"
  * **the situation of this city is pleasant** - This means that the city is located in a good place. AT: "this city is in a good place" or "this city is in a good location" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **as my master can see** - The men refer to Elisha here as "my master" to honor him.
  * **fruitful** - "producing good crops"
