===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the land where his father was staying, in the land of Canaan** - "in the land of Canaan where his father had lived"
  * **These were the events concerning Jacob** - This sentence introduces the account of Jacob's children in Genesis 37:1-50:26. Here "Jacob" refers to his whole family. AT: "This is the account of Jacob's family" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **seventeen years old** - "17 years old" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Bilhah** - This is the name of Rachel's female servant. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:29:28|29:29]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Zilpah** - This is the name of Leah's female servant. See how you translated this name in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:29:23|29:24]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **wives** - "concubines" (UDB)
  * **an unfavorable report about them** - "a bad report about his brothers"
