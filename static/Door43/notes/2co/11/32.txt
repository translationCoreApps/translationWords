===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the governor under King Aretas was guarding the city** - "the governor whom King Aretas had appointed had told men to guard the city"
  * **to arrest me** - "so that they might catch and arrest me"
  * **I was lowered in a basket** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "some people put me in a basket and lowered me to the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **from his hands** - Paul uses the governor's hands as metonymy for the governor. AT: "from the governor" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
