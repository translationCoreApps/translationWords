===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David accepts Abigail's counsel and gifts.

  * **May Yahweh ... be blessed, he who** - This can be translated in active form. Possible meanings are 1) "I praise Yahweh ... who" (UDB) or 2) "May all people praise Yahweh ... who." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Your wisdom is blessed and you are blessed, because** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "I thank Yahweh because he has blessed you by making you wise and because" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **bloodshed** - murder. The same word appears in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:25:25|25:26]].
  * **with my own hand** - The word "hand" here is a metonym for the action taken by the hand. AT: "by my own actions" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
