===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The ransomed of Yahweh** - To "ransom" means to "rescue." This refers to people who Yahweh has rescued. AT: "Those who Yahweh has rescued" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **everlasting joy will be on their heads** - This uses a person's head to mean the person as a whole. AT: "they will have everlasting joy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **gladness and joy ... sorrow and sighing** - The words "gladness" and "joy" mean basically the same thing, as do "sorrow" and "sighing." Together they emphasize the intensity of these emotions. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **gladness and joy will overtake them** - This speaks of the people being overwhelmed by gladness and joy by giving these emotions the human quality of being able to overtake someone by force. AT: "they will be overwhelmed by joy and gladness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **sorrow and sighing will flee away** - This speaks of the people no longer being sorrowful and sighing by giving these emotions the human ability to run away. AT: "they will no longer be sorrowful and sighing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
