===== translationNotes: =====

  * **is brought down by his evil actions** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "evil actions push over" or "evil actions destroy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Wisdom rests in the heart** - The word "heart" represents the feelings, attitudes and motivations of a person. AT: "Wisdom is in the attitude" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the discerning** - "a discerning person"
  * **she lets herself be known** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "she makes sure people know her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **she** - The word "she" refers to wisdom. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
