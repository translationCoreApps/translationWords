===== translationNotes: =====

  * **wormwood and bitterness** - Wormwood is a plant that gives a liquid which is very bitter to drink. "Wormwood" and "bitterness" have similar meanings and emphasize that this is something that is very bitter. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **I am bowed down within me** - This expression refers to depression and despair. AT: "my soul melts within me" or "I am in despair" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **But I call these things to mind** - The author is about to tell the audience what he has hope in.
