===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do not oppress your neighbor or rob him** - Here "neighbor" means "anyone." The meaning of this can be made explicit. AT: "Do not hurt or rob anyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **The wages of a hired servant must not stay with you all night until the morning** - Yahweh commands the employer to pay his servant promptly when his work is done that day. The full meaning of this statement can be made clear. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
