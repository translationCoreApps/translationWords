===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - John begins to explain what he saw in his vision.
  
  * **whose voice** - This refers to the person speaking. AT: "who" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **son of man** - This expression describes a human figure, someone who looks human. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a golden sash** - a piece of cloth worn around the chest. It may have had golden threads in it.
