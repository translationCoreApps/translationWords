===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - David continues his song to Yahweh.

  * **your eyes are against the proud** - Here the metonym "your eyes" refers to what Yahweh sees. This means Yahweh watches the proud person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **and you bring them down** - "and you destroy their pride"
  * **you are my lamp, Yahweh. Yahweh lights up my darkness** - This metaphor compares Yahweh to a lamp, which means he gives David light and helps him to see when things seem hopeless. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
