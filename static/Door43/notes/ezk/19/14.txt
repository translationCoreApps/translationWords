===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell a story in which the nation of Israel is a vine. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])

  * **no scepter to rule** - The implicit information may be supplied from [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:19:10|19:11]]. AT: "no branch strong enough for a ruler's scepter" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **will be sung as a lamentation** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must sing it as a lamentation" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
