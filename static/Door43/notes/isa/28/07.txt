===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But even these** - "But even the priests and prophets"
  * **reel with wine, and stagger with strong drink** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and emphasize that the leaders cannot or will not do their jobs. AT: "stumble about as if they are drunk" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **they are swallowed up by wine** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "they think they are swallowing the wine but it is like the wine is swallowing them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **staggering in vision and reeling in decision** - "they see visions, but they cannot understand what they mean" (UDB)
