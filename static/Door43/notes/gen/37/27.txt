===== translationNotes: =====

  * **to the Ishmaelites** - "to these men who are descendants of Ishmael" (UDB)
  * **not lay our hands upon him** - This means not to harm or injure him. AT: "not hurt him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he is our brother, our flesh** - The word "flesh" is a metonym that stands for a relative. AT: "he is our blood relative" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] ).
  * **His brothers listened to him** - "Judah's brothers listened to him" or "Judah's brothers agreed with him"
  * **Midianite ... Ishmaelites** - Both names refer to the same group of traders that Joseph's brothers meet. 
  * **for twenty pieces of silver** - "for the price of 20 pieces of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **carried Joseph into Egypt** - "took Joseph to Egypt" (UDB)
