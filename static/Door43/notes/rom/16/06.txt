===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Mary** - This is a woman's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **Andronicus ... Ampliatus** - These are men's names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])  
  * **Junias** - This could be either 1) Junia, a woman's name, or, much less likely, 2) Junias, a man's name. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **They are prominent among the apostles** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "The apostles know them very well" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my beloved in the Lord** - "my dear friend and fellow believer"
