===== translationNotes: =====

  * **comes out of his body** - This refers to the man's private parts. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **he becomes unclean** - The person whom other people may not touch is spoken of as if he were physically unclean. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **it is unclean** - "his body is unclean" or "he is unclean"
