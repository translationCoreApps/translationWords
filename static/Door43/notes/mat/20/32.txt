===== translationNotes: =====

  * **called to them** - "called to the blind men"
  * **do you wish** - "do you want"
  * **that our eyes may be opened** - The phrase "our eyes may be opened" is an idiom that means to be able to see again. This can be stated in active form. AT: "we want you to make us able to see" or "we want to be able to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **being moved with compassion** - "having compassion" or "feeling compassion for them"
