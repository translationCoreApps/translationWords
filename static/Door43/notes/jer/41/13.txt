===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Then it happened that** - This phrase is used here to mark an important event in the story. If your language has a way for doing this, you could consider using it here.
  * **when all the people who were with Ishmael** - Here "people" refers to those whom Ishmael and his men had captured.
  * **whom Ishmael had captured** - Here "Ishmael" represents all of his men. AT: "whom Ishmael and his men had captured" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
