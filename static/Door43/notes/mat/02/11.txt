===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Here the scene shifts to the house where Mary, Joseph, and the young Jesus were living.

  * **They went** - "The learned men went"
  * **worship** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:01:01|1:2]].
  * **their treasures** - Here "treasures" refers to the boxes or bags they used to carry their treasures. AT: "the containers that held their treasures" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **God warned them** - "Afterwards, God warned the learned men." God knew that Herod wanted to harm the child.
  * **not to return to Herod** - This can be translated as a direct quotation. AT: "saying, 'Do not go back to King Herod'" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
