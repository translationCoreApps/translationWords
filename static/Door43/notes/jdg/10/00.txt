====== Judges 10 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter begins the account of Jephthah. (See: Judges 10-12)

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Israel's punishment ==
In Judges, Israel's actions are connected to their obedience to Yahweh. When Israel does evil, they are oppressed. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:evil]] and [[:en:obe:kt:oppress]])
