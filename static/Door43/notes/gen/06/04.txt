===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Giants** - very tall, large people
  * **This happened when** - "The giants were born because"
  * **sons of God** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:gen:06:01|6:2]].
  * **These were the mighty men of old** - "These giants were the mighty men who lived long ago" or "These children grew to become the powerful fighters who lived long ago"
  * **mighty men** - men who are courageous and victorious in battle
  * **men of renown** - "famous men"
