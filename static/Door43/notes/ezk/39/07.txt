===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Gog.

  * **I will make my holy name known in the midst of my people Israel** - Here "my holy name" refers to Yahweh's character. This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will make my people, Israel, know that I am holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **Behold!** - This tells the reader to pay special attention to what follows. AT: "Look!" or "Listen!" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you!"
  * **is coming ... will take place** - These two phrases have similar meaning and emphasize the fact this this will really happen. AT: "is surely going to happen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
