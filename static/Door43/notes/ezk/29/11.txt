===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Pharaoh.

  * **No man's foot will pass through it** - Here "man's foot" represents people. AT: "No man will walk through the land of Egypt" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **no wild animal's foot will pass through it** - Here "animal's foot" represents animals. AT: "not even a wild animal will walk through it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]]) 
  * **For I will make the land of Egypt a desolation** - The abstract noun "desolation" can be expressed with the adjective "desolate." AT: "For I will make the land of Egypt desolate" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **I will scatter Egypt among the nations** - Here "Egypt" represents the people of Egypt, and "scatter" represents causing them to move to other places. AT: "I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations" or "I will make the people of Egypt go and live among the nations" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
