===== translationNotes: =====

  * **lay down my life** - "give up my life" or "die"
  * **Will you lay down your life for me?** - This remark appears in the form of a question to add emphasis to Jesus' statement. AT: "You say that you will die for me, but the truth is that you will not!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the rooster will not crow before you have denied me three times** - "you will say that you do not know me three times before the rooster crows"
