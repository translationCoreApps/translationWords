===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the one who has understanding walks a straight path** - The writer speaks of a person doing the right thing as if he were walking straight ahead on a path. AT: "the person who has understanding does what is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Plans go wrong** - "Plans fail"
  * **where there is no advice** - "when there is no one to give advice"
  * **advisors** - people who give recommendations as a guide to action
  * **they succeed** - "plans succeed"
