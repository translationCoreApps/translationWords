===== translationNotes: =====

  * **he has heard the sound of my pleading** - Here "sound" represents what the writer said. AT: "has heard what I said when I pleaded to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Yahweh is my strength** - The abstract noun "strength" can be stated as "strong." AT: "Yahweh makes me strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **my shield** - This represents Yahweh's protecting the writer. AT: "he protects me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my heart trusts** - Here "heart" represents the whole person. AT: "I trust" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **I am helped** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "he helps me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **my heart greatly rejoices** - Here "heart" represents the whole person. AT: "I greatly rejoice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Yahweh is the strength of his people** - The abstract noun "strength" can be stated as "strong." AT: "Yahweh makes his people strong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **he is the saving refuge of his anointed one** - Yahweh keeping the king safe is spoken of as if Yahweh were a place that the king could go for safety. AT: "he keeps safe the one he appointed to be king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **his anointed one** - The represents the king. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
