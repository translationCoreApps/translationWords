===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Moses continues to sing about how the people of other nations will feel when they see God's people.

  * **Terror and dread will fall on them** - These two words mean that fear will come upon them. AT: "Fear will come upon them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **dread** - Dread is extreme fear or anxiety about something that is going to happen or might happen.
  * **Because of your arm's power** - God's arm represents his great strength. AT: "Because of your great strength" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they will become as still as a stone** - Possible meanings are 1) "They will be silent like stone" or 2) "They will be motionless as stone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
