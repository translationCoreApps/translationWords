===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Answer a fool and join in on his folly** - Joining in on a fool's folly when answering him represents answering him in a foolish way. AT: "Answer a fool according to his folly" or "Answer a fool foolishly"
  * **so he will not become wise in his own eyes** - The eyes represent seeing, and seeing represents thoughts or judgment. AT: "so that he will not become wise according to his judgement" or "so that he does not consider himself to be wise" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Whoever sends a message by the hand of a fool** - Here the hand represents the fool's responsibility to deliver the message. AT: "Whoever sends a fool to deliver a message" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **cuts off his own feet and drinks violence** - Cutting off one's own feet and drinking violence represents harming one's self. AT: "harms himself like a person who cuts off his own feet and drinks violence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **drinks violence** - Violence is spoken of as if it were a liquid that someone might drink. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
