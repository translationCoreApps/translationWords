===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Who is like Yahweh our God ... at the earth?** - The writer asks this rhetorical question to emphasize that there is no one like Yahweh. AT: "There is no one like Yahweh our God ... at the earth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **who has his seat on high** - "who is enthroned on high" or "who rules in the highest position"
