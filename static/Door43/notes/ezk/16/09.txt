===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem as if the city were a young woman. The city represents the people who live there. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])

  * **embroidered** - different colored pieces of cloth sewn together to form a design.
  * **I adorned you** - "I added decoration to you"
