===== translationNotes: =====

  * **My Father is glorified in this** - You can translate this in an active form. AT: "It causes people to honor my Father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **My Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **that you bear much fruit** - Here "fruit" is a metaphor for living to please God. AT: "when you live in a way that pleases him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **are my disciples** - "show you are my disciples" or "demonstrate you are my disciples"
  * **As the Father has loved me, I have also loved you** - Jesus shares the love that God the Father has for him with those who trust in him. Here "Father" is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **remain in my love** - "continue to accept my love"
