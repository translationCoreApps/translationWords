===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - After the woman anoints Jesus with perfume, Judas promises to deliver Jesus to the chief priests.

  * **so that he might deliver him over to them** - Judas did not deliver Jesus over to them yet, rather he went to make arrangements with them. AT: "in order to arrange with them to deliver Jesus to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **When the chief priests heard it** - It may be helpful to state clearly what the chief priests heard. AT: "When the chief priests heard what he was willing to do for them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
