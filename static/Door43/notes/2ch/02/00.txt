====== 2 Chronicles 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

The story of Solomon's reign continues in this chapter. This chapter also begins a section on building the temple. (2 Chronicles 2-4 and [[:en:obe:kt:temple]])

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== House of God ==
The temple is repeated referred to as the "house of God" because Yahweh will dwell in the temple. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:houseofgod]])
