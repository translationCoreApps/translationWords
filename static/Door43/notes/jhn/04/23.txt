===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Jesus continues speaking to the Samaritan woman.

  * **However, the hour is coming, and is now here, when true worshipers will** - "However, it is now the right time for true worshipers to"
  * **the Father** - This is an important title for God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:guidelines_sonofgodprinciples]])
  * **worship in spirit and truth** - "worship him in the right way"
