===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The one seated on the throne continues to speak to John.

  * **the cowards** - "those who are too afraid to do what is right"
  * **the detestable** - "those who do terrible things"
  * **the fiery lake of burning sulfur** - "the lake of fire that burns with sulfur" (UDB) or "place full of fire that burns with sulfur." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:19:19|19:20]].
  * **the second death** - "dying a second time." This is described as eternal punishment in the lake of fire in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:20:13|20:14]] and [[:en:bible:notes:rev:21:07|21:08]]. AT: "the final death in the lake of fire." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:02:10|2:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_symlanguage]])
