===== translationNotes: =====

  * **It is a snare** - This speaks of something being dangerous as if it were a trap or a snare. AT: "It is dangerous" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **to say rashly** - to say something quickly and without careful consideration of what it might mean
  * **making his vow** - The person has made a vow declaring that something is holy and dedicated to Yahweh. AT: "dedicating it to Yahweh" or "declaring it holy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **winnows the wicked** - This speaks of the king separating the wicked people as if they were grain that he was winnowing. AT: "separates the wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the wicked** - This refers to wicked people. AT: "those who are wicked" or "the wicked people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_nominaladj]])
  * **and he turns a threshing wheel over them** - This speaks of the king punishing the wicked as if he were driving a threshing wheel over them. AT: "and he severely punishes them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **threshing wheel** - "threshing cart." This is a tool used to crush grain and help separate it from the chaff.
