===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my heart's deepest yearnings** - Here "my heart" refers to the writer. It may be helpful to state that the writer desires good health. AT: "My strongest desires" or "that I desire you to heal me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **my groanings are not hidden from you** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "you are able to see all my moans of sorrow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **My heart pounds** - This idiom means his heart beats intensely. AT: "My heart beats loudly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my strength fades** - "I become very weak"
  * **my eyesight dims** - "I can no longer see well"
