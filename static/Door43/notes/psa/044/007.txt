===== translationNotes: =====

  * **we have made our boast** - The word "boast" can be translated with a verb. AT: "we have boasted" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **we will give thanks to your name** - Here the word "name" refers to God, himself. AT: "we will give thanks to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
