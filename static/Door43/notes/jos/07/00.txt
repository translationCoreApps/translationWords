====== Joshua 07 General Notes ======


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Sin brought defeat ==

Taking what was to be destroyed  was great sin. Because of their sin, Yahweh withheld victory from the Israelites. Sin brought defeat in battle. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:sin]])
