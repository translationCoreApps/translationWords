===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The secret matters belong alone to Yahweh our God** - "Some things Yahweh our God has not revealed, and only he knows them"
  * **that are revealed** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that he has revealed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **we may do all the words of this law** - The word "words" is a metonym for "commands." AT: "we may do everything that this law commands us to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
