===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Please heal her, God, please** - Here "please" is repeated for emphasis.
  * **If her father had spit in her face** - This describes something that could have happened but did not. Spitting in someone's face was a terrible insult. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]]and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **Miriam was shut outside the camp** - Being sent out of the camp and not being allowed to go back in is spoken of as if there were a door that was closed behind her. AT: "Miriam was sent outside the camp" or "Miriam was kept outside the camp" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Miriam was shut outside the camp** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Moses shut Miriam outside the camp" or "Moses sent Miriam outside the camp" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
