===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the word that came to Jeremiah from Yahweh** - This is an idiom that is used to introduce something that God told his prophets or his people. See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:jer:01:01|1:2]]. AT: "the message that Yahweh spoke to Jeremiah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
