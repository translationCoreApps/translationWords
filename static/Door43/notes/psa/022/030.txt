===== translationNotes: =====
  * **A generation to come** - Here "A generation" represents the people of a generation. The phrase "to come" speaks about a future time as if it were something that travels and arrives somewhere. AT: "People in the future generations" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the next generation** - Here "generation" represents the people of that generation. AT: "the people of the next generation" or "their children" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **of the Lord** - "about the Lord" or "about what the Lord has done"
  * **tell of his righteousness** - The abstract noun "righteousness" can be stated as an adjective. AT: "tell of the righteous things he has done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
