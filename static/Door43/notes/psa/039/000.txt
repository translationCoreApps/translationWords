====== Psalm 039 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 39 is a psalm of deliverance from sickness. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:deliverer]]) 

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Life ==
Life on earth is short. The psalmist wanted God's healing so he can enjoy the rest of his life. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:life]]) 

This psalm shares some characteristics of another wisdom book called Ecclesiastes or Koheleth but is more positive.
