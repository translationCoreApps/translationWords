===== translationNotes: =====

  * **A cheerful heart is good medicine** - This speaks of a cheerful heart as being good medicine because it makes you feel better. AT: "A cheerful heart is like medicine that makes you feel better" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **A cheerful heart** - The "heart" represents a person's feelings, attitudes and motivations. AT: "Being cheerful" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a broken spirit** - Here the "spirit" represents a person's feelings and emotional state. A broken spirit refers to a poor emotional state. AT: "depression" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **dries up the bones** - A person's bones represent their physical health and strength. If a person's bones dry up it means that they are very sick and unhealthy. AT: "makes a person unhealthy and weak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
