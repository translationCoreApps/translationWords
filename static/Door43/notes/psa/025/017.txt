===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The troubles of my heart are enlarged** - Here "heart" represents a person's emotions. AT: "I feel more and more troubled" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **draw me out from my distress** - "bring me out of my distress." This speaks of distress as if it were a place that a person can be brought out of. AT: "rescue me from my distress" or "relieve me of my distress" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my distress** - The word "distress" is an abstract noun. AT: "the things that distress me" or "the things that cause me to be afraid" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **See my affliction** - "Notice my affliction"
  * **my affliction** - The abstract noun "affliction" can be stated as a verb. AT: "the things that afflict me" or "how afflicted I am" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **my toils** - The word "toils" is an abstract noun. AT: "the things that trouble me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **they hate me with cruel hatred** - "they hate me cruelly" or "they hate me fiercely"
