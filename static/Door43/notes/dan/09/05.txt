===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Daniel continues praying to the Lord about the people of Israel.

  * **We have sinned and have done what is wrong** - These two phrases express one idea in two different ways for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **We have acted wickedly and we have rebelled** - These two phrases express one idea in two different ways for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **your commands and decrees** - The words "commands" and "decrees" share similar meanings and refer to the whole law. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **We have not listened to your servants** - Here "not listened" means they did not obey their message. AT: "We have not obeyed the message of your prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **spoke in your name** - Here "name" refers to God's authority. AT: "spoke with your authority" or "spoke as your representative" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the people of the land** - Here "land" refers to Israel. AT: "the Israelite people" (UDB)
