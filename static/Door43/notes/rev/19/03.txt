===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They spoke** - Here "They" refers to the crowd of people in heaven. 
  * **Hallelujah** - This word means "Praise God" or "Let us praise God." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:19:01|19:1]].
  * **smoke rises from her** - This is an ellipsis. You can include the missing words that are implied. AT: "smoke of the fire that is burning in that city will rise" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **from her forever and ever** - "from the idolators forever and ever" or "from those who have prostituted themselves and will suffer forever" 
  * **from her** - Here "her" refers to Babylon. 
  * **twenty-four elders** - "24 elders." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:04|4:4]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **the four living creatures** - "the four living beings" or "the four living things." See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:04:06|4:6]]
  * **who was seated on the throne** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "who sat on the throne" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
