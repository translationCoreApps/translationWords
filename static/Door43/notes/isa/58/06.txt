===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **Is not this the fast that I choose ... break every yoke?** - Yahweh uses a question to teach the people about the kind of fast that pleases him. AT: "This is the fast that I choose ... break every yoke." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **To release wicked bonds, to undo the ropes of the yoke, to set the crushed ones free, and to break every yoke** - All of these phrases mean they should help those people whom the wicked are hurting and oppressing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **Is it not to ... your house?** - Yahweh uses another question to teach the people. AT: "It is to ... your house." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
