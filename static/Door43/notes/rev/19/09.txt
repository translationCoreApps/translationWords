===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - An angel begins to speak to John. This is likely the same angel who began to speak to John in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:17:01|17:1]].

  * **those who are invited** - You can state this in active form. AT: "the people whom God invites" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **the wedding feast of the Lamb** - Here the angel speaks of the joining of Jesus and his people forever as if it were a wedding feast. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I prostrated myself** - To prostrate oneself is to lie on the ground, face down, to show respect and willingness to serve. See note in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:19:03|19:3]].
  * **your brothers** - The word "brothers" here refers to all believers, male and female.
  * **who hold the testimony about Jesus** - Here holding stands for believing in or announcing. AT: "who speak the truth about Jesus" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **for the testimony about Jesus is the spirit of prophecy** - Here "spirit of prophecy" refers to God's Holy Spirit. AT: "because it is the Spirit of God who gives people the power to speak the truth about Jesus" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
