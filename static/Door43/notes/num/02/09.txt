===== translationNotes: =====

  * **All the number of the camp of Judah is 186,400** - This number includes all of the men in the tribes that camped under the standard of Judah. AT: "The number of the men camped under the standard of Judah is 186,400" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **They will set out first** - This means that they will set out first when the Israelite camp moves. AT: "When travelling, they will set out first" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **first** - "1st" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]])
