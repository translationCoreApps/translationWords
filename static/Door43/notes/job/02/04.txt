===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Skin for skin, indeed** - "Skin" is a metonym for Job's life. AT: "A person will do anything to save his own life, even accept the loss of possessions and loved ones." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **But stretch out your hand now and touch his bones and his flesh, and see if he does not curse you to your face** - Satan means that if God attacks Job, he will see how Job responds. AT: "But now, if you stretch out your hand and touch his bones and his flesh, you will see that he will curse you to your face"
  * **stretch out your hand** - Here "hand" refers to God's power to act. "But now use your power." Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:09|01:11]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **touch** - Here "touch" represents the action of harming. AT: "attack" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]) 
  * **his bones and his flesh** - This expression represents Job's body. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **curse you to your face** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:job:01:09|01:11]]. 
  * **to your face** - This refers to a time when God is paying attention. AT: "in your hearing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
