===== translationNotes: =====


  * **the offerings for Yahweh made by fire** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "the burnt offerings for Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that Yahweh commanded to be given them from the people of Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that Yahweh commanded the people of Israel to give to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **that he anointed the priests** - "that Moses anointed the priests"
  * **throughout all generations** - Translate these words the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:lev:03:15|3:17]].
