===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He plots evil** - "He plans evil" or "He prepares to do evil deeds"
  * **he always stirs up discord** - "he always causes discord" or "he is constantly looking for conflict and escalating it"
  * **Therefore** - "For that reason"
  * **his disaster will overtake him** - This implies that the disaster is chasing him like a person or an animal, and that it will catch him soon. AT: "his disaster will catch him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **his disaster** - This refers to the disaster that will happen to him, but also the disaster that he himself caused.
  * **in an instant; in a moment** - Both mean the same thing, and one or both of them can be replaced by "suddenly" or "very quickly".
