===== translationNotes: =====

  * **they still could not believe it because of joy** - "they were full of joy, but they could not yet believe that it was really true"
  * **ate it before them** - Jesus did this to prove that he had a physical body. Spirits would not be able to eat food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **before them** - "in front of them" or "while they were watching" (UDB)
