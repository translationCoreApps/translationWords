===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the house of Judah followed David** - Obeying David's rule is spoken of as "following" him. AT: "the tribe of Judah obeyed David as their king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **house of Judah** - Here "house" is used to mean "tribe."
