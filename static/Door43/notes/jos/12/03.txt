===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Sea of Chinnereth** - This is a place. Translate the same way as in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:11:01|11:2]].
  * **Beth Jeshimoth ... Mount Pisgah ... Ashtaroth ... Edrei ... Salecah** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Og, king of Bashan** - Translate these words in the same way you did in [[:en:bible:notes:jos:09:09|9:10]].
  * **the Rephaim ... Maacathites** - These are the names of people groups. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
