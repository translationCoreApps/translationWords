===== translationNotes: =====

  * **He looked around** - "Jesus looked around"
  * **grieving** - "deeply saddened" 
  * **the hardness of their hearts** - This metaphor describes how the Pharisees were unwilling to have compassion on the man with the withered hand. AT: "they were unwilling to have compassion on the man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Stretch out your hand** - "Reach out with your hand"
  * **Jesus restored his hand** - "Jesus healed his hand" or "Jesus made his hand the way it was before"
  * **formed a plot** - "made a plan"
  * **the Herodians** - This is the name of an informal political party that supported Herod Antipas.
  * **against him to put him to death** - The word "him" here refers to Jesus.
