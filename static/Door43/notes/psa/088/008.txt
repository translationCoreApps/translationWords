===== translationNotes: =====

  * **my acquaintances** - "those who know me"
  * **You have made me a shocking sight to them** - "You have made me a disgusting sight to them" or "Because of you, they are shocked when they see me"
  * **I am hemmed in** - The writer's physical condition that makes him repulsive to his friends is spoken of as if he were in closed into a confining space. AT: "It is as though I were in a prison" (UDB) or "I am trapped" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
