===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - God continues giving Moses instructions about the things in the tent of meeting.

  * **two tenths of an ephah** - This is about 4.5 liters. AT: "four and a half liters"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bvolume]])
  * **the table of pure gold before Yahweh** - This table is in the holy place, which is before the most holy place.
