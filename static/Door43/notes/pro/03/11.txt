===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - The writer writes as a father teaching his son.

  * **a son who pleases him** - "a son in whom he delights." This refers to the father's affection for the son, and not to the father's approval of the son's behavior. AT: "a son whom he loves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
