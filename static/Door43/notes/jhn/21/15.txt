===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Jesus begins to have a conversation with Simon Peter.

  * **do you love me ... do you love me** - Here "love" refers the type of love that comes from God, which focuses on the good of others, even when it does not benefit oneself. 
  * **you know that I love you** - When Peter answers, he uses the word for "love" that refers to brotherly love or love for a friend or family member. 
  * **Feed my lambs** - Here "lambs" is a metaphor for those persons who love Jesus and follow him. AT: "Feed the people I care for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **Take care of my sheep** - Here "sheep" is a metaphor for those who love and follow Jesus. AT: "Care for the people I care for" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
