===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh finishes speaking.

  * **They will go out** - Here "they" refers to all the people, the faithful Israelites and foreigners, who come to worship Yahweh.
  * **the worms ... and the fire** - Both clauses describe the same idea to emphasize Yahweh's punishment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the worms that eat them** - The worms represent the horror of decay and rot that are Yahweh's punishment on the wicked. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the fire that consumes** - Fire also represents Yahweh's judgment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will not be quenched** - This can be expressed positively. AT: "will burn forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **all flesh** - This expression represents all created living beings that shrink from the dead. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
