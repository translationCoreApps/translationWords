===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This continues to compare the nation of Israel with a field after it is harvested. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:isa:17:04|17:4-5]])

  * **Gleanings will be left** - The word "Gleanings" here represents the people who will still live in Israel. AT: "But there will be a few people left in Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **as when the olive tree is shaken** - People harvested olive trees by shaking them so that the olives would fall out. AT: "like the few olives that remain on the olive trees after people harvest them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **four or five** - The word "olives" is understood from the phrase before this. AT: "four or five olives" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **this is the declaration of Yahweh** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:14:21|14:22]].
  * **men will look toward their Maker ... will look to the Holy One of Israel** - Looking toward God here represents hoping that he will help them. AT: "men will hope that their Maker, the Holy One of Israel, will help them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **men will look** - The word "men" represents people in general. AT: "people will look" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **their eyes will look** - Here "eyes" represents the people who look. AT: "they will look" or "the people will look" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Holy One of Israel** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:isa:01:04|1:4]].
