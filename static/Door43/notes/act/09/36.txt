===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Luke continues the story with a new event about Peter.
  * **General Information:** - These verses give background information about the woman named Tabitha. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])

  * **Now there was** - This introduces a new part in the story. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_newevent]])
  * **Tabitha, which is translated as "Dorcas."** - Tabitha is her name in the Aramaic language, and Dorcas is her name in the Greek language. Both names mean "gazelle." AT: "Her name in the Greek language was Dorcas" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **full of good works** - "doing many good things" 
  * **It came about in those days** - This refers to the time when Peter was in Joppa. This can be stated. AT: "It came about while Peter was nearby" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **washed her** - This was washing to prepare for her burial.
  * **they laid her in an upper room** - This was a temporary display of the body during the funeral process.
