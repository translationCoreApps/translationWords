===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Asa** - This name was written as "Asaph" here. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_textvariants]])
  * **Joram the father of Uzziah** - Joram was actually Uzziah's grandfather's grandfather, so "father" can be translated as "ancestor" (UDB).
  * **Joram** - "Joram" was also known as Jehoram (UDB). (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_textvariants]])
