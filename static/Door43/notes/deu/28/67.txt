===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses speaks to the Israelites as if they were one man, so the words "you" and "your" here are singular. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])

  * **because of the fear in your hearts** - This is an idiom. AT: "because of the fear you are feeling" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the things your eyes will have to see** - Here "eyes" refers to the whole person. AT: "the terrifying things I will force you to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **I had said** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh.
