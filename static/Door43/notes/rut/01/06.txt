===== translationNotes: =====

  * **she had heard in the region of Moab** - "while Naomi was living in Moab she heard." It is implied that the news came from Israel. AT: "she heard from Israel while in the region of Moab" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Yahweh** - This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this.
  * **had helped his people in need** - God saw their need and provided good harvests for them.
  * **daughters-in-law** - the women who married Naomi's sons
  * **they walked down the road** - "they walked along the road." To walk down a road is an expression for walking away. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
