===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Sanballat, Tobiah, and Geshem** - These are the names of men.  Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:02:09|2:9-10]]. (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **I had rebuilt the wall ... I had not yet** - Nehemiah supervised the rebuilding of the wall. He did not build it by himself. AT: "we had rebuilt the wall ... we had not yet" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **any sections** - This refers to sections of the wall. AT: "any sections of the wall" or "any gaps in the city wall" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **sent to me** - This means that they sent a messenger with a message. AT: "sent a messenger to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Ono** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
