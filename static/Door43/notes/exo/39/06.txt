===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Bezalel's work crew continues to make the priestly garments.

 
  * **signet** - This was an engraved stone that was used to stamp a design in a wax seal. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:28:10|28:11]].
  * **twelve sons** - "12 sons" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **as Yahweh had commanded Moses** - "just as Yahweh told Moses to do." See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:39:01|39:01]].
