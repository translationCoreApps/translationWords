===== translationNotes: =====

  * **You see the trouble** - Here "you" is plural, referring to all the people mentioned in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:02:15|02:16]]. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
  * **its gates have been destroyed by fire** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "how our enemies destroyed its gates by fire" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **so we will no longer be in disgrace** - "so we will no longer be ashamed"
  * **the good hand of my God was on me** - God's "good hand" represents his "favor." AT: "my God's favor was upon me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **rise up and build** - This is an idiom. AT: "begin building" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **So they strengthened their hands for the good work** - The phrase "strengthened their hands" means to prepare to do something. AT: "So they prepared do this good work" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
