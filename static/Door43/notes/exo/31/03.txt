===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.

  * **I have filled Bezalel with my Spirit** - Yahweh speaks of giving Bezalel his Spirit as if Bezalel were a container and God's Spirit were a liquid. AT: "I have given my Spirit to Bezalel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **for all kinds of craftsmanship** - The abstract noun "craftsmanship" can be translated as "making crafts" or "making things." AT: "for making all kinds of crafts" or "so that he can make all kinds of things" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
