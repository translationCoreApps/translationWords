===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Adonijah comes to speak to Bathsheba.

  * **peacefully** - "with no desire to cause harm"
  * **all Israel** - This is a generalization. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **things changed** - "what we expected to happen did not happen"
  * **the kingdom was given to my brother** - This can be translated in active form. AT: "Yahweh gave the kingdom to my brother" or "my brother became king" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
