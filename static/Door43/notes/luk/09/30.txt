===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Behold** - The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
  * **there were two men talking** - This phrase introduces two new characters in the story. AT: "suddenly there were two men talking" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_participants]])
  * **who looked glorious** - This phrase gives information about how Moses and Elijah looked. Some languages would translate it as a sentence. AT: "and they looked glorious" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_distinguish]])
  * **his departure** - "his leaving" or "how he would leave this world." This was a polite way of talking about his death. AT: "his death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
