===== translationNotes: =====

  * **be earnest and repent** - "be serious and repent"
  * **I am standing at the door and am knocking** - Jesus speaks about wanting people to relate to him as if he wanted them to invite him into their home. AT: "I am like one standing at the door and knocking" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **and am knocking** - When people want someone to welcome them into their home, they knock on the door. AT: "and I want you to let me come inside" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
  * **hears my voice** - The phrase "my voice" refers to Christ speaking. AT: "hears me speak" or "hears me call" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I will come into his home** - Some languages might prefer the verb "go" here. AT: "I will go in to his home" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_go]])
  * **and will eat with him** - This represents being together as friends. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
