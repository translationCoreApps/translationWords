===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the beginning of the list of David's descendants who became king.

  * **Solomon's son was Rehoboam. Rehoboam's son was Abijah** - Solomon had more than one son. The same is true of other men in the list. AT: "Solomon was the father of Rehoboam. Rehoboam was the father of Abijah"
  * **Azariah** - This was another name for Uzziah, the better-known name for this king. Translators may decide to use "Uzziah" everywhere for this king.
