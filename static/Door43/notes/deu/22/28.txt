===== translationNotes: =====

  * **but who is not engaged** - "but whose parents have not promised another man that she will marry him"
  * **and if they are discovered** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "and if somebody finds out what happened" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fifty shekels of silver** - A shekel weighs eleven grams. AT: "fifty pieces of silver" or "550 grams of silver" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bmoney]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **He may not send her away during all his days** - This is an idiom. AT: "He must never divorce her during his entire life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
