====== Psalm 019 General Notes ======


=== Type of psalm ===

Psalm 19 is a praise psalm, praising God for his creation and for his law.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== God's law ==
God's law keeps people from sinning. There are two distinct themes in this psalm. Verses 1-6 are God's glory in the heavens and the second is praising God for his law. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:lawofmoses]], [[:en:obe:kt:glory]] and [[:en:obe:kt:heaven]])
