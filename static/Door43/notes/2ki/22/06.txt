===== translationNotes: =====


  * **General Information:** - The message from King Josiah to Hilkiah, the high priest, continues.

  * **Let them give money ... was given to them ... because they handled** - Here "them" and "they" refer to the workmen who are in charge of the house of Yahweh in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:22:03|22:5]].
  * **the carpenters, the builders, and the masons** - These are the same as the workmen who are in the house of Yahweh in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:22:03|22:5]]. Here the workers are described in more detail.
  * **carpenters** - workers who build with wood
  * **masons** - workers who build with stone
  * **no accounting was required for the money that was given to them** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "the workmen who were in charge did not have to report how they used the money that the temple guards gave them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **because they handled it faithfully** - "because they used the money honestly"
