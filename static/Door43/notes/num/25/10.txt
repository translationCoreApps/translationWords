===== translationNotes: =====

  * **turned my rage away from the people of Israel** - God's rage is spoken of as if it were something that could be physically pushed aside in order to stop it. AT: "caused me to no longer be angry with the people of Israel" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I have not consumed the people of Israel in my fierceness** - God is spoken of as if he were a fierce animal that could have eaten up the people of Israel. AT: "I have not destroyed the people of Israel in my terrible anger" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
