===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Tyre. The city of Tyre continues to be described as if it were a wrecked ship. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **General Information:** - The words "you" and "your" in these verses refer to Tyre.
 
  * **Cities at the sea will tremble** - Here "Cities at the sea" represents the people in those cities. AT: "The people of the cities by the sea" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **All those who handle oars** - "All the rowers"
  * **wail bitterly** - "cry out sadly"
  * **they will cast dust up on their heads. They will roll about in ashes** - These are expressions of grieving and mourning. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_symaction]])
