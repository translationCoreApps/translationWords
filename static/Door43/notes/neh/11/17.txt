===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Mattaniah ... Mica ... Zabdi ... Asaph ... Bakbukiah ... Abda ... Shammua ... Galal ... Jeduthun** - These are male names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who began the thanksgiving in prayer** - That is, who directed the singers (see UDB).
  * **his associates** - "his brothers"
  * **In all the holy city were 284 Levites** - "All the Levites in the holy city were 284" or "There were, in all, 284 Levites in the holy city." See how you translated numbers in [[:en:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].
  * **the holy city** - This expression refers to the city of Jerusalem.
