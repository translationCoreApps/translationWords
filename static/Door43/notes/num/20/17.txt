===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The messengers continue speaking to the king of Edom.

  * **We will not turn aside to the right hand or to the left** - Here "turn aside"  represents leaving the road. AT: "We will not leave the road in any direction" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **the king's highway** - This is the main road that connects Damascus in the north to the Gulf of Aqabah in the south.
