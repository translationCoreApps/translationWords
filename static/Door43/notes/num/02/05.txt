===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues telling Moses where each tribe and its army will camp around the tent of meeting. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **Nethanel son of Zuar** - Translate this man's name the same way that you did in [[:en:bible:notes:num:01:07|1:8]].
  * **division** - This is a military term for a large group of soldiers. Each tribe was its own "division."
