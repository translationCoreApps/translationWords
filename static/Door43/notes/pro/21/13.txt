===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The one who shuts his ears to the cry of the poor** - This is an idiom. AT: "The one who will not listen when poor people ask for help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will not be answered** - The word "answered" is a metonym for a person hearing another person ask for help and acting to help. This can be translated in active form. AT: "no one will do anything to help him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **appeases anger** - "makes an angry person feel better so he is no longer angry"
