===== translationNotes: =====

  * **But Amaziah would not listen** - Here "listen" refers to obeying the warning. AT: "However, Amaziah would not obey Jehoash's warning" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **So Jehoash king of Israel attacked ... Amaziah king of Judah met each other** - The armies of these kings went to battle with them. AT: "So Jehoash and his army went to fight Amaziah and his army and they met each other" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **Beth Shemesh** - This is a town in Judah near the border of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Judah was defeated by Israel** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "Israel defeated Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **every man fled home** - "all the men in the army of Judah ran home"
