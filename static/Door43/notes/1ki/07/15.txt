===== translationNotes: =====

  * **eighteen cubits ... twelve cubits ... five cubits** - A cubit is about 46 centimeters. AT: "about 8.3 meters ... 5.5 meters ... 2.3 cubits" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **in circumference** - Circumference is the distance or measurement around a circular object or area.
  * **two capitals** - decorations on top of each of the two pillars
  * **polished bronze** - They polished the bronze so that it would reflect sunlight. "gleaming bronze" (UDB)
  * **Checker latticework and wreaths of chain work** - "Crossed metal strips woven together and metal chains twisted together"
