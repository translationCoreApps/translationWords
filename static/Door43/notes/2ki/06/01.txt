===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The sons of the prophets** - This does not mean that they were the sons of prophets, but rather, that they were a group of prophets. See how you translated this phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:02:03|2:3]]. AT: "The group of prophets" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **let us go to the Jordan** - This refers to the area by the Jordan River. AT: "let us go beside the Jordan River" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **your servants** - Here one of the prophet refers to the of the sons of the prophets as Elisha's servants to show him honor.
