===== translationNotes: =====

  * **God has torn me in his wrath and persecuted me ... as he tears me apart** - This speaks of God causing Job pain as if he were a wild animal and Job were his prey that he was killing. AT: "Because God is very angry with me, it is as though he were a wild animal that tore my body apart with his teeth because he was my enemy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **my enemy** - Job refers to God as his "enemy" as he describes how he has caused him great pain. 
  * **fastens his eyes on me** - This is an idiom. AT: "glares at me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **People have gaped with open mouth** - To "gape" means to stare in amazement with open mouth.
