===== translationNotes: =====

  * **and wine** - The ellipsis can be filled in. AT: "and give wine" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
  * **in bitter distress** - "who's souls are bitter" or "who are in misery"
  * **his poverty** - The abstract noun "poverty" can be translated as an adjective. AT: "how poor he is" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **his trouble** - The abstract noun "trouble" can be translated as a clause. AT: "the bad things that are happening to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
