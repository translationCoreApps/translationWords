===== translationNotes: =====

  * **I called on your name** - AT: "I called to you for help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **from the depths of the pit** - Possible meanings are 1) "from a very deep well" or 2) "from the grave."
  * **You heard my voice** - AT: "You heard what I said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Do not close your ear** - AT: "Do not refuse to listen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
