====== Isaiah 54 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of this chapter, which is an extended quotation.

=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Yahweh's covenant faithfulness ==
This chapter focuses on Yahweh's faithfulness to fulfill his covenants. It references several different covenant promises in this chapter. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:covenantfaith]], [[:en:obe:kt:faithful]], [[:en:obe:kt:fulfill]], [[:en:obe:kt:covenant]] and [[:en:obe:kt:promise]])
