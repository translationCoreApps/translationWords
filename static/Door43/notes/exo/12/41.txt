===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Yahweh's armed groups** - This refers to the tribes of Israel. See how you translated "armed group" in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:12:17|12:17]].
  * **to be observed by all the Israelites** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "that all the Israelites were to observe" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **all the Israelites throughout their people's generations** - "all the Israelites and the all the generations of their descendants"
