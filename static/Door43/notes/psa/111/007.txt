===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The works of his hands** - Here the word "hands" refers to Yahweh, himself. AT: "The works that he does" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **They are established forever** - This means that Yahweh's instructions are unchangeable and will last forever. This can be stated in active form. AT: "He has established them forever" or "They will last forever" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **to be observed faithfully and properly** - Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh has given his instructions in a faithful and proper manner or 2) his people are to observe Yahweh's instructions in a faithful and proper manner. This can be stated in active form. AT: "for his people to observe them faithfully and properly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **holy and awesome is his name** - Here the word "name" refers to Yahweh, himself. AT: "Yahweh is holy and awesome" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
