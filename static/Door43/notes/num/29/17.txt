===== translationNotes: =====

  * **the second day of the assembly** - "the 2nd day of the festival." Here the word "assembly" refers to the Festival of Weeks. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_ordinal]]
  * **twelve young bulls, two rams, and fourteen male lambs** - "12 young bulls, 2 rams, and 14 male lambs" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **as were commanded** - This can be stated in active form. "as Yahweh commanded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **its grain offering, and their drink offerings** - The grain offering was to be offered with the burnt offering and drink offerings were to be offered with both the sin offering and the burnt offering. AT: "along with the grain offering and the drink offerings that accompany them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_possession]])
