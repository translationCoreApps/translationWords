===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They went out** - Here the word "They" refers to the Israelite army.
  * **thirty-two lesser kings** - Translate this as you did in [[:en:bible:notes:1ki:20:01|20:1]].
  * **Ben Hadad was informed by scouts that he had sent out** - This can be expressed in active form. AT: "Scouts that Ben Hadad had sent out informed him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **scouts** - A "scout" is a soldier sent to gather information about the enemy.
