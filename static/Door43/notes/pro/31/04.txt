===== translationNotes: =====


  * **Lemuel** - This is the name of a man. Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:31:01|31:1]].
  * **what has been decreed** - This can be translated in active form. Possible meanings are 1) "what God has decreed" or 2) "what the kings themselves have decreed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **pervert** - "change" or "twist" or "deny"
