====== Joshua 02 General Notes ======


=== Structure and formatting ===

This chapter begins the story of the conquest of the Promised Land. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:promisedland]])


=== Special concepts in this chapter ===

== Rahab's faith ==

Rahab expressed her faith in Yahweh. The statement "for Yahweh your God, he is God in heaven above and on the earth below" is a recognition of her faith. (See: [[:en:obe:kt:faith]] and [[:en:obe:kt:heaven]])


=== Important figures of speech in this chapter ===

== Metaphor ==

The Israelites were to completely destroy the evil Canaanites. They are compared to melting snow: "... melting away because of us."  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:obe:kt:evil]])
