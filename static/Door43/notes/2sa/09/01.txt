===== translationNotes: =====

  * **for Jonathan's sake** - "because of my love for Jonathan"
  * **Jonathan's sake** - Jonathan was Saul's son and David's best friend.
  * **Ziba** - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **I am your servant** - Ziba calls himself "your servant" to show great respect to David.
