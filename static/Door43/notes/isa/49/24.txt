===== translationNotes: =====

  * **spoils** - valuable items taken from the losers in a battle
  * **Can the spoils be taken from the warrior, or captives be rescued from the fierce?** - Isaiah uses questions to emphasize that a person cannot take anything from a mighty soldier or a very strong warrior. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **the captives will be taken away from the warrior, and spoils will be rescued** - This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will take the captives from the warrior, and I will get back the spoils" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **your adversary ... your children** - Here "your" refers to the city of Zion as if it were a person who has enemies and the people who live in Zion are her children. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
