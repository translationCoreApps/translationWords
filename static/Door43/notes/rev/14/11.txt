===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - The third angel continues to speak.

  * **The smoke from their torment** - The phrase "their torment" refers to the fire that torments them. AT: "The smoke from the fire that torments them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **they have no rest** - "they have no relief" or  "the torment does not stop" 
  * **day or night** - These two parts of the day are used to refer to all of the time. AT: "ever"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **Here is a call for the patient endurance of those who are holy** - "Those who are holy must endure patiently and be faithful." See how you translated a similar phrase in [[:en:bible:notes:rev:13:09|13:10]].
