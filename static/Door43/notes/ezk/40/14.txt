===== translationNotes: =====

  * **guard chambers** - Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:7]].
  * **sixty cubits** - about thirty-two meters (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_bdistance]])
  * **cubits** - Translate these "long" cubits as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:40:05|40:05]].
  * **portico** - This was covering in front of an entrance with columns or posts for support. Translate this as in [[:en:bible:notes:ezk:08:16|8:16]].
  * **fifty cubits** - about twenty-seven meters
  * **closed windows** - Possible meanings are 1) "closed windows or 2) "windows with shutters" or 3) "narrow windows."
  * **likewise for the porches** - "the porches also had closed window all around"
  * **each jamb** - jambs are the wooden frame of a door
