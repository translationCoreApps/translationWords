===== translationNotes: =====

  * **when my lips open** - Here "lips" represents a person's mouth, which which he speaks. AT: "when I open my mouth to speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **upright** - "proper" or "just"
  * **my mouth speaks** - Here "mouth" represents a person who speaks. AT: "I speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **what is trustworthy** - "what people should believe"
  * **wickedness is disgusting to my lips** - Here "lips" represents a person who is speaking. AT: "wickedness is disgusting to me" or "saying wicked things would be disgusting to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **wickedness** - Here the abstract noun "wickedness" represents wicked speech. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
