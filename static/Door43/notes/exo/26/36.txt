===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Yahweh continues to tell Moses how to construct the tabernacle.

  * **a hanging** - This was a large curtain made of cloth.
  * **blue, purple, and scarlet material** - Possible meanings are 1) "yarn that is dyed blue, purple, and scarlet," probably wool yarn, or 2) "blue, purple, and scarlet dye" to dye the linen. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:exo:25:03|25:4]].
  * **fine twined linen** - "finely twisted linen." This was cloth made from fine linen threads that someone twisted together to make a stronger thread.  
  * **an embroiderer** - "a person who sews designs into cloth" or "a person who embroiders"
