===== translationNotes: =====

  * **torn the kingdom out of your hand** - Samuel speaks as if the kingdom were a physical object that could be held in the hand and grabbed out by another person. AT: "made you so you are no longer king" or "made another person king in your place" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
