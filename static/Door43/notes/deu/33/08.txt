===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - Moses continues to bless the tribes of Israel, which he began to do in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:33:01|33:2]]; the blessings are short poems. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Your Thummim and your Urim** - These were sacred stones the high priest carried on his breastplate and used at times to determine God's will. Here "your" refers to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **your loyal one** - This refers to the tribe of Levi. AT: "your holy one" or "the one who seeks to please you"
  * **Massah** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:06:16|6:16]]. Translator may add a footnote that says: "The name 'Massah' means 'testing.'"
  * **Meribah** - Translate as in [[:en:bible:notes:deu:32:50|32:51]]. Translator may add a footnote that says: "The name 'Meribah' means 'argue' or 'quarrel.'"
