===== translationNotes: =====

  * **The anger of Yahweh burned against Israel** - AT: "Then Yahweh had an intense anger against Israel,"
  * **handed them over to Hazael king of Aram, and to Ben Hadad son of Hazael** - Here "them" refers to Israel. AT: "allowed Hazael the king of Aram, and Ben Hadad, his son, to repeatedly defeat the Israelites in battle."
  * **implored Yahweh** - "prayed to Yahweh,"
