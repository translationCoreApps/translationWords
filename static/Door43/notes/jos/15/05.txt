===== translationNotes: =====

  * **at the mouth of the Jordan** - The point as which the river empties into the sea is spoken of as if it were the mouth of the river. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **border ... ran** - "border ... was"
  * **Beth Hoglah ... Beth Arabah** - These are the names of places. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **the Stone of Bohan** - This was likely a large stone that someone set up as a landmark and named after the man, Bohan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
