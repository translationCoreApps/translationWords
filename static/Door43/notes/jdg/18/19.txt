===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Is it better for you to be priest for the house of one man ... a clan in Israel?** - They ask this as a rhetorical question to imply that it is true. This question can be written as a statement. AT: "It better for you to be priest for  a tribe and a clan in Israel than to just the house of one man." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **The priest's heart was glad** - Here the priest is referred to by his "heart" to emphasize his emotions. AT: "The priest was glad" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
