===== translationNotes: =====

  * **They were completely amazed** - If you need to be more specific, it can stated what they were amazed by. AT: "They were completely amazed at what he had done" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **their hearts were hardened** - The disciples did yet fully understand Jesus' power. This is a metaphor where "hearts" represents minds, the thinking ability of people. AT: "their minds did not understand" or "they did not understand" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **what the loaves meant** - The phrase "the loaves" refers to when Jesus had multiplied the loaves of bread. This can be stated more clearly. AT: "what it meant when he had multiplied the loaves of bread" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
