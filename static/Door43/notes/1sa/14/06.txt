===== translationNotes: =====

  * **his young armor bearer** - This was a teenage boy who was responsible for caring for his master's weapons of war. See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:1sa:14:01|14:1]].
  * **uncircumcised fellows** - a derogatory term used for non-Jewish men 
  * **work on our behalf** - "work for our support" or "help us"
  * **nothing can stop Yahweh from saving** - This double negative can be stated in positive form. AT: "Yahweh can save" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
  * **by many or by few people** - These extremes also include everything in between. AT: "by any number of people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **everything that is in your heart** - Here "heart" refers to Jonathan's desires. AT: "everything that you desire to do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
