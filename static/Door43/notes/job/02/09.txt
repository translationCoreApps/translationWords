===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Do you still hold fast to your integrity?** - This rhetorical question represents a statement. AT: "You should not still be holding fast to your integrity." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Curse God** - "Reject God"
  * **You talk as a foolish woman** - Job is saying she talks like a fool. AT: "You talk as if you were a stupid woman."
  * **Should we receive the good from God and not receive the bad?** - This rhetorical question represents a statement. AT: "We should certainly receive the bad from God as well as the good." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **receive the good** - "benefit from all the good things"
  * **the good** - This represents all the good things that God gives us. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **receive the bad** - "suffer all the bad things without complaining"
  * **the bad** - This represents all the bad things that God makes or allows us to experience. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_genericnoun]])
  * **sin with his lips** - Here "lips" represents the act of speaking. AT: "sin by speaking against God" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
