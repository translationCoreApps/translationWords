===== translationNotes: =====

  * **Connecting Statement:** - James begins to speak to the apostles and elders. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:act:15:05|15:6]])
  * **General Information:** - Here the word "they" refers to Paul and Barnabas. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:act:15:12|15:12]])

  * **Brothers** - "Fellow believers"
  * **in order to take from them a people** - "so that he might choose from among them a people"
  * **for his name** - "for God's name." Here "name" refers to God. AT: "for himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
