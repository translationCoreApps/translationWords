===== translationNotes: =====
 
  * **love covers over** - Love acts like a person who quiets trouble between people instead of stirring it up. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **on the lips of a discerning person** - "Lips" represents what a person says. AT: "in what a sensible person says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **a rod is for the back** - "Rod" represents strong, physical punishment and "the back" represents the person who receives the punishment. AT: "a person who has no sense needs forceful punishment." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
