===== translationNotes: =====

  * **General Information:** - This is the last event in the story of Paul and Silas in Philippi. (See: [[:en:bible:notes:act:16:11|16:12]])

  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here Luke tells the last event in the story that started in [[:en:bible:notes:act:16:16|16:16]].
  * **sent word to the guards** - Here "word" stands for "message" or "command." AT: "sent a message to the guards" or "sent a command to the guards" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **sent word** - Here "sent" means the magistrates told someone to go tell the guards their message.
  * **Let those men go** - "Release those men" or "Allow those men to leave"
  * **come out** - "come outside of the jail"
